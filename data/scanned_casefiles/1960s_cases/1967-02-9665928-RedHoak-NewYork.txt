11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was a wide rectangular strip. Yellow is white with more white. One red light 1/3 from left side. One red light center of object. Red lights were pulsating. There were lights only. No definite discernable outline. Object moved due West and disappeared below the horizons. Remained stationary at 45 degrees elevation for approx. 45 minutes, then started moving slowly due West and disappeared below horizon 15 min. later. Smooth steady flight path.
