10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Four air sightings of bright flash or explosion. Reports of white light moving in southern sky toward horizon at rate of shooting star. Short tail of sparks or debris & obj exploded with sufficient brilliance to light up sea for 5-10 seconds. Explosion described by some witness as like shower of sparks from sky rocket. MERINT rpt from USS Dunkirk.
