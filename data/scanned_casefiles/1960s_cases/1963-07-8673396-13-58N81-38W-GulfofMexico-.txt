At 0236 GMT on 9 July 1963 in Latitude 13-58N, Longitude 81-38W, course 351°true
Speed 15 Knots, bound Houston from the Panama Canal a satellite was observed.
The satellite was first observed at an altitude of 15°, bearing 366°, adjacent to
Star Liber'a lower describing what appeared to be a somewhat erratic course in a
southeasterly direction. The satellite disappeared near Star Vega at an altitude
of 56°, bearing 045°. The satellite was of the same brightness as the star Vega and
was in sight for 7½ minutes.
