11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers were looking in the West sky, when they noted a balloon type yellow object traveling toward the east at terrific speed. Object was visible for about 3 minutes before it disappeared behind a building. There was no sound, trail.

The sighting occurred at approximately sunset. From the information available it is possible that witnesses observed the sun's reflection off of an aircraft.
