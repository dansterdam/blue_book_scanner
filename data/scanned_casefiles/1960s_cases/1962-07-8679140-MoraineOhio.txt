10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Tim Barns and wife while driving east on Rt. 725 sighted two obj with bright intensity. They stopped car and watched obj for some time. Witness thought the obj were not aircraft. Remained stationary over a field. Obj remained in sight approx 15 min.

11. COMMENTS
Sighting was terminal. Location - Moraine, Ohio. Station - Wright Patterson AFB. Probably bright star Vega, which was in observation clearance.
