Here is the text from the document:

27 APC

May 2, 1962
Philadelphia, 50, Pennsylvania

National Aeronautical and Space Administration
17th Street
Washington D.C.

Dear Sirs,

We would like to report a flying object that was seen on Friday April 27, 1962 at approximately 8:20 P.M. E.S.T.

The object was roughly in the shape of a Mercury Capsule. It went out of range after less than ten seconds. It was going in a north-easterly direction. There was a sphere of light in the center. The light shone continuously. There was no sound whatsoever. Would you kindly send any available information on Unidentified Flying Objects to the first of the following addresses. Thank you.

Sincerely,
[Signature area blacked out]
Philadelphia, 50, Pennsylvania
