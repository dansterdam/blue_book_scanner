PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 3 September 66 04/01/0Z

2. LOCATION: Duluth, Floodwood, Minnesota (4 Witnesses)

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 5 - 45 Seconds

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: W

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Astro (METEOR)

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observers noted bullet, tear drop, cylinder shaped object that was blue and orange, red, white, and blue, burning white changing to blue green. One witness reported long red tail. Object was seen overhead coming down at 35-45 deg angle. Last witness saw it turn out and fall into swamp.
