Place: Centerville, Ohio
Phone: Tuytle 5-7765

One bright light, golden in color, size of a star, was seen in the north at about 20 degrees elevation. It hovered for about 5-10 minutes, then moved to the East slowly, picking up speed as it moved, then moved behind some buildings where it was lost observed.

The light was observed for about 10 minutes.

The witness who watched it with her sister appeared a desire to know what it was and requested she be called in the evening.
