10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Brilliant ball observed for some while. Passed over several thin clouds. Appeared white, occasionally red. Initial observation at 20:55 local. Moved slowly. Disappeared at 10 deg above 100 deg azimuth.

11. COMMENTS
Only had characteristics of astronomical body, however, to report was wrong way. Following up indicated initial was not astronomical. Verified with Central AF Tracking Station. Plotting showed object as the evening star, Jupiter was at 10 deg elev 100 deg azimuth. Was noted to this SE & might be evaluated as planet Jupiter.
