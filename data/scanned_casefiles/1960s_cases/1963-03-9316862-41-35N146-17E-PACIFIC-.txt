PROJECT 10073 RECORD CARD

1. DATE: 12 March 1963

2. LOCATION: 41.35N 146.17E (Pacific)

3. DATE-TIME GROUP: Local GMT 12/15072

4. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

5. PHOTOS: XE No

6. SOURCE: Northwest Airlines

7. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: two seconds

8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: one

9. COURSE: Stationary (?)

10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: Bright flash in sky, blue in color observed at 225 dar, just above the horizon. Considered possible meteor, although no movement was observed.

11. COMMENTS: Proximity to horizon and angle of entry could give the impression of little motion considering the short duration of the sighting. Since pilot considered the object possibly meteor, case is carried in the meteor category.

12. CONCLUSIONS: Was Astronomical Meteor
