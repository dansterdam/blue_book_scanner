11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
One round object, bright silver, hazy red and green around edges occasionally. Observed to be at 10 deg elevation, moved over the base at a fast speed to the North. Object then stopped and moved to the SSW for 22 minutes. Seemed to be moving in a circular direction and moved up out of sight. Visible for one hour 27 minutes. Observed with Bx.
