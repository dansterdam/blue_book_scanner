10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Ellipsoid with long direction pointing in direction of travel. Bright white to blue. While belgoobserved a piece fell off of main body and turned out. Too fast and bright for c/c. Too slow for meteor. Tail 7X as long as main body. No sound. Got dimmer as it crossed sky. Initial observation at 20-30 deg elevation in NE. Disappeared at 20-30 degs in SE. Sighting at night. Object visible 5-10 seconds. Believed possible reentry. Rumor that Airline crew also observed obj. No confirmation as to date.
