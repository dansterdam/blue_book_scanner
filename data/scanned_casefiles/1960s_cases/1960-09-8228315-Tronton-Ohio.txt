0030 - Received the following collect call from Mr. in Trenton, Ohio. Call Nr. individual claimed to have tried contacted interested personnel since 2230 including his local CAP, but to no avail.

"UFO sighted at 2200 in direction of Ohio River from Ironton, Ohio at about location of 2 o'clock in the sky. UFO looks like a bright star rotating in a clockwise direction and then in a counterclockwise motion. Occasionally will go right or left but will return to original pattern. While in a counterclockwise rotation the UFO will increase speed tremendously and a gust of light could be seen behind it. UFO still seen at time of call. In addition, a radio announcer at station WRIO in Trenton observed the UFO with Mr."
