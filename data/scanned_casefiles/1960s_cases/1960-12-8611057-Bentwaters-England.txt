10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Found, size of half dollar, bright green in center, yellowish green at sides. Appeared to move at high rate of speed. Appeared 55-60° elevation, 30° azimuth. Disappeared 25-30° elevation, 310° azimuth. Straight flight path. Object appeared to have rotating motion. Disappeared behind cloud deck, reported meteor sighting at same time 4 mi. N.

11. COMMENTS
There were no satellites neartering at this time. Description and duration are characteristic of a meteor.
