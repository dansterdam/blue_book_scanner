11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer watched a round shaped object move toward the north. Another object was observed in the eastern sky. The object in the east appeared to be getting lower before disappearing. Three different objects were seen during a two minute period. A red light was connected with the objects.

7. COURSE
North & East

☐ Yes
☒ No

At 03/0200Z the 97th BW executed an exercise during which 26 aircraft departed the base in two waves during a period of four hours. At 0215Z aircraft began to take off following the remaining aircraft in the wave at approximately one minute intervals.

☐ Yes
☒ No
