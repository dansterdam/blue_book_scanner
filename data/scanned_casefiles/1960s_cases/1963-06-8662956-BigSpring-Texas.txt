10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Firey or steady burning object, generally bluish green turning to white before fading observed to appear to separate into two pieces. No tail or trail. At explosion or separation, sound heard. Generally SW to NE flight. Brightness and slowness characterized flight, with duration 5 sec to 1 min. Sighting both ground and air visual. One sighting believed object meteorite and helo search made over area where impact appeared to be indicated. Search extended to 4 hrs without result.
