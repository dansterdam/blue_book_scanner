No

9. CONCLUSION
Aircraft (possible)

10. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noticed a red and white blinking light in the SSW part of the sky. The light intensity varied from very dim to very bright. It then moved slowly then stopped, dim to very bright. It then moved vertically at a high rate of speed, and disappeared.

Several aircraft were in the area at the time of sighting. The description is consistent with that of an aircraft.
