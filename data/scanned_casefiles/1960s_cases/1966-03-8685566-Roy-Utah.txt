11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers reported sighting 11 object maneuvering above the area of observation point at 184.52. The object were moving in diverse directions and individually and collectively fading from view. Three objects observed above and to the rear of approaching aircraft, two side by side, one following, 7 or 8 appeared to be moving in a circle at an extreme elevation. There were several aircraft in the area at the time of sighting. There was nothing unusual on radar reported.
