10. CONCLUSION
INSUFFICIENT DATA
The report was not called into the local AF Base till long after the sightings. The observer never saw the object fly, it can not therefore strictly fall under the heading "UFO". The observer but were not listed.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer stated that he saw a shiny yellow object that was pointed at the top, tent shaped, and sat on 4 legs. The object was larger than a car and smaller than a house. Observer left area because of a rainstorm.

Covers CONTD: A Form 164 and later a Form 17 were sent to observer requesting more information and a first hand account of the sighting but neither were returned.
