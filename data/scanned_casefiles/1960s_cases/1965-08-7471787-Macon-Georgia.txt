4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS
One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
5 minutes

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers saw a bell-shaped amber object. Object was
approx 15-50 deg above the horizon in a 30 deg azimuth.
No movement or maneuvers of object were observed. There
were heavy cloud formations in the area and two a/c were
in the area at the time. A cloud cell appeared on radar
in the general area of sighting.
