11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
No definite shape of the objects, but about the size of a golf ball and yellow in color. When object was first sighted, it was at a 20 deg angle to horizon, and at 45 deg on horizon when it disappeared behind a building. 26 Sept. 1967

Called UFO officer at Waverly AF Station. Asked if he had positively identified the object and if anyone at the control tower had been seeing anything. He replied that no one at the tower had seen any thing, and he was almost positive that it was a balloon.
