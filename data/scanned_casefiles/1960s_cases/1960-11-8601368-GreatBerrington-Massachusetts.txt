10. BRIEF SUMMARY
About 9pm just jagged a pinpoint at west. About 3 in diameter at arm's length. Glowed red occasionally flashing bluish-green. Two still; one moving forward. Formed a triangle approx 45° above horizon. 2 still objects disappeared after about 20 min in same position. Third moved in a straight line directly overhead then went behind light - lost and disappeared - was visible about 3 min.

11. COMMENTS
Witness probably saw a combination of things with audio. A cloud of light, stationary objects were probably stars and moving object an a/c. Three were bright stars in the F. Etchells, Mugel, and Aldebaran. It is believed 2 still objects were Delta Aquarii and Alpha Orion. Third object viewed by witness was probably an a/c. A cloud rendering bright witness and stars could account for the appearance.
