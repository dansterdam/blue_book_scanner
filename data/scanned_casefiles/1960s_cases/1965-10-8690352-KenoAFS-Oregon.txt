11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
FIRST OBJECT: Round, white. Both objects approximately the size of a Satellite. No tail or trail. Disappeared over Western horizon. Traveled East to West in a straight course, and was observed for 4 minutes.

SECOND OBJECT: Round, covered with flame off fire and trailing flame. Flame colored (orange). Trail, about twice as long as object. Almost directly overhead, North to South on a straight line course. Observed about one minute after first observation.
