11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was pie shaped, flat on top, about the size of a dime held at arms length, white in color and appeared to have something dangling from the bottom. Object appeared to rotate white in flight. Moved from the SE to W to E. Made a circle and went up and down with vibrating motion. Moving fast at times. Disappeared over tree.
