11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was oval in shape, black in color, and about the size of a penny. There was a cloud formation above the object. Object was seen directly overhead and was circling. A six ft. diameter spherical balloon was released at Ft Carson, Colorado at 2220. It is believed that this balloon was the cause of the sighting. Release time and winds are right for it.
