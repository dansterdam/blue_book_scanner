SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

TO: Operations Services Division

1. On 4 Nov 60 a call from the Secretary of Champaign T.V. station revealed that two local citizens made reports of unidentified flying objects. Investigation of the reports revealed the following:

2. Description of Objects:
   a. Objects were described as round flat discs.
   b. Size of objects approximate a baseball basket held at arms length.
   c. Two objects observed.
   d. Appeared as trailing formation.
   e. No discernible features.
   f. No tail or exhaust visible.
   g. No sound audible.
   h. Movements of objects appeared to be back and forth tracking motion and was observed for 30 minutes to one hour before disappearing.

3. Description of Course of Objects:
   a. Mrs. [REDACTED] sister called by phone to attract attention initially.
   b. Mrs. [REDACTED] and Mrs. [REDACTED] then observed the objects from 30 minutes to one hour outside the house of Mrs. [REDACTED] before reporting to Champaign T.V. station.
   c. Elevation and azimuth when first observed was estimated to be 20 to 25 degrees above the horizon in a direction of west to southwest.
   d. Angle or elevation upon disappearance same as above 20 to 25 degrees above the horizon.
   e. Description of flight path was as a staggered or circular motion or tracking back and forth.

1. Object disappeared suddenly as in a short burst to the northeast.

4. Manner of Observations
   a. Objects were observed by naked eye.

5. Time and Date of Sighting:

   b. Sky conditions at time of sighting was dark and cloudy near objects and clear sky in opposite quadrant.

6. Location of Observer:
   Sighting was at Monticello, Illinois on Route 47. Coordinates are 40 degrees 02 minutes north and 88 degrees 34 minutes west.
