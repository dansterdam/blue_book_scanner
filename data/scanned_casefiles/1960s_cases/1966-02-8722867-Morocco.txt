A NON IDENTIFIED OBJECT IN THE SKY. A LUMINOUS OBJECT, WITH THE SHAPE OF A SPHERE WITH ENGINES WHICH LOOKED LIKE JET ENGINES, HAS BEEN SEEN THIS EVENING IN SAFI AT 1930 HOURS BY SEVERAL WITNESSES IN SIDI OUASSEL DISTRICT. MANY TRUSTWORTHY WITNESSES STATED: " WE WERE IN FRONT OF THE COMPLEX RESTAURANT AT THE ANGLE OF PORTUGAL AVE AND SIDI OUASSEL ROAD WHEN SUDDENLY OUR ATTENTIONWAS DRAWN BY A LIGHT WHICH WAS MOING IN THE SKY AT A VERY LOW ALTITUDE AND AT A VERY HIGH SPEED. IT WAS A RED SPHERE WITH FOUR JET ENGINES; IT LIT THE WHOLE DISTRICT HEN PASSING ABOVE IT AS IF IT WERE BROAD DAYLIGHT. IT WAS FANTASTIC. SAYS ANOTHER WITNESS, WHO DECLARES THAT THE OBJECT WENT TOWARD THE SOUTH, ALL THE PERSONS WHOM WE QUESTIONED ABOUT THE OBJECT CONFIRM THAT IT WAS A SPHERE WITH FOUR WINGS. THE METEOROLOGY STATION IN SAFI ALSO CCONFIRMED THE PASSING OVER OF A LUMINOUS NON IDENTIFIED OBJECT.
