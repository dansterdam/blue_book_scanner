Approximately 2030 hrs --

Received a call from FAF, Danville, Telephone. They said they had received a call from a

Richmond, Va. telephone comments.

"Turning home at approx 7pm, South of New Madison on Rte 121 - approx 10 miles South of Greenville, Ohio.

"Saw a white, crescent moon shape moving East to West - Then changed from white to range - Then turned to a funnel shape - continued West - Then changed to a sphere of red and turned red - then white. Turned from sphere shape to saucer shape; while in the saucer shape, two dark streaks appeared on left side.

This information being second hand and not verified to get F.O. form 164, suggest that this form be mailed to more complete details, if interested.

Mailed from Danville
OTD Duty Officer - 16 Oct 66
