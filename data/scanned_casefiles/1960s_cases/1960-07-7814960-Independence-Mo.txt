10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Found and oblong, size of pea, red in color. Sighted 20° elev, approaching from 360°. Disappeared 30° elev, about 180°. Traveled SSE at high speed. Faded out of sight to the S. Also described as round, size of a dime. Glowed and faded out to S. Appeared at 20° elev, 360° az. and disappeared at 20° elev, 190° az. Level flight.

11. COMMENTS
Duration is characteristic of a meteor. There were two objects observed, indicating that the witness observed a meteor after its had begun its breakup. The meteor was apparently a large one as it traversed most of the visible sky before it disappeared.
