11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
10-1: High round objects in low, north to south, scattered formation. No actual fall or exhaust. Observer on highway 9, about 5 miles west of Cresco. Might have been 10 miles away. Slightly above horizon. All at a point. Slight motion on edge of thunderstorm area. Lightning. Very low flying. Under cloud cover.

NOTES: Sighting at 10PM. Multiple objects in a staggered formation. Altitude under cloud cover. Probably a star. Possibly under the same cloud area. Probably celestial. Unresolved description seems to lack a flight of lights with the motion of the objects being reported in motion. The north point of objects was seen.
