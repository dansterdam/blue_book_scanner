MARINE DATA REPORT

Date of Report: 11th June 1963

Addressee: Kd. Leslie
United Fruit Company, Pier 3, North River, New York

At 0650 gmt, on the above date, in D.R. position 25°18 N; 89°56 W., what was presumed to be a Satellite of magnitude 2.0, was observed, rising between HERALD & PISCES, in the constellation of URSA-MAJOR. The bearing was 320 T. At 0658, the Satellite disappeared behind cloud on a bearing of 305 T.

At the time of observation, there was clear atmosphere and bright moonlight. The wind was from the East at 10 mph, the temperature of the air was 80°F. Clouds were scattered cirrus at 20,000 ft.
