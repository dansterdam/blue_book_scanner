11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Two youths 11 and 13 observed object between 4 and 5 AM on the morning of 13 July. Initial impression was moonlight. 2d boy said it moved. Moved in different directions. Observed for about one hour. Whitish, metallic sheen. Seemed to be four of the lights. Foggy night. Lights hovered over the nearby houses. Faded away about 5 AM. Then boy decided to tell parents. Object drifting South. Located in West. Moon at 19.4h Right Ascension, placing it in the SW 10 deg elevation 230 deg azimuth. Full moon on 13 July at 1700. Form 164 sent but not returned. Detailed based on limited data. Possible revaluation if additional data is presented.
