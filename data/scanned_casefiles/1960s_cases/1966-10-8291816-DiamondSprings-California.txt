11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noted round shape aluminum colored objects that at times had red and green flashing lights. Sometimes objects appeared to move, stayed for several minutes then got set. SUBJECT: UFO Report

TO: USAF (PTP)

1. The following information was reported to Sergeant Claude W. Gibson, Operations Branch (PTP):

a. Description of the object observed:
(1) Shape - round
(2) Size - the size of an airplane
(3) Color - aluminum
(4) Number - one, two and three at a time
(5) Formation - not close together or in formation
(6) Discernible features - lights that seem to go around it; red and green flashing lights most of the time, something like a web hanging from the side.
(7) Tail, trail, or exhaust - none
(8) Sound - noise sometimes that did not sound like a motor.
(9) Unusual features - none

b. Description of course of object:
(1) Six year old son said one went by his window at night, waking him up, about three weeks ago. Thirteen year old son and ten year old daughter have seen them from time to time in the last three weeks. She stated she had seen one last night and at several other times (exact dates unknown).

(2) Angle of elevation and azimuth - above the tree tops and hills.

(3) Description of flight path - sometimes objects appeared to move; could watch for several minutes then they seem to fade out of sight. Seemed to go from west to east.

A check with Base Operations at Mather AFB, California, revealed there were seven T-20 two aircraft and two T-52s flying in the local area between 0107 and 0200, 25 Oct 1966. This check revealed that the VCR station is located 10 miles northeast (040°) of the base and that the holding pattern extends for another 10 miles east of the VCR station.

k. In view of the number of aircraft flying in the local area from Mather AFB and the description of the objects given by "S.______" it is the opinion of the preparing officer that the sightings were of aircraft.
