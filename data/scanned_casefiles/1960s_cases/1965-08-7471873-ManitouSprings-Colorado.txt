8. PHOTOS: n. Yes   x. No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: n. Yes   x. No

10. CONCLUSION: BALLOONS (TOY)

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Found object silver in sunlight when sky was background and dark or black against clouds. Appeared to be in flight to the West. Disappearing behind mountains.

Radar scan with radar and balloon being cold. These gas filled balloons were observed in Colorado Springs and Manitou Springs floating free. Individual who made initial report called back and informed investigator of the identity of known object. Additional calls from other observers of same or similar objects.
