SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object (UFO)

TO:   Operations Services Division (OSD)

1. General:
   a. On 29 September 1960, From the Champaign-
   Urbana Courier, reported that he had received a phone call
   reporting a U.F.O. Subsequent investigation revealed the
   following:

2. Description of Object:
   a. The object was shaped like a star and was about the size
   of a star.
   b. It was white with a pulsating dull-red light trailing
   behind it.
   c. There was only one object.
   d. It has no discernible features.
   e. There was no trail of exhaust.
   f. There was no sound.

3. Description of the Course of the Object:
   a. Observer stuck his head out of the car window to look up
   to see if the sky was cloudy and observed the object directly
   overhead.
   b. Observer watched object cross sky to the north/north east
   and disappear beyond the horizon.
   c. Object was visible for an estimated 15 to 20 seconds or
   about three times the speed of Echo I.

4. Manner of Observation:
   a. Ground - visual
