11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
One saucer shaped object about the size of a silver dollar was seen in the east at 10 deg elevation. Object appeared to be stationary at first then moved from east to west south on a slight accent. Faded out of sight at 15 degrees elevation in the south. Object contained three blinking lights. The area of the sighting is constantly traversed by civilian and military aircraft. Lights duration and flight path are consistent with an aircraft sighting.
