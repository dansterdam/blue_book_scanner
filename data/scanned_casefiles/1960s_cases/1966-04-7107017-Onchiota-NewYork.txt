PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 18 April 66 19/0125Z
2. LOCATION: Onchiota, New York
3. SOURCE: Civilian
4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 15 Minutes
6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual
7. COURSE: E to S, Hovered
8. PHOTOS: No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No
10. CONCLUSION: AIRCRAFT
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observers sighted object low in the sky in the east or SE. It seemed to loop, hovered in the sky, yet moved fast at times. Went from East to South then rose in sky to nearly overhead. Faded in the East over the hills. Object was round, white in color changing to red, green and white.

KC-135 aircraft number 836 was on a local flight just prior to the sighting. The aircraft was in the vicinity of the sighting South and Southeast of Plattsburgh AFB at 24,000 ft, then returning to holding area East of Plattsburgh AFB. Both red rotating beacons were on as well as white and green navigation lights.
