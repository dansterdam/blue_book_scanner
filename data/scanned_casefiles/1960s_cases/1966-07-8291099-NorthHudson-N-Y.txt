11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noted white specks in sky in the north. Aircraft like object but observer heard no sound. Object was traveling toward the south at a slow rate of speed. The lights were white and blue in color and circulating.

At the time of the sighting at KC - 135 was refueling a B-52 bomber in the altitude and general direction of sighting.
The aircraft's lights would explain observer's description of circulation white and blue lights.
