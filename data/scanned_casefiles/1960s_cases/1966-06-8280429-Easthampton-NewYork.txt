PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 22 June 1966 23/0250

2. LOCATION: Easthampton, New York

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 5 - 7 Seconds

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Climbing

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Possible (AIRCRAFT)

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: He thought was an airline type aircraft and at one time thought the UFO and airliner were going to collide.

There were two F-101's airborne at the time of the sighting from SCATB. Observer stated that UFO passed across nose section of what airliner was going to collide.

Observer noted somewhat round white object that was blinking on and off while walking at night. Object was at an elevation of 75-90 deg when observed. Object gradually faded out of sight.
