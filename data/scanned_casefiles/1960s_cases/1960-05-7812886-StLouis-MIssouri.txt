  FROM SOUTH TO NORTH ON A DIRECT COURSE.

  EIGHT HUNDRED FEET, AN ONRUSH OF HUGE, LUSTROUS, BRILLIANT, SATELLITE DIRECTLY FROM EAST - DIRECTLY TO WEST. STARTED TO SWAY UP AND DOWN, THEN HOVERED, AND REMAINED AT A BEARING STEADILY.

- 11:33 P.M. AT ALTITUDE OF ABOUT 825 FEET (EIGHT HUNDRED AND TWENTY FIVE FEET), SUDDENLY APPEARED THROUGH CEILING, A VERY RED, BRIGHT OBJECT LIKE A RED BALL IN SHAPE. IT SEEMED TO BE A SOLID MASS OF FLAMES. SWAYED BACK AND FORTH; THEN HOVERED, ROUTE - DIRECTLY FROM SOUTH, TOWARD DIRECT NORTH.

NUMEROUS ELONGATED EJECTIONS EARTHWARD SEEMED TO COME FROM THE SAME -- PRECEDED AND ACCOMPANIED BY BRIGHT, LIGHT-YELLOW-ELECTRIC LIKE FLASHES. AFTER WHICH, SOMETHING HUGE, CLOUD-LIKE, LIGHT IN COLOR, NOT-LUMINOUS'S SEEMED TO TRAVEL JUST FROM THE RED OBJECT, TRAVELED A BIT WESTWARD, SWAYED UP AND DOWN, BACKWARD A BIT; AND, AFTER A TIME BECAME INDISCERNIBLE IN CLOUDS WITHIN THE DARK SKY.

THE RED OBJECT SEEMED TO BE BURNING MORE FURIOUSLY EVERY MOMENT AFTER THAT -- THEN ABOUT A THIRD OF IT SEPARATED, MOVED SLOWLY NORTHWEST A BIT, AND SEEMED TO BURN UP COMPLETELY, A HEAVY ODOR (ODOUR) LIKE THE VAPOR OF MOLTEN, BURNED IRON PERMEATED THE ATMOSPHERE TO WITHIN ONE HUNDRED FEET OF EARTH. THE WONDER OF THE RED OBJECT ROSE IN ALTITUDE ABOUT TWENTY FIVE FEET, HOVERED FOR QUITE SOME TIME, CONTINUED TO BURN; THEN WHEN LOST TO VIEW, SEEMED TO EXIST NO LONGER.
