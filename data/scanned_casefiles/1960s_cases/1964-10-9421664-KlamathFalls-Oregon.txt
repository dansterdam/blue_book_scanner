PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 9 October 1964
2. LOCATION: Klamath Falls, Oregon
3. SOURCE: Civilian
4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 3 Minutes
6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual
7. COURSE: NW
8. PHOTOS: No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No
10. CONCLUSION: AIRCRAFT
    No data presented to indicate that the object could not be a light a/c with landing light on.
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
    Large fireball with rays of light extending downward from sides of object. Reddish color to clear translucent color. No features noted, no trail contact or sound. Speeds of object about the same as that of an a/c. Initial observation in west to 1.5 degrees. Flight to NW on a straight course from Klamath Falls. Faded at 20 deg elevation in distance.
