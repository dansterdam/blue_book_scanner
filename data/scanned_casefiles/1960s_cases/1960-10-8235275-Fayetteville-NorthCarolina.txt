10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Cigar or banana shaped, silver in color, size of half-dollar. Observed in 1 cm ground space 10 ft from 60 ft television tower looking at top of tower. Saw a flash in sky directly in line of vision. Called to another observer nearby. 10° was on top of tower. Appeared approx 10°, approx 625. Disappeared: observed N-1-10°, approx 350°; observer N 2- approx 65°, approx 340°. Just disappeared.

11. COMMENTS
Object was probably due to the reflection of the sun from a high-flying a/c. Two investigating officers visited the area.
