11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object looked like a very high satellite. Object was blinking approximately every two seconds. There was no traail, tail or exhaust and no sound noted. The object was in a orbital type traveling northwest to southwest and was zagging and jerking. Object finally disappeared in the SE. The blinking of the object suffests an aircraft.
