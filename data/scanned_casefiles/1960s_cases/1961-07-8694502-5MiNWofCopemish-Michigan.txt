10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
First appeared as a round white light, then changed to a larger light with 4 objects about size of baseball. Moved very slow. Appeared to be very high altitude. Moved behind tree tops. Lights gradually disappeared.

11. COMMENTS
There was no known c/c operation. Lights were observed intermittently over 1 period of 1 hr. Although there was no known a/c in area, it is possible that lights were on high altitude balloons. However, this report will be carried as unidentified until it can be proven that will allow a valid solution.
