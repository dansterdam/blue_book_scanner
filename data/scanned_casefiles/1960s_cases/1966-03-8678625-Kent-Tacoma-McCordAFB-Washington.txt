11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
One, two, or three objects all close together. One witness described them as looking like a transparent kite. The other witness said they resembled a biplane. Objects were sighted in the west and appeared to be traveling west. They faded as night fell.

The time is right for a balloon. Weather balloon launches at 600PM. Description of the object and its flight is consistent with a balloon.
