10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Oval object greenish changing in size which
seemed to shine through clouds or mist. Tended
to diminish or fade during phases of clear
sky. Moved back and forth from South to SW.
Pinkish glow around object. Visible at about
60 deg elevation. Still in sight upon retiring
for night.

11. COMMENTS
Case investigated by LtC Fried.
Searchlights in operation to the
South of observer. Case evaluated
as searchlight activity reflecting
on the mist/haze and clouds.
