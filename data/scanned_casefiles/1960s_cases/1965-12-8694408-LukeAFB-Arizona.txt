PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 15 December 1965 16/0432

2. LOCATION: Luke AFB, Arizona

3. SOURCE: Military

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: ONE

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 15-20 seconds

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Air-Visual

7. COURSE: North

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Other (SATELLITE DECAY)
All characteristics of decay present

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer sighted a round bright white object with tail. Appeared as a meteor. Object descended towards the North training area intensely and apparently sped with a tail. Forming as it fell. Burnout occurred level with object going to pieces and dulling to orange color.

Object 1965-1023 (U.S.) decayed this date. No report from SPADATS on time.
