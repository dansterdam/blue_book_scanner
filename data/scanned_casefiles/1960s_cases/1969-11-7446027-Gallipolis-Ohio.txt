Probable Aircraft

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
6 minutes

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
The observer sighted a red and a white light (assumed to be attached to one object) that were about the same brightness as an airplane light. The lights traveled from the SW to the SE and were visible for approximately 6 minutes.

COMMENTS: No evidence was presented that the stimulus was not an aircraft.
