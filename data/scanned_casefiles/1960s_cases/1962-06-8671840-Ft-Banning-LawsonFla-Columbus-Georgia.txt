10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
A 2nd star like obj was observed West of Columbus, Ga at approx 1930 hrs & viewed for approx 1 hr to 10 mins. The obj was rptd by/from binoculars. The color of the obj was rptd to be silver & there was no apparent motion.

11. COMMENTS
The obj of this sighting was prob ably the planet Venus. It was at an azimuth of 275deg & an elev of 28deg at the time of the sighting. The planets magnitude on this date was -5. The planet did not set until 2229.
