PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 3 August 65 04/0050Z

2. LOCATION: Gregory, Texas

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 2 1/2 Hours

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual (7X50 BK)

7. COURSE: Reported South

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Astro (VENUS) - Planet VENUS setting at time of disappearance. Object has characteristics of an astronomical report.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Multi colored light, red, orange and black like a kite. Had a yellow light on object. Appeared like a bat at times. Remained in the same place and at times appeared to climb. Observed at 1850 local and was gone after return from reporting at 2030 local. Appeared to move South. Observed for 2 hours and 30 minutes.
