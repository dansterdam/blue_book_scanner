5. PHYSICAL: Yes

6. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 2 min

7. SOURCE: Civilian

8. SUMMARY OF SIGHTING:
A/c like glider or "chicken hawk" hovered in area. Followed course of dark brown or black bottom. No sound. Left 25-30 ft. above ground. Plane-like material dropped in 5 drops at 10-15,000 ft. Plane had insignia not readable with binoculars. Plane descended after drops with tremendous speed. No sound. Plane call to one witness recalled wings like U-2, and established altitude as 15-16,700 ft. Substance covered large area. Sample sent to ATIC for analysis.

9. COURSE: N

10. COMMENTS:
Analyst was nylon thread. Air pollution control drop of some sort. Not verified with State of Kentucky air pollution control to delay in receiving report.
