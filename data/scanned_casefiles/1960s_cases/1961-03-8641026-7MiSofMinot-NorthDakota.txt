10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Round Obj. size of auto headlight at aprox 1 half mile distance with alternating to bright red. Very brightly at low altitude, moving up and down with no forward motion noticed. No noticeable movement.

11. COMMENTS
Apparent up and down movement probably due to movement of car witnesses were riding in. Obj was reported to be at low altitude, which varied with observers. It was not observed to appear.
