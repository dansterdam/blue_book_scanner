10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
An object, similar to an aircraft with sunlight reflecting off of it was seen from Bethel, Alaska. Object was observed for 30 sec. and appeared to be silvery. Disappeared in fog bank to the N.

11. COMMENTS
At 0205Z, 29 Jan 62, Echo was 6° of equator. Object viewed by this witness was probably an aircraft with sunlight reflecting from it. There is no evidence available in this report which indicates object is an interplanetary vehicle, bearing life or intelligence in any form or that its presence constitutes a threat to national security.
