2. Mrs. [redacted] sighted an unidentified object during the
   month of December 1966.

3. Description of the object:
   a. The object looked like a hat, with a flat top and no windows
      or openings.
   b. The object was about the size of a two-engine aircraft.
   c. Only one object was sighted.
   d. Object was copper colored, with a dull finish.
   e. No trail or exhaust was visible.
   f. No sounds were heard from the object.

4. Description of course of object:
   a. The observer saw the object while standing in her back yard.
   b. The angle of elevation was about 45 degrees.
   c. The object came from behind a cloud, remaining stationary for
      about a minute, then going back into the same cloud.
   d. The object did not reappear from the cloud.
   e. The object appeared NW of Mrs. [redacted] house.

5. Manner of observation:
   a. Ground visual was the method of observation.
