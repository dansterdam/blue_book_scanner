10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Two white starlite objs. 1 obj stationary, 2nd obj approached lat. 1st obj located directly overhead. Second obj approached from North. Objs appeared higher than a/c. 2nd obj did not disappear. 1st obj disappeared in South.

11. COMMENTS
Time of EOROI passage off by 1/2 hr. EORO 1 not visible. SECA at 170 deg az 12 S, 45 deg elev. RESULTS at 24 deg az in 34 deg elev. ARCTURUS at 70 deg elev 130 deg az. ARCTURUS more closely approximates the overhead position. Obj probably one of those stars. No planets visible.
