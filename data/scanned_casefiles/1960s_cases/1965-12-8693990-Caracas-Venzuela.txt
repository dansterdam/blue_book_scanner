PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 11 December 65 12/Night

2. LOCATION: Caracas, Venezuela

3. SOURCE: Military

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: ONE

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 40 seconds

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: West, then North

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: INSUFFICIENT DATA FOR EVALUATION

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer sighted an orange-red spherical object. It appeared as a flashing sphere with a slight tail approx. one-third the diameter of the object. There was no sound and it travelled a steady course to the west turning north. View of object was obstructed by building and object was not seen afterwards. There were no a/c in the area.

FOREIGN REPORT. Sounds like Meteor except for duration and turn course (if correct). Rules out decay. Could be jet with after burner except no jets in area.
