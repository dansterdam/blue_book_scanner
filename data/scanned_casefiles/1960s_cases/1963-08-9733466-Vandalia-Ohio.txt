10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Two bright red lights moving very fast. Disappeared behind clouds. No positional data obtained. Appeared like childs rulers about 1 inches apart. Observed through windshield of moving car.

11. COMMENTS
No mention of disappearance other than disappearing behind clouds. This tends to rule out reflections and indicates that the objects were in the air. Nothing conflicting with a/c analysis. Sighting of a/c lights at night for brief duration. Case considered as an a/c sighting.
