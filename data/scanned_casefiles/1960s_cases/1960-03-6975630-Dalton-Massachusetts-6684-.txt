SUBJECT: Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object

1. On the evening of 23 February 1960, at approximately 1800 hours, Mr. JOHN DOE, a resident of Dalton, Massachusetts, reported sighting an unidentified flying object over his home.

2. According to Mr. DOE's statement, he was standing outside his home when he noticed a bright light in the sky moving in a westerly direction. The object appeared to be elliptical in shape and was emitting a bright, pulsating light. Mr. DOE stated that the object was at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet and moving at a high rate of speed.

3. Mr. DOE's sighting was reported to the Pittsfield, Massachusetts, Police Department, who in turn notified the Air Force through proper channels.

4. This report has been forwarded to Project Blue Book for further investigation.
