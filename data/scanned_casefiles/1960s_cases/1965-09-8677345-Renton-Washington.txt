Large round disc. Bright blue and red in color. Two objects extending from disc, one red, one blue. No exhaust. No sound. Witness put cat outside. Saw object. Object went around in circles. Witness ran inside house and looked out side. Object was gone. Witness little girl. Sighting lasted 30 seconds.
