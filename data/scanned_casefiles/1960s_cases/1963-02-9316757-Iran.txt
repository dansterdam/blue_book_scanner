SUMMARY:

(C) Iranian Border post reports sighting an unidentified flying object, shaped similar to a rocket. Object was sighted on 17 Feb 63 at 1200 hours, near EMSAN ELMEN (37°42'N 49°58'E). Estimated altitude of object was 1000 meters. Object was seen flying from southeast to northwest. Source also reports observing smoke coming out of its tail section. Smoke was reported by source to be "thick".
