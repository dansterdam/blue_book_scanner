10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Appearing as bright light in sky beginning near ground level rising rapidly at an angle of about 45 degrees. Disappeared by going out or light. Seemed to move in a zigzag manner.

11. COMMENTS
This is over Baltimore at 0503 GMT. Can be seen by many witnesses. Possibly satellite. More likely an illusion of some kind. Possible atmospheric phenomenon by using out of focus light. Possible meteor.

12. CONCLUSIONS
Insufficient Data for Evaluation
