11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
The observer sighted an object that was shaped like a blimp. The front was red, the middle blue and the rear a very intense white. The observer estimated the height to be less than 100 yards, heard a swishing sound, and stated that the rear was brighter than any fluorescent light.

COMMENTS: In her report to the duty officer the observer stated that the object was in view for 6-7 seconds and that it passed behind a cloud at one time. In the 11/7 she gave the duration a minute and stated that the object was less than 100 yards high.

The Salem police dept. stated that they had not received any similar reports of unusual aerial objects in the month of March.
