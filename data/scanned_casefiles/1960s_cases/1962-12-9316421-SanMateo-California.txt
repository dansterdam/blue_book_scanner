10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: Sighting in morn by 13 yr old boy of obj about 100 ft in diameter, round smooth or oval shape at a distance of 100-160 yds exhibiting speeds of 100 mph to 1,000 mph. Direction of flight to North. Maneuvers included standing still, straight up, and side-ways. Color was red like a ball of flame. No sound. Duration was 1 1/2 hours. Approaching 27 secs, hovering & three fogs. Sees going away. Sky conditions fog.

11. COMMENTS: Duration indicates aircraft sighting. Ball of flame indicates jet/afterburner or possible meteor; however, sighting at dawn & brightness not indicated. No mention of how time figured. 27 sec hovering not consistent with a/c analysis. Alt of 100 yd extremely low. Detail should have been noted during hovering. Sound should have been audible at such low level. Conflicting data- unreliable rpt.

12. CONCLUSIONS: Other Unresolvable Data, Insufficient Data for Evaluation
