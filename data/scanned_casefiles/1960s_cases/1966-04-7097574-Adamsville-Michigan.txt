11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer sighted a light in the sky which was changing colors from red to white. Object was described as circular in shape tapering at the outside edges. There were no maneuvers or movement. The object remained stationary. Was not under observation when it disappeared. Object sighted at 90 degrees azimuth and 25-30 degrees elevation. The star Arcturus at this time was at 100 degrees azimuth and 45 degrees elevation.
