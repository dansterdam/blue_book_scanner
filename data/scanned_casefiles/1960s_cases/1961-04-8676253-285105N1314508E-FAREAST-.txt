10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
White object, stationary, true position, alt 10 degrees approx. as a warm bright light. Moved very slowly. Appeared to be stationary. Remained in clear sky. Moved very slowly.

11. COMMENTS
Checked with space track. Showed that the object was not echo. Slight possibility that the object was a weather balloon. However, object was not a space vehicle at altitude. It is possible that a satellite other than space vehicle was seen. However there is no confirming data and the case is carried as insufficient data.
