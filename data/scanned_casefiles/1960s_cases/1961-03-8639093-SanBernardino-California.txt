11. COMMENTS
Obj. of this sighting was probably a/c. Witness indicated obj. moved fm an elevation of approx 20° W to zon. If it had a ground speed of 573 kts a/c would have passed through angle he describes in 3 min. If it had a ground speed of 8 mi would have capability of our jet a/c. Bright white light on bottom of a/c was probably light observed by witness.
