The following, extracted from Lt Lehman's Report, dated 27 July 1960, is quoted for your information.

1940 - Received call from the Air Force Command Post with info concerning a UFO. A Navy aircraft (#1130) sighted an object at 3000-12000 feet at 272212z. Its heading was 340°T toward Flores Island at an altitude of 300 feet. The object was white with a red fringe, broke into two and began to disintegrate at which time it gradually turned death. It was in sight approximately five seconds.

1955 - Called Maj Friend (330-25) and related information given by the Air Force Command Post. Maj Friend summarized the object may have been a satellite recently, it could possibly have been a satellite recently.
