10. CONCLUSION: INSUFFICIENT DATA

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer sighted an orange and flashing red light. There was no sound and after 3 minutes the object just disappeared. The object appeared as a light and was brighter than bright still.
