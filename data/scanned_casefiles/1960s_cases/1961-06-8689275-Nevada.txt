10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Object of undetermined size, shape and color observed through a theodolite by weather observer. Object arrived from South and orbited in area. For 2 1/2 hours, clean-arcing. Held altitude estimated to be 80-120,000 ft.

11. COMMENTS
The object was probably an s/c of the U-2 type. U-2 flights are known to take place from Nellis AFB, Nevada and the flight characteristics are in altitude.
