10. BRIEF SUMMARY
Brilliant, round tapered cone-shaped obj. Conflicting reports on size, from time size to 4-6 inches at arms length. Vivid red, green, and blue. Trailed fire and sparks. One observer hears sound like sonic boom. Extremely high speed. Appeared at unknown elevation, 300°. Disappeared below trees, 300°. One observer said obj broke into 3 or 4 pieces and disappeared behind trees.
