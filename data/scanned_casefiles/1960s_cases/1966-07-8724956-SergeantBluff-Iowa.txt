1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 31 July 66 31/0600Z

2. LOCATION: Sergeant Bluff, Iowa (1 Witness)

3. SOURCE: Military

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 30 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: N

8. PHOTOS: [X] No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: [X] No

10. CONCLUSION: BALLOON
Prob. Met

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observers watched object start and stop while moving in its northerly direction. The object circled to the left, rose and was gone evidently to the north. Observers were approximately 7 miles SW of Sergeant Bluff.

A weather balloon was released from the top of the administrative office located at the Sioux City Municipal Airport in Sergeant Bluff, Iowa. The general direction of the balloon was to the north and rising.
