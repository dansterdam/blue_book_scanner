11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
All objects appeared as bright lights, bluish green, red and yellow color and moving to the left and right and up and down.
One was in the SE, two was in the SW and one was in the west.
The objects were undoubtedly stars or planets.
The culprits were most likely the following objects:
SPICA: 165 deg azimuth, 35 deg elevation.
BETELGEUSE: 275 deg azimuth, 10 deg elevation.
JUPITER: 290 deg azimuth, 15 deg elevation.
SIRIUS: 250 deg azimuth, 5 deg elevation.
The objects listed above were at the position indicated at 0300Z.
