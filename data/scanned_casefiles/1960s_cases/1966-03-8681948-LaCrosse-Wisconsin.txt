PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 29 March 1966 29/0830Z

2. LOCATION: LaCrosse, Wisconsin

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 30 Minutes, Plus

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: NW

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Astro (MOON)

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer noted stationary object in the northwest at about 0130. The object looked just like the moon only much larger. Observer stated she thought it was the moon, but it was in the northwest, which is not where the moon would be.

The moon was setting at the time of the sighting at 315 deg azimuth.
