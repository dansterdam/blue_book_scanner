11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer was out star gazing when he began to notice object at the handle of the Big Dipper. There were 5 different object visible during the 4 hr, 25 min observation. There was no unusual activity in the area at the time of the sighting, although numerous aircraft were in the area, it is more probable that the observer was observing astronomical bodies instead of aircraft since a telescope was used.
