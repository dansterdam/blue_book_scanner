11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Two objects sighted two minutes apart. One was traveling south to north and weaving side to side. The other was traveling from NW to SE and weaving side to side. Objects disappeared into the clouds.

Military and civilian air traffic in the area at the time of the sighting. Also ECHO satellite southeast bound at this time.
