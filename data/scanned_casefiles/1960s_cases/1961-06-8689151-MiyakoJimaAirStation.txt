10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Round object, twice the size of a pinhead at arm's length, blue-white. Appeared to be moving. Color and shape comparable to a star. Appeared stationary for the North with a compass reading 350 deg. Disappeared at 60 deg elevation to the south with a compass reading of 190 deg. Moved across the sky in an arc. Compass and map showed object.

Too slow for meteor and duration is too long. Balloon. Motion more like wind at altitudes above 6,000 ft. Speed not too slow to be a balloon. No conclusive evidence. Insufficient data for evaluation.
