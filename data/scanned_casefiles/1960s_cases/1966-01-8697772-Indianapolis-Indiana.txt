January 14, 1966 - Friday Evening - 6:30-6:50 P.M. (timed by watch). We were 6200 North and I block west of College Ave., Indianapolis, Indiana. Sighted at 6:30 p.m. and just walked out the door of the above address (Doctor's Office). Spotted it hovering about 200 feet above or parallel to aerial, standing length, about 30 degrees angle, whirling or in motion, colors of red, yellow and white. Became dim at 6:45 P.M. went very rapidly (west). 6:45 p.m. completely disappeared, back again at 6:49 P.M. and left permanently at 6:50 P.M. (Night was hazy not many stars)

We had two witnesses.
