PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 4 August 65 05/0330Z

2. LOCATION: Albany, Oregon

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 20 - 35 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: North

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Astro (ARCTURUS) Observation characteristic of Astro Body. ARCTURUS in the approximate position of the reported object. Initial elevation regarded as being in error.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Bright white star shaped object flashed red and white just before going over the horizon. Initial sighting at 30-35 deg elevation in West moving to 10 deg elevation in NW at time of disappearance. Flat arc type of trajectory.
