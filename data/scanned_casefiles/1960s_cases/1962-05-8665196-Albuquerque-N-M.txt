10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
An object size of Venus was first seen in daytime by both ground and airborne witnesses. At sunset object appeared pink then dark.

11. COMMENTS
Subsequent investigation of this case brought to light that a sky balloon was launched in the area and remained practically stationary for the period of this sighting. Characteristics displayed and course of a balloon. Concluded that this object was probably a sky balloon.
