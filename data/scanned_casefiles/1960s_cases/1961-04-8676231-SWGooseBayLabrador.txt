The following, extracted from Capt Ingholt's Report dated 11 Apr 61,
is quoted for your information:

Air Force Command Post phoned at 0200 local with the following
UFO report: Object sighted by SAC KC-97 Zenn Bay of Goose Bay,
flying at 13,000 feet, appeared above and ahead of aircraft
line of flight. On last split time two parts and burned at
12/0613. Message was relayed to Mr. Goard, 4F3, by phone.
