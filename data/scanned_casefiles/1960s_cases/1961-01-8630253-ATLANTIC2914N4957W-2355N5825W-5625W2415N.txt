10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
1st Rpt: illuminated body observed to 300° at approx altitude 60°. Started falling to 25°. Surrounded by cloud of smoke. 2nd report triangular flying object, glowing and smoking. 3rd report: strange brilliant object like a moon with big blue-white luminescence. Running speed in sky 265° to 230°, 20° over horizon.

11. COMMENTS
Obj in Cape Canaveral. Firing schedule fm Cape indicates that an Atlas was fired a few minutes before this sighting. The Azimuth (firing) of the Atlas coincides with the direction reported by witnesses. It is concluded that object was probably the Atlas fired from Cape Canaveral.
