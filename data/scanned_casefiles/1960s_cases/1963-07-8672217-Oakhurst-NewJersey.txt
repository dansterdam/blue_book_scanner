Dear Sirs,

Starting about 7:33 P.M.

Monday July 1, 1963 a saw an unidentified flying object (U.F.O.). This U.F.O. was sighted traveling from north northwest to east southeast until losing sight at about 15 degrees above the horizon.

My mother and father saw this also. Could you give any information about what I saw.

1. Reference the attached correspondence from Donald Hopkins
   Edward Winkelmann requesting information on unidentified
   flying objects. This correspondence is forwarded to your
   office for whatever action you deem necessary.

2. Agent Mr. Hopkins' letter, if the time of the sighting
   was Daylight Saving Time, the object was Echo; if not, there
   is insufficient data for evaluation.

   at the time of the sighting. The object was not Echo but
   could have been an aircraft.
