PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 10 August 65 11/0340Z

2. LOCATION: Prairie Village, Kansas

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One Object

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 3 - 5 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Maneuvered to North

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: A/c (WITH EXPERIMENTAL LIGHTING)
Description of object and flight including turn and duration indicates possible light a/c with unusual lighting configuration such as a large landing light or infra red equipment.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
One object like a ping pong ball at arm length with color of red or re-orange. Intense glow so bright that observer could not look directly at it. Had a triangular shape with silvery white color just below the red glow. Light appeared larger than a/c. Came from SE, turned 60 mph. Altitude estimated at 3,000 ft. Speed estimated at 60 mph. Duration 3 minutes possible 5 minutes. Inversions at 1,000 and 7,000 ft. Slow speed, large red light. Possible experimental type a/c or infra red photo type plane from description. More likely experimental since red glow was reported as above the silver triangle (A/C).
