10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Size of full moon, glowing silver, shape of round plate whose edges were not sharp by ray. Strongly of light was like glass of strong northern light. Slowly light in middle of plate disappeared until it looked like ring of light which disappeared completely at 0420. Altitude 20-30° above horizon.
