11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer watched a brightly colored object in the western sky for about 25 minutes. The object was disc shaped and all colors. A relatively had seen a similar object on the previous night so the witness thought she could see it again. The object was slightly above the horizon in the western sky. The object was still in sight when the witness made her calls to the local AFB. No radar contact with the object.

The description is consistent with that of an astronomical observation. Venus was in the western sky and quite bright.
