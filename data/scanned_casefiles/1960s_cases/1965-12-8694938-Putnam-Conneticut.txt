11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Airline pilot sighted a round unknown object. It was pink, then orange, then white. Pilot flying at 19,000 ft, heading 260 deg. Object passed 4m directly in front of airplane. After 7 min object changed course, picked up speed and disappeared. A Navy a/c flying in the area did not not see the object. Not stated how close object was when it passed in front of pilots a/c.
