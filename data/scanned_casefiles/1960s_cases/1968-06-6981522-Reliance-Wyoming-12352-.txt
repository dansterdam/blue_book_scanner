1536 Connecticut Avenue, N.W.
Washington, D.C. 20036

1. Reference is made to your letter dated 1 June 1968, concerning the incident which occurred on 12 May 1968 near Reliance, Wyoming.

2. This incident has been investigated by the United States Air Force and the following information is provided:

a. At approximately 2200 hours on 12 May 1968, a private citizen reported observing an unidentified flying object near Reliance, Wyoming. The object was described as being oval in shape, approximately 30 feet in diameter, and emitting a bright reddish-orange glow.

b. The witness stated that the object hovered for several minutes and then accelerated straight up at a very high rate of speed, disappearing from sight.

c. An investigation was conducted by personnel from Lowry Air Force Base, Colorado. No evidence was found to substantiate the report.

3. Based on the information available, this incident is classified as "Unidentified". The United States Air Force is unable to provide a positive identification or explanation for the object observed.

4. If you have any additional information concerning this incident, please forward it to this office for further evaluation.

5. Your continued interest in the investigation of unidentified flying objects is appreciated.

FOR THE CHIEF OF STAFF:

GEORGE S. BROWN, Colonel, USAF
