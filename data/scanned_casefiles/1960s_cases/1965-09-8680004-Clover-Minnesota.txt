11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer sighted a rather large, solid, well-lighted sphere at about 4 ft. in diameter. It had drifted and was on a small projection at the bottom. The sphere was near the third parallel, about 75 yards, above ground. The observer momentarily, and then repeatedly, watched the object drift into the clouds. It then came back into the clouds. The observer believes it was probably a balloon.
