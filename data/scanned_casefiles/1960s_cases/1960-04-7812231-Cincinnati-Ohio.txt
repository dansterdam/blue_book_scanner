10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Several different sightings of red, green, and white lights by six children. Red warning lights at various times during a fifteen minute period. Longest sighting lasted only a few seconds. Always disappeared and usually with random motion. In city of Cincinnati, Ohio with WX cloudy with haze.

11. COMMENTS
In view of the random movement and location in the city together with low clouds and haze it is quite possible that the children were observing light reflections from the haze or clouds.
