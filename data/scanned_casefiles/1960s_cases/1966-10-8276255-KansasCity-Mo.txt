5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 5 Minutes

7. COURSE: Straight-Up

8. PHOTOS: [X] No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: [X] No

10. CONCLUSION: BALLOON (JAH)
Colored balloons were released at a football game at approx 1940Z from wind data the balloons would have been known to have traveled toward the observers house.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Objects were spread over a large area and had indefinite shape of a star. Appeared to change in mid air about 5 minutes and then began to ascend. Objects disappeared quickly straight up into the sky. No maneuvers, only floating. Investigating official stated several colored balloons were released by a FUND dealer approximately 11 miles away. Exact time unknown.
