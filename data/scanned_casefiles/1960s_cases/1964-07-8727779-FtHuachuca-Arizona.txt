11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Two round white objects observed while expecting observation of own 1st object. Sighted and spent 10 min. in area. Two objects moved in a 3 to 5° arc and at 3 to 4 times the angular speed of each other. One object turned to east. Changed course several times and slowed tests. According to the yearbook, "Object in view for 10 minutes."

Remarks: 1st obj. of Satellite WOO II, 2nd object during same pass. Viewed from 10,000 to 16,000 ft. Obj. 16,000 ft. probably a balloon. Duration of 10 minutes too long for A/C height. Correct for wind at 15-20 knots. 2nd object estimated as balloon.
