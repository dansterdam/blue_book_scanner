10. SIGHTING DESCRIPTION
Object brighter than other stars, white, that moved around in the SW sky with a red flashing light. Appeared like a moving star. Elevation estimated at 30 deg. Appeared solid.

11. COMPANY
Star Antares was in the SW at about 20 deg elevation and was the only Astro body in the last magnitude in that portion of the sky at the time. WX included haze and the object had the appearance of a star. Motion attributed to atmospheric conditions present.
