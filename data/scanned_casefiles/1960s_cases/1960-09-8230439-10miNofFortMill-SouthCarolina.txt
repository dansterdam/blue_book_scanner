10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Missile, approx size of C-47 fuselage, white and orange, brilliant light. Object struck ground about 100 miles E of Charlotte, North Carolina and was visible for 10-20 seconds after striking ground, then light went out.

11. COMMENTS
Upon investigation it was found that the Army at Ft Bragg had been on maneuvers and had fired a flare at the time of this sighting.
