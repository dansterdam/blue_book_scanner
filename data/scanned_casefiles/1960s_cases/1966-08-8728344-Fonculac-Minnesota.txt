UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT

Initial call received at 1730z, 17 Aug 66, from Bonnie
Redtennint. Detailed information was later received from
her father, Rowland Redtennint, of 419 Minnesota Avenue,
Duluth, Minnesota telephone 724-5531. Mr. Redtennint is
an employee of the Minnesota Power & Light Company.

I. Description of Object

A. Like an aircraft with long, straight wings not swept back.
B. The size of a large airliner or transport. Its wingspan
would cover the full moon.
C. Dark shape with red and green lights at ends, white lights
in between, all lights flashing. No fuselage was visible, and
the observers seemed confused as to the location of the lights -
whether on a fuselage or on the center of the wing.
D. One object.
E. No exhaust.
G. No exhaust.
H. No sound.
