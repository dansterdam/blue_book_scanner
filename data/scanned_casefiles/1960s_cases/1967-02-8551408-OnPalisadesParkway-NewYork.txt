11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer watched a oblong shaped object with red and white colored lights travel toward center of the road. The object seemed to have portholes underneath. Gradually came dimly lighted with a pale white light. The object had a straight flight path from right to left.

The description is consistent with that of an aircraft observation. La Guardia and Kennedy Airport are nearby.
