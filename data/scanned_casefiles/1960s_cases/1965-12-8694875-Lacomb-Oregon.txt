PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 20 December 1965, 21/32/44
2. LOCATION: Lacomb, Oregon
3. SOURCE: Civilian
4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two or Three
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 15 minutes and still visible
6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual (EX)
7. COURSE: Going back and forth
8. PHOTOS: No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No
10. CONCLUSION: A/C
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observer sighted a round large object which was glowing with an amber color. It sometimes changed to white. They were in formation but they split up at times. No tail, trail, or exhaust. No sound. It was going back and forth over the given area in very fast flight. Sometimes both moved and sometimes one moved while the other stayed in place. Had not disappeared at time of report. Observer used binoculars.
