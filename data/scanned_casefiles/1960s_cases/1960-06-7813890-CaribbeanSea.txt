Subject: Two UFO Reports

1. Col. Gensen (26th Air Division D.I., Syracuse, 
Clearview 8-5500, ext 468) reported a UFO and described 
the two samples he obtained:

    a. A piece of material, maximum dimension 6 
    inches, light in weight as a sponge.

    b. A lemon shaped piece of very light metal.

Complete report will follow by wire.

2. This information came from the British West Indies 
Airlines through NORAD, then through the AF Command Post 
(WAFB, Maj Nebbal). At 26/0207z a British Viscount Airlines 
enroute from San Jan Puerto Rico to Barbados spotted a 
bright white object with a green center which disintegrated 
into a green-colored object.

This report was received at 2340CST 25 Jun.
