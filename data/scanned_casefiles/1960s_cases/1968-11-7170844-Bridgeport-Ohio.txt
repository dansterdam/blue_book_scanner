11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
The observer was driving his car when he saw one of the lights
of a TV tower start to move. The light moved up the tower and
then moved away to the west. The observer reported that the
light or object was red, cigar shaped, about 30 feet long, and
had a grayish color top.
