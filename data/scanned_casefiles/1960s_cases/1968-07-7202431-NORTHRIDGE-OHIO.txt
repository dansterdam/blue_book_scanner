The observer reported to the Duty Officer that she had sighted a bright white blue light that traveled from the North to the SSE. The light was seen for 1½ min but the observer did not hear any noise.

COMMENTS: The observer was requested to complete and return Form 117 but has not done so as of 5 Sep 63. The observer could be of a satellite or an aircraft, but since the Form 117 has not been returned it is being evaluated as insufficient data.
