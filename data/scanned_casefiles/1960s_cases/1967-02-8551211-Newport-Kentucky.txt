11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observers watched a white mixed with transparent blue colored object move up and down in the sky. The alleged UFO was compared to a Chinese Tree ornament hanging in the sky. There was no indication of the direction the witnesses were looking.

A study of the submitted data revealed that the witnesses probably were watching an astronomical body. There is no indication of direction could have been observed.
