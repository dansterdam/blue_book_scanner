PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE/TIME GROUP: 21 January 66 22/0700Z

2. LOCATION: Big Sur Hot Springs, California

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 4 - 5 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Northerly

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: No data presented to indicate object could NOT have been an A/C

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
The main object appeared as a bright light about the size of a softball. Its color was bluish white and was moving slowly across the sky parallel to the horizon. The secondary object was red in color, moved at the same speed as the main object and seemed to be following it. Observer stated that it looked like a tall light on a small plane but the two objects did not appear to be connected. Observer estimated the speed of the objects to be about 60 mph.
