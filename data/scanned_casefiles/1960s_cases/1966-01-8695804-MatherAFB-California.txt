11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers saw a green object the size of a grapefruit descending at a 15 deg angle with the horizontal. Object had a green tail about 2-3-20 ft long. When the object disappeared, it appeared to Point and distance was 15-20 miles from ground. The object did not make any maneuvers.
