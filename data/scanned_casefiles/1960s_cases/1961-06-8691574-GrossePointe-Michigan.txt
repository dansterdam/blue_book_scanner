UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT (UFO), Grosse Pointe, Michigan, Sighted By

ATIC 26-21-209(26)

1. The following information reported by the Office of the Fifth District Air Force, dated 10 June 1961 in the Special Investigations Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio concerning UFO, is furnished for your information and action deemed appropriate.

a. Description of the object:
(1) Shape - saucer
(2) Size - about the size of a film or nickel held at arm's length
(3) Color - white
(4) Number - 1
(5) No discernible features or details
(6) No tail, trail, exhaust or sound
(7) Observer stated object was bright and seemed to be hovering or at least was motionless

b. Description of Course of Object:
(1) Observer awoke from sleep for some reason and lay looking out her bedroom window that faces the east. Brightness of the object caught the observer's attention.
(2) Angle or elevation of object when first observed - approximately 50° from the horizon
(3) Angle or elevation of object upon disappearance - approximately 10° to 15° just as quickly gone
(4) Object just faded out like you would cut down a character at a fast rate
(5) Object was visible for about 10 minutes

c. Manner of Observation:
(1) Ground - visual
(2) Sighting made from bedroom
(3) No photograph taken - no physical evidence
