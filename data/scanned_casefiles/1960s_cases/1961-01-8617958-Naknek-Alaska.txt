10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: Obj 250° in dist. Accompanied by whistling sound. Obj glowed white. Pilot and passenger changed when hovering changed. Initial observation 0.30° at 10° elev. Obj moved to 210° elev 5° during 20 min observation. Lights reported 50-30°.

11. COMMENTS: No further details in file. Unusual sighting but little contrary to observer reliability. Probably a natural sighting. Observer doubtful.
