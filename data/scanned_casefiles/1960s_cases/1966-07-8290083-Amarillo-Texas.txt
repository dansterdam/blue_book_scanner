Brief Summary and Analysis:
Observer noted object that was traveling toward the NE. The front of the object was flat and the rear seemed to have a tail. A white light was also visible. At one time it looked like a jet afterburner was on. The object disappeared like a switch being turned off. The observer just happened to look up, he has seen other UFOs.
