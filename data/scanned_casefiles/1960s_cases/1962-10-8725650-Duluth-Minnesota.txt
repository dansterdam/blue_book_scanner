10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Bound object smaller than dime, sil-very. Observed at 30 dgr elevation 160 dgr azimuth. Disappeared at same position 2 hours later. Motion slow. Faded. Observation during day.

11. COMMENTS
Venus had a magnitude of -4.3 (exceptionally bright) & was at 30 dgr elevation 160 dgr azimuth at the time of the sighting. Venus was 20 dgrs from the sun with a declination of -22 degrees.
