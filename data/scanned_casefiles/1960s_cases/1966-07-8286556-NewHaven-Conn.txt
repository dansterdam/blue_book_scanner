11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers watched a silver colored object disappear six times behind a two story building. The object was completely round in shape and looked like a large aluminum sphere about 5 or 6 feet in diameter.

The description is consistent with that of a balloon observation.
