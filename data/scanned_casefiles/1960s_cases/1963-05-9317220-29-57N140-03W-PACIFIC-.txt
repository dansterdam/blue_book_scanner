PROJECT 10073 RECORD CARD

1. DATE: 9 May 1963

2. LOCATION: 29.57N 140.03W (PACIFIC)

3. DATE-TIME GROUP: 04/00157

4. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

5. PHOTOS: No

6. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: over one hour

7. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: Silver colored balloon observed drifting slowly SE. Balloon well above 33,000 feet at 2 jet at a time. Sighted from Ocean Station Victor. Also reported as UFO by Pan Am flight 818 and United Flight 99.

8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: one

9. COURSE: SE

10. SOURCE: Military

11. COMMENTS: Initially reported as UFO. Evaluation and subsequent rpt indicated obj was a balloon. Evaluated as "no threat" by Navy.

12. CONCLUSIONS: Possibly Balloon
