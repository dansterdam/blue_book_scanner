DETAILS OF INCIDENT:

Mr. [redacted], Stock Clerk at Aerojet, contacted Police Headquarters and stated he had observed a bright, white object, much like a satellite, about the 5 1/2 or a pea, traveling overhead in a straight line. The object was observed for approximately 7 minutes until it disappeared over the horizon. This occurred at approximately 2145 hours, 31 July 1964. Base Operations and ISO were notified and the above information relayed. SMAMA Command Post was also notified.
