Comments: Echo I and II not over Washington D C at the time
reported. However sighting is characteristic of a satellite.
Obj reported as being observed through a TV and three lights
visible. Speed and alt estimates inconsistent. Sighting on

11. EVALUATION
Some occasions of satellites. This particular observation
more likely a high flying a/c in view of the reported lights.

BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING:
Bright obj with three lights. Erratic motion to SE. No sound.
Bright yellow with a little sliver. Estimated speed of obj at
over 1,000 mph and alt above 200,000 ft. Shaped like bottom
of an iron. Sighted previous night going the opposite way.
