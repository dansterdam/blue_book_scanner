11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was cone shape light, extending downward, like center
of light of a police car. Red and white. Light was blinking
alternately, red and white at one second intervals. The
white light was going to the ground, looked like a plane
with landing lights on except flashing. Object rose from
E to perpendicular then down over W horizon. Object faded out
of sight as it approached horizon.
