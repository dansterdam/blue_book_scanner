11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers watched an object in which a small person was
seen standing in the doorway. The person was approximately
3-4 feet tall and was dressed in black. The object however
was about 15 feet approximately above the ground. On
particular a low humming sound was noted. Object traveled NE
toward Harlinger at a high rate of speed. On 3 Sept. 66
another member of the family observed an object that caused
the TV to go on the blank. The house was lit up with eerie
red and yellow light. On 5 Sept. 66 another member of the
family observed a small person dressed in form fitting with
enter into the dark bedroom. Several minutes a bar of light
appeared from the room.
