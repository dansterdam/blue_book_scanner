1. PURPOSE: This report is in response to Work Order Number 67-37 submitted by Major Quintanilla (DETE), to determine the identity of the object photographed.

2. ANALYSIS: The photography appears genuine insofar as content is concerned. No satisfactory explanation could be made of the unidentified object. Examination of the submitted print has negated retouching. Attempts to make a double exposure could not be defined. Source stated the object was photographed with a Polaroid camera, yet two small black dots appear on the frame border indicating that automatic processing was used to develop the print. As a result, it is impossible to determine whether the print was made from an original Polaroid or other type negative. The camera focal length is therefore unknown and a scaling factor to size the object was precluded. The object appears to be circular in planform and basically flat in cross-section. A small degree of highlighting is visible along the top contour of the object. The unidentified object is possibly a result of an attempt at photographing a general outdoor scene from indoors through a window, and recording the reflection of a light source (i.e. an illuminated floor lamp or other type shaded interior light). Source stated that the object was about 25 feet in diameter, but if the object is a reflection of an interior light, the diameter is likely not to exceed one or two feet.
