MEMORANDUM FOR THE RECORD

[Redacted], Central Airlines Agent
2030 local time 2 August 1962 Liberal, Kansas

1. [Redacted] received a telephone call reporting bright objects moving East to West at a very fast speed. He tried to see the objects but could not. He talked with Captain [Redacted] pilot of the flight (PF) from Wichita to Liberal who reported several green and blue objects flying East to West at terrific speed. The objects were in sight some 10 to 15 seconds.

2. Captain [Redacted] an airline pilot, reported an extremely bright object looking like an aircraft with landing lights stationary in the eastern sky. About 2100 hrs. when outside and observed a white object moving from East to West at a high speed. The object was in sight for two seconds and appeared to pass overhead.

3. There were numerous phone calls made to Mr. [Redacted] office regarding these objects. One witness reported a roaring noise. Mr. [Redacted], while in Conk Wichita public cafe, Guyman City, was informed that they saw the objects also.

4. The stationary object was Jupiter at a magnitude of -2.4, its maximum brightness, and at a 10 degree elevation. Jupiter was in the East at azimuth of 100 degrees. The moving objects are evaluated as meteors.
