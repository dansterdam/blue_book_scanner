PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 17 April 66 17/2330Z

2. LOCATION: Teaticket, Mass (2 Witness)

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 10 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: W

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: AIRCRAFT Prob. Jet

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer sighted two red flashing circles the size of two apples in formation side by side. Upon disappearance three appeared below and between the two red lights like a rocket thruster lighting. Observed in the West traveling west and ascending above the horizon. Level flight until ignition. After the thrust then object ascended above the horizon. The probable cause of the sighting was the after glow and navigational lights of an F-100 which was airborne OHS AFB at the time of the sighting.
