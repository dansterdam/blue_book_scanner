11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noted 4 silver disc hovering by each wing. The objects stayed with the aircraft as aircraft made turn. New Orleans aircraft, reported National airlines aircraft landing at the time of sighting. Crew members nor passengers noticed anything unusual. It appears that aircraft engine exhaust was responsible for sighting.
