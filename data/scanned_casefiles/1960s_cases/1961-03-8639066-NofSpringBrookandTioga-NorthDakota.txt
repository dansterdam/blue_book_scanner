PROJECT 10073 RECORD CARD

1. DATE: 1 Mar 61
2. LOCATION: N of Spring Brook and Tioga, North Dakota
3. DATE-TIME GROUP: 2050
4. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual
5. GMT: 020502
6. PHOTOS: No
7. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 15-20 min
8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: 1
9. COURSE: Descending
10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: White circular object.. Attracted attention of observer because of brightness. Appeared on horizon in NW and disappeared below horizon after 15-20 min. Moved N and S. Multiple reports fm general area.

12. CONCLUSIONS:
- Was Balloon: No
- Probably Aircraft: No
- Was Astronomical: Probably Planet Venus
- Possibly Astronomical: Venus

COMMENTS: Object was probably Planet Venus. Time of sighting was in direction of Venus. Venus had mag of -3. Atmospheric refraction probably caused distortion and gave apparent motion to obj. Sighting coincided with setting of planet.

SOURCE: Civilian and Military
