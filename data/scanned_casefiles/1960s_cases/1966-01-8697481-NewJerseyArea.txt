MEMO FOR THE RECORD

6 January 1966

Colonel Holcolm From New Jersey called to get a copy of AFR-200-2.

A UFO report was given to him. A man and his sons saw a round, disc object flying 200 feet above the ground traveling about 75 m.p.h.

The duration was five minutes. This object was spotted at 2315 local time on 4 January. Sgt. Moody told him to advise future observers to report to their nearest Air Force Base, which in this case is McGuire Air Force Base.
