11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer sighted two lights going different directions. Object 2 was first seen in the SW and disappeared in the SE. Object 1 traveled from south to north. They seemed to cross paths. They were both amber in color and object 2 passed at 2 points. Object 2 was in sight for 4 minutes and object one was in view for 10 minutes.

Investigating officer felt that the objects could have been commercial type aircraft that are known to utilize the general area where the objects were observed.

Pegasus II at 1933 EST(0032) Moving SW to SE.
