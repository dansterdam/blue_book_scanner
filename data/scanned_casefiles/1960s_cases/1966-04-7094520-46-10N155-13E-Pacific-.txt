11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was initially a ball, then broke into 2 pieces about the size of a pea. Brilliant white center with red and blue fringing at the trailing edge of flame. The trail was dear drop shaped. Tail of fire began to fall at once. Cone seemed to break into two pieces more than continued to fall for about five seconds more.
No satellite decays reported on that date. Probable meteor.
