4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
One saucer shaped object, aluminum silvery in color. Object suspended in sky about 35 deg above the horizon and zero deg azimuth. Obj stayed suspended in air about 50 seconds, obj disappeared from sight into a cumulus cloud. Obj took two seconds to make the climb.

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
8 minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION
Ground Visual

7. COURSE
Northeast

There were aircraft in the area at the time departing from Baldwin County airport and traveling north, the same direction that witness saw object travel. It is possible that observer saw one and/or both of these aircraft with sunlight reflecting on it. However, carried as insufficient data for evaluation, since observer did not return 164.
