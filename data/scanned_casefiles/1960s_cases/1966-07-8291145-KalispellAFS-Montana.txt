PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 15 July 66 16/0320

2. LOCATION: Kalispell AFS, Montana (2 Witnesses)

3. SOURCE: Military

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 2 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Varied

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Other (UNCLASSIFIED) Very likely, Not

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observers noted two distinct white objects that appeared as two circular bright lights. Both objects would disappear into the clouds, and then reappear again. The flight path would cross each other and disappear behind clouds, then reappear heading the opposite direction. This pattern was continuous for approximately 2 minutes.
