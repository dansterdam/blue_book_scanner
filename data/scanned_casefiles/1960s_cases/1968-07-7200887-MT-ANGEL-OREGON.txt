1. The Aerial Phenomena Office is in receipt of an unidentified
flying object (UFO) report from Oregon which occurred between
Mt. Angel and Silverton. The sighting was made on 15 July 1968
at approximately 7:37 AM.

2. The witness stated that he observed a slightly cigar shaped object,
that appeared metallic, like silver, when flying it made sharp angles
and disappeared by going straight up. The center of the base appeared
to be revolving and reflecting sunlight.  Total observation time was
eight minutes.

3. Did you receive any reports of unusual objects for this date?
We would appreciate your comments as to a possible cause for this
sighting.

4. Thank you for your assistance on this matter.
