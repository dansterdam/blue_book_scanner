BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Three reports of statlike object with erratic flight. ECHO II over the area at the time of sighting. Differences in direction and apparent erratic flight including hovering is attributed to reporting error and/or atmospheric conditions. Description of object similar to that of satellite.
