11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers noted bluish green object that rapidly change to a more orange color. The shape was round, and appeared larger than an aircraft. There was no radar pickup of alleged UFO. The extreme brilliance was said to be the cause for the sighting.

There was a satellite decay object No. 1966-59H (2302) on the date of the sighting; however no decay location is available.
