10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Object resembling shooting star except 10-15 times wider, with smoke trail observed about noon at 8000 ft. Color of object brilliant white as a though of burning magnesium. Appeared to move slower than shooting star. Appeared 30° above horizon and disappeared 15°. No direction or object given although traveling toward Indian Ocean.
