10. CONCLUSION
Pos. Satellite - 1 without diverting conflict, but may be in error.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers reported seeing white lights in the sky at different times. In both cases the object was comparable to a star. First object disappeared behind a cloud - the second behind some trees. Object was round being comparable to a ball.

Civilian aircraft were in the area and could have been responsible for the sightings.
