1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 26 January 66 27/0210

2. LOCATION: Bridgeport, New York

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 5 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Toward the observers and down, slight to the N

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Astro (MOON) - Moon at 23h44m (setting in W). Sirius at 40 deg elevation in SW (too small). Sound like A/C. 164 sent for more information.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observers saw an object at about 30 deg elevation in the SE. The object was circular like the bottom of a half moon, and "as yellow then grey in color". It seemed to be surrounded by clouds or smoke, gave a very hazy appearance. At first they thought it was the moon but it is seemed to move somewhat to the North. It seemed to move towards the observers and downward. It vanished after about five minutes.
