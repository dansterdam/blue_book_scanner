11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noted a saucer shaped object in the western sky. It was white in the middle, red on the bottom, and green on the top. The light just was hovering there; however, it did look like it was floating and dipping. The object just faded from view after approximately one hour.

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
one hour

The description is consistent with that of an astronomical observation. Venus would have been quite bright in the western sky.
