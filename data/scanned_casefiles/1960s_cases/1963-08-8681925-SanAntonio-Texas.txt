Round obj changing colors from white to blue to red observed at 90 deg elevation heading on course of 330 deg. Disappeared at 60 deg elevation in North after 15 min observation. No radar pickup from local ACW squadron. Witness upon 5X and could not determine any additional details. Windbat surface from 180 deg and generally North West at altitude.
