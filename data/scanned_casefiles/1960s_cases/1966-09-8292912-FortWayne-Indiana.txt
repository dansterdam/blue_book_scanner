1. DATE : TIME GROUP
8 Sept. 66  1450Z

2. LOCATION
Fort Wayne, Indiana

3. SOURCE
Civilian

10. CONCLUSION
Aircraft (possibly)

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS
One

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer noted circular object slightly larger than a grapefruit at arms length. The object was silvery white and was very bright and of constant intensity. When first observed the object was traveling from the NE to SW in a descending attitude. Object turned due west, maintained this heading momentarily then altered course to the NE. Faded from view in the NE.

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
45 seconds

Description is consistent with that of an aircraft. Without any estimate of altitude, speed, rate of descent, etc. a comprehensive evaluation is not warranted.

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION
Ground Visual

NE
