Dear Sir:

At 9:15 PM, September 21, 1964, Mr. [redacted], Berea, Ohio, telephonically contacted this office and furnished the following information:

Mr. [redacted] stated that at 9:11 PM, September 21, 1964, he and his wife saw an unidentified flying object traveling from the southeast to the northwest at a terrific rate of speed. He described the unidentified flying object as a very bright light flashing on and off with yellow to white color. It was visible shape and it was almost blinding to the eye. Mr. [redacted] stated that someone outside looking up at the sky have seen this light.

The above is being submitted for such action as you deem appropriate and no investigation is being conducted by this office.
