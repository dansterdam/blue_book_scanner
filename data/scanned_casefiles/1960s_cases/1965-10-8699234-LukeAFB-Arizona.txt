PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 25 October 65 26/0232z

2. LOCATION: Luke AFB, Arizona

3. SOURCE: Military

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 1 hour, still there

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: Stationary

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION:
1. Astro *(VENUS)
2. Astro*(JUPITER)
Two planets. Observed Venus in 5W, Jupiter in SSE.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Object resembled a star or planet. White, changing to orange and black to white. 40 to 60 deg elevation to 5W, movement very erratic, with changes in direction from 3 to N to SE to E. Also changes in elevation. General direction of movement was west to east. Disappeared, and reappeared after about 1 hour. Second observation from coast to coast. Object still visible with very high thin clouds.
