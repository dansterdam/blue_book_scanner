10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Large starlike flashing object, flashing at rate of about twice a second, magnitude of planet white. Moving much faster than a satellite. Observed at 70 dgr elevation from a/c at 35,000ft at 300 dgr azimuth. Object disappeared at straight flight of 1 min 30 secs by fading prior to reaching the horizon at 120 dgr azimuth. Other a/c tracking intercepts on witnesses a/c were at lower altitude. Looked like a/c at 70,000 ft with speed of Mach 3.

11. COMMENTS
Duration of sighting indicates that speed excessive for satellite. Case evaluated as a/c sighting.

Was Aircraft
Probably Aircraft
Possibly Aircraft
