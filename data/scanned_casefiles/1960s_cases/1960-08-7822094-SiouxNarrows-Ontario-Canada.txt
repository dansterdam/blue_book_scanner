10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Bright object spotted at zenith on 105° course. Disappeared behind tree. Seemed too fast to be Echo. Object 75° as bright as Jupiter.

11. COMMENTS
The sighting was made in Aug 60, but ATIC did not receive report until Feb 61. The report contains insufficient data for a valid conclusion, and the time lapse between the sighting and receipt of the report is too long for an investigation. To be initiated. Therefore, this report is evaluated as insufficient data.
