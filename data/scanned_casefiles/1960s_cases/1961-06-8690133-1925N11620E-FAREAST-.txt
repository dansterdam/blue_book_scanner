From: Commander U.S. Naval Defense Forces Eastern Pacific
To: Commander, ATIC, Wright Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, Ohio
Subj: Unidentified flying object

1. The following report was received by the Master of SS NORMAN BEAR of the Pacific Far East Lines and is forwarded for information.

"At 101158 (June 1961) while taking evening stars, observed a bright object at latitude 19.25N - 116.25E bearing 320 degrees, altitude 60 degrees. Brilliancy identical to that of Jupiter. Vehicle was directly overhead at 1124 emitting fire and fragments and continuing on course bearing 140 degrees, altitude 20 degrees at 1120 before is disappeared behind a heavy cloud formation with a brilliancy of a 1st magnitude star."

2. The above sighting was confirmed by two other officers and appeared similar to Echo satellite in orbit direction, but did not reappear.

3. Comment: Master is _______, a sincere, intelligent individual who has furnished reliable information in the past.
