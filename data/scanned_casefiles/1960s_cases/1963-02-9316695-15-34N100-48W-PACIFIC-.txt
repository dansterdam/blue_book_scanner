From: Commanding Officer, USS MARYSVILLE (EPCER-857)
To: Commander, U.S. Naval Oceanographic Office, Washington, Maryland

Subj: Satellite sighting; report

1. The USS MARYSVILLE (EPCER-857) in position 19-34.5N, 100-48.3W, course 060°T, speed 6-4 knots, at approximately 1520 GMT, sighted a Satellite bearing 147°T at an altitude of 42° which when first sighted appeared slightly lower than the star Antares and moved in a northward direction. And disappeared on a bearing of 095°T. The entire observation taking about five (5) minutes. The satellite appeared to be in a polar orbit.

2. The sky was broken with Cumulus and Cirrus clouds. Air temperature 31°F and Barometric pressure 29.12 inches, base calm.
