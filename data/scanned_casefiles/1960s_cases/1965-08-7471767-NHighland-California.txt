11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Two objects like stars travelling at a very high altitude. #1 was moving NW and #2 was moving NE. Object changed from red to blue green to white.

COMMENTS: Object #1 moving NW did not have duration. Characteristic of Satellite except for direction. Evaluated as an A/C. Object #2 observed in NW and appearing to move to the NE had the characteristics of an Astro Body. This object was observed for two hours. The Star ARCTURUS was in the West at 20 deg elevation and would appear to move NW and to the North from 230 deg azimuth at 2200 to the horizon at 295 deg azimuth two hours later. Disappearance also in accord with this analysis. The colors reported are also in accord with this analysis.
