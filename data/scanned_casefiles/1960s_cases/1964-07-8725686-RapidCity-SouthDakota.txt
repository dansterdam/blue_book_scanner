On 8 Jul 64 T/Sgt David Moody received a call from Lt Scott, Base PIO, Ellsworth AFB, Rapid City, South Dakota. Two reports of a UFO's were called in to the local TV station. One by a tourist, the other by a local resident. Names of the two witnesses were not given. The sighting occurred at 0520 local time. Object described as looking like a rocket with a green tail by the tourist. The resident said that the object came from the Southeast and had a bluish glow. This is all the information that was obtained. Since the names of the witnesses were not given a further check was not made. Lt Scott was informed that no report would be necessary since all information had been given over the telephone.
