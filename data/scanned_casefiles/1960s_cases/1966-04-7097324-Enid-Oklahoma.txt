11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was described as being 4 feet diameter, 6 feet long and round like a light bulb. Object appeared bright and spread to be across the street but did not illuminate the area. The flight path was east to west with some slight descent. Investigating officer stated that several people in the area observed a meteor at the time of the observation and in the same relative position as described by the observer.
