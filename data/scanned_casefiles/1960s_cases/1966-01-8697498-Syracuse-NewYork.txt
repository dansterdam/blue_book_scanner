PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE-TIME GROUP: 5 January 66 05/2300Z

2. LOCATION: Syracuse, New York

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 2300Z @ 9400Z

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: N/A

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: AIRCRAFT

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observer saw a red-white object from 2300 to 2400Z. The object had possible wings and three lights. The size was comparable to a large passenger airliner. The flight of the object seemed to be erratic.

Verona test had a 13-57 351, col sig "FIDES 23", flew over Syracuse-Oneida Lake at 23052 to 03002 at 15,000 feet.
