1. DATE - TIME GROUP
10 August 65  11/0152

2. LOCATION
Orlando, Florida

3. SOURCE
Civilian

10. CONCLUSION
Astro (VENUS)
VENUS near horizon in West setting at time of report. That object reflected light on lake indicates that it was at a low angle. Colors and motion in accord with description. Nothing in detail of vapor trail attributed to imagination of witness.

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS
One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
20 - 25 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION
Ground-Visual

7. COURSE
West

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Round object with color changing from red to yellow-orange and white and back to red. Appeared round and moved like a gyroscope. Disappeared leaving a vapor trail. No sound. Would move then reappear object. Light reflected on nearby lake. Disappeared in West. WK described as a cloudy night. VENUS at 11.18 RA with WNW -3.4. At 220 deg azimuth, just above horizon in process of setting at time of report. Motion and color change regarded as illusion distorted by the imagination of the witness.
