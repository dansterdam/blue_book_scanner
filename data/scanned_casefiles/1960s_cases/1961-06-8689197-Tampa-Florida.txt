9. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Star-like object sighted on course of 140 deg from Tampa. Color bluish red changing to white. About the size of a star except brighter. Altitude very high. Object picked up on radar with a bearing of 055 deg at 30,000 ft. Moved up and down, but maintain a same relative position.

10. COMMENTS
Radar and visual targets were different locations on the witness was requested to send additional information. No reply to this request. Speculation on the incident was that the object viewed visually was an a/c, but there is no confirmation of data. No attempt is made to determine the radar sighting in view of the limited data this case is listed as insufficient data.
