AIRCRAFT

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Three observers called Craig AFB and reported seeing an orange glowing object in the sky. It moved in a straight line. It appeared short, round, and blunt.

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
About 10 Minutes

The investigating officer said that he and another officer at approximately 1811 CST were on the flight line and observed a B-52 east of Craig AFB pulling a short contrail. Both the B-52 and the contrail were glowing orange in color. It was his opinion that this is what the three witnesses saw also.

UFO - 31 March 66

At 2125E the following info was called in by a Major Phillips, Emergency Returns Officer at Craig A7B, Ala.:

A Mr. Rutledge of radio station WHBB, Selma, Alabama called Major Chapman to report the following three UFO sightings which were phoned in to the radio station:

1. @ 1818 a young lady - no name, age, or address given - called to report a sighting at 1814 c. Reported a very small, very high, pale orange object, moving in a straight line. Object appeared to be short, round, and blunt and was visible for approximately 10 minutes.
