10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Oblong object, size of softball, variously described as bluish-white, or possibly with tail 20 times longer than main body. Appeared to have more motion than fall, meteor-like. Was much brighter and fall in a slow arc. Disappeared by burning out.

11. COMMENTS
Multiple sighting of same object indicates variations in description of object, even disagreement on color. Probably a meteor.
