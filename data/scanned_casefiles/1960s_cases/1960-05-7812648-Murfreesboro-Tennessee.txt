10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Smooth object, somewhat like a disc, size of a star, very bright blue like Venus observed approximately 6 or 7 small lights. It got bigger and then after 5 minutes, object disappeared and was replaced by 6 or 7 small lights like a constellation. These faded out after about one minute.

11. COMMENTS
Description of sighting conforms exactly to the description of a balloon in flight, and then bursting. The windsend direction of flight of the object are identical, as is the duration of the sighting and the distance traveled by the object prior to it's disappearance. Case listed as a balloon observation.
