10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Dark obj with orange tail size of F-86 or F-94 and about 15' in length. Observed in a/c heading 268°. Obj 45° fwd of right wing when sighted. Disappeared 45° rear of right wing. Obj's altitude approx 60,000'. Speed estimate 5000 mph.

11. COMMENTS
Obj of this sighting has all characteristics of a meteor. There is no evidence which would indicate that this obj was not a meteor. Was probably a meteor of "fireball" class and initial experience of observer with type; therefore resulting in misidentification. It is concluded that obj of sighting was probably a meteor.
