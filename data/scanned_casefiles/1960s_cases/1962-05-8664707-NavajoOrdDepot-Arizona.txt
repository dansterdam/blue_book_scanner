10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
An object which appeared white. Bright and on a light was observed by engineer of a Santa Fe train headed 7 between Flagstaff and Williams. Radians. Object appeared high on horizon. Did not maneuver. Disappeared high on horizon. No trail or exhaust visible. Object appeared to move from left to the right of the witness.

11. COMMENTS
Check with personnel of the Navajo Ord Depot revealed that an ordinance display which was given armed Forces Day was responsible. For this sighting. (Confirmed by telephone call to Adjutant of Navajo Ord Depot.)
