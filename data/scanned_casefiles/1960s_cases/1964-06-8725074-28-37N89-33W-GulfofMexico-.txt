10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Received on radio at New Orleans Coast Guard Station, message that a mushroom cloud was observed in mushroom cloud that looked like an atomic explosion with cloud about 10 miles high and about 10 min. Nothing fell into water. Ship went to investigate. A/C sent to aid investigation. No explanation of explosion. Visual and Radar search made of area. Corrected position was 23.37N 89.33W.

11. COMMENTS
Description indicates munitions test. Eglin AFB and other activities using this area for testing.
