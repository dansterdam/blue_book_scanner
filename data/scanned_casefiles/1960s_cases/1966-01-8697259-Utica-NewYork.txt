BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer stated that he saw one object which appeared and disappeared several times during a 3 hour 45 minute time period. The object appeared as a starlike bright light and was hazy to the naked eye. At times the object traveled in a straight line, arced overhead, and hovered. At times the object moved at speeds estimated at over 1,600 mph, and at other times was stationary.
