10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Egg-shaped object, green turning to white with bluish green long flame. Object had a descending straight path, appeared to burn out with sparks from the body. Object was the size of a dime.

11. COMMENTS
Object was observed by many individuals throughout the state of Louisiana. From all indications the object appears to have been a meteor.

UFO    Major Cathcart, TAC COC

Unidentified flying object sighted by 401st Tactical Fighter Wing personnel. Five sources at England AFB sighted object between 1700 and 2100 CST. One was a Klodt pilot on way to Keesler AFB, 1Col DeWitt, 401st tower personnel, the England AFB Airdrome Officer, and a Braniff Airlines pilot over Monroe, La. Object was described as appearing like a bright light moving slowly and giving off sparks. Disappeared to the northwest. AF pilot states light bright enough to read all instruments.

Airdrome Officer states unlike any meteor he ever seen. New Orleans control center states no known A/C with problems in the area. England AFB reports no local flying in the area.

Major Cathcart
