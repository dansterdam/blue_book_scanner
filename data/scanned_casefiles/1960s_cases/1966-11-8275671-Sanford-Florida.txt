11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observer watched a triangle shaped object with red, white,
and green lights hover over some tree top level and dive
down to the ground then reappear and move off toward the
north. Object was in view for about 2-4 minutes before its
disappearance.

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
2-4 minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION
Ground Visual

7. COURSE
Northward

The description is consistent with that of an aircraft
observation possibly that of a helicopter. Observer was
four miles south of the Sanford Naval Air Station. It
appears that she observed a helicopter over the base.
