11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Light colored cigar-shaped object sighted at dusk. No sound trail or unusual features. In west and appeared to be several hundred feet above buildings. Altitude same with object moving to the North. Speed was moderate. Duration 5-10 minutes. Black clouds visible below the object. Sky clear behind and above.
