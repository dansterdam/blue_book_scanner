PROJECT 1007 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 6 June 66 C7/0255Z

2. LOCATION: Tyler, Texas

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: Not Reported

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

7. COURSE: East

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Possible (AIRCRAFT) Prob. Jet

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer noted two similar objects that were going: east fast as a jet, saw 10,000 feet or so high, and on the zenith. The smaller light traveled on the south side of the larger object across the sky, drawing closer, lagging, behind and finally to the north of the arc out of sight. Description is consistent with an aircraft flying at a high altitude with sun reflection off the fuselage.
