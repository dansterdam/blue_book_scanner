PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 27 September 66  27/1728Z

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 20 Seconds

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Air-Visual

7. COURSE: ENE

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: Other (MISSIL. ACTIVITY)  Prob. Fact.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observer noted round shape object bright green in color traveling toward the ENE. Object approached the aircraft in descending fashion. Object slowed down and appeared like it was floating with a parachute. Object was visible for about 30-40 seconds before disappearing into a cloud bank. The location of the sighting coincides with the location of the Soviet Mid-Pacific Impact area; however, there were no launch's announced by the Soviets.
