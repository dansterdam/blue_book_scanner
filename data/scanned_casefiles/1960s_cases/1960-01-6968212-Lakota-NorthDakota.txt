10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Object described in great detail for an observation lasting 5-10 seconds. Crescent shaped object with nose pointing up. Color yellowish-green or aqua. Appearance on left horn of pan shaped light. Large cone surrounded a shaft between the horns of the crescent. Bright light emanated from the object in its entirety. First observed low on the horizon and faded into ground fog.

11. COMMENTS
Second overall description without the detail. Confirms to a meteor observation. Duration of the sighting and motion is consistent with a slow moving meteor. The lyeids meteor occurs between 12-23 January and is a slow moving shower. It is possible that this was a meteor from this stream.
