10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Small circular or egg shaped object, light reddish color, in steady flight. Fast speed. Observed for only 10 seconds. Total duration 4-5 seconds. Two observers 6 miles apart. Second observer described object as having pointed nose with glowing white plane high rate of speed. Possible trail. Observed to pass overhead in North to South flight. Duration 10 seconds.

11. COMMENTS
Description, duration and general pattern indicate meteor observation.
