BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observers sighted an object that was "white at one time and red the other". The object was as bright as a street lamp and had antennas on top.

COMMENTS: The reporting officer wrote that "The flight path of the observed object falls on a traffic pattern of Lubbock Municipal Airport". The landing lights and confirmation of landing traffic by Lubbock airport controlling agencies, lead this investigator, to believe that the object sighted was an airplane at the Lubbock Municipal Airport.
