PROJECT 10073 RECORD

1. DATE-TIME GROUP: 21 July 65  22/0130Z

2. LOCATION: Monticello, New York

3. SOURCE: Civilian

4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Two

5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 2 Minutes & 5 Minutes

6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual (GV)

7. COURSE: Nonverved

8. PHOTOS: No

9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

10. CONCLUSION: No data presented to indicate that the objects could NOT have been A/C. Four jet air routes intersect 20 mi South of observer location.

11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Report submitted in August over one month after sighting. Consisted of triangular shaped object white with red nose and a bright light aft. Described as Coast watching in the sky. 1st object was 40-50 deg elevation, 2d object 5-10 deg elevation. 2d disappeared over the horizon. Fast speed reported is inconsistent with a duration of 2 minutes.

SUBJECT: UFO Sighting

A. Description of the object:
1. The UFO was triangular in shape.

2. The UFO was the size of a pea to the eye, the size of a nickel in a 6 power telescope.

3. The object was white to the eye. The object had a red nose, and a white body with a bright light aft in the scope.

4. There was one UFO.

5. N/A

6. Lines on front and port of nose.

7. A bright light, like a "sparkler aft".

8. None.

9. None.

B. Description of Course of Object:
1. Observers attention was drawn to object because of its fast movement "fastest thing in the sky".

2. The UFO was at an angle of 40° to 50° above the horizon when sighted first. It reappeared at the angle of 5-10° above horizon.

3. The UFO faded out near vertical and passed over the horizon on second sighting.

4. Zigzag, or like lights flashing on aircraft wing tips. The UFO went from North to South, then passed from East to West near Southern horizon. It moved faster than a satellite and unlike a meteor. It moved to fast to be observed in any but the finder scope of observers astronomical telescope.

5. Faded near vertical and passed over horizon second sighting.

6. The UFO was first visible less than two minutes, then reappeared about 20 minutes later for about five minutes.
