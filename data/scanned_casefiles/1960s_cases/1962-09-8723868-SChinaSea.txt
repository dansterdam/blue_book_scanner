4-6 seconds

7. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Object moving from South to North passed from ship and disappeared into clouds. Under observation for 4-6 secs. Distinguish from nearby 1) Near proximity to ship 2) sparks trailing object 3) body length. Thought to be satellite junk burning.

8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS
One

9. COURSE
North

10. COMMENTS
None of the indications given are reasons for the object not being a meteor; rather, all are indications of a meteor sighting. Duration in line with this analysis. Satellite decay of longer duration; although, 62A Sigma decayed on 10 September. Case evaluated as a meteor sighting.
