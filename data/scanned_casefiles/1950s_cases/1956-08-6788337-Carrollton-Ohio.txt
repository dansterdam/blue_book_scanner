10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One cigar-shaped object approx 150 feet long. Color was silver changing to blue with glowing red fringe. Object moved up and down, back and forth, heading in a northwesterly direction at an altitude of 2,000 to 3,000 feet. Speed was variable, humming sound was noted. Object was observed for twenty minutes visually and through binoculars. Object had been seen for past two days.

Investigation of this sighting assigned to PIt 3-B, 4602d AISS will be forwarded.
