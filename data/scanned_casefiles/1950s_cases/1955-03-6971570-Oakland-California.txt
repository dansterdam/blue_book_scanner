10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object, size of a pin head held at arm's length, silver in color, was observed by a GOC Observer while on duty in the Oakland Bay area. A B-47 type aircraft was in the air at the time of the sighting, near the location of the sighting.

11. COMMENTS
D/L 28th ADW gave the opinion that sighting was caused by a reflection from stated B-47 aircraft which was coming in over the water for a landing approach.
