10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Three round yellow objects the size of a quarter at arm's length. Objects were in the formation of a triangle and flew from approximately East to West. Objects were seen through binoculars for five seconds.

11. COMMENTS
Description indicates that this sighting was probably caused by aircraft. Speed & duration indicates possible meteor sighting. Object moved through 20 dgr arc in 5 seconds at extreme elevation yet disappeared into fog bank.

Insufficient Data for Evaluation
Due to conflicting information
