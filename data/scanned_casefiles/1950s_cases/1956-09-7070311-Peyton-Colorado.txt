10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Three objs observed on 1,2,3 Sep 56. Objs compared "size" of hail-dollar at arm's length, light, revolving, ground & white light. Approx 22502 at 3006le. Obj moved W to about 2150 & disappeared behind mountain at approx 4200. At 03062, 2nd obj moved N to 2959 at 03082 disappeared over horizon at 0500Z. Initially at 03452, obj move E at 06000Z when observer stopped watching, obj was still in sight at 50dgr azimuth, 25dgrel. Duration 2 hrs.

11. COMMENTS

Object #1 was planet Saturn,
Object #2 star Arcturus
Object #3 star Capella
