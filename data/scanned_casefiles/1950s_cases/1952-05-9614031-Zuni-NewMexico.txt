Subject: FLYOBRT

In accordance with AFR 200-2, dated 20 April 1952, Subject: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting (Short Title:FLYOBRT), the following report of unidentified flying object is submitted:

On 24 May 1952, the pilot of T.A. 220, sighted that appeared to him to be two reddish colored tornados over Zuni. Vicinity: Eagle. The pilot made this sighting at approximately 0242 about 6 to 7 miles southwest of Zuni. These objects appeared directly in front of his lone. One of 2 objects appeared to be stationary, hovering, and during that time well through an arc of 60 degrees to the southwest. Heading in close, the second object was seen, a very sharp, neat cone. The speed and distance of the objects could not be estimated.

The reliability of the informant is considered excellent.
