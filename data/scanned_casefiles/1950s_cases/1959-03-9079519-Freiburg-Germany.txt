20 MAR 1959

CLASSIFICATION: UNCLASSIFIED

DATE OF INFORMATION: 9 March 1959
FROM: Naval Attaché, Bonn

SUBJECT: GERMAN FEDERAL REPUBLIC Unidentified Flying Object

BRIEF REPORT (ONE PAGE ENTER CAREFUL SUMMARY)

1. The permanent observer of the Climatic Station of Koenigafeld (Black Forest) of the Freiburg Weather Bureau saw on Thursday evening at 2229 hours a shining yellow ball which was surrounded by a greenish halo. The ball had a speed of about 600-800 per hour. Two other inhabitants also observed the ball on a its flight from North North West to South South East.

2. On the same evening, about the same time, a young American couple - the husband to spraying photos - the Freiburg university, saw the metallic flying object in the sky in Freiburg. According to statements of the couple, the disk made its appearance like a shooting star, then changed the direction of its flight and immediately afterwards went out of sight. The speed allegedly was considerably high. The observers described the object as shining white with a slight yellow shimmer.
