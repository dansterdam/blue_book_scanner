GLOWING OBJECT BELIEVED METEOR-1/4 radiant celestial phenomenon which had the shape of a ball with a tail was observed on July 20 by a number of persons in central Sweden. The Stockholm observatory at Saltsjobaden was unusually bright at the time. It was also believed that defense observers in northern Sweden the same night that they had seen a huge fire ball which resembled a rocket. It was seen in the southwestern sky at about a height of 1,500 feet. A similar phenomenon was observed in Finland and Norway. (Stockholm, English, July 21, 1958, 1300 GMT-E) (UNCLASSIFIED)

UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT-1/3 strange bright object was seen moving in the sky in south Finland at 2200 on July 20. According to eyewitnesses, the object was shaped like a goblet, one end shining brightly. It was seen for four to five seconds, moving northwestward. It was also reported from Norway that at 2230 Finnish time two strange lights had been seen moving rapidly northwest, leaving behind a greenish-yellow trail. (Helsinki, Home, July 21, 1958, 0430 GMT-L) (UNCLASSIFIED)
