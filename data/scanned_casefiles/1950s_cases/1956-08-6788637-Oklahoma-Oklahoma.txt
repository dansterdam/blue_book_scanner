10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One cigar-shaped object hallowed in yellow light. Color was between orange and yellow. Size was about size of grapefruit when observed through binoculars. Object appeared to move from north. Object moved from 250° to 200° azimuth and ascended. Observed for period of three hours visually and with binoculars. Disappeared slowly until no longer visible.
