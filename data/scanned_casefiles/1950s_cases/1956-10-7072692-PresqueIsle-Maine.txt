10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One orange-red, round object with a stream or tail size of a dime at arm's length. Object's tail was same size as object proper & was tapered toward the rear. Object was initially sighted at 30dgr elevation in the north and traveled at great speed to the east where it dropped out of sight below the horizon. Object was viewed visually for 2 or three seconds.
