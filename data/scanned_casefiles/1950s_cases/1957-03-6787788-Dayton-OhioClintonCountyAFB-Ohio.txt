10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One white flash of lights seen moving from west to east on a level flight path. Object had a reddish trail one-half the size of the flash. Object illuminated 2-3 seconds. Object was seen visually for three to five seconds.

11. COMMENTS
Description, duration, flight path, manner of disappearance (fading rapidly) indicate that this sighting was probably caused by a fireball meteor.
