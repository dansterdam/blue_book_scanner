PROJECT 10073 RECORD CARD

1. DATE: 1 August 1956

2. LOCATION: Shalimar, Florida

3. DATE/TIME GROUP: Local 02/0155Z

4. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground-Visual

5. SOURCE: Military

6. PHOTOS: No

7. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: six seconds

8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: one

9. COURSE: see below

10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING: One round brilliant white object with slightly flattened sides, size of pea at arm's length. Object seemed to twinkle or radiate like a star. Object appeared as a stationary light in the sky. Object moved very slowly in a horizontal, slightly downward path. No change in direction. Object was initially sighted at 40 dgr azimuth, disappeared behind trees in the foreground at 70 dgr azimuth. Slow movement fm observer.

11. COMMENTS: Rpt rec'd fm Hq Air Proving Ground Command, Eglin AFB, 23 Aug 56 via AF Form 112. Concur with preparing officer that sighting caused by a light. Details of sighting correlate w/ notes of RF-89 which carried stroboscope light. Light seen for only 6 seconds because obscured by trees. Slow movement explained by a/c flight away fm observer.
