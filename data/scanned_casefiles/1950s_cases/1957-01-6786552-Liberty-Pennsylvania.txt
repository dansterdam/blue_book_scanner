10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One white round light, the size of a basketball at arm's length. Four white trails were visible until object was almost out of sight, then four more were visible. Object was first seen at 20 dgr elevation, 330 dgr azimuth, and disappeared at 10 dgr elevation 90 dgr azimuth. Object was seen travel ing in a SE direction. Object appeared to roll as it approached the far end of flight path. Object was seen visually for 3 to 5 minutes.
