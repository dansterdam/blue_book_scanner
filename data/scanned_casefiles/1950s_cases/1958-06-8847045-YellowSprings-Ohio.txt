38. In your opinion what do you think the object was and what might have caused it?
1st impression believed it to be a satellite, but ruled this out because of circular motion, 2nd impression believed it to be an aircraft, however this is doubtful since no sound could be heard or any blinking lights seen. Flight path of object was uneven as though it was oscillating. Instantly, object was similar to the 1st magnitude of a star. At sighting object appeared to be accelerating.
