From Dayton Daily News - 1 June 52

Ball of fire exploded over Washington state at 1230 a.m. on Thursday night, 29 May 52 (actual) 0020 PST. Six-miles similar to one that exploded on 11 May. Was blinded white light, silent, and into pieces. Astronomers say it was a meteor. Seen by State Cop. Seemed to be over Port Townsend.
