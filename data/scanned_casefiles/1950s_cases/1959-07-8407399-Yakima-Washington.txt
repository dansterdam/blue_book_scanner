SUBJECT: UFO Sightings, State of Washington

1. The 6th District, Washington State Congressman contacted the Aerospace Technical Intelligence Center at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base stating it had been brought to the Congressman's attention that during July and August of 1959 there were a number of sightings of unidentified flying objects in the area of Bremerton, Yakima, Bellingham, and Sequim, Washington.

2. These objects were described as red, green, white, blue, and yellow by the various witnesses; all sightings occurring at night.

3. ATIC contacted the 25th Air Division, McChord Air Force Base, Washington and requested an investigation of these sightings. The Directorate of Intelligence of the 25th Air Division informed ATIC on 15 September 1959, that these light phenomena emanating from known friendly sources. A telephone call to the base commander, McChord Air Force Base, Washington, determined that these friendly sources were SAC aircraft conducting a refueling mission in the area.

4. Will you please inform Congressman [redacted] these findings and that the letter be addressed to Mrs. [redacted]
