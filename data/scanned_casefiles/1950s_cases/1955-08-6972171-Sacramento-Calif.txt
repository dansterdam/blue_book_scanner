10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
The egg shaped, flat amber obj, size of a pea held at arms length appeared in the south, went south-west, and the disappeared overhead. Seen thru binoculars.

11. COMMENTS
Exhaust or after-burner of jet aircraft would account for amber color of obj. Duration of sighting also compared to time a jet would take to cover distance indicated. Seen from angle round jet exhaust gases would appear egg shaped and flat. Sighting possibly attributed to jet exhaust or after-burner.
