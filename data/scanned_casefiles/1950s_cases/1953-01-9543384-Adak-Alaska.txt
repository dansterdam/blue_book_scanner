Adak, Alaska                                                   4 January 1953

I.   Description of Incident

At 0910 a bluish spherical light with a tapering tail was observed moving southwesterly at a tremendous speed and at a great distance, parallel to the horizon, for 5 seconds.

II.  Discussion of Incident:

The report indicates that the object was probably a meteor. The description given closely approximates a meteor's performance.

III. Conclusion

Probably astronomical phenomenon.
