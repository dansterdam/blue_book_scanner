10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One oval-shaped object appearing as a brilliant light with a thin bright tail was a green light. Object was very small. Object was initially sighted at 180 degs azimuth, 15 degs elevation, and traveled a straight course to 360 deg azimuth; 60 deg elevation. Object was viewed visually for 30 minutes and disappeared by fading out of sight.
