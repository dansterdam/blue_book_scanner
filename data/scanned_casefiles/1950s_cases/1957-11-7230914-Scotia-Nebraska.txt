This will confirm telephonic communication between Lieutenant Colonel William J. Spratt, Special Agent in Charge Joseph E. Thompson on the morning of November 13, 1957, concerning an alleged sighting of a balloon in the vicinity of Scotia, Nebraska, on November 5, 1957, at about 8:30 PM.

The following information was furnished by Mr. Joseph R. Partington at Red Cloud, Nebraska, by Mr. [redacted] at Hastings, Nebraska, an employee of the [redacted] in Hastings. He informed Mr. [redacted] he had recently been visiting at Scotia, Nebraska, and while there had talked with two boys who were farmers and who advised about 5:30 PM, November 5, 1957, while it their farm home, they heard a noise overhead which sounded like a helicopter, and instantaneous from ground level a similar to burning fuel. According to these two boys, a large dark object was seen in the sky and looked like a balloon and which appeared to be descending. Upon sighting this object, they indicated they observed it to be paused for a short period of time. The object did not reach the ground and began ascending into the air again and disappeared.
