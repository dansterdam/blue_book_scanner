10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One square, white, object size of a basketball held at arm's length was observed to appear and fade for a period of approx two hours. Object seemed to circle.

11. COMMENTS
Source's description of the object closely follows that of searchlight activity. Nearby modest airport and the possibility of rotating searchlights used for openings of movie premiers, etc. are the probable cause of sighting.
