9. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Two sightings. Objects appeared from front with tapering back. The size of a quarter at arms length. Appeared dull against sky. Objects appeared to emit flame. The second object had light similar to a/c blinker light. Both object flew from S to N.

10. COMMENTS
Concur with opinion of the rpt-ing officer that both of these sightings were in all probability caused by aircraft.

11. CONCLUSIONS
Probably Aircraft
