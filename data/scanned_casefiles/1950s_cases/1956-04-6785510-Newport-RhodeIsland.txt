10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One circular object, the size of a large grapefruit, held at arm's length, silver in color, appeared in the north at an azimuth of approximately 350 dgrs and at an angle of elevation of 45 dgrs. The object disappeared by "whirling into the haze." Seen thru binoculars of unknown posr. Visibility was unlimited with no surface wind at the time of the sighting.
