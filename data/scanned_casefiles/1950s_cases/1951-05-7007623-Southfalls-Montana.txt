1. On 23 May 1951 source received a telephone call from a person identifying herself as hrs. of Great Falls, Montana, who reported than she sighted an unidentified aerial object over the city of Great Falls between 1430 and 1500 hours (ADT) on 22 May 1951. The woman stated that the object, which was silver or a bluish white shape, appeared to be shaped like a ball or a three-pointed star and gave off a glow similar to that of an electric light bulb.

2. Source contacted the Great Falls AFB weather detachment and the Gore Field weather station. The former had no weather balloons in the air at that time, but the latter reported that early in the afternoon of 22 May 1951, a balloon was white and moved in a west-to-east direction in its ascent.

3. A Great Falls radio station reported that a Northwest Airlines pilot reported seeing the same unidentified aerial object.

4. Another newscast on 23 May 1951 reported that an unidentified object of similar description was sighted over Kansas City (Mo).
