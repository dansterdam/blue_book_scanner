1. NOTICE: On 7 August 1950, at approximately 0600 hours (LT), 1st Lt. A. G. Wertman, 3rd Fighter Quadron, while flying at 30,000 feet observed that appeared to a an aircraft at 25,000 feet descending. Observed same object which appeared to be a silver metallic disc flying at an indicated speed of 620 miles per hour.

2. DETAILS: On 7 August 1950, while arrived that while over Santa Fe, New Mexico, at a precisely 30,000 feet, he observed with his C-47 aircraft a C-47 or a 2nd Corporate type aircraft flying at approximately 20,000 feet. At first he was advised that he was tracking the object and was unable to follow the object which disappeared in the distance.

b. Time of Sighting: 0600 LT, for approximately five minutes
