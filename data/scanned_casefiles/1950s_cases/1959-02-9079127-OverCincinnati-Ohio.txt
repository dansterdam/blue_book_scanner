Following is an extract from Captain Merwin J. Sherline's report dated 20-21 Feb 59 for your information and necessary action:

2315. Captain Martin (Sugar Mike) Flight Service, WPAFB reported UFO report as follows: (copied verbatim)

"Object sighted by a Lt. Bradley, 165th TAC Recon Sq. Ky Air National Guard, based at Standiford, Kentucky. He was flying over Cincinnati (B-57) at 41,000 feet approximately 02000, 21 Feb 59. Sighted object flashing alternately red, white and green. Object appeared to be round and whole object lighted brilliantly in colors alternating red, white, green.

Object was approximately 15 miles west of pilot in level flight. Pilot Bradley gave chase on heading 270° under direction of Oak Hill (GCI control). He chased object for about 200 miles. Object gradually pulled away. Bradley flying B-57 at 0.8 mach. This object also sighted by three other a/c, SAC Numbers Archie 23 and Archie 32 (other not reported). Reported to Moonshine and Paycheck. Further reports are forthcoming."

                                    JOHN C. McNEILL
