10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Circ. obj. ten times larger than a star. Color varied in white to blue. Obj. amber. Part of obj. was swinging/pendulum. At one time part of object separated. Fm main body. Later rejoined. Obj was shooting sparks or rays. Object sighted for over 2 hrs visually & then binoculars. Was high in sky & moving east. Obj still in sight at time of initial rpt. same obj viewed one week before.
