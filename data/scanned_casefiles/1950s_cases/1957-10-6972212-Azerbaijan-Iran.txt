SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects over Azerbaijan

SUMMARY: (Unclassified information subject as above)

1. The following unclassified information subject as above was submitted by the American Consul, Tabriz to the American Embassy, Tehran on 18 Nov 57:

"During the past six months unidentified flying objects have been sighted over Azerbaijan on several occasions. The first report was in "Mikhail" of 21 May 1957, stating that a Comet had been seen over Rezaieh.

The next occurrence was in July of 1957, one sighting on the evening of the 13th and two others within a few days of that date. These sightings, all observed by Iranians, were during the hour and half after sunset (2030-2200) and the object observed appeared as a large red light which moved rapidly across the sky over Tabriz from east to west. There was no accompanying sound. Observation with 6 power binoculars revealed no more of its nature than by the naked eye."
