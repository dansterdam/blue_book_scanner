10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Shape-Generally round with a hazy inverted truncated cone on top. Size 3 ft diameter. Color Blue white soft light. No trail or exhaust. Speed was stationary to 110 knots. No sound. Object first reported stationary 7,000 ft moved SSW reducing altitude one thousand ft, then moved NNW for 10 mi, and moved in arc SE to E to NE to W to NW. Lost from view while switching fuel tanks.

TO:   CO, Air Materiel Command, Attn: MCIS

Report of Information on Unconventional Aircraft in accordance
with TCL-200-1:

a. Brief description of object:
   Shape-Generally round with a hazy inverted truncated cone on top
   Size-3 Feet diameter
   Color-Blue white soft light
   Number- 1

   Aerodynamic Features-None observed
   Trail or Exhaust-None observed
   Propulsion system- None observed
   Speed- Stationary to 110knts
   Sound- None heard

   Maneuver- Object first reported -estimated 7000 ft about 10mls.
   of the Capitol Dome and stationary, it then moved S/W for about
   10 miles reducing altitude to about 1000 ft, then it moved N/W for about
   10 miles until at 1000 ft, there it moved in a trail changing to
   E to W to N to W which placed it a few miles SW of the center of
   Alexandria Va.

   Matter of Disappearance- Object disappeared while observers were
   changing fuel tanks.
