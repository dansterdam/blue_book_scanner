10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object, fifty times bigger & brighter than a star, light blue, very hazy. Sighted while traveling in car. Also rptd by GOC at Spartanburg, S.C.

11. COMMENTS
Due to the fact that the sky was clear w/visibility unlimited except by darkness, & the fact that the object was observed thru an automobile windshield, it is the opinion of this hg that the observer(s) possibly saw the planet Jupiter and/or the star Sirius which were both bright and in the SW at the time of the sighting.
