ATIC Document 108992, Flying Saucers over Indonesia, 23 Feb 53

A strange egg-shaped object was observed over Gorontalo in North Celebes on 23 Feb 53. This object radiated a grayish-green light and a red light was seen from the tail. The object flew swiftly in a north to south direction.
