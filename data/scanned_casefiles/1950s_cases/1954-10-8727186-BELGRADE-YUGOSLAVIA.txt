Late October, 1954
Yugoslavia

IR-793-54, ATIC 205022 (lo 133) (c)
Subj: Flying Saucers Over Yugoslavia
OAIRA, Belgrade, Yugoslavia dated 24 November 54.
Report contains translated newspaper articles concerning a rash of flying saucer sightings over Yugoslavia in late October 1954. The increase in sightings was noted immediately after considerable publicity had been devoted to the subject by the French Press.
