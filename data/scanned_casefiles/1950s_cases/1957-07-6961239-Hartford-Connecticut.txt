AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

Location: Connecticut
260th Air Reserve Center
475 Capitol Ave., Hartford 6, Conn.

Date of Collection: 10 July 1957
Date of Report: 12 July 1957

Individual: Lt Col Frank F. Woolley Jr.

Source: Brilliant White Light

Evaluation: Approx 70,000

References: None

Subject: Compared with a star only 10 times or more larger.

Summary:
"The sighted object was a brilliant white light, it appeared to be unevenly round in shape and could be compared to a star except 10 times or more larger. Color was brilliant white with only one object in appearance. Course of object was West to East directly overhead, angle of elevation 50°, azimuth 90°. Keeping stationary to East course, object appeared in my sight for approximately forty five minutes and was visible from the ground and also sighted through binoculars. In the date 23JUL 1957 at the dusk, The observer was located Latitude - 41° 43' N, Longitude 72° 39' W. No other observers, other than myself, at this location. Weather conditions were excellent with excellent visibility, weather station facilities were not available. Calling was unilimited. Nearest air traffic was at Brainard Field and this consisted of only light private planes."

FRANK F. WOOLLEY, JR.
Lt Col, USAF
Commander
