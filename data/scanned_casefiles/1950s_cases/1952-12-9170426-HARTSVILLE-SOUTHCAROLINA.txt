PROJECT 10073 RECORD CARD

15 Dec 52

15/0935EST
15/1152

HARTSVILLE, SOUTH CAROLINA

AF Pilot

10-15 sec     1

Circular silver object appeared size of half a dollar from a ten mile distance. Object approaching to coastline, losing altitude rapidly. Observed by pilot of RF-50 a/c, at altitude of 15,000' traveling 300 kph.

1. This report was checked against a similar one from Greensboro, N.C., 120 miles N with which book place 10 minutes after this sighting. Results: No apparent correlation between the two as the speed is too high for balloon or jet.

2. This object described a conventional balloon.
