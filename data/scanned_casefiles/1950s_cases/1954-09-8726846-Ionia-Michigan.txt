WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22, 1954.

Capsules of News
On Michigan Front
Flint Woman Heads State Library Board.
Lansing—(Citizen Patriot Lansing Bureau)
Election of Mrs. Verna Barkey of Flint as
chairman of the state board for libraries was announced
Wednesday. She is school librarian.
Arthur Yabroll, business manager of the Detroit public
library, is vice chairman.
Other members of the board are Donald W. Kohlstedt
of Grand Rapids and Miss Anne E. Lindblom of Kalamazoo.
The board voted to ask the state department of administration and appropriate legislative committee to include
the state library and its law division in the proposed justice building. Headquarters have been in rented space
since the 1951 state office building fire.

3 Glowing Sky Objects Reported.
Ionia -(AP)- Amateur astronomers Tuesday reported
to civil defense officials they had tracked three glowing,
but unidentified objects, across the sky Monday night.
The observers said the objects were traveling at tremendous speed at an extreme height. Spokesmen for the
air filter defense center in Grand Rapids said there was no
radar contact with the objects.
The astronomers were Clarke Burgeon, 25, and William McAughlin, 32, of Ionia.
