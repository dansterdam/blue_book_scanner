10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round, orange object, size of silver dollar. Light goes on when obj is moving, and goes out when obj stops. Obj was initially sighted at 45 dgr elevation, 135dgr azimuth & moved on a straight light path to 45dgr elevation, 35dgr azimuth. Obj was observed visually & through binoculars for 20 minutes. Sources stated that obj compared at 025Z, moved in same direction as before, was seen for 4 minutes & disappeared behind buildings. No Report in File.

11. COMMENTS

Shape, size, light, duration, course, wind direction tend to support balloon as cause. Second sighting not believed to be related to first due to short duration & disappearance behind building. Second sighting probably aircraft.
