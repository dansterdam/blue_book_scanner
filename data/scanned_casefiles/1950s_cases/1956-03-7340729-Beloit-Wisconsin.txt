10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One black oblong object, size unknown, appeared to be at approximately 1,000 feet, The object was seen through binoculars and appeared to remain stationary until it gained altitude and disappeared.

No report in file.

11. COMMENTS
Due to the duration of the sighting and the color of the object it is the opinion of the headquarters possibly generated by a balloon of the plaid type B 2238 was the time of the end of the sighting, it is possible that the observers saw a plaid type balloon released at Beloit at 2100Z. It is unlikely that a balloon would remain stationary for over 1 hr for evaluation.
