Because of the high number of copies of this report, a visit to Vaughn, New Mexico was made in the area. 1950 to investigate.

The following people were interviewed: Colonel Haynor, Lt. Albert, Sgt. Holmes, Mr. Proctor, Mr. Bott, and Mr. Gottler. Approximately eight people from Vaughn were also interviewed.

A cancellation was made of all activities at Holloman Air Force Base as follows:

1. It was agreed that possibly three coin models could be established at Vaughn with continuous work, as follows:

(1) Airship management
(2) Geodetic survey
(3) Frequency spectrum analysis by system

4. It was learned through Major Max Holloman Air Force Base that Cambridge Research Laboratories had been assigned a secondary investigative photographic headquarters at Albuquerque, New Mexico. The Commanding Officer of Holloman Air Force Base was advised to appear to cooperate with this investigation.
