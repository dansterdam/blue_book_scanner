10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One object round, slightly larger than a baseball. Color red to orange at approx 1000 ft altitude. Object had rapid movements up, down, sideways and hovered. Upon approach of police car obj rapidly disappeared to West. As police got closer to obj, they lost radio contact with station at Valley City. Valley City also lost contact with Jamestown, S.D.
