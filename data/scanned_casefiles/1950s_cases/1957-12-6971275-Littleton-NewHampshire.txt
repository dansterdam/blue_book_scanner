(g) Tail, trail, or exhaust, including size of same compared to size of object - None
(h) Sound - None

(i) Other pertinent or unusual features - The object was stationary in the sky and appeared to brighten and dim without regularity

(b) Angle of elevation and azimuth of the object when first observed - The angle of elevation was 20° and azimuth unknown. However, the sighting was toward the southwest from the point of observation

(c) Angle of elevation and azimuth of object upon disappearance - The object remained stationary and in view the entire period while it was observed

(d) Description of flight path and maneuvers of object - Stationary at all times

(e) Manner of disappearance of object - The object remained in view during the entire period of observation
