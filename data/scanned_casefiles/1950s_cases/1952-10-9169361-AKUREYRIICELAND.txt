SECURITY INFORMATION
UNCLASSIFIED

Unidentified Flying Objects
Iceland

30 October 1952

Milton Radzevsky, Capt., USAF

1400th Air Base Group
Keflavik Airport, Iceland
F-6
"Orgundisdir", 29 October 1952

A copy of this report will be furnished J-2, IDF.

1. Four or five unidentified flying objects were reported proceeding in a southerly direction at a great speed near Akureyri, on Iceland's north coast last Sunday, 26 October 1952 at about 09:00.

2. These objects held a short-oil-like shape, emitted neither sound nor flue, two clearly visible, and was under observation for two to three minutes.

3. Cloud base was at 600-1000 meters, with no wind.

4. These objects were observed from two separate locations in Akureyri by three people. It is reported that they were also later seen proceeding.

5. This report was not transmitted electrically as the information is now three days old.
