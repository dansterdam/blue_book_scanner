AERIAL OBJECT-- On Feb 3 almost all Semarang residents witnessed a white object which suddenly appeared in the sky. The object appeared the size of a star against a clear blue sky but neither sparkled nor moved. So far the object is still unidentified. (Jakarta, Feb 4, 1953, 2320 GMT-B)
