11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Silver spoke observed through field glasses while observers were watching geese. Object flow a square pattern over the city before disappearing in the WSW. Into haze. Converging were to the center of the city. Description of the object by the three witnesses was conflicting. Altitude, 400-500 ft. Shape circular, round, one reported with blunt wings. One thought might be a feather balloon. Size same as a plane at altitude. Color metallic silver. Object was slow, hovering.

Subject was sighted by three witnesses on 30 March 1950, in midwestern flying object. The observers were Dr. [redacted], veterinarian at Sioux City stockyard, Dr. [redacted], Sioux City, Iowa, stock yard employee; and [redacted], 517 1/2 Jackson Street, Sioux City, Iowa, stock yard employee. The three were south of the stock yard watching some object through field glasses when a silver object was observed. The three watched the object through fixed glasses for a time of approximately 10 minutes during which Dr. [redacted] reported the object flew a square pattern over Sioux City before disappearing into the haze on a WSW heading.

No photographs or sketches are available. Because of the apparent inconsistencies in the observation of the witnesses their answers to specific questions are only:

(1) Number: All agreed that there was one object.

(2) Shape:
Dr. [redacted]: Appeared as a straight-edged ruler
Mr. [redacted]: Appeared to have wings at times. Could be weather balloon.
Mr. [redacted]: Blunt, blunted, slender.

(3) Size of object:
Dr. [redacted]: "Unknown, hard to determine"
Mr. [redacted]: "Same as a plane at high altitude"
Mr. [redacted]: "5 to 6 inches across"

(4) Color of object:
Dr. [redacted]: "Had a metallic sheen"
Mr. [redacted]: "Silver"

(5) Speed:
Dr. [redacted]: "Moving slowly"
Mr. [redacted]: "Undetermined, but slow"

(6) Heading:
Dr. [redacted]: "West, southwest"
Mr. [redacted]: "Unknown"
Mr. [redacted]: "Southwest to east, then west to southwest"

(7) Maneuverability:
Dr. [redacted]: "Flew a square pattern without banking"
Mr. [redacted]: "None"

(8) Altitude:
Dr. [redacted]: "Estimated 20,000 feet"
Mr. [redacted]: "Very high"

(9) No sound was heard and no exhaust trail was seen by any of the three witnesses.
