10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object with a number of points which later disappeared leaving a tear-shaped object. Object was size of a silver dollar at arm's length. Object was much brighter than a star and had the same color. Object was initially sighted at approximately 90 dgr azimuth & 45 dgr elevation. Obj was viewed visually for 35 minutes and disappeared after sunrise.
