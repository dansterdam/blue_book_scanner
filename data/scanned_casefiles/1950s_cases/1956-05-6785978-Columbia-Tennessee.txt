AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

COUNTRY OF ORIGIN: U.S.

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects

Observer: Det #6, 4717th Ground Observer Squadron, Nashville, Tenn.

DATE OF REPORT: 13Jan56
SOURCE OF INFORMATION: GOC Post Visual Observation
DATE OF INFORMATION: 20May56

SUMMARY:
At 2315Z 20 May 56, Columbia, Tennessee GOC Post (CF35B) reported citing four or more unknown flying objects four or five miles ESE of post between two banks of clouds on a generally clear and sunny day. Objects described as somewhat flat and round and reflecting or flashing light. Generally hovering but also moving up and down and sideways at times. Altitude estimated 20-30,000 feet. Objects in view about three or four minutes and one adult GOC volunteer from conventional or known aircraft by three-teenage girls.

Report immediately reported by AF Instructor on duty to TMIGG and OAK Hill Filter Center. TMIGG had a 1st in the area but disallowed any radar sightings in vicinity of Columbia, Tennessee.

AF Instructor believed no other action necessary, also failed to notify Filter Center, Ground Observer Center had AFR 200-2 on record and not acquired follow-up reports. AF Form 112A not available at support base, photographed enclosure is substituted.
