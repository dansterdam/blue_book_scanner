10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One (1), round, silver object, trailing white smoke, was estimated to be the size, 15-20 feet in diameter. The object gave off a steady light, and after a period of 10-15 seconds it disappeared behind a group of trees. Estimated speed of 100 MPH. The object traveled from NWS toward the south and then turned west.

12. CONCLUSIONS
Possibly Balloon
Possibly Aircraft

11. COMMENTS
The two (2) observers were questioned by service personnel and the witnesses filled out questionnaires on the sighting. It is the opinion of the investigating deputy that the sighting could possibly be attributed the aircraft.
