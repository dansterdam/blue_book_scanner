10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One (1) white object, size of a head of pin held at arm's length, w/no other discernible features was observed by civilian pilot. Obj was seen flying approx east at 23,000 ft (closing Stratocruiser). Obj was but at a that of jet a/c. Observer had seen many meteors, but did not believe this sighting was astronomical.

11. COMMENTS
Due to brevity of sighting, 5-7 seconds, & the improbability of significant investigation producing low-ap investigation authorized. Although the observer did not recognize the obj as astro, the description of this sighting, & the fact that observer did not view obj for any period of time led to opinion that sighting probably astro (Meteor).
