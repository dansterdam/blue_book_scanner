10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Three objects in a cluster were sighted from the ground at Keesler AFB, sighted from the air, and picked up on ground radar. By utilization of a theodolite it was ascertained that the objects were balloons of a large type, tied together. The balloons were moving towards the east at 60 knots.

11. COMMENTS
Definitely identified as balloon cluster.

Probably Balloon
Possibly Balloon
