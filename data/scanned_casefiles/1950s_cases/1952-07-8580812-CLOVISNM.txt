PAGE 2 OF 2 PAGES

1. Captain J. H. Titus, Adjutant, Medical Group, 140th Fighter Squadron, who was in Clovis, New Mexico, reported two (2) oval shaped objects approximately 3-4 miles high and traveling west to east at approximately 100 degrees at 1030 hours. The two (2) objects were traveling a definite formation approximately 1-1.5 miles apart on a level course leaving no vapor trails and having a definite formation. The objects appeared to turn 60 degrees head-in-northerly direction. Captain Titus viewed these objects approximately four (4) minutes until they disappeared. He reported them as oval shaped, metallic with very little wind and the evening was clear. No additional information was obtained. Sighting was between 2130 hours and 2200 hours.

2. Second article was clipped from Clovis News Journal on 22 July 1952 and refers to sighting in area drive-in theater mentioned.

UNCLASSIFIED
