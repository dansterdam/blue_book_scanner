SUMMARY:
A single unidentified, unconventional flying object was sighted and observed by [redacted] Age 13, [redacted] Age 11, [redacted] Age 12 and [redacted] Age 13. The object was described by the boys to Ltr. [redacted] Street, East St. Louis, Illinois, who notified this office of the sightings.

The object was described as being aluminum colored and disk shaped with a slightly raised dome on the top side. It appeared to have a dark almost black edge with silent exhaust on the underside both emitting smoke. The object appeared from the north approaching overhead, toward the southwest and disappeared with great speed to the NW. Object appeared larger than conventional aircraft. Frequently observed in this area.

Severe thunder storms were recorded in area of sighting preceding and following sighting. Scattered clouds at 3500' and 8000' with winds from S.E. at twenty knots and 15W at 15 knots respectively.
