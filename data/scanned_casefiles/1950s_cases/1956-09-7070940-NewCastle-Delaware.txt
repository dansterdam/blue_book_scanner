SUMMARY:
At 2330Z, 17 September 1956, an unidentified flying object was observed and reported to the New Castle County Air Base by three (3) civilians. The three (3) observers saw the object from different locations in the New Castle area. Although they disagreed as to the shape of the object, they all agreed as to its approximate size and color and as to the manner of disappearance. They were in agreement also as to the general weather conditions at the time.
