10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Round, white object, size of half-dollar, low hum heard on amplifier while object was in sight. Object traveling at a very high speed. Had very bright light and was at high altitude. Faded from sight with increasing distance.

11. COMMENTS
The characteristics are those of an a/c seen at a very high altitude, and the connection between the hum on the amplifier and the object is just misinterpretation.
