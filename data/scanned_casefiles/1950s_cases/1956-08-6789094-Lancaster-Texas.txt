10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One bright, white, oval-shaped object, the size of a basketball at arm's length, object moved E to N in straight path. Object was viewed visually for six to eight hours. Faded away in sunrise. Object disappeared at 10 to 20 dgr elevation and 170 dgr azimuth.

11. COMMENTS
With exception of angle of elevation and azimuth upon objects disappearance, this sighting meets the characteristics for Mars. The angle of elevation & azimuth upon disappearance can possibly be in error. Evaluated as Mars.
