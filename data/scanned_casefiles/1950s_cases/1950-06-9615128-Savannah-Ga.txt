B between 0415 and 0500 EST on 28 June 1950 an unidentified aerial object was observed by personnel from Chathan City housing project in Savannah, Georgia. Object was described as a round ball of colorless light which moved in an erratic manner vertically and horizontally over an area NE of the city of Savannah and Chatham Air Force Base, Georgia. Object was observed for 45 minutes until it disappeared, climbing swiftly to the NE.
