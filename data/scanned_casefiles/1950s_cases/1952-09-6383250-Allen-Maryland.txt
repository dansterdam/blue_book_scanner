SUMMARY:
A report was received at 02:52, 12 September 1952, from the Baltimore Ground Observer Corps Filter Center stating that an unconventional flying object had been sighted at 02:45, 12 September 1952. The object was round with greenish, flashing green, white light with red ring moving northeast at approximately 2,500 feet. Object was observed through field glasses for a period of thirty five (35) minutes during which time no rain occurred over the area. The subject was still aloft. A check of the area of reported sighting was made by this station using all electronic equipment available and no targets were detected.
