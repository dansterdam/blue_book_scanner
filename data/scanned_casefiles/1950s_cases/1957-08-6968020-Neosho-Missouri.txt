1. The following information concerning an unidentified flying object in this Filter Center area is submitted in accordance with instructions contained in paragraph 7, AFR 200-2:

(1) Description of the object:
    (a) The object appeared to be round in shape.
    (b) According to the observer the object appeared to be about the size of a aircraft at very high altitude.
    (c) The color was aluminum.
    (d) One such object was sighted.
    (e) Not applicable.
    (f) No other details were observable concerning shape or color.
    (g) None.

(2) The object, according to the observer, had a whirring sound comparable to a dynamo.

(3) Description of course of object:
    (a) The observer was first attracted to the object by the whirring noise it made.
    (b) The object appeared to be at about an 10° to 85° angle from the observer in height.
    (c) It appeared to go to right angles north and east from the observer for two more specific movements and then disappeared straight up at a rapid speed.
