10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Thin, whitish obj, size of pea, similar to star in appearance. Silver area (cloud) obj varied from greenish to whitish color. Observation for 30 mins through binoculars, observed for 5 days. Obj moved at random. Disappeared by fading.
