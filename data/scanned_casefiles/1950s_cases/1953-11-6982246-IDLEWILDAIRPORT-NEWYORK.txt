SYNOPSIS:
Investigation revealed that on 15 December 1953 and to the east of Idlewild Airport at 22.8 hours, EST, a group of witnesses sighted six (6) unidentified flying objects over Idlewild Airport. The objects appeared as reddish-orange flying objects measuring several feet in diameter and were observed from a period of approximately one (1) minute to one and one-half (1-1/2) minutes. The objects were sighted at an elevation of 30 to 40 degrees above the horizon and were traveling from the east to the west at a speed estimated to be between 1500 to 1800 knots. The objects were observed to make sudden turns and make a high rate of climb during the sighting. Traffic control indicated that helicopters, were not in operation during the time of the reported sighting. The sighting was reported to the Military Air Transport Service and investigated by On-Site Inspector for the Air Force.
