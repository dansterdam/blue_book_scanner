Country of Activity Reporting: Local Div, Hq First Air Force
Date of Information: 5 March 1958
Date of Collection: 7 March 1958

Name of Source: Lt. Howard W. Borell
Evaluation: B-6

References: (Sighting by Borell 26 Nov 57 (Forwarded by IAF New DPL 11-123.20 Nov 57))

SUMMARY:
In observer observed a disk-like object which seemed to trail airliners departing New York airfield, hovering over Fort Monmouth, NJ area, then rose vertically and disappeared. He along saw the object on 5 March, and saw the same object at the same time/place on 6 March in the company of another observer.
