TO: All Concerned

1. In compliance with letter, Department of the Air Force, subject as above, dated 6 February 1948, the following information is submitted pertaining to an object believed to be a "Flying Discs" that was seen in this area on the night of 24 April 1950.

b. Weather at the time: Weather was clear.

c. Sgt. [name redacted], Weather Observer, Weather Detachment 25-9L, Shaw Air Force Base, Sumter, South Carolina.

d. No photographs available.

e. Sketch of object's configuration: [A hand-drawn sketch of an elongated object]

(1). Number: One (1)
(2). Shape: Sort of cylinder shaped at front then tapering off in rear.
(3). Size: About fifty (50) ft in length and possibly five (5) feet in diameter.
(4). Color: Firing Red
