10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Source reported object penetrated wall of his home, through metal screen & imbedded self against wall, Point of impact against wall approximately 6 inches lower than holo through screen.

11. COMMENTS
Object was examined and found to be an H-131 parachute flare. From the angle of entry the house it was fired by someone in the neighborhood and not in an a/c as originally alleged. House occupied by a cripple. Considered to be a matter for police after obj identified & similar spot flares found in neighborhood. Results of investigation phoned in to HQ USAF, 12 Dec 57, per their request.
