SUBJECT: Insuf. Data To Evaluate Sightings of Unidentified Flying Objects in Peru

SUMMARY: On two occasions within the last two weeks two different sightings have been made of unidentified flying objects in Peru. In both cases the reporting sources appear educated and intelligent.

The first case is covered in the following quoted telegram from the Inspector of Indian Affairs at Andahuaylas (Department of Apurimac), Peru, dated 29 October 1957 "To the Minister of Work and Indian Affairs - Lima: Yesterday 28 at approximately 6:05 P.M. a luminous spherical shooting object was seen, aqua color: Size 60 centimeters slightly enlarged and emitting strange before calling to earth. It fell apparently in the Talavera District Zone. We looked without success until this minute."
