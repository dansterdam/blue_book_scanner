Report of Incident.

UNCLASSIFIED                                                17 September 1951

1. Captain DOUGLAS A. NEILSON, A07143485, Provost Marshal, 664th AC&W Squadron, Lockbourne, advised the undersigned on 17 September 1951, of the following information received from a Lt. HART at Selfridge AFB, Michigan:

a. Mr. [redacted] w/residence at [redacted] was flying his Cessna aircraft NC86878, on 16 September 1951, approximately 3 miles North and East of Marion, Ohio, at 2800 feet, at 1217 EST, at a heading of 230 degrees, when he observed an object with a high tail, 45 degree sweepback wing, long pointed nose, and painted black, coming towards him at a 50 degree reciprocal heading. He advised that he barely escaped colliding with this object.

2. Capt. NEILSON advised that Lt. HART had proffered this information to District Office #25, who recommended that he contact Lockbourne AFB and transmit same to this District.

JOHN F. O'CONNELL
Detachment Commander
