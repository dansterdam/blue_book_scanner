Answer.        Just a brief glimpse, they seemed to disappear like a flash.

Question 2. Did the objects appear to be in an orderly formation?
Answer.        Yes. After they passed over I agreed with my husband on his version of their formation.

Question 3. Were the objects similar in shape to any aircraft that you may have seen?
Answer.        No. The brief glimpse that I had of them, they appeared to be very different to the shape of ordinary aircraft.
