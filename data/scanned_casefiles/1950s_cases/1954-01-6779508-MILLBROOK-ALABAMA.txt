Hiden Gardner Millbrook

Chief Officer Joseph Reid of the Canadian SS. Sunjaru reports observing a meteor on January 11, 1954, at 0055 G. M. T. in lat. 17°21' N, lon. 42°27' W. The meteor, of a brilliant white color, having a blush tinge and leaving a brilliant afterglow, was observed for a period of approximately 8 seconds. The meteor was first observed at an angle of approximately 140, midway between Canopus and Achernar.
