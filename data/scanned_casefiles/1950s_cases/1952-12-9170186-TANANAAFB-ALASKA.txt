IRAN FOR YOUR INFO:

"FLYING SAUCERS HAVE COME TO IRAN. IRAQ
G2 HAS RECD NUMEROUS REPTS OF STRANGE OBJECTS
IN AIR OVER NORTHEAST IRAN-USSR BORDER.

OBJECTS APPEAR AT NIGHT AND IN SOME INSTANCES
SEEM TO BE CONTROLLED IN THAT THEY ARE REPT'D
TO FLY OVER BORDER INTO IRAN AND THEN RETURN
TO USSR. THIS INFOR SUPPLIED BY CONFIDENTIAL
AMER SOURCE WHO OBTAINED IT FROM IRAN G2.
SOURCE BELIEVES OBJECTS MAY BE SOVIET FLARES
USED TO ILLUMINATE BORDER AS PRECAUTION AGAINST
ILLEGAL BORDER CROSSERS. FLARES AND VERY
PISTOLS KNOWN TO BE EXTENSIVELY EMPLOYED FOR
THIS PURPOSE AND FOR SIGNALLING. INDIAN MIL
ATTACHE REPTS ONE FLYING OBJECT RECOVERED ON
IRAN SIDE AND SENT TO TEHRAN AFTER WHICH
IDENTIFIED AS METEOROLOGICAL BALLOON.

SPECULATION ON SIGNIFICANCE OF THESE REPTS
IS CONJECTURAL AT BEST. WHILE UNDOUBTEDLY
EXAGGERATED AS RESULT OF CHRONIC JITTERS OF
BORDER PEOPLE, REPTS PROBABLY INDICATE
INCREASED SOV SURVEILLANCE AND TIGHTER
BORDER SYSTEMS. THEY MAY ALSO BE DELIBERATELY
INTENDED TO CONFUSE IRANIANS IN CURR WAR
OF NERVES."
