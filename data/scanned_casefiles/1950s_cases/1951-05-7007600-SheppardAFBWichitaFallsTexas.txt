SUMMARY:
Unconventional aerial sightings observed at Sheppard Air Force Base, Wichita Falls, Texas during evening of 4 May 1951.

Sightings consisted of unidentified round objects traveling in a Southwest to Northeast direction at unknown altitude and speed. Observed sightings emitted a soft, steady fluorescent glow. Observations made for approximately 30 to 45 seconds.
