10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object, with jagged outer edges, which appeared red, orange, yellow, and blue, appeared as a bright light. Pilot from local F15 Squadron made repeated passes over location and though he appeared to pass thru the object he noted nothing. Binoculars used by observers.

11. COMMENTS
At time of sighting Saturn was at an altitude of 30°, and in the SW, the area of sighting. Possibly the observer may see Saturn thru haze and clouds. Binoculars could account for the color and size.
