UFO from SAC Command Post to our Command Post

Two steel trap aircraft, 99th bomb wing, sighted a UFO at 1205 zulu, object was silver - 16 degrees - 18 minutes north 56 degrees, 14 minutes west. Aircraft were on a heading of 260 degrees at 34,000'. Object was sighted 20 degrees to the right and 60 degrees above the aircraft. It was kept in sight for 1 hour, 20 minutes at which time the aircraft changed heading 330 degrees and then changed back to 260 degrees. Last sighted at 58 degrees north, 61 degrees, 5 minutes west.

An Aircraft from the 99th Bomb Wing sighted silver object at 1205.

13 Nov 1959. Original location of aircraft was 151st 561od. Aircraft on heading of 260 degrees at 34,000 feet. Object sighted 20 degrees to right and 60 degrees above observing aircraft. Aircraft changed course to 330 degrees, then back to 260 degrees, at which time lost sight of object. At this time aircraft location was 58 degrees N 61.5W. Object viewed for one hour 20 minutes.
