SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Sighted on 27 October 1958

TO:   Commander
       Air Technical Intelligence Center
       Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
       Dayton, Ohio

The following quoted information was received from the Detachment Commander of the Mitchel Detachment OSI on 20 November 1958 and is forwarded in accordance with paragraph 14b(1), Air Force Regulation 200-2:

"At 1530 hours on 18 November 1958, Lt Conlon, United States Naval Liaison Officer, Marine Division, Sperry Gyroscope Company, advised as follows: Mr. [redacted], Roosevelt Field, New York, advised that he had received information from Mr. and Mrs. [redacted], Uniondale, Pennsylvania, that on 27 October 1958 they had witnessed an unidentified flying object over their farm near Union Dale. They stated that the object was flying at a very high rate of speed, just clearing the treetops, and making a loud swishing noise. The object had the appearance of a large, grey cigar with a horizontal crosspiece resembling an aircraft elevator on its tail. Mr. and Mrs. [redacted] advised he looked like a large jet aircraft without wings. Lt Conlon advised that he was unable to furnish any information as to the time of day of the sighting, or weather conditions at the time of the sighting. Mr. and Mrs. [redacted] stated that they would be willing to furnish any other information, if called upon."

FOR THE COMMANDER:
[Signature]
JAMES L. O'CONNOR
Major, USAF
