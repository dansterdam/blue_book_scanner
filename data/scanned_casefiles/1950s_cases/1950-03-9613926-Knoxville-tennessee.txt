11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Two successive nights returns of stationary objects. On third night slow motion was reported, 1st night 1 hour 55 minutes, 2d night Not stated. 3d day report of object size of C-47 with track speed estimated at 200 knots.

Report from 3 March probable A/C. First two nights returns of stationary object large targets since they were of long duration. No Weather data included. Should be an investigation in the area at the time in order for this to be a cause of the report.
