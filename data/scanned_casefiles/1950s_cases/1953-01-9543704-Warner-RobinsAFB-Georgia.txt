Warner-Robbins AFB, Georgia                                      7 January 1953

I.   Description of Incident

At 0200 2 two civilian sources observed an orange glowing object traveling west at a slow rate of speed north of Warner-Robbins AFB. The object was visible for 6 to 7 minutes.

II.  Discussion of Incident

There were no weather balloons released in the area, nor was there any reported aircraft traffic, but it is felt that an aircraft did cause the sighting because of the description.

III. Conclusion

Possibly aircraft.
