UNCLASSIFIED

SUMMARY OF INFORMATION                                                                 DATE 10 July 1950

Preparing Office: 111th CIC Detachment, Headquarters Third Army, Fort McPherson, Georgia

SUBJECT: Unidentified Object Sighted over Ft. McPherson, Georgia

At approximately 1025, 10 July 1950, WOJG [redacted] of the 111th CIC Detachment sighted a metal object in the [redacted] in the direction of northwest. This object was also sighted by WOJG Francis J. Riley of the 111th CIC Detachment. The object appeared small and had a bright metallic appearance. The object was in plain view for about fifteen minutes. It was later obscured by low hanging clouds. Object was moving in an easterly direction at a slow rate of speed. It was at an unpredictable height, very high. OSI, Marietta, Georgia was notified immediately. (B-2)
