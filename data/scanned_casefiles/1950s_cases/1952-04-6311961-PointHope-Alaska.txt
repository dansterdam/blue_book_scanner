ALASKA - UNIDENTIFIED AIRCRAFT SIGHTED.
THE CG OF THE ALASKAN AIR COMMAND REPORTS THAT ON 12 APRIL
A GROUND OBSERVER AT POINT HOPE NORTHWESTERN TIP OF ALASKA SIGHTED
AN UNIDENTIFIED MULTI-ENGINES AIRCRAFT HEADING SOUTHEAST, ALTITUDE
AND SPEED UNKNOWN.
CABLE COMMENT: THERE WERE NO KNOWN FRIENDLY MULTI-ENGINES
AIRCRAFT IN THAT AREA AT TIME OF THE SIGHTING. THIS MAY BE A POSSIBLE
INDICATION OF THE FIRST SOVIET OVERFLIGHT OF ALASKAN TERRITORY.
