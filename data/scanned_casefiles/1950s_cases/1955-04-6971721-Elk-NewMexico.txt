10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object with a fan shaped tail the size of a baseball held at arm's length. Color was seen in the bright sunlight. Color of object was a green-purple with an orange tail. Object first seen at an angle of 300 due south. Upon disappearing it exploded two feet above the ground. Object appeared to fall at same rate as strong would. Observer seated in a truck at time of observing.
