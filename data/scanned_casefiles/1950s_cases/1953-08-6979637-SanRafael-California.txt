CIRVIS.

At Turner AFB Albany, Georgia and aireg adjacent flying object sighted travelling on a constant heading of 270 degrees. Object appeared from South, circular in shape, outline determined by reflection ground lights, size estimated to be equal or greater than F 84 aircraft. Witnesses reported yellow variable intensity ball of light or flame which changed to reddish color as object disappeared to west. Altitude estimated to be 3,000 to 4,000 feet. Velocity 30 to 40 MPH, no sound, chemical reaction with atmosphere, or residue was reported by observers at Turner AFB and vicinity.

Initial observation by Capt W Knowlir, residence located 1 mile west of Turner AFB, neighbor drones officer Turner AFB, sighting made from Turner AFB tower.
