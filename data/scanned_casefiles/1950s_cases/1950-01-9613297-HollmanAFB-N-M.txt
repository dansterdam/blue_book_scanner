      (1) Laterally or horizontally: Undeterminable (due to unknown size of object).
      (2) Angle of elevation from horizon: Approx. 15°.
      (3) Altitude: Undeterminable. (Due to unknown size of object).

   g. Time in sight: Was observed for a period of 45 minutes, at the end of which time it was still in sight.

   h. Appearance of object:
      (1) Colors: Predominately white-bright, comparable to Venus.
