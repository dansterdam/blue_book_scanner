1. DATE

2. LOCATION
Howell, Utah

3. DATE/TIME GROUP
Local
GMT 26/04567

4. PHOTOS
No

5. TYPE OF OBSERVATION
Ground-Visual

6. SOURCE
Civilian (16 & 17 yrs of age)

7. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION
four seconds

8. NUMBER OF OBJECTS
one

9. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round white object, the size of a nickel at arms length, like a white glow, with a trail of flame. The obj was descending. Object exploded.

10. CONCLUSIONS
Bolide type meteor

11. COMMENTS
Color and trail of flame indicate meteor. Duration of sighting substantiates. Explosion indicates bolide type meteor.
