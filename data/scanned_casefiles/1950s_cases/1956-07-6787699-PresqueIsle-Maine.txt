10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round object, size of grapefruit at arms length, Yellow in color and unusually bright. Seen for fifteen minutes through binoculars 7X50 power. Then obscured by dark cloud cover.

11. COMMENTS
Dr. W.F. Jordan, Prof of Astronomy, University of Maine, rptd that the Planet Venus was a very bright yellow in the Maine sky at the position indicated by the source & at the time of the sighting.
In addition, the Presque Isle AFB WX officer was consulted & he confirmed the obj as Venus, in view of the conclusive evidence, concur w/rting officer in eval- uating obj as Venus.
