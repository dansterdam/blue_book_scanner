10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One oblong object with a yellowish white green tail. Size about size of football at arm's length. Object was tapered, object traveled three quarters of the sky in a westerly direction at altitude of eight to ten thousand feet. Object was observed visually and through binoculars for period of one minute. Object disappeared gradually, decreasing in size.

11. COMMENTS
Probably meteor sighting with slight overestimation on duration. Description does all other data support this analysis.
