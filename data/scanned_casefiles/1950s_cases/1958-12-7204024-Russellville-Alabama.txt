1. The Intelligence Division of the 4225th Air Base Wing, Columbus Air Force Base, Mississippi, received a letter dated 14 December 1958 from a civilian, Captain Carlin Farris, relating to a white or yellowish object seen on 25 December 1958 in a civilian, Russellville, Alabama.

2. Mr. [redacted] was contacted by the Intelligence Division and was stated to have found a white or a "white as 15" object approximately 25 feet to 20 feet in length, or one end, about the size of a basketball. The location was near Russellville, with two acres, northwest for approximately 1/2 mile on the farm. The object was light in color, approximately 1760 to 1800 feet long, with two ends of solar radiation located in the center of the box.

3. Mr. [redacted] was requested to turn the box over to the National Guard Commander on 15 January 1959 for safekeeping. Location of the object is unknown.

4. Headquarters Second Air Force requested that the box, and all information concerning it, be forwarded to your headquarters for internal filing.

5. It is requested that the Intelligence Division, 4225th Strategic Air Wing be notified of your findings as a matter of record. No further action is anticipated at this station.
