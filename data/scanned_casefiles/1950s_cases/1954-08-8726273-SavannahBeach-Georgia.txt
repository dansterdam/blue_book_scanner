SUMMARY: (Enter concise summary of report. Give significance in final one-sentence paragraph. List enclosures in lower left. Begin text of report on AF Form 112-Part (1).)

On the night of 22 August 1954 at 2223 the sources observed one point of light moving rapidly across the sky. The object was a pale yellow-white color. The object was observed for about fifteen minutes. Sources location at the time was Fort Screven on Tybee Island, Savannah Beach, Georgia. There was no unusual condition or activity which might account for the sighting.

No particular significance can be attached to this report.
