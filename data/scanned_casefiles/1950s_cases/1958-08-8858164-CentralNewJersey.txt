10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Silver obj, shaped like a twisted piece of foil. Traveled approx 100-1500 mph. Traveled approx 700 ft; approx 150-3000' high. Followed curvature of earth--up hill & down.

11. COMMENTS
Sighting was made in Aug 58 but was not rptd until Dec 60, over 2 years later. The info rptd is limited in nature, & due to the late date of rpt, an investigation is impracticable. The witness rptd very little pertinent info preceding an evaluation. Case listed as unreliable rpt.
