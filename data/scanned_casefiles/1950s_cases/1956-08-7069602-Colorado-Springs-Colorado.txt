10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One round very bright white object about the size of a silver dollar at arm's length. Brightness of object varied and it had a bouncing movement. Object was sighted at approximately 90 dgrs azimuth and 40 dgrs elevation, and was viewed visually for 4 minutes and was still visible when observer went to bed.

No Report In File

12. CONCLUSIONS
Possibly Balloon

11. COMMENTS
Object was same as previously rptd by source on 14 Aug 56 (AISS-UFRO-56). Source was contacted by phone and again object was determined to be the planet Venus shining through a temperature inversion.
