The second incident was reported to the Reporting Officer by a
citizen working for a mining concern. Edificio Rímac, 4th floor, Lima (a mining concern). He reported that on 11 and
12 November at Sunchobamba (spelled Suchohamba - 7°46'S/77°39"W on WAC 1011), as

star, being much too low. This object has also been observed for a period of two
weeks and plotted by a native German named who runs a weather
observing station high in the mountains between Cajamarca and Suchobamba. Reportedly has tracked this object between 75° and 77° latitude during this period.
This object has also been seen by a Mr. manager of (SUCRI) near
