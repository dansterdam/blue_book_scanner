10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Light blue light, same as 2nd magnitude star, size of head of pin, descending rapidly, but not as fast as meteor. Disappeared below horizon to South.

11. COMMENTS
Obj was probably a slow moving meteor. Witness was at 12,000 ft alt over the ocean, which would account for his seeing it longer than usual.
