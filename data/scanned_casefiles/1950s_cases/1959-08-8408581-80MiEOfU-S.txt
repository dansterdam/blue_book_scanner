10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Seven separate sightings of blips on Radar. 1st on 1/16 at 1/16 in width observed to be approx 5-3 times the size of a 9-12H a/c. Speed estimated fm 3,000 to 7,800 m.p.h. Each blip lasted for 1 min each. Final sighting two blips were noted +/- same character'tic. These were intermittently. All blips disappeared abnormally.
