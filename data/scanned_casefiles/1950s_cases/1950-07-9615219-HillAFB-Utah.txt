Subj is Unidentified Aerial Object.

At 1840 hours MST, 5 July 50, 3 Staff Sergeants on duty at the Hill AFB Radar Station observed an unidentified aerial object flying at an estimated 25,000 feet altitude on a course of approx 150 degrees and passing almost directly over Hill AFB. Object reportedly under observation for approx 3 minutes, and described as about 60 feet in length, circular in shape with diameter 4 to 5 times ratio of ground speed of between 5 and 700 miles per hour. No sound or exhaust trail observed.

Described as silver in color. Radio ask was switched on shortly after object was sighted. However Ogden disappeared before radar set warmed up sufficiently for tracking. Advise extent of investigation this sighting and whether reports covering any future sightings are desired.
