Summary of Information:

On 29 April 1952, S/A Joel L. Cerr received a telephone call from Mrs. Walter Westerman, Homewood, Illinois. Telephone: Homewood 2229-R. Source stated that at approximately 1700 hours, 29 April 1952, she and Mrs. Walter Westerman, same address, had observed an unusual object in the sky, located at 135th Street, Homewood. The object, resembling a white parachute, was apparently circling a large airplane (description of which could not be given) silver in color, which was traveling southeast at the same altitude as the object. The object, and plane were observed for approximately three (3) minutes, when they disappeared from view.
