1. On 22 April 1954, 1/Lt O'DONNELL & Lt GALLACHER(N), 6205th Operations Squadron, Carswell Air Force Base, telephonically notified Special Agent PAUL A. HILLS, OSI District No. 23, concerning a visual sighting of an unidentified flying object near the 1000 feet. The object was cited at CARSWELL AFB, Fort Worth, Texas.

2. Lt GALLACHER, M-1947, at about 2140 hours, the description of the object was related. It was sighted at CARSWELL AFB, and was white and silver in the world class of view. He advised that the object was, at the time of the telephone conversation, on the Carswell Air Weather Station Radar Detector at about 110 degrees true heading, 4,000 feet and about 1/4 mile from Carswell AFB. GALLACHER stated that air traffic was also sighted and he further stated that the Base Operations tower operators had visually observed the object. In addition, GALLACHER stated that their Radar Site had picked up the object on their radar, positioned as reported by the Carswell AFB weather station.

3.
