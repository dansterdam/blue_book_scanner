FLYING SAUCER OVER NORWAY - Oslo, Norway - On Mar. 19, 1958 observers in Oslo, Norway, reported seeing a flying saucer over northern Norway. The reports said the object was elliptical in shape, and all preliminary and follow-up investigations by the Norwegian Air Force disclosed that an unidentified flying object had been observed. It was tracked on radar as it passed over the "far northern island of Feeroer". A Norwegian Air Force officer said there were several witness in the area at the time.
