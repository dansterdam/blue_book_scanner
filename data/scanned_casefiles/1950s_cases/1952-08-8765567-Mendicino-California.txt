SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

TO:   Memo to May Concerns

At approximately 1025, 14 August 1952, I observed aluminous object headed NE about 5000 feet altitude. At first the object was stationary, moving to lower in one spot which I picked up on Mathieson Chemical Corporation's it would move from side to side, up and down, shoot ahead, and then stop. Finally, it disappeared into two distances, its color turning a darker and darker red, until it was gone.

This was definitely not any known aircraft. It was also observed by my son, age 12, and my wife. There was no smell or exhaust. We watched the object for about 50 minutes.

Lake Charles, La
