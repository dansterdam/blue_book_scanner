13. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
USAF pilot flying T-33 type aircraft observed object make pass at his aircraft from a head-on position level at 20,000 feet. Object was characterized by a white light, varying in intensity and flashing at a set frequency. No trail or exhaust was visible. No sound was heard. There were no other pertinent features. The object appeared to climb to approximately 35,000 feet.

11. COMMENTS
In view of the fact that the sighting was for only 25 seconds and the incident was reported eight days after the initial sighting, a follow-up investigation was not deemed worthwhile.
