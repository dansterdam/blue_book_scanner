PRESS REPORT 28 OCT 57 PROVIDES RESUME OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT. THESE ARE THE FACTS CONFIRMED BY AIR MINISTRY: AT 21:13 MONDAY 21 OCT FLYING OFFICER ... SWEENEY FLYING METEOR AT 25,000 FT, WEST FROM RAF STATION NORTH LUFFENHAM (52 DEGREES 38'N 36 DEGREES 38'W) ALMOST COLLIDED WITH AN OBJECT WHICH, HE SAID WAS MOVING SLOWLY AND SHOWING SIX LIGHTS.

AFTER TAKING VIOLENT EVASIVE ACTION SWEENEY APPROACHED OBJECT FROM STARBOARD SIDE. AS HE CLOSED IN THE LIGHTS WENT OUT AND OBJECT VANISHED. HE ESTIMATED THAT HIS ACT WAS DIRECTLY OVER GAYDON (52 DEGREES 11'N -02 DEGREES 32'W) WHERE ATOM-BOMBER CREWS TRAIN IN VALIANTS. A RADAR SIGHTING REPORT FROM RAF STATION LANGTOFT CONFIRMED THAT THE OBJECT WAS AT APPROX 25,000 FT OVER GAYDON.

PRELIMINARY CHECK ON MILITARY AND CIVIL ACT MOVEMENTS INDICATED THAT METEOR WAS ONLY ACT IN THE AREA AT THE TIME. AIR MINISTRY IS INVESTIGATING THE SIGHTING AND THIS OFFICE ENDEAVORING TO SECURE COPY OF INVESTIGATION REPORT.
