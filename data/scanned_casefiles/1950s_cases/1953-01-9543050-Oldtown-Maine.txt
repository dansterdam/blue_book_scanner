Oldtown, Maine                                                1 January 1953

I.   Description of Incident
     An airman sighted an unidentified flying object at 0815 B. The object was
     round and small and appeared to be whirling at its top. It moved NNE, changing
     color in sequences of red, blue and white. From time to time, it maneuvered
     erratically.

II.  Discussion of Incident
     The report is lacking in background data. From the description, the object
     could possibly be a balloon.

III. Conclusion
     Insufficient data to evaluate.
