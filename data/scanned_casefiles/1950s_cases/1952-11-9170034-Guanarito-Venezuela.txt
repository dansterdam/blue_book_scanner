SUMMARY: (Under single summary of report. Give significance in final introductory paragraph. List locations at least first.)

According to press report flying saucers were seen by the people of GUANARITO, VENEZUELA on 10 November and for some hours. The whole population of this town watched for several seconds two mysterious aircraft of an unknown type flying at a very fast speed in a north direction. Later these two aircraft or another aircraft of a smaller size, then their aircraft disappeared into the clouds.

CONTENT of Preparing Officer:

The last time the papers reported flying saucers (August 1952), Lt. Colonel Filk, Konin HORNO, Acting Chief General Staff and Chief Venezuelan air force, advised the president that he had jet planes flying in the area at the time of the incident reported by civilians on the ground.
