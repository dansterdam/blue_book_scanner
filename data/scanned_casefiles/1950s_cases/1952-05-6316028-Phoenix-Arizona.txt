Summary of Information:

On 7 May 1952, in Phoenix, Arizona, was interviewed and stated in substance as follows: At 2200 
hours 5 May 1952, he observed an object in the sky which he believes was a flying saucer. The object was moving in a northwesterly direction at an altitude of approximately ten thousand feet and was according to 
scale of white lean drawing. The object was of bluish-green light similar in color to 
the arc made by an electric welder. The speed of the object could not be determined, but was blacked to be in excess of 1,000 miles per hour. The object was observed for about two seconds. No sound was attributed to the object. (P-6)

2d Lt., USAF
Wing Intelligence Officer
