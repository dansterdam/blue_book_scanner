1. At approximately 0900 hours on 14 January 1953, Andrew J. REID, Chief Agent, G2, Fort Monmouth, New Jersey, directed William L. McKELVY, Agent, G2, to interview personnel of 113th ASU, 21st Signal Co., 21st Students Co., and 181st Medical Detachment, in reference to an unidentified object or aircraft.

2. At approximately 0950 hours on 14 January 1953, Agent McKELVY interviewed Sgt. in Ward #12, Post Hospital, where he is a recovering patient.

Col. stated that at approximately 1750 hours on 13 January 1953, while standing on the pavement in front of Ward #12, he noticed an object in the sky, south of Fort Monmouth, approximately over Asbury Park, New Jersey.

It stated that the object, in his opinion, was at an altitude of approximately 5 to 8 thousand feet, and traveling at a very high rate of speed in a northerly direction.

Col. also stated that this object was reddish orange in color, shaped like a basketball, and left a white vapor trail. It was what could be seen for approximately 1 to 2 minutes after the object had disappeared.

He further stated that the object disappeared in approximately 30 to 60 seconds, in the direction of New York City, passing over the Post Hospital in its flight.

He stated that the object disappeared from view at approximately 1751 hours on 13 January 1953.

3. At approximately 1030 hours on 14 January 1953, Agent McKELVY interviewed Sgt. in Ward #2, Post Hospital, where he is a patient.

Sgt. stated that at approximately 1750 hours on 13 January 1953, while standing on the pavement in front of Ward #12 with Col., he noted an object in the sky, north or south of Fort Monmouth, but only observed it for approximately 1 second before it disappeared.

Sgt. stated that the only thing he noticed was that the object looked to him like a round ball, orange in color.

4. At approximately 1120 hours on 14 January 1953, Agent interviewed in his office at Dispensary, Post Hospital.

Sgt. stated that at approximately 1710 hours on 13 January 1953, while on his way to his care, in front of Ward #12, he noticed an object in the sky south of Fort Monmouth, approximately over Asbury Park.
