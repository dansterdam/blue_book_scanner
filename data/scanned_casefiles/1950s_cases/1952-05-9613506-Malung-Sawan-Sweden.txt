Ref Control No. A-1085.

On 14 May instead of 12 May at 2250Z 4 civilians driving in a car about 12 miles southwest of Malung Saran-object similar to an illuminated white aircraft passing from northwest to southeast. Very quickly and visible only approximately 10 seconds. The route appeared to approach the earth somewhat. No smoke or fire was observed. The height could not be estimated. The Swedish Defense Staff has received no other observation or info on this object, and they estimate that it was meteoric.
