10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Saucer shaped illuminous object. Altitude of 150'. Seen while driving auto. Color of moon, "a yellowish, radiant color," and had a flat top, with a spherical bottom. Remained stationary for 30 seconds, then the bottom portion appeared to assume a funnel type shape, obj. seemed to diminish in size. Dark cloudy night. No moon or stars visible.
