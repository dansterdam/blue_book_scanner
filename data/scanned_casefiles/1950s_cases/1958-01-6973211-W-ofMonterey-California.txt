Jet pilot sighted obj size of silver dollar. Color silver, appeared motionless, remained in that position for approx 10 mins, then moved towards the SW. Alt. estimated at 60,000ft plus.

NAVY JET 1A1677, PILOTED BY LDR RALPH E. SYKES, SIGHTED AN UNIDENTIFIED
FLYING OBJECT ON 13 JAN 1958 IN THE VICINITY OF MONTEREY, CALIFORNIA.
THE SIZE OF THE OBJECT IN PERSPECTIVE WOULD COMPARE ROUGHLY TO THAT OF A
SILVER DOLLAR HELD AT ARM'S LENGTH BUT HAS NO TYPICAL SHAPE. ITS COLOR
WAS SILVER. THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE MOTIONLESS AS IT TOOK APPROXIMATELY
10 MINUTES FOR THE OBJECT TO PASS FROM A 1030 POSITION TO A 0730 POSITION.
IT WAS ESTIMATED TO BE 20,00 FEET ABOVE THE SIGHTING AIRCRAFT WHICH WAS
FLYING AT 12,00 FEET.
