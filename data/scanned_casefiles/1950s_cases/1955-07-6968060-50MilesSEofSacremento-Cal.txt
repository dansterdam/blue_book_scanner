10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One (1) object, similar to an a/c at a distance estimated to be a fighter a/c, appeared to be silver in color. Fell straight down but no indication of explosion as a striking ground-radar and air-visual sighting.

Agree with the Director of Intelligence of the 28th ADly, that the sighting can possibly be attributed to a falling balloon. Weather balloons are released from base in that area.
