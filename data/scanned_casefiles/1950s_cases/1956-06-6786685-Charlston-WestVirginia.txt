10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
One half moon shape object the size of a nickel held at arms length, color of white and changing to bluish. Object was sighted through 7x50 power binoculars for a period of 4-5 minutes. While object was blinking it appeared white in color & while it was continuous it was bluish in color.

11. COMMENTS
Since there was ground fog & breaks in the overcast, it is believed that this object was possibly caused from either a searchlight or a spotlight of some sort. Possibly searchlight.
