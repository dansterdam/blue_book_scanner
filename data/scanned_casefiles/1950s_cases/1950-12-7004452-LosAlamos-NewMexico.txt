11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Object was round, brilliant green. Very fast speed. Thought at first it was a meteorite as it appeared suddenly, light orange, yellow in color; shot at 1 of flame. In immediately began to flare up, grew brite green, larger and brighter - seemed to sputter or fade two or three times emitting short spurts of red flame around edges. tall van close by, seen 28 December 1950 in the area 
of Los Alamos, New Mexico, an Atomic Energy location.
