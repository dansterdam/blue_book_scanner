10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Four objects the size of a pea at arm's length. Objects looked like streaked reflected light, like that of a light being reflected from an a/c wing. Objs were first seen strung up and disappeared at 35 to 40 dgr azimuth, 30 to 45 dgr elevation. Objects disappeared by fading out of sight after having been observed visually for 5 seconds.

11. COMMENTS
Duration, description, indicate meteor sighting.

12. CONCLUSIONS
Was Astronomical Meteors
