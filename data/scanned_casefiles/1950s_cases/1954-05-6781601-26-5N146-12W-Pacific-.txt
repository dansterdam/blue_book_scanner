PACIO, HATS, APO 953, c/o PM, IR-117-54
San Francisco, California

Source: Aircraft Commander - Captain D. B. Mutchess, AO 662073
32nd A.T.S., McChord Air Force Base, Washington

Scientific Intelligence:

1. At 13/0220 HST, Pearl Harbor Rescue received an unidentified flying object
report from a C-124, Aircraft #1030, Trip #523, enroute from Hickam Air Force Base
to Travis Air Force Base. Actual time departure from Hickam 13/0645, estimated
time arrival Travis 13/1762.

2. Message received as follows: "At 13/1045, position 20° 51N-146° 12W
object was sighted. Bright light moving lost parallel with aircraft. Aircraft
11,000 were blacked out. Reported object spiraled back to aircraft. After
5 minutes object moved away to NE at very rapid speed climbing at this time.
Weather was 3/8 CU tops 6000, clear above."

3. Duty Officer, Pearl Rescue, advised CINCPAC and COMPAC passed information
to Hickam AFB. Hickam sent following wire to Travis AFB: "Request Travis TIG
notify Intelligence to debrief crew on Aircraft #1030 on object sighted and submit
appropriate report."

Comment: As far as can be determined by the preparing officer, no other friendly
aircraft were in the area at the time of the sighting. It is assumed that the
Intelligence section at Travis Air Force Base will submit full report to the proper
authorities.
