2. Unidentified Radar Targets spotted in GA azimuth scope at 1°05'52. Plotted in vicinity of Misawa Homen approximately 12 miles from station. Objects seen on both 2 and 10 mile azimuth scopes. Traffic check revealed no known aircraft in vicinity. F-94 assembled at 1010032. Object not plotted or seen visually by aircraft. Aircraft reported radar interference. GCA operators ground radar. Objects seen on GCA scopes and plotted, lower flying at approximately 50 knots. Weather was VFR. Targets were solid and definite.
