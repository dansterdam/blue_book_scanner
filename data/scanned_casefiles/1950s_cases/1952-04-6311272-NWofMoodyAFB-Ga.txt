10. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING
Ten foot diameter metal sphere with copper-tan color. Object traveled from an apparently stationary position with a large oscillation to small oscillation after acceleration. Object had exhaust of zigzag pattern. Object appeared solid and was sharply outlined.

11. COMMENTS
This sighting was not reported until seven years after it occurred. Too old to be of any value.
