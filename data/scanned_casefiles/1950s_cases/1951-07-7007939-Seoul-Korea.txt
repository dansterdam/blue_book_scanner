1. This report of unidentified flying objects was extracted from the Operations Summary of the 1st Marine Air Wing for the period 012000/X to 022000/X, 2 July 51. This report was forwarded through Navy channels, at time of receipt by ComNavFE, and is being forwarded now to insure that it is made available to all Air Force agencies requiring the information.

a. At 2250/X, four night pilots in different areas simultaneously reported a large green ball, very bright, and trailing streams of red which soon turned blue, passing overhead at 10-15000 feet on North-Northeast heading. Pilots' locations at the time of sighting were: one PBJ-5N at CT 1070, one PBJ-5N at CT 1020, one F7F-3N at CU 2010. Pilots concur that object was moving with tremendous velocity.

COMMENT BY PREPARING OFFICER: It is not known if a detailed report was submitted on this incident. No further information is presently available at this headquarters.
