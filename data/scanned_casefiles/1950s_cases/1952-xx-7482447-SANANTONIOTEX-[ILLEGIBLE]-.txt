In accordance with Air Force Letter 200-5 dated 29 April 1952, the following information is submitted:

(1) A single, cigar shaped object was seen flashing low across the sky by Mrs. Alice Dieter, a civilian employee at Kelly AF Base, Texas. Object appeared to be observer to be approximately three by six feet and emitted a green, phosphorescent color. No aerodynamic features were noted by observer. Observer could not recall having seen any trail or exhaust nor hear any noise made by object. Object flew a straight and level flight at a constant speed.

(2) The object was uncertain about the time object was sighted but states that time between 2300 hours 15 July 1952 and 0200 hours 16 July 1952, Central Standard Time. Length of time observed estimated at between three to five seconds.

(3) A visual ground observation was made.

(5) Reliability of observer unknown.

(6) Fair weather prevailed at the time of the sighting.

(7) No apparent logical explanation for the sighting.

(8) No physical evidence of sighting.

(9) No interception or identification action taken.
