On 15 October 1948, my pilot and I started out on a routine mission off the northwest coast of Kyushu. When, at 2305 I, we were approximately 50 miles at 330째 from Fukuoka, I picked up an airborne target. It showed up at a range of five miles, dead ahead and slightly below us.
