CONFIDENTIAL

34 District OSI Ser Investigation Report ELIS Case #26-249 26 Aug 1954

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania - On the evening of 25 August 1954, at approximately 9:00 PM, a citizen of Philadelphia reported that he had observed four bright objects in the sky over Philadelphia. He stated that the objects were large, round, and very bright, and that they were traveling in a tight formation, apparently under intelligent control. The objects were observed for approximately 15 minutes and then disappeared from view. 

The citizen who reported the sighting, a Mr. George W. Gorman, is considered to be a reliable observer. Mr. Gorman is a veteran of World War II, and is employed as a purchasing agent for the Bell Airoraft Corporation. Mr. Gorman stated that he was an enthusiast of astronomy and was well familiar with the appearance of various celestial objects. 

This report is forwarded for your information. At this time, no further investigation of this incident is contemplated by this office.
