As of 07:30, 15 March 1950, an unidentified object was observed over Bradford, Illinois, by the pilot of a commercial airliner flight from Chicago to Keokuk, Iowa. The pilot reported the sighting through the Burlington (Iowa) radio station to the 4602d Air Intelligence Service Center. No photographs or records of the object were received by the Wright-Patterson Flight Service Center, but the pilot declared the object to be shaped like a disc, about one mile per hour and it was headed southeast.
