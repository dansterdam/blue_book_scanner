SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SIGHTING
DATE: AUGUST 6, 1966
LOCATION: WESTHAMPTON BEACH, LONG ISLAND, NEW YORK
TIME: APPROXIMATELY 10:30 P.M. EDT
WEATHER CONDITIONS: CLEAR SKIES, LIGHT WINDS
OBSERVER: EDWARD S. WAGNER, HAMPTON BAYS, NEW YORK
DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT:
A BRIGHT LIGHT WAS OBSERVED IN THE SKY OVER THE OCEAN OFF WESTHAMPTON BEACH, LONG ISLAND, NEW YORK. THE LIGHT APPEARED TO BE STATIONARY FOR SEVERAL MINUTES, THEN BEGAN TO MOVE IN A HORIZONTAL DIRECTION AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED. THE LIGHT THEN STOPPED SUDDENLY AND HOVERED FOR ABOUT 1 MINUTE BEFORE ACCELERATING VERTICALLY AND DISAPPEARING FROM SIGHT. THE LIGHT WAS OBSERVED TO BE LARGER THAN A STAR AND BRIGHTER THAN THE SURROUNDING STARS IN THE SKY.
ACTIONS TAKEN:
THE SIGHTING WAS REPORTED TO THE SUFFOLK COUNTY POLICE DEPARTMENT AND PROJECT BLUE BOOK, U.S. AIR FORCE.
SIGNED: EDWARD S. WAGNER
HAMPTON BAYS, NEW YORK
