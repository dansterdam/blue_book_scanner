FAIRFIELD, ILLINOIS 8501
6977416 8-6-63
1. On 8-6-63 at approximately 2200 hours, Subject observed an unidentified flying object hovering over the Fairfield, Illinois airport.
2. Subject describes the object as round in shape, approximately 50 to 75 feet in diameter, and a dull, metallic gray color. It had no visible markings or other identifying features.
3. The object hovered silently for about 15 minutes before suddenly accelerating at a high rate of speed and disappearing from view.
4. Weather conditions at the time were clear with good visibility. No other witnesses were reported.
5. This incident has been logged into the files of Project Blue Book for further investigation.
