HEADQUARTERS
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
George Air Force Base
Victorville, California

14 May 1952

STATEMENT OF OBSERVER

Statement concerning a certain unidentified object observed by me on 9 May 1952.

1. The object that I sighted was too high to make any determination of its size or shape possible. Its color was that of a military aircraft, but its appearance is described, my impression of the object was as it would be if it were reflecting sunlight after sunset.

2. The time of observation was 12:50 hours on the date given. The length of observation was approximately 20 seconds.

3. I observed the object with the naked eye from the ground.

4. It was located on the eastern side of George Air Force Base at a time when I observed the object. The object was located vertically above me at an altitude of 30,000 to 40,000 feet and was moving in no direction.

5. I am a civilian light aircraft pilot and have a high school education.

6. Visibility was excellent, with clear skies and slight winds prevailing.

7. No activity or condition, meteorological or otherwise, which might account for the sighting.

8. No physical evidence, such as fragments or photographs, of the sighting.

9. No interception or identification action taken.

10. One aircraft, a T-6, was flying in the vicinity of the sighting at the time of observation.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
DDC DIR 5200.10

UNCLASSIFIED
