SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Report

1. At approximately 2100 hours, 7 May 1957, an unidentified flying object was observed in the sky over Edwards Air Force Base, California, by the following witnesses:

a. Captain XXXXXXXXXX, 6511th Test Squadron, Edwards AFB, California.

b. Master Sergeant XXXXXXXXXX, 6511th Test Squadron, Edwards AFB, California.

2. The object was first observed at approximately 45 degrees elevation, traveling in a northeasterly direction at an estimated altitude of 30,000 to 35,000 feet. The object was approximately the size of a small aircraft at that distance. It appeared to be metallic in nature, circular in shape, and had no visible means of propulsion. The object traveled at an estimated speed of between 600 and 800 miles per hour.

3. The object was observed for approximately 2 minutes before disappearing from view. No sound was heard during the observation.
