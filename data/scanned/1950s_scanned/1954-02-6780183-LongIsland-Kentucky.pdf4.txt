METEORS NORTH ATLANTIC
The Master of the Greek S.S. Hellenic reports that at 0100 G.M.T. February 25, 1954, in lat. 60°05' N., long. 20°05' W. he observed an object travelling at a very high speed. It was moving to the west and finally exploded with a loud noise and a bright flash, so great that the whole sky was illuminated for several seconds.
