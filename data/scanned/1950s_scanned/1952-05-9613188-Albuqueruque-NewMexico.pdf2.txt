ALBUQUERQUE, NEW MEXICO
9 MAY 1952
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
1. AT APPROXIMATELY 5:30 P.M., 7 MAY 1952, A CIVILIAN SIGHTED AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT IN THE SKY ABOVE ALBUQUERQUE, NEW MEXICO. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS A BRIGHT CYLINDRICAL-SHAPED OBJECT TRAVELING AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED ON A NORTH-SOUTH COURSE. THE OBJECT WAS SIGHTED FOR APPROXIMATELY 30 SECONDS.
2. THE OBSERVER REPORTED THIS SIGHTING TO THE 4800TH AIR DEFENSE SQUADRON, KIRTLAND AIR FORCE BASE, ALBUQUERQUE, NEW MEXICO.
3. NO AIRCRAFT WERE KNOWN TO BE IN THE AREA AT THE TIME OF THE SIGHTING.
