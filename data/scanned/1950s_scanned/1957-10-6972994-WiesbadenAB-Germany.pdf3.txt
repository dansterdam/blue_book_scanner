AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

DATE OF REPORT:
6 Oct 1957

SUBJECT:
AAT Disastorants (sic)

ORIGINATOR:
CHARLES F. EDMONDS, 1st Lt, 445ks

DATA:
Part II of this report concerns the receipt and investigation of an unidentified flying object (UFO) that was observed at Wiesbaden Air Base, Germany on 24-25 Oct 1957.

The 12th AF Tactical Control System and the 15th Weather Squadron made airspace information and weather data available to assist in this investigation.

This information will be of interest to the Air Technical Intelligence Center.
