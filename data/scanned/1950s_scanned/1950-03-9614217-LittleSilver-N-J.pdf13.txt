MEMORANDUM TO THE OFFICER IN CHARGE

1. On 23 March 1950, [redacted], of 258 Oceanport Avenue, Oceanport, New Jersey, was interviewed by investigators from Military Intelligence Section, Office of Special Investigations, with regard to the subjects employment, The Engles Real Estate Company, 34-36 Main Avenue, Red Bank, New Jersey.

[Redacted] stated that at approximately 1600 hours on 15 March 1950, he was standing in the yard of his home and noticed three objects with white vapor trails traveling toward the ocean. The objects were described as three dark specks with white vapor trails.

Traveling toward the ocean, the objects were sighted and were emitting no noise that could be heard. The three objects continued on their course and disappeared toward the ocean.
