DESCRIPTIC:
Observation of an unidentified flying object by approximately (50) people in the Center Sandwich and Sandwich, New Hampshire areas. The object was described as a cigar-shaped or torpedo-shaped object, whitish in color, with somewhat of a iridescent sheen. It was estimated to be about the size of a bomber aircraft. The object was at an altitude estimated to be from 15,000 to 20,000 feet and traveling at an estimated speed of 700 to 800 miles per hour. The object was observed in the southwest sky for about 20 minutes before disappearing.
