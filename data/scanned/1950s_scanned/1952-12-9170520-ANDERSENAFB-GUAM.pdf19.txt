SUBJECT: Unidentified Object Sighted by Pilots of 9th Bomb Wing (M)
1. a. On 6 Sep 52 at approx 1255L, while on a routine training mission, the pilot and co-pilot of a B-29 aircraft of the 9th Bomb Wing (M) sighted an unidentified object in the sky. 
b. The object was first observed near Andersen AFB, Guam, at an estimated altitude of 10,000 to 12,000 ft. It was observed for approximately 30 seconds, then disappeared.
c. The object was described as being silver in color with a bright silver glow. The shape was roughly circular, with a flat bottom and a slightly domed top. The size was estimated to be approximately 50 ft in diameter.
d. The object appeared to be hovering and made no noise. It was observed to make no abrupt changes in direction or speed during the sighting.
e. A similar or identical object was subsequently observed by the pilots of a bomber aircraft returning to Andersen AFB from a training mission on the same date.
2. This report is forwarded for your information and appropriate action.

ROBERT L. McDOUGALL
WING INTEL OFFICER
