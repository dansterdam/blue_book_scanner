TITLE: UNIDENTIFIED AIRCRAFT

CHARACTER: SPECIAL INQUIRY - UNIDENTIFIED AIRCRAFT

Remarks: This is an initial report.

Investigation was initiated by the District Commander, 15th OSI District, upon receipt of a request from the Inspector General, Headquarters 544, to make inquiry into the incident. On 6 January 1950, Captain ROBERT E. WILLIAMS, OSI, Office of Special Investigations, and 1st Lt HAROLD G. BEASON, OSI, Offutt AFB, Omaha, Nebraska, saw an unidentified object near Howard, Kansas. The object, 30 to 60 feet in length, shaped like a football and bright silver in color, was proceeding in straight level flight at approximately 2,000 feet, with an estimated speed of 300 to 500 miles per hour. The object was in sight for less than two minutes and then faded from view. The weather was reported as CAVU.
