TELETYPE COMMUNICATION 
TO: DIRECTOR, FBI, WASHINGTON 25, D. C.
FROM: SAC, CHEYENNE  (62-0)
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
DATE: August 7, 1956

SUMMARY OF INCIDENT:
On August 1, 1956, at approximately 2:30 a.m., CAA CONTROLLER PAUL TRENT, CHEYENNE, WYOMING, OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WHILE DRIVING ON HIGHWAY 87 APPROXIMATELY 10 MILES SOUTHWEST OF CHEYENNE, WYOMING. 

OBJECT DESCRIPTION:
TRENT DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS BEING ROUND IN SHAPE, ABOUT 50 TO 60 FEET IN DIAMETER, HAVING BLUE AND GREEN LIGHTS FLASHING ON THE UNDERSIDE. IT APPEARED TO HOVER FOR A SHORT PERIOD AND THEN ACCELERATED RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.

INVESTIGATION:
TRENT IMMEDIATELY REPORTED THIS INCIDENT TO THE AIR FORCE BASE AT CHEYENNE, WYOMING, AND AGENTS OF THE OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS WERE DISPATCHED TO INTERVIEW HIM. TRENT'S STORY WAS CORROBORATED BY HIS WIFE WHO ALSO OBSERVED THE OBJECT.  THERE WERE NO OTHER KNOWN WITNESSES TO THIS INCIDENT.
