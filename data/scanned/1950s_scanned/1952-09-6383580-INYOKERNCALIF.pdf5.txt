SUBJECT: Report of Mysterious Object Seen in the Sky

TO: Commanding General, Sixth Army
Presidio of San Francisco, California

1. The undersigned was notified today by Lt Col James D. Logsdon, Infantry, PMST at the University of Nevada, that a man named John Barney, Jr., of Inyokern, had observed a mysterious object in the sky between 2200 and 2230 hours on 1 September 1952.

2. Transcript of the conversation pertaining to the mysterious object is attached herewith.

3. A copy of this letter and the enclosure has been forwarded to the Commanding Officer, Stead Air Force Base, Reno, Nevada, for his information and suitable action.

I.I. Yoel
Major of Enl (DEP)
