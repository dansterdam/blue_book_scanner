SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORTED

TO: DIRECTOR

OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS 

UNITED STATES AIR FORCE

WASHINGTON 25, D. C.

1. On the night of 9 January 1953, at approximately 2035 hours, an unidentified flying object was reported sighted over the city of Suwon, Korea. The object was first observed by a ROK [Republic of Korea] Army Lieutenant who was on guard duty at the Suwon Airport Control Tower. He reported that the object appeared to be a bright light, round in shape, and that it traveled at a high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 30 seconds, and then disappeared from sight.

2. Shortly after the initial report, the object was observed by two other personnel on duty in the Suwon Airport Control Tower. They also described the object as a bright light, round in shape, and traveling at a high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 30 seconds.

3. No further details are available concerning this sighting. No photographs were taken. This report is being forwarded for your information and any action deemed appropriate.
