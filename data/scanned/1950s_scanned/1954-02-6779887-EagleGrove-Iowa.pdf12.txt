UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON REPORT

DATE OF REPORT: 6 February 1954
LOCATION: Eagle Grove, Iowa
INVESTIGATOR: CAPT Mcrver, USAF
DETAILS OF INCIDENT:
On the evening of 6 February 1954, at approximately 1915 hours, a mysterious aerial object was observed over Eagle Grove, Iowa, by several witnesses.

The object was first sighted by Mr. George Bradley, a local resident, as he stepped outside his home. Mr. Bradley described the object as circular in shape, bright orange in color, and approximately 50 feet in diameter. He stated that the object was traveling at a high rate of speed and made no sound.

Mr. Bradley immediately called his wife and several neighbors to witness the phenomenon. All of the witnesses corroborated Mr. Bradley's description of the object. The object was observed for several minutes before it rapidly accelerated and disappeared from sight.

No physical evidence or photographic documentation was obtained during this incident. This report is based solely on the eyewitness accounts provided by the observers.
