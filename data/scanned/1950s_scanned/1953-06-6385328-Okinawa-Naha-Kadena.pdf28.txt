AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
DATE OF INFORMATION: 5-3-53
REPORT NO.: 53-3-29
PLACE: Okinawa, Naha, Kadena
DATE/TIME GRP: 26 May 1953 1700Z
DIGRAM: At 1700Z on 26 May 1953 personnel on duty in the Operations Building at Kadena Air Force Base, Okinawa, sighted several disc-shaped or oval-shaped metallic objects in the sky. The objects were observed visually by a number of persons through the Operations Building windows. The objects made no sound and were estimated to be 20-30 feet in diameter. 
The objects were observed to be moving at a high rate of speed, estimated at 800-1000 miles per hour. They were seen to make abrupt turns and changes in direction, and at times appeared to come to a complete stop, then accelerate rapidly and disappear from sight.
After being observed for about 10 minutes, the objects disappeared from view and were not seen again.
