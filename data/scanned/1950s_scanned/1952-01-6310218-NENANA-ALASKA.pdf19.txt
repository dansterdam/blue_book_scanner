INFORMATION REPORT

LOCATION: NENANA, ALASKA
DATE AND TIME: 6 JANUARY 1952, 1940 HOURS

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. At approximately 1940 hours on 6 January 1952, a lighted object was observed in the night sky over Nenana, Alaska, by the following individuals:

WITNESSES:
1. MR. JOHN DOE
2. MRS. JANE DOE
3. MR. RICHARD ROE

2. The object was observed to be moving in a southwesterly direction at an estimated altitude of 8,000 to 10,000 feet. The object was described as being approximately 30 to 40 feet in diameter, bright white in color, and moving at a high rate of speed. No sound was heard from the object.

3. The object was observed for approximately 2 to 3 minutes before disappearing from sight.

4. This report has been prepared in accordance with Air Force Regulation 200-2.
