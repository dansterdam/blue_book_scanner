SUBJECT: Report of Unidentified Flying Object
1. On the night of November 17, 1952, at approximately 9:15 PM EST, Mr. JOHN DOE, of 123 Main St., Florence, S.C., reported sighting an unidentified flying object.
2. Mr. DOE stated that he was outside his home when he observed a bright light moving rapidly across the sky from north to south. He estimated that the object was at an altitude of approximately 5,000 feet and that it was traveling at a very high rate of speed. Mr. DOE further stated that the object appeared to be round or disc-shaped and that it emitted no sound.
3. Mr. DOE reported the sighting to the local authorities, who in turn notified the appropriate Air Force representatives. An investigation was initiated, but no further information or evidence was obtained.
