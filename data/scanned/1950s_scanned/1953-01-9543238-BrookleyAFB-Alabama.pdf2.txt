UFO REPORT
TO: ASSISTANT DIRECTOR
FROM: CAPT. KJ LOGAN  
SUBJECT: REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT SIGHTING 
An unidentified aerial object was sighted over Brookley AFB, Alabama on 9 January 1953 at approximately 1240 hours.
THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS BEING CIGAR-SHAPED, APPROXIMATELY 50 FEET IN LENGTH AND GRAY IN COLOR. IT WAS OBSERVED TRAVELING IN A STRAIGHT AND LEVEL FLIGHT PATH FROM NORTH TO SOUTH AT AN ESTIMATED SPEED OF 300 KNOTS AND AN ALTITUDE OF 10,000 FEET.
FOUR AIRCRAFT WERE SCRAMBLED TO ATTEMPT VISUAL CONTACT BUT THE OBJECT INCREASED SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
THIS REPORT IS FORWARDED FOR YOUR INFORMATION AND FURTHER ACTION AS DEEMED APPROPRIATE.  
CAPT. KJ LOGAN
ASSISTANT OPERATIONS OFFICER
