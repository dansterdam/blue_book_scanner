IDENT AND TRAVEL FLIGHT OF 29 JUNE 1953 CONDUCTED NEAR SHAW AFB, SUMTER, SOUTH CAROLINA, BY UNIDENTIFIED INDIVIDUAL
THIS REPORT INDICATES THAT ON 29 JUNE 1953, WHILE IN FLIGHT, THE PILOT OF AIRCRAFT OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS A DISC-SHAPED VEHICLE WITH LIGHTS AROUND ITS CIRCUMFERENCE. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 3 TO 5 MINUTES AND THEN DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT. THE PILOT STATED THAT THE OBJECT WAS EXTREMELY FAST AND MANEUVERABLE. A MILITARY AIR TRANSPORT SERVICE AIRCRAFT IN THE AREA AT THE TIME ALSO OBSERVED THE OBJECT.
