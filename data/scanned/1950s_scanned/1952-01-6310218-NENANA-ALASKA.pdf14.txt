UNCLASSIFIED
SECURITY INFORMATION
HEADQUARTERS FIFTEENTH AIR FORCE 
OFFICE OF THE ASSISTANT CHIEF OF STAFF, A-2
APO 667
4 February 1952

SUBJECT:  Report of Unidentified Flying Objects

TO:		Commanding General 
		Air Defense Command
		Ent Air Force Base, Colorado

1.  The following report of unidentified flying objects was received from Nenana, Alaska on 6 January 1952.

2.  On 6 January 1952 at approximately 1600 hours, local time, a group of citizens of Nenana, Alaska observed a formation of brightly lighted objects moving rapidly at high altitude in a northeasterly direction.  The objects were described as having a brilliant white light, oblong in shape, and estimated to be traveling at a speed in excess of 1,000 miles per hour.  As the objects passed over Nenana, the lights dimmed but the objects could still be observed.  The duration of the sighting was approximately 2 to 3 minutes.

3.  No further information is available at this time.  This report is being forwarded for your information and any action deemed appropriate.

FOR THE COMMANDING GENERAL:

/s/ Frank F. Everest
Colonel, USAF
Assistant Chief of Staff, A-2
