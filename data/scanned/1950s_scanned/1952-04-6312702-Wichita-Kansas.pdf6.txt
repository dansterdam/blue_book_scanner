UNCLASSIFIED

SUBJECT: Wichita, Kansas
22 July 1952
Major John J. Donahue, Intelligence Officer
at the USOGS:

ON 22 APRIL 1952, a report was received of the sighting of two round luminous objects, approximately 30 feet in diameter, seen over Wichita, Kansas at approximately 12:15 am. The objects were observed to be maneuvering at a high rate of speed and then disappearing. Photographs were taken but they did not show the objects clearly. The observation was estimated to be 200 yards in altitude.
