5 March 1954

1250 - FOR INFORMATION OF COMMANDER
1300 - Above observation confirms report received 1730 6 March about bright lights on desert area near Oujda. Additional details as follows:
1310 - Two bright lights observed about 4-1/2 miles apart, 3 to 4 miles on other side of Oujda. Lights appeared at 2000 feet, moved west very slowly, then disappeared.
1720 - A small object observed moving very slowly over Oujda, appeared to be at 2000 feet, dropped rapidly and disappeared.
1730 - No details available
1740 - Two objects, brightly lit, moving slowly over Oujda area, disappeared.
1750 - Observation by several pilots, all agreeing to above and that no standard aircraft
 in area at the time.
