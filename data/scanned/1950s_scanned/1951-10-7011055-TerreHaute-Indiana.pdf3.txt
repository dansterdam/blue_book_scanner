12D 1-11-100, SUBJECT: UNKNOWN SUBJECT, Unidentified Aerial Object Sighted Over Indiana. Aerial Object Sighted, Terre Haute, 9 October 1951

Observed no vapor trails; heard no noise and observed no protruding fins or other projections on this aerial object. Described as about 250 feet in diameter, shaped like a disc, and that he observed the object pass from directly over his head to the horizon in approximately 2 seconds, which he considered to be a conservative estimate of time. The day was clear, a bright sun, no clouds or haze; sighting was not obstructed by buildings or other objects. Due to the speed of the object, no further details of object could still be observed at the time it disappeared over the horizon.
