LIGHT SIGNAL PROCEDURES FOR AIRPORT TRAFFIC CONTROL
The text provides detailed information about the different light signal colors and their meanings, including:
- Steady Green: Cleared for takeoff
- Flashing Green: Cleared to land
- Steady Red: Stop
- Flashing Red: Taxi clear of landing area
- Flashing White: Return to starting point on airport
- Alternating Red & Green: General warning signal

The diagram shows a segmented circle that indicates the traffic pattern, as well as the location of the wind indicator and landing strip indicator.
