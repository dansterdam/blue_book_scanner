UNCLASSIFIED
EXCERPT - SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS
Headquarters 4602nd Air Intelligence Service Squadron, Arizona
20 April 1953
16 April 1953 - Cactello

On 14 April 1952, some observers sighted 5 unidentified bright lights climbing in a circular pattern over Tucson, Arizona. The objects were pulsating and changing colors rapidly. The observer stated that they appeared to be at high altitude and seemed to be manoeuvering between cloud layers. No sound was heard. The objects disappeared from view due to their altitude and distance. The observer reported the case for traffic air force, Los Gatos, California.
