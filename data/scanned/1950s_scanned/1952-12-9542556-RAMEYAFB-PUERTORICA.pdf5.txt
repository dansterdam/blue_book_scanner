MEMORANDUM FOR: THE ADJUTANT GENERAL, DEPARTMENT OF THE ARMY 

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Roswell, New Mexico

1. Reference is made to your letter of 26 June 1952, file AG 322 (26 Jun 52) GAS-IN, subject as above.

2. The following additional information has been obtained:

a. On 8 July 1947, a master sergeant stationed at the Roswell Army Air Field, New Mexico, reported that an unidentified flying object had been found on a ranch near Roswell and had been recovered by personnel from the Field.

b. The object was described as hexagonal in shape, approximately 3 feet in diameter, and thicker in the center than at the edges. It was further described as being constructed of a metal-like substance, although extremely light in weight, and as having a surface texture resembling tinfoil.

c. The object was shipped under Colonel Blanchard's orders to higher headquarters.

3. No further information has been obtained since the original report in July 1947. It is suggested that any additional information desired on this subject be obtained from the Department of the Air Force.

FOR THE DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE:

/s/ GEORGE F. SCHULGEN
Colonel, USAF
