AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

REPORT NO: 6965680
DATE: 15 JAN 1955
FROM: MCPSE-2
TO: DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE,
HEADQUARTERS USAF,
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

TEXT:
1. AT APPROX 1718 HOURS ON 14 JAN 1955 AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY AN AIRMAN STATIONED AT ROBINS AFB, GEORGIA.
2. THE OBJECT WAS FIRST OBSERVED BY THE AIRMAN WHILE HE WAS DRIVING ON US HIGHWAY 41 ABOUT 5 MILES SOUTH OF MACON, GEORGIA. THE AIRMAN IMMEDIATELY PULLED HIS CAR TO THE SIDE OF THE ROAD AND OBSERVED THE OBJECT FOR APPROXIMATELY 20 MINUTES.
3. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS BEING ROUND IN SHAPE, SILVER IN COLOR, AND APPEARED TO BE HOVERING 50 TO 75 FEET ABOVE THE GROUND. NO SOUND WAS HEARD FROM THE OBJECT. AFTER APPROXIMATELY 20 MINUTES THE OBJECT ACCELERATED AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
4. FURTHER DETAILS ARE NOT AVAILABLE AT THIS TIME.
