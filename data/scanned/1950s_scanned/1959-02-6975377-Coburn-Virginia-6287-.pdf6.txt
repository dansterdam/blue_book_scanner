SUBJECT: Unidentified flying object

1. On 6 February 1959, this office received a report from Mrs. Virginia Coburn concerning an unidentified flying object observed by her on the evening of 5 February 1959.

2. Mrs. Coburn stated that at approximately 7:30 PM on 5 February 1959, she was outside her home when she observed a bright light in the sky to the south. She described the object as circular in shape, emitting a bright white light, and moving in a southerly direction at a high rate of speed. Mrs. Coburn stated the object was in view for approximately 2-3 minutes before disappearing from sight.
