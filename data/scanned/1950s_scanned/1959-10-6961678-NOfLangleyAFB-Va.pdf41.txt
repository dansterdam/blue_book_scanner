SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LANGLEY AIR FORCE BASE, VIRGINIA
6 OCTOBER 1959

1. AT APPROXIMATELY 1900 HOURS, 6 OCTOBER 1959, A FLYING OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY SEVERAL PERSONNEL AT LANGLEY AIR FORCE BASE, VIRGINIA. THE OBJECT WAS FIRST SEEN OVER THE CITY OF HAMPTON, VIRGINIA, TRAVELING IN A WESTERLY DIRECTION. IT THEN CHANGED COURSE AND HEADED IN A NORTHERLY DIRECTION TOWARDS LANGLEY AIR FORCE BASE.

2. WITNESSES STATED THAT THE OBJECT WAS ROUND OR CIRCULAR IN SHAPE, ESTIMATED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 30 TO 40 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND APPEARED TO BE METALLIC IN SURFACE. IT ALSO EMITTED A REDDISH-ORANGE GLOW AROUND ITS PERIPHERY. THE OBJECT MOVED AT A RELATIVELY SLOW SPEED AND MADE ABRUPT CHANGES IN DIRECTION WITHOUT BANKING OR SLOWING DOWN.

3. PERSONNEL WHO OBSERVED THE OBJECT INCLUDED OFFICERS AND ENLISTED MEN FROM SEVERAL ORGANIZATIONS ON LANGLEY AIR FORCE BASE. THE SIGHTING WAS ALSO CONFIRMED BY RADAR OPERATORS IN THE BASE CONTROL TOWER.

4. AFTER MAKING SEVERAL PASSES OVER THE BASE, THE OBJECT ACCELERATED RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT TO THE NORTH.

5. THIS REPORT IS BEING FORWARDED FOR YOUR INFORMATION AND APPROPRIATE ACTION.
