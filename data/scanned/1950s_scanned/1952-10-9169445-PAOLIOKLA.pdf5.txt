SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS REPORT

1. On 5 October 1952, the Air Force received a report of an unidentified flying object sighting in the vicinity of Paoli, Oklahoma. The report was submitted by [REDACTED], a civilian resident of Paoli, Oklahoma.

2. According to the report, [REDACTED] sighted an unidentified flying object approximately one mile south of Paoli, Oklahoma, at about 1845 hours on 4 October 1952. [REDACTED] described the object as being silver-colored, circular in shape, and about the size of a basketball from his point of view. He stated that the object was traveling in a straight line at a high rate of speed and disappeared from sight in a matter of seconds.

3. [REDACTED] further reported that he had observed the object for a period of approximately two to three seconds. He stated that the object made no sound and left no trail or exhaust.

4. This report has been forwarded to the proper authorities for further investigation and analysis.

APPROVED: [SIGNATURE REDACTED]
DIRECTOR OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS
