DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

UNCLASSIFIED

7 August 1953

SUBJECT: ALLEGED OBSERVATION OF UNKNOWN
FLYING OBJECT NEAR ATLANTA, GEORGIA
SPECIAL INQUIRY

TO:  Commander
    Air Technical Intelligence Center
    Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
    Ohio

1. Attached for your information are two (2) copies of Closed Reports of Investigation by Special Agent L. ADAMS, 6213 OSI DET, Robins Air Force Base, Georgia, file 24-161, dated 31 July 1953, subject as above.

2. Investigation was predicated upon information from the Federal Bureau of Investigation, Atlanta Field Office, Georgia, that three (3) men had seen an unknown flying object near Atlanta, Georgia on the night of 8 July 1953.

3. No further investigative action is contemplated, and the files of the Office of Special Investigations are considered closed in this instant case.

Attention is invited to the provisions of AFR 124-1, dated 12 July 1952, which prohibit disclosure of classified information to unauthorized persons.
