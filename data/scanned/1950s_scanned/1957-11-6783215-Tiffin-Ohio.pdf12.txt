DISPOSITION FORM
FROM: EID/Report from 550 Report 6 December 57 
TO: AFCIA4
SUBJ: SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT - TIFFIN, OHIO - 6 NOVEMBER 57
1. Reference is made to your letter of 7 November 57, subject as above, and concerning inquiry by this office regarding the sighting of a "Bright object" in the sky near Tiffin, Ohio, on 6 November 57. Inquiry has been made into this matter and the following information obtained:
a. This object was sighted at approximately 7:00 P.M. on 6 November 57 by two persons residing in or near Tiffin, Ohio. The object was described as a bright light, approximately the size of a large star, moving rapidly in an erratic path across the sky. 
b. The object was observed for approximately 2 to 3 minutes before disappearing. The observers stated that the light gave the appearance of changing colors from a bright white to an orange or reddish hue as it moved.
c. Inquiry through the local Sheriff's office and the local National Guard unit at Tiffin disclosed no other reports of the sighting of this object.
d. Based upon the information obtained, this office considers the sighting of this object to be one of the "usual" type of unidentified aerial phenomena, the nature of which cannot be positively determined.
2. It is requested that this report be considered as the final disposition of the matter concerning the sighting of this unidentified aerial object in the Tiffin, Ohio, area on 6 November 57.
T. J. CODINAR, JR
MAJ USAF
ASSISTANT
