AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

AREA COVERED ON: New York State
SUBJECT: Report on Unidentified Flying Objects (Project \'Blue Book\')

DATE OF INFORMATION: 29 00 1 Mar 53 
EVALUATION: C-3

PREPARED BY: CAPT. V. KURI, 1ST LT. U.S.A.F.

SUMMARY: During the period 22 Feb 53 to 1 Mar 53, a total of 20 aerial sightings were reported in the New York State area. None of these sightings were confirmed as the result of foreign aircraft, missiles, or other identifiable objects. Inquiries and investigations of these sightings have failed to produce any evidence of activity by foreign powers. No objects have been photographed or recovered. The majority of the sightings are attributed to natural phenomena or are otherwise unidentified.

New York (33-0017): Sightings were made in all but one case by qualified observers.
The quality of the reports was very good. Highblow was the
only qualified observer.

APPROVED:
DONALD K. NUTTER
1st LT., USAF
