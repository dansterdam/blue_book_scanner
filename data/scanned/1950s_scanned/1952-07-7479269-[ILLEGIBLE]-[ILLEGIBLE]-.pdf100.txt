1. At 1320Z time on 19 July 1952, the Director of Intelligence, 26th Air Division (Defense) received the following report of unconventional aircraft:

a. A single star-shaped object, traveling very fast, in a south or southwest direction. Object gave off a dazzling light.

b. Sighted on 19 July 1952 at 0210Z. Object was in sight for approximately one and one-half (1 1/2) minutes.

c. Visual observation from the ground.

d. Observer at DELHI, MARYLAND. Object at 3T 3037.
