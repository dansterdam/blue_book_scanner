Unidentified Flying Object

PREPARED BY (OFFICER) SAC, LANGLEY AFB, VIRGINIA
DATE 30 August 1954

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

On Tuesday evening, 17 August 1954, a newspaper reporter from Hampton, Virginia, two officers of the Newport News, Virginia, police department and a civilian, reported sighting an unusual phenomenon over the Hampton Roads area. At approximately 2030 hours, 18 August 1954, they observed an unusual phenomena over the Hampton Roads area, appearing from the Northeast and moving in a southwesterly direction at a very fast rate of speed, according to the individual observers. This sighting lasted for about 5 minutes.

The Wednesday morning edition of the "Daily Press", Newport News, Virginia, carried an article stating that several people at the Jefferson Hotel, Newport News, Virginia, had called in and stated that they had witnessed the phenomena.
