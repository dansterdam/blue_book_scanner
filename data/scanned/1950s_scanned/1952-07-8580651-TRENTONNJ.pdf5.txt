ON 23 JULY 1952 AT APPROXIMATELY 2100 HOURS A RESIDENT OF TRENTON, NEW JERSEY, REPORTED SIGHTING A ROUND, LUMINOUS OBJECT WHICH APPEARED TO BE HOVERING OVER TRENTON. HE STATED THAT THE OBJECTS APPEARED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 30 FEET IN DIAMETER AND EMITTED A BLUE-WHITE GLOW. HE ADDED THAT THE OBJECTS SEEMED TO HOVER AND THEN ACCELERATE AND DISAPPEAR AT HIGH SPEEDS. THE OBSERVER CLAIMED THAT THESE SIGHTINGS WERE BECOMING A COMMON OCCURRENCE IN THE TRENTON AREA.
