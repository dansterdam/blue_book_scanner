No Case (Information Only)
19 March 1956
Castro Valley, California

Mary Noltie, Castro Valley, California, called the sheriff's office and reported she had seen a circular object come down to about 40 feet above the ground and then rapidly ascend out of sight. This incident occurred at about 4:30 PM on 3/19/56. Callers said the object was about 30 feet in diameter, silver in color, and made no noise. The object appeared to be tilted as it descended and ascended.
An investigation was made but no other witnesses were located. The object could not be identified as any known aircraft or phenomenon. The case remains unidentified.
