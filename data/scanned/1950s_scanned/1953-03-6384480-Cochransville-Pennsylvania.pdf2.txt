16 August 1953
SUBJECT: REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT 
TO: ADC ANT AND COLORADO SPRINGS COLO.
YACTION: INFORMATION
FLIGHT OF AGE AIRCRAFT WITH LIGHT ATTACHED WAS OBSERVED BY WITNESS ON 1331-1335, EST, 16 AUG 53 IN VICINITY OF COCHRANSVILLE, PA.
AIRCRAFT WAS OBSERVED TO BE SILVER IN COLOR AND FLYING AT MEDIUM HIGH ALTITUDE.
AIRCRAFT MADE RAPID ASCENT, THEN DESCENDED SHARPLY AT HIGH SPEED. NO SOUND WAS HEARD. PILOT FALL WITH LIGHT WAS SIGHTED.
CLOUDS SCATTERED AT 12,000 FT. REPORTED TO EARL BY LT. PEARSON.
CONCLUSION: 5f1
ANDERSON RESO.
ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER
