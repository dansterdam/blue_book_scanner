REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
Subject-unidentified Aerial Objects
Date-6 September 1952
Place-Tucson, Arizona
Witnesses-Mr. & Mrs. J. M. Jordan

On 6 September 1952, Mr. and Mrs. J. M. Jordan reported seeing several unidentified aerial objects in the sky over Tucson, Arizona. The objects were described as glowing, circular, and flying in a formation. The Jordans observed the objects for approximately 20 minutes before they disappeared from view. This incident was reported to the United States Air Force and investigated as part of Project Blue Book.
