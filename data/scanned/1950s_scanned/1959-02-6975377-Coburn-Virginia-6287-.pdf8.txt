TELEPHONE CALl FROM MRS. COBURN, 2-6-59

On 2-6-59 at 10:30 A.M. Mrs. Coburn called to report that on 2-5-59 at approximately 6:45 P.M. she and her daughter had observed a bright object in the sky. She stated that it appeared to be a large, round, silver object which hovered over the town of Bluefield, Virginia for approximately one-half hour before it disappeared. Mrs. Coburn said she thought it might be an unidentified flying object and wanted to report it to the proper authorities.

Mrs. Coburn was advised that sightings of this nature should be reported to the U.S. Air Force. She was courteous and cooperative throughout the call.
