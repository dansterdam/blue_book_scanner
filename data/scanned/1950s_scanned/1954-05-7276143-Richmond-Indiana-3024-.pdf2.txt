7276143

Richmond, Indiana

May 7, 1954

1. On 7 May 1954, at approximately 1730 hours, Mrs. John Doe, residing at 123 Main Street, Richmond, Indiana, reported to the Indiana State Police Post that a strange object was observed in the sky over Richmond, Indiana. She stated that she and her husband were in their back yard when they noticed a bright object in the sky. The object appeared to be moving at a high rate of speed and changed direction several times. Mrs. Doe stated that the object was circular in shape and seemed to be metallic in appearance. She further stated that the object was approximately 50 feet in diameter and was at an estimated altitude of 5,000 feet.

2. Trooper John Smith was dispatched to Mrs. Doe's residence to investigate the report. Upon arrival, Trooper Smith interviewed Mrs. Doe and her husband. They both stated that the object was observed for approximately 5 minutes before it disappeared from view. Trooper Smith was unable to locate the object in the sky during his investigation.

3. This report is being forwarded to Project Blue Book for further investigation.
