UNCLASSIFIED
REPORT NO. 52-106
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object
AREA REPORTED: Korea
LOCATION: Kaesung, Korea
DATE OF OBSERVATION: 11 June 1952
TIME OF OBSERVATION: 0800 to 0930 hrs
DESCRIPTION: This report contains information obtained from 2-94 crew members who experienced a visual sighting and observation of an unidentified flying object. The object was observed to be stationary and hovering at an estimated altitude of approximately 30,000 feet. It was circular in shape, silver in color and approximately 40-50 feet in diameter. It remained stationary for a period of approximately 30 minutes and then accelerated and climbed rapidly out of sight. The crew members stated they were amazed at the brilliance and climbing ability of the object, and are convinced it was not any known type of aircraft.
