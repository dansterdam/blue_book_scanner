1. North Korea - 26 May 1952
An F-94 flying a mission was told by ground radar that there was an unidentified object on their scope. The F-94 looked on with its radar at 7000 yds. and sighted the unknown. It turned and stuck the unknown a brilliant white light straight ahead. The unidentified performed a steady climbing turn and descended away from the F-94 which now had lost its after-burner. The pilot was unable to close and the 14 sec. the object at 2600 yds. after 15 seconds of contact.
