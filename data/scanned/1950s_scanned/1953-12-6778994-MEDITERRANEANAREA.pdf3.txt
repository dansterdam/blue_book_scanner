AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Multiple unidentified flying objects observed on radar in the Mediterranean area.
DATE OF INFORMATION: 16 December 1953
CONCLUSIONS: 
1. Unidentified flying objects were observed on radar by personnel of US-operated "Z" radar equipment.
2. It is the opinion of electronics personnel in this Division that such a return is an "anomalous propagation".
3. Possible causes:
a. Subterranean radar jamming detachment "Z" radar.
b. Genie or U.S. Rio in flight.
4. Logical deduction is that one of the above mentioned possibilities is the cause for this radar return.
