AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

(1) Shape: CAPT-shaped, and at times, it "T" shaped.
Color: Luminous
Number: One, approximately 30 feet in diameter.
Observed for 7 to 8 minutes.
Phenomenon appeared stationary, then moved slowly and rapidly in varied directions, then stopped and hovered, then moved upward at a very fast rate and disappeared.

(2) 1310 MST 28 July 1952, observed for 7 or 8 minutes.

(3) Visual

(4) Observers at KAPA, New Mexico. Longitude was 106°59' and Latitude was 35°22'

(5) Reliability of observers good. All are WARS at KAPA.

(6) Visibility 60 miles, wind 7 MPH

(7) Maneuvers: Hovered, moved in various directions at varying speeds, and then gained altitude rapidly and disappeared.
