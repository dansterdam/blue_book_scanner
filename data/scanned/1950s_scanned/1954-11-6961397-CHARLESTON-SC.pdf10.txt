25 MAY 1960

Dear Mr. Barry:

This is to acknowledge your letter of 25 April 1960 addressed to this office. There Pells Air Station and Griffiss Air Force Base, New York concerning an unidentified sighting at 19:15 on 13 November 1954, near Watertown, New York.
