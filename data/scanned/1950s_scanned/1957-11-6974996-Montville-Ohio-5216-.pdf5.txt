PROJECT BLUE BOOK SPECIAL REPORT NO. 14
UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS 
6 NOVEMBER 1957
MONTVILLE, OHIO, CASE 5216

On November 6, 1957 at approximately 7:30 p.m., the witness, a resident of Montville, Ohio, reported sighting a bright light in the sky. The light was observed moving rapidly across the sky, making several abrupt turns, and then disappearing. The witness, who was outdoors at the time, stated that the object was approximately the size of a basketball and was reddish-orange in color. The object was observed for approximately 5 minutes before it disappeared from view. No other witnesses were reported, and no photographs were obtained.
