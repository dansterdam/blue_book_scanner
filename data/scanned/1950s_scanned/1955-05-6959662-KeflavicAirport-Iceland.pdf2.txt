AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
8 MAY 1955
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Keflavik Airport, Iceland
REPORT DATE: 12 May 1955
SYNOPSIS:
On 8 May 1955, at approximately 1940 hours, a bright light was observed by the control tower personnel at Keflavik Airport. The light was described as being much larger and brighter than a normal aircraft light and it appeared to be hovering or moving very slowly. The object was observed for approximately 10 minutes before it disappeared from view.
