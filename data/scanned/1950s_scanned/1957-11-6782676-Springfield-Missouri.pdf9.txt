July 24, 1964

Dear Mr. and Mrs. [redacted],

This is in reply to your letter of July 3, 1964, addressed to the "Project Blue Book" of the U.S. Air Force regarding an unidentified flying object sighted on November 11, 1957.

Enclosed also is a self-addressed envelope for your convenience in returning the form.
