Referring to your letter and sketches of 14 December 1957 concerning unidentified aerial objects which you observed near the Mexico City Airport:
Although we have received no reports or notice of anything unusual from the Mexico City Airport, or military units in the area at that time, your report will be made a part of our continuing study of the subject.
Attached please find Department of Defense material on the subject of unidentified flying objects.
Your reporting of the incident and your interest in this matter is appreciated.
Sincerely,
