"Green Fireball" Seen
April 21, 1951 - Southeasterm New Mexico
A green fireball was observed over a 10 minute period at about 10:00 P.M. in the southern portion of Lea County near the Texas border. It was seen by several persons who reported the object approached to within a few miles and discarded red and green particles and burned itself out. Its apparent speed was low.
