SPECIAL REPORT
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
ON 8 OCT 54 AT APX 1200 CST A SILVER ACORN SHAPED OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY JAMES LOGAN AND OTHERS AT THE CARTER FLIGHT SERVICE CENTER, ATLANTA AP, TO SUDDENLY BECOME VERY BRIGHT AS IT TURNED SLOWLY TO THE NORTHWEST, THEN QUICKLY DISAPPEAR IN A FLASH OF LIGHT. OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY ONE MINUTE. CARTER FLIGHT SERVICE CENTER SAID IT DID NOT SHOW ON RADAR.
SPECIAL INVESTIGATION BEGAN.
EXACT SHAPE AND BRIGHTNESS CHANGEABLE ACCORDING TO THE OBSERVER. SOME SAID OBJECT WAS LARGER THAN AIRCRAFT AND MUCH BRIGHTER THAN ANY AIRCRAFT THEY HAD EVER SEEN.
BEST TWO SIGHTS:
OPERATIONS OFFICER, MAXWELL FLIGHT SERVICE.
