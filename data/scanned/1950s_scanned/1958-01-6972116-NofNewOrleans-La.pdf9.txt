UNCLASSIFIED
HEADQUARTERS 4703D AIR DIVISION
United States Air Force
CAFUWELL AIR FORCE BASE, TEXAS

SUBJECT: Report of Interview

TO: Commander
Keesler Air Force Base, Mississippi

1. This is in reference to a sighting of an unidentified object reported by Mr. Carroll J. Dale, of Dallas, Texas, on 6 January 1958. On that date, the report was made to a special agent of the 23rd District OSI, Keesler AFB, Mississippi.

2. As forwarded by the OSI, this report stated that Mr. Dale, a meteorologist, Louisiana, to Hattiesburg, Mississippi, on U.S. Highway 11, about 1950 hours, 2 January 1958, when they sighted a blinking object hovering over the earth at approximately 1000 feet. One to one and one-half miles ahead of them. The object remained in this position for several minutes. It then moved rapidly in a vertical direction at high speed and disappeared.

3. Mr. Dale then wrote to Senator Lyndon B. Johnson stating that he was puzzled by the object and requested an investigation. Due to this Congressional interest, this base was requested to conduct an investigation. The purpose of determining the "object's" exact nature of impact with the earth's surface. Inclosure No. 1 is a report of the completed investigation with the results and findings.

4. This report is considered necessary in view of the facts uncovered by this interview with Mr. Dale.

5. Upon withdrawal of Inclosure, this correspondence may be downgraded to UNCLASSIFIED in accordance with Paragraphs 3-78h, AFR 205-1.
