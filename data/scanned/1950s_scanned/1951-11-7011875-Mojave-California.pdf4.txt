Report of Investigation
1. This Case is submitted for your information and appropriate action.
2. On 8 November 1951, this office received a report of an unidentified flying object (UFO) sighting which occurred at approximately 7:05 p.m. on 7 November 1951 near Mojave, California.
3. Briefly, the facts are as follows:
The witness, name withheld, a resident of Mojave, California, was driving his 1951 Dodge automobile westbound on U.S. Highway 466 near Redrock, California, when he observed a bright light in the sky to the north. The light appeared to be stationary and was brighter than a conventional aircraft light. As the witness continued driving, the light began to move rapidly in a northwesterly direction and then suddenly disappeared. The sighting lasted approximately 30 seconds.
4. The witness reported this sighting to the Deputy Sheriff's Office in Mojave, California. The Deputy Sheriff took the witness' statement and forwarded it to this office for investigation.
5. This office has interviewed the witness and obtained a signed statement. The witness appears to be a reliable individual and there is no reason to doubt the accuracy of his report.
6. No photographs or other physical evidence were obtained.
7. In view of the foregoing, it is recommended that this case be filed as an "Unidentified" sighting.
