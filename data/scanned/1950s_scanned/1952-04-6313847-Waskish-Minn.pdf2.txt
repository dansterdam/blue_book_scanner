SUBJECT: 
Observation of Unidentified Flying Object near Waskish, Minnesota

EVALUATION OF INFORMATION: (COVER SHEET) 
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUMMARY: 
On 29 February 1952, at approximately 11:45 PM, a bright object was observed approximately 5 miles northeast of Waskish, Minnesota. The object was seen by the ground observer corps and exhibited the following characteristics: Round, glowing reddish-orange in color, about 30 to 40 feet in diameter, with no visible means of propulsion. The object remained stationary for several minutes, then suddenly accelerated vertically and disappeared.
