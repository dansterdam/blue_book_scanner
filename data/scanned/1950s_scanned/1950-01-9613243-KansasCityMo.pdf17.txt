REPORT OF INVESTIGATION 
TITLE: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON 
22 JANUARY 1950
LOCATION: KANSAS CITY, MISSOURI (27-21-30)

SYNOPSIS: This is an Initial Report.

Investigation was initiated by the District Commander, 13th OSI District. According to the report, a person in Kansas City, Missouri reported sighting an unconventional aircraft at 12:26 hours on 22 January 1950. The object was described as being bright white and of a peculiar shape. The object was observed for several minutes as it hovered in one position and then suddenly disappeared. The object was a brilliant white and a bright red flash was observed from the outer edge of the object. The rod-like flash was not constant but would be silently interrupted. Speed could not be estimated. The object appeared to hover in one position for a period of time, after which it suddenly disappeared. No motion or sound was detected. The document concluded that the object observed was not due to any airplanes, dirigibles, or balloons of any type.
