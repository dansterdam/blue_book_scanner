SUBJECT: Sightings of Unidentified Aerial Phenomena, 10 September 1951

On 10 September 1951, our information indicates that two hollow balloons were sighted at 1:12 north of Sandy Hook, New Jersey. The balloons were 75 degrees east at an elevation of 30 degrees, northeast. This location is in the bay north of Sandy Hook. No reply was received.
