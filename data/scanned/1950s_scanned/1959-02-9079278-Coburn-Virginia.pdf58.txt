STATEMENT OF

On approximately 20 May the source and his friend were watching the sky when they spotted a round object approximately the size of a basketball. It was white and red and had white runes. The night was clear and warm with many stars. Looking upward, they saw the object hover above for 15 minutes, then seemed to lower the altitude and then moved toward Norton. Source and his friend went home and told his mother.
