JAN/7/1952 

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. On the evening of 7 Jan 1952, at 1710 hours, an object was observed in the sky over Jersey City, New Jersey. The object was described as round, white in color, and exhibiting a pulsating or flickering motion. It appeared to be about the size of a medium-sized aircraft at a distance.

2. The object was observed for approximately 5 minutes before it disappeared from view. No sound was heard and no trail was observed. Weather conditions at the time were clear.

3. This incident was reported to the authorities and eventually came to the attention of Project Blue Book for investigation.

4. After a thorough review of the available information, Project Blue Book was unable to determine the nature or identity of the observed object. It remains classified as an "unidentified flying object."
