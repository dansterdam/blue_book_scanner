(d) Markings: One (1)
(e) Movement: Slow, hovering, (no sound or unusual features)

(g) Path: Traveled or drifted from horizon 
(h) Sound: None
(i) Other pertinent or unusual features: Relatively

(2) Description of course of object:
Object: That first called the attention of observer to the object was a sudden beam of light coming out of his right side which illuminated several tree tops around him. This was followed by the actual sighting of the object, which at first appeared as a lighted dish and suddenly became black against the night sky. The object then moved in a westerly direction and gradually went out of sight behind a hill. The object was observed for a period of 15 to 20 minutes according to the observer.
