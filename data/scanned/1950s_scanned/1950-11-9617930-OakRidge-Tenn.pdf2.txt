SYNOPSIS: On 5 November 1950 at 1155 hours Mr. ______ observed an unidentified flying object over the northwest corner of Oak Ridge area.

DETAILS:
1. The object observed was a rapid change of shape varied to a much smaller object and then back to its original size. The object appeared to be translucent. The mass of the object was contained and had definitely outlined lines.
2. VISUAL: The description - The object observed by the witness changed shapes rapidly. It seemed to change to a smaller size and then back to its original size. The object appeared to be translucent. The mass of the object was contained and had definitely outlined lines. The size could not be estimated but was described as "rapid and darting". The observer said the object moved with great speed and the background blur indicated a very high rate of speed.
3. Further described the core as assuming the whole body, that is, the core bore the semblance of the whole object, with some small variations in lights. The motions of the object were described as "rapid and erratic" - "darting and very rapid in movements." The observer stated could not be seen nor could the means of propulsion be determined.
