9 MAR 1956

Mr. L. J. McClaren  
Fremont, Nebraska  

Dear Mr. McClaren: 

The information concerning your sighting of an unidentified flying object in December 1955 has been forwarded to the Air Technical Intelligence Center for reply.

Your letter and illustrations have been studied with interest; however, additional information is required in order that a complete analysis can be made. Therefore, will you please fill out and return the enclosed technical information sheet for this purpose.

You may be assured that your interest in this program is greatly appreciated.

Sincerely,

WALLACE W. ELWOOD
Lt. Col., USAF
Assistant Adjutant
