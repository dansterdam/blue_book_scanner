SUBJECT: REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
1. THE FOLLOWING IS SUBMITTED FOR INVESTIGATION: 
ON 12 JULY 1953 AT APPROXIMATELY 1935 EST, MR. JOSEPH EMERSON, RESIDENT OF KEY WEST, FLORIDA, OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT AT APPROXIMATELY 1000 FEET ALTITUDE OVER BOCA CHICA FIELD, KEY WEST, FLORIDA.
2. THE OBJECT WAS FIRST OBSERVED TO BE A LUMINOUS OBJECT LARGER THAN THE AVERAGE STAR OR PLANET. IT WAS TRAVELING IN A SOUTHERLY DIRECTION AND SUDDENLY CHANGED DIRECTION TO A WESTERLY COURSE. THE OBJECT THEN STOPPED AND HOVERED FOR APPROXIMATELY 45 SECONDS AND THEN ACCELERATED TO A HIGH RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT IN A NORTHEASTERLY DIRECTION.
3. MR. EMERSON STATED THAT THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE ROUND OR DISC-SHAPED AND THAT IT DID NOT GIVE OFF ANY FLAME, TRAIL OR EXHAUST.
4. THIS REPORT IS UNCLASSIFIED.
