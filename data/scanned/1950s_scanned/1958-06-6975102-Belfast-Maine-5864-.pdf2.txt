BELFAST, MAINE
JUNE 6, 1958
REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
1. On 6 June 1958, this office was notified that several residents of Belfast, Maine had observed unidentified flying objects in the sky over the city.
2. Subsequent investigation revealed the following:
a. At approximately 2100 hours on 6 June 1958, several residents of Belfast, Maine observed bright lights in the sky which appeared to be moving in an erratic manner. The objects were observed for a period of approximately 30 minutes.
b. Photographs were taken of the objects by one of the observers. These photographs are attached to this report.
c. Several other residents of Belfast also reported observing the objects, and their descriptions corroborated the initial report.
d. There were no known aircraft operating in the area at the time of the sightings.
3. This office has forwarded the photographs and a copy of this report to higher headquarters for further analysis and evaluation.
