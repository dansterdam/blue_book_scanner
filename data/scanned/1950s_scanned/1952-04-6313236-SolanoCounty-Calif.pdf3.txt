CONFIDENTIAL - SECURITY INFORMATION
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
THE INSPECTOR GENERAL
OFFICE OF OPERATIONS
REPORT OF INVESTIGATION

UNCONTROLLED AIRCRAFT
Sighted over Solano County, California
20 April 1952

CHARACTER: SPECIAL INQUIRY
REFERENCE: NONE. THIS IS AN INITIAL REPORT.

Investigation initiated by District Commander upon receipt of information that an unidentified aircraft was sighted across the sky, estimated interview witnesses in 0230 hours, 20 April 1952, from Solano County, California.

The object was viewed from the northwest horizon to the southwest horizon and speed of object was not estimated. The altitude and speed of object were not estimated. The object did not appear to be a meteor or any other known aerial phenomena. It did not appear to be a meteor or any other known aerial phenomena. NO LANDING OR TAKEOFF WAS REPORTED AND NO PHYSICAL TRACE OF THE OBJECT WAS FOUND.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
