SUMMARY: On 10 July 1952, an unidentified flying object was sighted by the crew of National Airlines Flight 12 while in flight over the State of Virginia. The object was sighted at 1830 hours local time at an altitude estimated to be 20,000 feet.
DETAILS: Single object was sighted by aircrews members of National Airlines Flight 12 while in flight approaching Richmond, Virginia on 10 July 1952. The object was sighted at 1830 hours local time at an altitude estimated to be 20,000 feet. The object was observed for approximately 10-12 minutes and was described as a bright metallic, disk-shaped object with no visible wings, tail or landing gear. The object was maneuvering at a high rate of speed and appeared to be under intelligent control. The object made a high-speed pass from the southwest to the northeast and then reversed its course and made another pass from the northeast to the southwest. During the observation the object appeared to change direction and speed instantaneously. The weather was clear with excellent visibility.
