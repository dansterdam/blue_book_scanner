HEADQUARTERS 5 CORPS (GROUP)
OFFICE OF THE ASSISTANT CHIEF OF STAFF G-2 
APO 301

SUBJECT: Investigation of UFO Sighting 060300 November 1957

TO: 5 Corps (Group)
APO 301

1. On 6 November 1957, a citizen who desires to remain anonymous, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object at 0600 hours in the vicinity of Kaisong, Korea.

2. Pursuant to this report, the following investigation was conducted:

a. Civilian Division and Intelligence Personnel interviewed 19 persons who stated they observed the object during the period 0600-0630 hours on 6 November 1957.

b. The witnesses unanimously stated that the object was in view for a period of approximately 30 minutes and that it was spherical in shape, appeared to be metallic, and emitted no smoke or exhaust.

c. The object was reported to have been hovering at an estimated altitude of 2000 feet and to have been completely silent in operation.

d. The object suddenly disappeared from view in a vertical ascent at a very rapid rate of speed.

e. There were no aircraft of any kind in the area at the time of the sighting.

f. The witnesses were reported to be reliable persons, and their testimony was considered credible.

g. That the bright light sighted was probably an extraterrestrial phenomenon consistent with this type of sighting appears probable.

CONFIDENTIAL
