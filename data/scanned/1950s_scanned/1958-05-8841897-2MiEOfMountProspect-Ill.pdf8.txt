July 30, 1959

Miss Maribelle Cormack
Museum Director
Roger Williams Park Museum
Providence 3, R.I.

Dear Miss Cormack:

In Dr. Menzel's absence from the Observatory, on an extended European trip, I wish to acknowledge and thank you for your letter of July 24. When he returns, on approximately September 11, I know he will be interested to read your informative note and its enclosure.

Sincerely yours,

Doris Beausoleil
Secretary to Dr. Menzel
