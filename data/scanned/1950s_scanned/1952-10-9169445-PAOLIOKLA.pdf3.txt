SUBJECT: Unconventional Type Aircraft Sighted 18.8 miles Northwest of Jacksboro, Texas, on U.S. Highway 281, 1911 hours, 29 October 1952.

TO: Director of Special Investigations
Headquarters, United States Air Force
Washington 25, D.C.

KHYD-13, an Air Force civilian employee of unknown reliability, voluntarily reported sighting an object in the sky while en route via automobile to Fort Worth, Texas, from Jacksboro, Texas, approximately 1911 hours, 29 October 1952. The object was observed from the northeast, flying a straight course at an estimated speed of 750 to 800 miles an hour, at an altitude of approximately 1,500 to 2,000 feet. It was described as very bright, silvery in color, emitting a steady glow. The object was observed for approximately 10 to 15 seconds, after which it disappeared from view. Two (2) other exhaust flashes lasting approximately the same duration of time, were observed as the object disappeared.
