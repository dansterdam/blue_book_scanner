AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
PLACE: JAPAN
ORGANIZATION: 12th Bomber Squadron, 13th Bomber Wing, FEAF
DATE OF INCIDENT: 26 October 1952
DATE OF REPORT: 27 October 1952
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Kyushu, Japan, 26 Oct 52
DESCRIPTION: On 26 October 1952, a C-119 reported an unidentified flying object at approximately 1640 hours local time while en route from Ashiya to Itazuke, Japan. The object was described as round in shape, white in color, and with a center light. It was traveling at an estimated speed of 700-800 knots and at an estimated altitude of 20,000 feet. The object was observed for 2-3 minutes before disappearing from view. No further investigation or identification of the object was made.
