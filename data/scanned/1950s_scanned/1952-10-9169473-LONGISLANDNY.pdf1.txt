PROJECT 10023 RECORD CARD
1. DATE: 29 Oct 52
2. LOCATION: LONG ISLAND N Y
3. TYPE OF SIGHTING: Visual Ground-Visual
4. SOURCE: Pilot (Civilian)
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 20 min
6. BRIEF SUMMARY OF SIGHTING:
Dark object with bright light pursued by two F-94 a/c. Object maintained flight for a period of 1 to 2 minutes, escaped at high speed.

7. CONCLUSIONS:
1. Light was seen 3 hrs and 50 min after APB. Object performs flight orbits, rises rapidly, upward and disappears, acts like a balloon. Possibility that it can be attributed to unscheduled balloon release from Mitchell AFB.
