TO ASSISTANT CHIEF OF STAFF INTELLIGENCE USAF WASHINGTON
SUBJECT: UFO OVER MODOC POINT. OREGON MARCH 5. 1958
1. IT IS REQUESTED THAT THIS REPORT BE FULLY INVESTIGATED AND ALL PERTINENT INFORMATION FURNISHED TO THIS OFFICE FOR EVALUATION. A SERVICE REPORT WAS RECEIVED ON THE ABOVE DATE FROM DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE. WASHINGTON APC. 
2. DETAILS OF THIS OBJECT ARE AS FOLLOWS:AT APPROXIMATELY 2005H PACIFIC. THE ON-DUTY AIR TRAFFIC CONTROLLER AT THE MODOC POINT OPERATIONS TOWER OBSERVED A BRIGHT LIGHT IN THE SKY. IT FIRST APPEARED AS A WHITE FLASH OF LIGHT OR EXPLOSION WHICH LASTED SEVERAL SECONDS. THEN IT APPEARED TO DECREASE IN SIZE AND CHANGE FROM WHITE TO RED AND BLUE-BLACK IN COLOR. IT REMAINED IN FULL VIEW FOR ABOUT TEN MINUTES BEFORE FADING OUT. THERE WERE NO THUNDERSTORMS OR OTHER ATMOSPHERIC PHENOMENA IN THE AREA AT THE TIME. THE OBJECT DID NOT RESEMBLE ANY KNOWN AIRCRAFT OR MISSILE. PLEASE ADVISE THIS HEADQUARTERS OF THE RESULTS OF YOUR INVESTIGATION AT THE EARLIEST POSSIBLE DATE.
/s/ HOWARD TURNER
COLONEL. USAF
DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE
