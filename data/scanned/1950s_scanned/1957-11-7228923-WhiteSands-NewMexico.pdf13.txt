AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: Unclassified
DATE: 7 November 1957
LOCATION: White Sands Proving Ground, New Mexico

1. SOURCE: Capt. F.L. Perry, Air Force, A-2 Section, A-2 Detachment, White Sands Proving Ground, New Mexico.

2. REPORT: This individual claims to have observed a bright, round, white object hovering over the White Sands Proving Ground area, at approximately 1230 hours, 7 November 1957.

3. DESCRIPTION: The object was described as being round, white in color, and approximately 50-75 feet in diameter. It was hovering at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet, and was observed for a period of approximately 10-15 minutes before suddenly accelerating, and disappearing from sight at a high rate of speed.

4. EVALUATION: The witness, Capt. F.L. Perry, is a military officer and a reliable source. His report is considered to be factual.

5. CONCLUSIONS: The object observed is unexplained at this time. Further investigation is considered desirable to determine the nature and origin of the object.
