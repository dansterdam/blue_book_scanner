13 January 1955

SUBJECT: UNCLASSIFIED SPECIAL REPORT

TO: Commanding General
Air Materiel Command
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio

1. The following information is to verification of information previously submitted CA 24 January 1955.

2. At approximately 2130 hours, 7 January 1955, this office was verbally informed by telephone from Detachment "A", 1009, USAF Security Service, that within recent hours an object had been visually observed in the air in the vicinity of Bradenton, Florida.

3. In conjunction with the previous incident, the undersigned was directed to gather further data and to attempt to establish the location, size, trajectory and composition of the observed object.

4. Investigation disclosed that at approximately 2045 hours, 7 January 1955, two civilian observers, a housewife and a retiree, who were standing in the yard of the retiree's home, located at 3408 46th Street, West, Bradenton, Florida, observed a bright white object moving in a generally northerly direction at an estimated altitude of 2,000 feet. The object was observed for approximately one minute before being lost from view. It was described as being approximately 6 to 8 feet in diameter, round or disc-shaped, and silently moving at a rapid rate of speed. The witnesses claimed that the object appeared to be lit from within and that no exhaust or contrail was visible.
