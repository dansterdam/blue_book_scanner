BALTIMORE, MARYLAND
UFO REPORT
DATE: 6 MAY 1955
INVESTIGATOR: CAPTAIN GEORGE T. GREGORY,
USAF
WITNESS: MR. JOHN DOE, AGE 42
LOCATION: WITNESS' RESIDENCE, 
BALTIMORE, MARYLAND
TIME: APPROXIMATELY 11:15 PM
DESCRIPTION OF INCIDENT:
THE WITNESS REPORTS OBSERVING A BRIGHT, ROUND OBJECT HOVERING OVER HIS RESIDENCE FOR SEVERAL MINUTES. THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 30 FEET IN DIAMETER AND EMITTED A BLUISH-WHITE GLOW. IT MADE NO SOUND AND DISPLAYED NO VISIBLE MEANS OF PROPULSION. AFTER SEVERAL MINUTES, THE OBJECT ACCELERATED RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
INVESTIGATION AND EVALUATION:
CAPTAIN GREGORY INTERVIEWED THE WITNESS AND FOUND HIM TO BE CREDIBLE AND RELIABLE. THE WITNESS HAS NO KNOWN HISTORY OF MENTAL ILLNESS OR DRUG/ALCOHOL ABUSE. THERE WERE NO OTHER KNOWN WITNESSES TO THE INCIDENT. NO PHYSICAL EVIDENCE WAS RECOVERED. AFTER REVIEWING THE FACTS, CAPTAIN GREGORY CONCLUDED THAT THE WITNESS OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OF UNKNOWN ORIGIN AND NATURE.
CONCLUSION:
THIS INCIDENT REMAINS CLASSIFIED AS "UNIDENTIFIED" IN PROJECT BLUE BOOK RECORDS.
