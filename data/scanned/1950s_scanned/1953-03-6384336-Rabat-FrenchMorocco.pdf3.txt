AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Nouasseur Air Base, French Morocco
DATE: 20 April 1953
LOCATION: Nouasseur Air Base, French Morocco

On 20 April 1953 at approximately 2300 hours, an unidentified flying object was sighted at Nouasseur Air Base by 1st Lt. USAF, who was on duty at the Nouasseur Naval Station Radar Site.

At approximately 2300 hours, the radar operator observed an unidentified target on the radar scope traveling at an estimated speed of 300 knots. The object was observed for approximately 15 minutes and then disappeared from the radar scope. An investigation was conducted at Nouasseur Air Base by the Reporting Officer.

The following report is based on ATIC Form 332:
