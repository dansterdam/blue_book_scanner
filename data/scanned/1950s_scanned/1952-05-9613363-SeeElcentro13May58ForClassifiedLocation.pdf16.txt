AT YUMA AIR FORCE BASE, ARIZONA

1. On 25 July 1952, Captain STEPHEN P. JEWELL, 4750th Air Base Group, Yuma Air Force Base, Arizona, was interviewed by the undersigned. Captain JEWELL is also Wing Intelligence Officer and in connection with this duties as control Intelligence Officer, he has become interested in sighting of unidentified objects on 13 May 1952, pertaining to subject matter.

2. On 25 July 1952, the Commanding Officer, 1903d AACS Squadron, Yuma Air Force Base, produced the tower log which revealed the following information:

115 May 1952 - At 0900 HST 31 Cenvio reported seeing fast moving object. Radio called to have me get a look-see but never spotted anything.

0920 - Spotted object at 3000 TRIP. Notified radio. Later check - it appeared to be a light from cars going down Highway out beyond Pilots Reach, which is in a northwesterly direction from the tower.

0930 - That moving object reported near Salton Sea.

Stated the last report probably came over direct line from Flight Service Center, March Air Force Base, California.

3. _ stated that the tower operator on duty and made the pre-described notations. Further, he departed Yuma Air Force Base on the morning of 25 July 1952 on a furlough status. His residence is at Inglewood, California. He indicated that he planned on visiting relatives at this time.
