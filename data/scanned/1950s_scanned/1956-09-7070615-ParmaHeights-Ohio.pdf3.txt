INFORMATION COPY
FOR 6TH DISTRICT OSI
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
OHIO

5D-24-21-180                                           12 September 1956

SPOT INTELLIGENCE REPORT

SUBJECT: (Unclassified)
Report of Unidentified Flying Object as seen by civilian, 11215 Snow Road, Parma Heights, Ohio, on 4 September 1956. He observed the object for several minutes.

TO: Director, Special Investigations
Headquarters OSI
Washington 25, D.C.

1. On 4 September 1956, at approximately 2200 hours, a civilian, residing at 11215 Snow Road, Parma Heights, Ohio, observed an unidentified flying object which he watched for several minutes from his home.

2. DETAILS: On 5 September 1956, this office interviewed the civilian, (U) Detachment, Room 611, 1367 East 6th Street, Cleveland, Ohio, who reported the following information concerning the unidentified flying object in conformance with Air Force Regulation 200-2, dated 14 August 1954:

a. Description of the object:
(1) Shape: That of a star.
(2) Size: That of a star.
(3) Color: Glowed in yellow, green, blue, orange and red.
(4) Number: Only one (1) sighted.
(5) Formation: Only one (1) sighted.
(6) Distinguishing features or details: Blinked off and on and brightness comparable to surrounding stars.
(7) Trail, tail, or exhaust: None discernible.

b. Description of course of object:
(1) When first called the attention of the observer to the object, it attracted the attention of his 12 year old son, who was looking at the stars.
