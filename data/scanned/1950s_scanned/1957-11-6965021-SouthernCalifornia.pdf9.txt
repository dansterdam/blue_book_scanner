A Flying Saucer, the Size of a Tractor-diesel, in Alagoas
(Diário de Notícias, Porto Alegre, Dec 5, 1957)
According to a report received from the town of Maceiό, State of Alagoas, a flying object in the shape of a tractor-diesel was sighted on the evening of July 5, 1957 at 10 o'clock at night.
The first appearance of the strange object occurred at 10 o'clock at night on the 5th.
Flávio Amorim states that the luminous object remained in view for 30 minutes and this distant object was described as having the shape and size of a tractor-diesel.
