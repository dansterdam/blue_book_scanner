1. On 20 November 1958, a Mr.  HAROLD C. HODGES, 14th District Office of Special Investigations, was advised that he had observed a photographic slide on 26 September 1958. HODGES advised that one of these pictures had been a 8 x 4 1/2 slide, and as he recalled, he took this picture of the slide which he had not noticed when he took the picture a red dot in the picture which he had not noticed when he took the picture.

2. HODGES also advised that as he recalled he had taken this picture in the evening and that there were no other aircraft in the air.

3. On 29 November 1958, Special Agent JAMES C. SMACK received a telephone call from a contributor that the slide is returned to him after observation by proper authorities.
