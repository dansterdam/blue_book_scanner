AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
South Eastern Indiana near Rushville
19 Nov 57
Unknown
Lt Col William H. Parrent
UP CR4
On Friday 15 November 1957 at 19:33 hours an object was sighted by witnesses traveling at a rapid rate of speed over the town of Milroy, Indiana. The object was described to be a white or silver in color and oblong in shape. It made no sound and was illuminated in a bright orange glow around the edges. The object was estimated to be 200-300 ft in diameter and maneuvered abruptly at times, coming from an easterly direction and heading in a westerly direction, being visible for approximately 3-4 minutes.
