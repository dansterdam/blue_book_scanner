OFFICE MEMORANDUM UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT
DATE: 7 January 1956
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER INDIANAPOLIS, INDIANA
 
1. On 7 January 1956, this office received a report of an unidentified flying object observed over Indianapolis, Indiana, on 28 December 1955.
 
2. The witness, John Doe, reported that on the above date at approximately 2100 hours he observed a bright object in the sky over the western horizon. The object appeared to be at an altitude of approximately 15,000 feet and was moving in a northerly direction at a speed estimated to be 500 to 600 mph.
 
3. The witness stated that the object appeared to be approximately 30 feet in diameter and was elliptical in shape. It had a bright, white light on the leading edge and a flickering red light on the trailing edge. There was no sound detected.
 
4. The witness was an experienced private pilot and had observed many aircraft in flight. He stated that the object was unlike any aircraft he had ever seen.
 
5. This office has been unable to identify the object. Project Blue Book has been notified and the case will be included in the next quarterly report.
