CONGRESS OF THE UNITED STATES
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES  
Washington, D.C.
October 2, 1958

Office of Congressional Liaison
United States Air Force
539, Old House Office Building
Washington, D.C.

Attention: Major Corbin

Dear Sir:

Please find enclosed a thermofax copy of a letter I have received from my constituent, Mrs. Sheffield Lake, of Ohio.

I am forwarding this to you in accordance with the instructions you gave me and trust you will find this enclosure self-explanatory.

I would appreciate your advice, and assurances that attention and consideration will be given to Mrs. Lake's properly advised summary.

Sincerely yours,

/s/ A. D. Baumhart, Jr., M.C.

ADBRTW
