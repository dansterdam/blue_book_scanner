"SEVERAL POLICEMEN ASSURE THEY SAW 4 FLYING SAUCERS"
In the sky over finca San Luis Buena Vista, an ambulant police squad affirm having seen four flying saucers whiz by, traveling northward in low formation and at high speed.
News of this event was received by telegraph at National Police Headquarters yesterday where no further information was given save that the report was detailed per did it say if all the members of the squad "saw" the fantastic machine in action.
The incident took place at exactly 1100 hours when the squad was carrying out its regular activities.
