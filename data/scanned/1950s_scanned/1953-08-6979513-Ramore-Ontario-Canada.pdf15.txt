STATEMENT

On August 16, 1953, at approximately 22:00, a report was received at the 912 Aircraft Control and Warning Squadron, Ramore, Ontario, Canada, from a civilian observer the details of which are as follows:

At approximately 21:00 on 16 August 1953, a civilian observer saw a strange object which was later identified as an unidentified flying object. The object was seen for approximately three minutes while it was hovering at a high altitude. The object was described as being luminous and very bright, much brighter than any star in the sky. The size of the object was estimated to be approximately ten times the size of a star. The object was observed to move slowly from east to west and then to the north, after which it disappeared from sight.
