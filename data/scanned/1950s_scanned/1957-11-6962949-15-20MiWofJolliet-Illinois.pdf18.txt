AERONAUTICAL CHART SUPPLY
To insure that the airman has the latest information available, most U.S. World Aeronautical Charts are revised at
6-month intervals. The charts listed belowmaybe obtained from the U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey, Distribution
Section, Washington 25, D.C., or from authorized sales agents.
CIVIL CHART SUPPLY
Authorized agents for the sale of aeronautical charts have been designated in certain states and principal cities through
out the United States. The charts may be purchased from these agents or from the U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey,
Washington 25, D.C.
