Paper's Photos Show 'Saucer' in Kentucky

Louisville, Kentucky, June 27 - The Louisville Times stated photos of a "flying saucer" were taken here Saturday.

Sheriff Frank Tomblyn said persons in three sections of the city reported today that they spotted the object over a span of 15 to 20 minutes. It was first sighted high in the sky, then flew close to the ground, and finally vanished into the sun's rays.

Bert Ritenbaugh, a cameraman, said he took movies of the object, which was visible for about 10 to 15 seconds. He said it would zoom close to the ground and then shoot skyward at a terrific rate of speed, disappearing in a matter of seconds.

"During that time," he said, "it seemed like a balloon."
