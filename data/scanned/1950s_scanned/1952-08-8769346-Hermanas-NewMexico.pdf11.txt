SUBJECT: PHENOMENON OBSERVED NEAR HERMANAS, NEW MEXICO, 8 AUGUST 1952

1. The time observed approximately from 2235 to 2300 hours.
2. The duration of the incident was reportedly from 15 to 30 minutes.
3. The distance and direction was approximately 7 miles south from Hermanas, New Mexico.
4. The number of objects observed was two.
5. The color of the objects was white or luminous white.
6. The objects were observed as two glowing, rounded, white lights traveling in a horizontal path in the sky.
7. There was no noise or trail.
8. The objects disappeared in a shrinking manner, getting smaller and smaller until they disappeared completely from view.
9. No further observations were made.

Witnesses: Colonel Gerald G. Johnson, J-2 Air Division, Kirtland Air Force Base, Albuquerque, New Mexico.
