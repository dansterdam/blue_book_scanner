SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, 5 December 1952
On 5 December 1952, a white, phosphorescent, egg-shaped object was observed near the airfield at Big Spring, Texas. The object was 20-25 feet in diameter and hovered at an altitude of 1,500 feet for approximately 15 minutes before disappearing in the east. No noise or exhaust was noted. The object was tracked on radar, which recorded its speed at varying from 0 to 200 mph.
SIGNAL
This office has been informed that the object sighted is not yet identified.
