AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

Venezuela
ANDREWS-DMA, GARAGES, VENEZUELA, 14 MAR 56

LOCAL PEOPLE
AT: 3A
Unidentified Flying Object Reported Seen from Turquestein
AT: 1. An unidentified luminous object, about the size of the Moon, with a violet colored luminous atmosphere around it, was reportedly observed from 2319, 13 March to 2333, 13 March, from Turquestein, Venezuela. The object remained stationary for about 15 minutes, then rapidly accelerated and disappeared from view. There was no sound heard during its flight.
2. It was also reported from Caracas that the luminous object had been seen from there.
GREGORY L. HALL
