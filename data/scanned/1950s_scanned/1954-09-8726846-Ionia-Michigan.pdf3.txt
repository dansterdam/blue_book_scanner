WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22, 1954.

Capsules of News 

On Michigan Front

Flint Woman Heads State Library Board.
Lucile Anna Wernette, vice-chairman of the State Board for Libraries, was announced Wednesday as the new chairman of the state board for libraries. She is a vice chairman.

Other members of the board are Donald W. Kohlstedt of Grand Rapids and Arvine E. Lindsay of Kalamazoo.

The board voted to ask the state department of administration to investigate legislative committees to include the state library and its law division in the proposed justices building since the 1954 state office building fire.

3 Glowing Sky Objects Reported.
Ionia -(AP)- Amateur astronomers Tuesday reported to civil defense officials they had tracked three glowing objects "across the sky" Monday night. 
The observers said the objects were traveling at tremendous speed and disappeared at an extreme height. Spokesmen for the Air Filter Center at Grand Rapids said there was no radar contact with the objects.
The astronomers were Clarke Burgeon, 25, and William McLaughlin, 23, both of Ionia.
