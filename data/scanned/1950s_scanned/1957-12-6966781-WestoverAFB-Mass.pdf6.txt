HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
THE INSPECTOR GENERAL
OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS
DISTRICT OFFICE NO. 22 - GRIFFISS AIR FORCE BASE
ROME, NEW YORK
11 December 1957

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects

TO: Commander
Air Defense Command
Ent Air Force Base
Colorado Springs, Colorado

1. Reference is made to paragraph 7a(2)(a), AFR 200-2, dated 12 August 1954, concerning above subjects.

2. Attached as inclosures are copies of two letters from the Albany, New York, Office of the Federal Bureau of Investigation dated 2 and 3 December 1957. The letters concern the sighting of an unidentified flying object on 3 December 1957 at New York City and Wappingers Falls, New York, by a Mrs. J. Dexter J. Cooper, who describes the sighting of an unidentified flying object on 3 December 1957 approximately eight (8) miles north of Schenectady, New York.

3. The indices of this District Office reflect no record of
this matter.

4. This matter is being referred to your Headquarters in accordance with the requirements of paragraph 7a(2)(a), AFR 200-2, dated 12 August 1954.

5. By carbon copy of this letter the Directorate of Special Investigations, Washington 25, D.C., and the Commander, Griffiss Air Force Base, New York, have been furnished copies of the attached letters.

LEON F. BUCK
Lt. Colonel, USAF
District Commander
