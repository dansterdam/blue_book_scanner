DATE: 15 June 1954

At 0742 eastern standard time the object was first spotted and told us about the object and told us to take pictures of it. We spotted the object right away and began taking pictures. The first roll was used up to increase the film wasn't any good as it had been in the camera for some time, the second roll was taken. The object was as described in form A. Bound with the what appeared to be a string hanging from the bottom. It was first spotted to the east, it appeared to be moving away and slightly to the right. The object was below the clouds which the weather department estimated to be 31,000 feet. There was no appreciable movement or change during the time it was in sight. At 0932 eastern standard time the object disappeared from sight. It seemed to disappear in the distance. In my opinion the object was a balloon.
