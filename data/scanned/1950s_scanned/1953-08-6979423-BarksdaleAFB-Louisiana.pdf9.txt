SUBJECT CASE FACTS: 

ON 6 AUGUST 1953 AT APPROXIMATELY 1700 HOURS, AIR POLICEMEN ON DUTY AT BARKSDALE AFB, LOUISIANA, REPORTED OBSERVING SEVERAL UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS MANEUVERING OVER THE BASE. THE OBJECTS WERE DESCRIBED AS OBLONG IN SHAPE AND SILVER IN COLOR. THEY WERE SEEN MOVING IN VARIOUS DIRECTIONS AT HIGH SPEED AND WERE VISIBLE FOR ABOUT 10 MINUTES. NO SOUND WAS REPORTED TO BE COMING FROM THE OBJECTS. 

INVESTIGATION OF INCIDENT: OFFICERS FROM THE AIR FORCE BASE INVESTIGATED THE INCIDENT AND INTERVIEWED THE WITNESSES. THEY WERE UNABLE TO DETERMINE THE IDENTITY OR ORIGIN OF THE OBJECTS. THE INCIDENT WAS REPORTED TO HIGHER AIR FORCE HEADQUARTERS. NO PHYSICAL EVIDENCE WAS OBTAINED.
