Case 215. Berea, Ohio, Sept. 8, 1956--Driving over Highway 252 at 9:30 a.m., Mrs. Harold Thomas, watched a round, tailless, wingless object. It color a dull silver. She did not stop her car but had the impression that it was motionless. No sound was audible, says that of her car.
