HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
OFFICE OF INFORMATION SERVICES
REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
TITLE: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT
DATE/TIME EST: 27 JANUARY 1950, 1715 HOURS EST
LOCATION: SCULLVILLE, NEW JERSEY
SYNOPSIS: None. This is an initial report.
Investigation predicated upon receipt of letter from Mr. H. to the Air Materiel Command, dated 27 January 1950, at Scullville, N.J. advising that on 27 January 1950, he observed a white streak moving through the sky and noted that it went into a zig zag motion and disappeared. He observed the phenomena for approximately 1/2 minute. The phenomena appeared to be ascend at about sixty (60) degrees angle. The phenomenon had appearances and indications of a vapor trail.
In that there is but one sighting, and the information available concerning this sighting does not contain sufficient technical data, no further investigation of this phenomena is considered advisable to expend further time toward the investigation of this phenomenon.
