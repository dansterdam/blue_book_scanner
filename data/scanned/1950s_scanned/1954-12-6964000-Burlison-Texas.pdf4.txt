AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
REPORT NO. 1
LOCATION: Burleson, Texas
DATE-TIME GRP: 1954 DEC 6
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFED FLYING OBJECT
1. On 3 Dec 54 a witness reported observing a bright, luminous object in the sky at Burleson, Texas. The object was observed for approx 15 to 20 mins.
2. Object was described as spherical, bright, and luminous. It was observed to change colors from red to blue to white. The object remained stationary and then suddenly disappeared from view.
3. No other details are available at this time. This report has been forwarded to the Directorate of Intelligence for further analysis and evaluation.
