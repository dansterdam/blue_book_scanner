TERRE HAUTE, INDIANA - 9 OCTOBER 1951

I. DISCUSSION OF 7TH INCIDENT
On 7 October 1951, a CAA Chief Aircraft Communicator observed a silver object pass directly overhead while he was at Bunyan municipal airport. The object was about the same size as a 50 cent piece held at arm's length. The object passed from directly overhead to the horizon in about 15 seconds. There was no sound or vapor trail until the horizon was reached, when it could be seen to be moving at a tremendous speed.
