AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
PERU
SIGHTINGS OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS IN PERU
On two occasions within the last two weeks two different sightings have been made of unidentified flying objects in Peru. The details are in the following quoted telegram from the Inspector General of Aeronautics (Department of Americas), Peru, dated 29 October 1957:
"THE MINISTER OF WAR AND INDIAN AFFAIRS INFORMS US THAT YESTERJAY AT 8:15 P.M. IN THE VICINITY OF ANDANGUILAS PROVINCE AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WAS OBSERVED OVER A MOUNTAINOUS AREA FOR APPROXIMATELY TWENTY MINUTES EMITTING RED, GREEN AND YELLOW LIGHTS. OBJECT DISAPPEARED APPARENTLY IN THE TALAURA DISTRICT ZONE. WE LOOKED WITHOUT SUCCESS UNTIL THIS MINUTE."
