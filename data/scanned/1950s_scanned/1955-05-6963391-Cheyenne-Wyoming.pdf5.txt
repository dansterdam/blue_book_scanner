MEMORANDUM FOR: Commander
449th Technical Training Wing 
Francis E. Warren Air Force Base

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects

Reference is made to AFB 200-2, dated 12 August 1954, subject as above. On 26 May 1955, this office was advised by both of the 
2483rd Electronics Installations Squadron and the 3435th Radar Bomb Scoring Squadron, Francis E. Warren Air Force Base, that they had observed unidentified flying objects at approximately 2300 hours, 22 May 1955, approximately 30 miles south of Cheyenne, Wyoming. 

2. At approximately 1600 hours, 26 May 1955, both airmen were interviewed and the following information was obtained:

a. Description of objects:
(1) Shape - Objects Nos. 1 and 2 - slender, rectangular, 
in vertical position. Objects Nos. 3 and 4 - oval, 
with protruding top.
(2) Size - Objects appeared slightly larger than baseball.
(3) Color - Objects Nos. 1 and 2 - white; Objects Nos. 3 
and 4 - dark, with intermittent bright-purple illumina-
tion.
(4) Number - Four (4).
(5) Formation - Side by side.
(6) Features - Nos. 1 and 2 - slender, vertical, rectangular. 
Nos. 3 and 4 - oval, with protruding top.
(7) Tail, trail or exhaust, including size. Negative.
(8) Sound. Negative.
