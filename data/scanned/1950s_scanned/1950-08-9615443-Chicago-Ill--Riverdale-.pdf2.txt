HEADQUARTERS FIFTH ARMY
1526 EAST HYDE PARK BOULEVARD
CHICAGO 15, ILLINOIS

ALFBP-1                                                                10 August 1950
SUBJECT: "Flying Disc"

TO : Commanding General
      Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
      Dayton, Ohio
      ATTN: MCIT

1. In compliance with letter, Department of the Army, GSUSA, CSOID
   452.1, dated 23 March 1948, subject: Unconventional Aircraft, control
   number A-2929, the following information is hereby submitted:

   "Chicago Herald-American" - 7 August 1950
   "REPORT 'DISC' OVER SUBURB - A flying disc, traveling 'very high
    and very fast,' was reported circling over Riverdale yesterday.
    Witnesses, 32, saw an object in the suburb, said he
    heard what sounded like an airplane and then spotted a flat
    circular object passing overhead at a low house.
    'He said it was too round to be an airplane and was going too
    fast to be a balloon.'
2. No further investigation is contemplated by this headquarters.

FOR THE COMMANDING GENERAL:

JAMES RANDOLPH
Lt Colonel, GSC
Acting AC of S, G-2
