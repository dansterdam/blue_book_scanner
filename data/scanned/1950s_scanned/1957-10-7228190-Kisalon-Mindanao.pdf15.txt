Kisalon, Mindanao 
7 October 1957

These are things of significance:

1. Findings:
The object was about 30 feet in diameter, metallic in appearance, and seemed to have a dome on top. No other details were noted except that it was hovering about 3 feet above the ground.

Description: The object floated silently and moved slowly, circling the area for about 15 minutes before rising rapidly and disappearing into the sky.

Two persons witnessed this object. Their names have been withheld.

People in village were excited and frightened by the event.

Could mean interesting lunar flight.

Magunguet received information. Suggest complete report should be prepared.
