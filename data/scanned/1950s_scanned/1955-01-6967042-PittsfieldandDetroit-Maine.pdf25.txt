UNCLASSIFIED
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

DATE: 12 JAN 58
TO: GDECC

SUBJECT: SIGHTING REPORT

LOCATION: PITTSFIEDL AND DETROIT, MAINE

DESCRIPTION: AT APPROXIMATELY 1805 EST ON 6 JANUARY 1955, TWO BRIGHT LIGHTS WERE OBSERVED NEAR DETROIT, MAINE, BY SEVERAL WITNESSES. THE LIGHTS WERE REPORTED AS MOVING RAPIDLY AND ERRATICALLY AT AN UNDETERMINED ALTITUDE. THE LIGHTS WERE OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 15 MINUTES. SHORTLY AFTER THE INITIAL SIGHTING, ANOTHER BRIGHTLY LIT OBJECT WAS OBSERVED NEAR PITTSFIEDL, MAINE, ABOUT 20 MILES FROM THE DETROIT SIGHTING. THIS OBJECT WAS ALSO REPORTED AS MOVING RAPIDLY AND ERRATICALLY AT AN UNDETERMINED ALTITUDE. NUMEROUS WITNESSES REPORTED THESE SIGHTINGS TO LOCAL AUTHORITIES AND SEVERAL OF THEM WERE SUBSEQUENTLY INTERVIEWED BY AIR FORCE PERSONNEL.

CONCLUSION: THE SIGHTINGS REPORTED HAVE BEEN CLASSIFIED AS UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENA (UAP). THE SIGHTINGS EXHIBITED CHARACTERISTICS NOT CONSISTENT WITH KNOWN AIRCRAFT, SATELLITES, OR OTHER IDENTIFIABLE OBJECTS. FURTHER INVESTIGATION IS PLANNED TO ATTEMPT TO DETERMINE THE NATURE AND ORIGIN OF THE UAP.
