HEADQUARTERS, UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
OFFICE OF THE DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE
WASHINGTON

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Report

TO: Director of Intelligence
Headquarters, United States Air Force
Washington 25, D.C.

1. The following report is submitted in accordance with the provisions of AFR 200-2.

2. Date and Time: 9 January 1953, 1540 hours.

3. Location: Suwon, Korea.

4. Observation: Two unidentified flying objects were reported observed maneuvering at high altitude over Suwon, Korea, on 9 January 1953. Witnesses state the objects were bright and metallic in appearance, flew in a straight line, then made a sharp turn and disappeared at high speed.

5. Identification: Unidentified.

6. Evaluating and Interpreting Agency: Air Intelligence Service Central Command, Far East.

7. Comments and Recommendations: This report is forwarded for your information and any action deemed appropriate.

FOR THE COMMANDER:
