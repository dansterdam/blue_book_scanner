SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT OBSERVED IN THE COLORADO SPRINGS COLORADO AREA

1. ON 7 JULY 1952 AT APPROXIMATELY 2300 A CIVILIAN COUPLE OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT IN THE COLORADO SPRINGS, COLORADO AREA.

2. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS A BRIGHT WHITE LIGHT WHICH CHANGED DIRECTION FROM EAST TO WESTERLY HEADING AND THEN BACK EAST AGAIN. THE OBJECT WAS ESTIMATED AT 2500 FEET OR A CHANGING COURSE OBJECTS SPEED WAS ESTIMATED AT AROUND 300 MILES PER HOUR. THE OBJECT CHANGED COLOR FROM YELLOW TO RED AND BACK AGAIN DURING OBSERVATION.

3. OBSERVER LOCATED THE OBJECT 11 FOR 15 MINUTES AND ESTIMATED ITS VISUAL SIZE AS LARGER THAN A DIME HELD AT ARM'S LENGTH. THE OBJECT WAS NOT HEARD OR SEEN TO LAND NEAR THE OBSERVERS WHILE UNDER OBSERVATION AND DISAPPEARED WITHOUT TRACE.

4. DESCRIPTION AND LOCATION OF OBJECT CORRESPONDS WITH PREVIOUS UNIDENTIFIED REPORTS FROM SAME GENERAL AREA ON OR ABOUT THE SAME DATE.
