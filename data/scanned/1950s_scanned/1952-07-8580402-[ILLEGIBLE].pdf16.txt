AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
COUNTRY: Canada
SUBJECT: Sighting of unidentified objects
DATE OF INFORMATION: 25 July 1952
REPORTING UNIT: 10th Tactical Reconnaissance Squadron, 66th Reconnaissance Technical Squadron, APO 239
OBSERVER: M. Srs., AF, AO 19110-87
SUMMARY: A group of round, white, luminous objects was observed approximately 6 to 10 in number. They appeared to be a half-mile in size and were traveling at an estimated 25 miles per hour. The objects were observed for approximately 30 minutes. They were first observed in the southwest and then traveled across the sky towards the northeast. The objects were observed to oscillate up and down and then observed to split into two groups, one group proceeding to the northeast and the other to the northwest.
