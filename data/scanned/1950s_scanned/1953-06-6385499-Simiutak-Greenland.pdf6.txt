AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT (1220 HRS)
SIMIUTAK, GREENLAND
4 JUNE 1953

SUMMARY
1. At approximately 1220 hrs, 4 June 1953, an unidentified aerial object was observed by T/Sgt ALEX K. BAKER, let Lt THOMAS F. ADAMS, and T/Sgt WILLIAM B. GARRIOTT of the 1st Rescue Squadron, Air Rescue Service, Greenland.

2. The object was a round, white, shiny object which was observed to travel in a straight line at a high rate of speed for approximately 30 seconds when it then disappeared.

3. There was no sound associated with the object.

4. An attempt was made to identify the object as some known type of aircraft, but no such identification could be made.
