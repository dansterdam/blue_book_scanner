1 July 1957

MEMORANDUM FOR FILES

SUBJECT: Investigation of UFO

At 1430 hours, 27 June 1957 the undersigned had a telephone conversation with Col. Deems, Operations Officer, Air Force at Aberdeen Proving Ground, Maryland. It was determined at this time by his records that on the night of 24 and 25 May 1957 at approximately between the hours of 930 and 1030 EST EDT that a demonstration did take place over the area Whorton Creek, Maryland, and that World War II parachute Flares were dropped. This drop was for the benefit of West Point Cadets who were visiting the Aberdeen proving Ground.

Elvin R. Hill
OLIVER D. HILL
Maj/cpt, USAF
