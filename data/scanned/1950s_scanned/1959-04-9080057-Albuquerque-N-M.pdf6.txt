TELETYPE MESSAGE TO ATIC
19 MAY 53
CAPTAIN GEORGE PAYNE JAMES MADE REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SIGHTING TO 1ST WEATHER GROUP, KIRTLAND AFB, ALBUQUERQUE, N.M. ON 9 MAY 53 AT APPROX 2230 HOURS. OBJECT WAS OBSERVED THROUGH PERISCOPE OF AIRCRAFT FOR APPROX 15 MINUTES. OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS LARGE, ROUND, BRILLIANT WHITE LIGHT WHICH CHANGED COLOR TO RED, MOVED ERRATICALLY AT HIGH SPEED, AND MADE SHARP TURNS. OBJECT WAS TRACKED ON RADAR AND SEEN IN VISION BY PILOT AND CO-PILOT OF AIRCRAFT. OBJECT PASSED TO THE NORTH AND DISAPPEARED. INTELLIGENCE OF THIS HEADQUARTERS IS REQUESTED REGARDING THIS INCIDENT. PLEASE ADVISE.
