SECRET INTELLIGENCE REPORT
SUBJECT: Sighting of Unidentified Flying Objects, Guatemala City Airport, Guatemala, 15 March 1950.

TO: Director of Special Investigations 
Headquarters USAF
Washington 25, D.C.

1. SYNOPSIS: Captain USAPR and pilot for commercial airline, states that three American Crew members, TACA, commercial airline, saw "flying saucers" at 2315 hours 15 March 1950 passing directly over runway Guatemala City Airport, Guatemala.

2. DETAILS: Captain USAPR, while on a two week active duty tour at Brookley AFB, Alabama, disclosed the following information:
a. On 16 March 1950 HALL was the pilot on a TACA aircraft in flight from San Salvador to Guatemala City Airport, Guatemala. On arriving at the Guatemala City Airport, the TACA pilot, engineer, and co-pilot stated they had sighted "flying saucers" over the airport. 
b. The TACA pilot, engineer, and co-pilot all of them Mexican, Louisianans, and others, stated they had sighted four "flying saucers" over Guatemala City, Guatemala.

a. To the best of my recollection the information furnished is as follows:
(1) Date of sighting - 15 March 1950.
(2) Time of sighting - approximately 2315 hours.
(3) Observer's position - standing in front of Air Terminal, Guatemala City Airport, Guatemala.
(4) Terrain: Guatemala City Airport, Guatemala. 
(5) Observed: Four disc-shaped objects, they say they had sighted four objects, were in a single formation.

UNCLASSIFIED
DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
