AF FORM 112 - PART II
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

ON JULY 21, 1957 (SUNDAY) THERE WAS IN THE PATIO OF OUR HOUSE AND SAW A FLYING OBJECT RISE IN THE SKY OVER A HOUSE LOCATED ABOUT 350 YARDS AWAY WHICH APPEARED AS A LUMINOUS ORANGE OBJECT WHICH EXPLODED NOT FAR ABOVE OUR HOUSE. THE EXPLOSION WAS QUITE BRIGHT AND DID NOT LAST LONG. THE OBJECT MOVED IN A HORIZONTAL DIRECTION AND LEFT A DARK TRAIL BEHIND IT. AFTER THE EXPLOSION, THERE WAS VISIBLE A RED LIGHT WHICH SEEMED TO BE DESCENDING TO THE GROUND. I ALSO SAW THE EXPLOSION OF WHAT SEEMED TO BE THIS RED LIGHT ON THE TOWER AT UNITED AIR LINES. HE ALSO SAW THE EXPLOSION.
