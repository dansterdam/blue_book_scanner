MEMORANDUM 
W A C K- D a t e : 29 April 1958

FOR: DIR ETOR OF INTELLIGENCE
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
1. Reference is made to your request for information concerning reports of unidentified flying objects in the vicinity of OTTAWA, OHIO, on 6 April 1958.
2. Attached herewith is a copy of the Project Blue Book file on this incident. This file contains the original report and all information pertaining to the investigation conducted by the Air Force.
3. A summary of the incident is as follows:
At approximately 1950 hours on 6 April 1958, a local resident of OTTAWA, OHIO, observed an unidentified aerial object in the sky over his home. The witness stated that the object was round in shape, appeared to be metallic in color, and was illuminated.
The witness reported the sighting to local police, who in turn contacted the 4727th Air Defense Squadron, LOCKBOURNE AFB, OHIO. An Air Force investigator was dispatched to the scene and interviewed the witness. The investigator's report states that the witness appeared to be a reliable and credible individual.
A search of the area was conducted with negative results. No other witnesses were located. The Air Force was unable to identify the object observed.
4. Based on the information contained in the attached file, it is the opinion of the Project Blue Book staff that this incident remains UNIDENTIFIED.
