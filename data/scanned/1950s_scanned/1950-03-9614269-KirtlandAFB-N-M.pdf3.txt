AREA INDENTIFIED ON: Kirtland AFB, N.M.

SUMMARY: (Under instructions to report, give significance in brief and detail in succeeding paragraph. Put initials at lower left. See AF Pam)
Sighting of military AT Kirtland AFB as officer and three enlisted men observed an unusual aerial phenomenon. This was within the radar coverage of the base but was not detected on the radarscope. Phenomenon had a good definitive metallic appearance and was somewhat saucer-shaped. It was observed moving in a vertical as well as a horizontal manner at a high rate of speed. This was ruled out as a jet aircraft or other aerial target.
