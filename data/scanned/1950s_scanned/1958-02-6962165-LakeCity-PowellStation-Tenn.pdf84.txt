No. Case (Information Only)                  22 February 1954
Columbus, Ohio

Watched a 40-foot-long orange-colored object for a period of several minutes. The object had a series of 5 or 6 white lights arranged in a single line on its underside. It was flying at an altitude of about 2,000 feet.
