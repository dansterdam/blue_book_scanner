SUBJ: "Flying Saucer" (cont'd)
UNCLASSIFIED 14 July 1950

Flying saucer reported seen by: Dr. C.S. Tomkins, Chicago, Illinois, 1 July 1950.

"Flying from N to S at terrific rate of speed. Covered distance from N horizon to S horizon in 2 seconds." 
Description: Round, intensive, brilliant roundless. Not a meteor. Dr. claims he has seen meteors and knows what they look like. This object, like a ball of fire, the Dr. claims, was at a high altitude and traveled a horizontal course over West Chicago."

2. No further investigation is contemplated by this headquarters.
