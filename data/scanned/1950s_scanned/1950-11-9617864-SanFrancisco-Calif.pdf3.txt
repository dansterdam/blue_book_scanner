SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT
Observed directly overhead San Francisco, California at approximately 2320 hours, 3 November 1950.

TO: Director of Special Investigations, USAF,
Washington 25, D.C.

SUBJECT: Unusual object observed directly overhead San Francisco at 2320 hours, 3 November 1950.

DETAILS: On 3 November 1950 Mr. _____ was interviewed and stated that he in company with eight or ten other people observed a bright object in the sky directly overhead. It while flying towards the west from the east and disappeared after about 20 seconds. The object was observed for a period of twenty (20) to thirty (30) seconds and appeared to be of a silver dollar color, white, larger than an ordinary star. No sound or dynamic features were observed. It appeared to be suspended in the sky with no evidence of any drive mechanism. The object was stated to have been observed at approximately 20,000 feet. This was based upon comparison with a medium type jet aircraft in the area. The dimensions were approximated to be smaller than a medium type jet aircraft with no previous aeronautical experience.

3. ACTION: None.
