New Milford, Ohio
Dear Mrs. [REDACTED],

Your letter of 6 December 1954 has been received and read with interest by the Air Technical Intelligence Center.

From the description of the objects and their characteristics, no positive identification could be made. By this center, it cannot be stated with certainty that the objects observed were unidentifiable aerial phenomena. However, the report is retained in our files and is available for any further analysis of your objects.

Thank you for your interest.

Sincerely,
