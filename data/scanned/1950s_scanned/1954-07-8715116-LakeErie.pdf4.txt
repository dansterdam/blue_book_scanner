FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION

TITLE: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS (REPORT OF SIGHTINGS) CT> PAREN 
DATE OF ACCIDENT: 7/22/54 
LOCATION: LAKE ERIE, OHIO 
ON 7/22/54 AN INVESTIGATION WAS HELD REGARDING AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT WHICH WAS OBSERVED OVER LAKE ERIE ON ABOVE DATE. INVESTIGATOR TALKED TO NUMEROUS WITNESSES WH0 OBSERVED THE OBJECT ON THAT DATE. STATEMENT MADE BY WITNESSES INDICATE THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE CIRCULAR OR OVAL IN SHAPE AND SILVER IN COLOR. ITS MOVEMENT SEEMED ERRATIC AND RAPID, HOVERING AT TIMES AND THEN SPEEDING AWAY. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR A PERIOD OF FROM 30 MINUTES TO 1 HOUR. ALL WITNESSES STATED THEY DID NOT BELIEVE THE OBJECT TO BE A CONVENTIONAL TYPE AIRCRAFT. THE INVESTIGATION IS BEING CONTINUED.
