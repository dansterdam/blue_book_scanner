HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE 
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS (UFOS) 

TO: DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE 
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE 
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

1. REFERENCE IS MADE TO YOUR LETTER DATED 15 FEBRUARY 1957, SUBJECT AS ABOVE.

2. THE ATTACHED REPORT, PREPARED BY PROJECT BLUE BOOK, WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO, DETAILS INFORMATION CONCERNING THE SIGHTING OF AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER MACON, GEORGIA, ON 6 MAY 1957.

3. PERTINENT DETAILS ARE:
A. DATE AND TIME OF SIGHTING: 6 MAY 1957, 2210 HOURS EST
B. LOCATION: MACON, GEORGIA
C. DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT: ROUND, WHITE, LUMINOUS OBJECT APPROXIMATELY 1/2 THE SIZE OF THE FULL MOON, MOVING IN A STRAIGHT LINE FROM SOUTHWEST TO NORTHEAST AT MODERATE SPEED.
D. DURATION OF SIGHTING: 20 SECONDS

4. THIS INCIDENT HAS BEEN CLASSIFIED AS "UNIDENTIFIED" BY PROJECT BLUE BOOK.

5. THE SIGHTING WAS REPORTED BY TWO WITNESSES WHO WERE APPARENTLY CREDIBLE AND RELIABLE.

6. NO FURTHER INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE. IF ADDITIONAL DETAILS ARE REQUIRED, PLEASE ADVISE.

BY COMMAND OF THE CHIEF OF STAFF:

SIGNED: XXXXX XXXXX
BRIGADIER GENERAL, USAF 
DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE
