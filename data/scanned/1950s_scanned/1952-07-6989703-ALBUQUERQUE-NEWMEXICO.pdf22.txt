WORLD AERONAUTICAL CHART INDEX
To ensure that the airman has up-to-date information available, the U.S. Army Aeronautical Chart Service is charged with the responsibility of maintaining the Aeronautical Chart Index. Data received from various sources are continually being processed and the chart index is being printed on the face of the chart index below the state of the aeronautical information.
