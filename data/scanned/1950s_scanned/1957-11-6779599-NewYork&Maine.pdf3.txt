SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SEP 11 1957
LOCATION: Colebrook, N.H.
DATE-TIME GROUP: 6/1957 
WITNESSES: Various observers, names withheld
DESCRIPTION: A saucer-shaped object, approximately 50 feet in diameter, was observed in flight in a horizontal position at an altitude of approximately 6,000 feet. The object appeared to be silvery-white in color and was traveling at a speed estimated to be in excess of 500 mph. It was tracked by observers for a distance of approximately 50 miles.
INVESTIGATION: A thorough investigation was conducted by Special Agents of the United States Air Force. Statements were obtained from all available witnesses. The object was tracked on Air Force radar at three separate sites. No satisfactory explanation could be determined.
CONCLUSION: The object observed remains unidentified. The Air Force is continuing its investigation.
