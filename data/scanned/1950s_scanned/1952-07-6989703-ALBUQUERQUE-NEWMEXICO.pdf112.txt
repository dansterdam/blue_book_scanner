AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: FLYOBJECT
In accordance with APL 200-2, dated 29 April 1952, subject: "Unidentified Flying Objects (FLYOBJECT); the following report of an unidentified flying object is submitted:
On 29 July 1952 at approximately 2230 MST, an employee of an air conditioning company at Albuquerque, N.M., observed this object for two to three minutes. This object was observed at an elevation of approximately 20 to 25 degrees above the horizon. The object seemed to be a brilliant light, stationary at first, then it began to move rapidly from northeast to southwest of the city. It changed in color from white to yellowish-white, then to orange and then back to white. This changing color as the image itself. Before and after change and disappearance, the object seemed to vanish abruptly.
The reliability of the observer is considered excellent.
