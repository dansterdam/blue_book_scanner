Local Man Sees Mysterious Object In Nighttime Skies Over This Area
The man, a resident of Roanoke, reported seeing what he described as a bright object in the sky. He said the object appeared to be round, about the size of a plane, and that it moved in an erratic manner across the sky. The man said the object made no sound and was visible for about two minutes before disappearing from his sight.
