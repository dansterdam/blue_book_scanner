CORRECTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. The Aeronautical Chart Service is making every effort to furnish the user with accurate charts. The user will materially assist in this effort by making corrections as appropriate.
2. Mark corrections on face of chart or under "Remarks" below.
3. Fold chart along register fold lines, but with back out.
4. Paste an extra fold along stated line and staple at points as marked.
5. Refill in the return address and drop the chart in any U.S. mail box.
