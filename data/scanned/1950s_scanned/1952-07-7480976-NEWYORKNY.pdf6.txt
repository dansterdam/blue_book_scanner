JULY 7, 1952
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS
1. On the evening of 7 July 1952, the Federal Communications Commission, New York City, received a telephone call from a resident of New York City who reported observing several unidentified aerial objects over the city.
2. The informant stated that the objects were observed at approximately 9:00 p.m. on 7 July 1952, from a location near 96th Street and Central Park West, New York City. He described the objects as being disc-shaped, approximately 25 to 30 feet in diameter, and flying at an estimated altitude of 1,000 to 2,000 feet. The objects were observed to move at a high rate of speed, making abrupt changes in direction and altitude.
3. The informant further stated that the objects were observed for approximately 10 to 15 minutes, and that they appeared to be under intelligent control.
4. This information was immediately relayed to the Air Force.
