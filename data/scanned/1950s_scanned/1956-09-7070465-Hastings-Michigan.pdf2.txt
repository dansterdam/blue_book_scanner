RECORD 105 WAS MADE BY WITNESS AND STATES ON 9/7/56
SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT NEAR HASTINGS, MICHIGAN
REPORTED THAT OBJECT WAS TRIANGULAR IN SHAPE AND APPEARED TO BE OF METALLIC CONSTRUCTION.
OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROX. 3-5 MINUTES AS IT PASSED OVER HASTINGS AT ABOUT 10,000 FT. ALTITUDE.
WITNESS BECAME EXCITED AND RAN TO GET HIS CAMERA BUT OBJECT HAD PASSED OVER AND DISAPPEARED.
WITNESS STATED HE WAS SURE IT WAS NOT AN AIRCRAFT, SATELLITE OR METEOR AND DESCRIBED IT AS DEFINITE STRUCTURED CRAFT.
