ID-AEC-71-16
29 JUL 1952

UNCLASSIFIED

TO:

SUBJECT: ANALYSIS OF SIGHTINGS

THE CONTROL TOWER OPERATOR AT NAHANT, MASSACHUSETTS, ON 23 JUN 52 OBSERVED A STRANGE OBJECT IN THE SKY. IT WAS DESCRIBED AS APPEARING TO BE TRIANGULAR IN SHAPE, HAVING FIXED BLUE-WHITE LIGHTS ON EACH CORNER, AND MOVING IN A STRAIGHT LINE AT A HIGH SPEED.
THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR ABOUT 2 MINUTES AND WAS THEN LOST FROM SIGHT. IT PASSED WITHIN APPROXIMATELY 5 MILES OF NAHANT, MASSACHUSETTS, AT AN ALTITUDE OF 1,500 TO 2,000 FEET.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
DOD DIR 5200.10

UNCLASSIFIED
