SUBJECT: Unidentified Balloons

The report states that on 26 Feb 53, two unidentified balloons were observed at approximately 10,000 feet drifting in a northeasterly direction at a speed of about 30 knots. The balloons were white in color and appeared to be approximately 50 feet in diameter. Attempts to locate the point of origin and purpose of these balloons have been unsuccessful to date.

This headquarters has received a further message from the 520th ACWG which stated that three additional balloons were observed in the same area on 28 Feb 53. These balloons were similar in appearance to those observed on 26 Feb 53.
