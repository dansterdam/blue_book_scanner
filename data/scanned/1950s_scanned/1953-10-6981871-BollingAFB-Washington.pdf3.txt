AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT(S)
Date of Sighting: 26 September 1953
Time of Sighting: 1635 
Reporting Officer: S. Lt. E.F. Williamson
Location: Vicinity of Bolling AFB, Washington, D.C.
Observers: S/Lt. E.F. Williamson, Capt. P. Rockney, 2nd Lt. J.D. Jenkins

Observations: Investigators found a large black aircraft (approximately 60 feet in diameter) at the location described by the observers. The aircraft was hovering at an estimated altitude of 500 feet. It was silent and did not have any visible propulsion system. The object departed at high speed.
