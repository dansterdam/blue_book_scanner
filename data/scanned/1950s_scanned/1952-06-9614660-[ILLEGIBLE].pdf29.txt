AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
ORIGIN: 4704th Air Intelligence Squadron (Sp), Eglin AFB, FL
DATE OF INFORMATION: 23 July 1952
SUBJECT: Unidentified Aerial Object
AT 1120 HOURS, 24 JULY 1952, CIO OBSERVATION: A group of about 5 objects, described as disc-shaped, silvery or gray in color, were observed in the sky by a volunteer civilian observer. The objects were sighted for approximately 2 to 3 minutes. The objects were observed at an altitude of approximately 30,000 feet, traveling in a southwest to northeasterly direction at an estimated speed of 800 to 1000 mph. The objects were initially spotted by the observer while he was standing in his yard. The objects appeared to be travelling in a straight line and were investigated by an Aircraft Control and Warning Squadron. No further information is available.
APPROVED:
WILLIAM E. HOLNES
Colonel, USAF
