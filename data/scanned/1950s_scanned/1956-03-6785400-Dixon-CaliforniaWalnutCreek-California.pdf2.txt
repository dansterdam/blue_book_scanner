FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION
U. S. DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE 
INVESTIGATIVE REPORT
SUBJECT: FLYING OBJECTS
OFFICE: SAN FRANCISCO, CALIFORNIA
SYNOPSIS:
On or about March 6, 1956, Mr. Dixon, residing at 1660 Oak wood Avenue, Walnut Creek, California, reported to the local police and the Air Force that he had observed strange objects in the sky the previous evening at approximately 8:15 p.m. Mr. Dixon stated that the objects appeared to be circular in shape, emitting a reddish glow, and estimated the size of each object to be approximately 40 feet in diameter. The objects were observed for approximately 20 to 25 minutes and appeared to be following a westerly course.
DETAILS:
A preliminary check of the area by police and Air Force personnel failed to reveal any additional information or witnesses to substantiate Mr. Dixon's report. HEADING PORTION: CRIMINAL CIRCULAR MIMEOGRAPH TO CRIME CONVENTION.
