AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
Sighting of Unidentified Object
9 April 1952 
3-8 April 1952
Major Joseph W. Healey, USAF Dir of Intel
TDY SAF/OLLII

The object was reported by M. Chasseveers at Draughon-Miller Airport, Temple, Texas, on 6 April 1952 at approximately 2043 hours for a period of 10 to 45 minutes. The object appeared to be round, ball of fire giving off an extremely bright white light with a reddish outer edge. It traveled in a straight south to north course. Witnesses included 20 observers.

Two observers at Brant, Texas, were in an RB-36 at 35,000' altitude flying south when they observed the object at ground level. Weather conditions included clouds over 20,000'.
