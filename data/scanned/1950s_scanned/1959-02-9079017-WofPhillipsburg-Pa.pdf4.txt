OFFICIAL FILE COPY

SAFIS-3 (Major L. J. Tacker)

19 AUG 1959

1. In answer to a request by Dr. Olivier, the following information is submitted:

a. 20 April 1959, 2019 EST - Two aircraft at 9000 feet altitude. Observed through 7x50 binoculars for approximately 45 minutes descending to 4000 feet over Thomasville, Georgia.
b. It appeared to be two round or elliptical objects joined, the color was intense reddishorange or pink. Then took dark co-equal to clouds, seemed to contain a surface of light points on it that appeared to glow just disappeared. Objects in view for four seconds.
c. 15 July 1959, 0103 EST - As 25 July 1959, 23 degrees 25'N, 71 degrees 25'W, an aircraft at 9000 feet altitude sighted the following: an object was observed for approximately 45 seconds travelling north to south at a fast rate of speed 800-900 knots, twice the speed of a jet fighter. The object was oblong, metallic-looking and brilliant white in color. It made a slight change in direction from vertical to horizontal and then back to vertical before disappearing.
d. 24 December 1958, Smithtown, N.Y. 0015 EST - A brilliant light, yellowish-white, slowly larger than a quarter with a dark red tail seven to eight feet long was observed from beginning to disappearance in approximately 10 seconds.
e. 14 December 1959, Town River, Harrisburg, Wrightstown, McGuire A.F.B. - Observed and sighted as a round object, size of a large orange, golden color, traveled from horizon to horizon in approximately five seconds.

CONCLUSION: This covers the information requested.
