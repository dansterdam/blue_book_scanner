SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
ST. LOUIS MISSOURI: 17 September 1957

TO: Commander
AIR DEFENSE COMMAND
ENT AIR FORCE BASE
Colorado Springs, Colorado

DETAILS: On 16 September 1957, Special Agent CHARLES F. GRUTEFELD, St. Louis, Missouri received a telephone call from STEPHEN MALANAPHY who stated he sighted an unidentified flying object at approximately 16:00 hours on 17 September 1957.

In accordance with the provisions of paragraph 7, AFR 200-2, the following report is submitted:

(1) Description of the object:
(a) Disc-shaped
(b) Approximately 500 yards in diameter
(c) Color was a metallic gray
(d) The object appeared to be of a metallic construction, negative except that the object did not have the conventional aircraft design.
(e) Speed of the object was estimated to be that of a 100-200 mph conventional jet aircraft.
(f) The object was in sight for approximately 20-25 seconds.

(2) Description of the course of object:
(a) Sound first called observer's attention to object and was described as a whistling or humming sound.
(b) The object came into view from the Northeast and proceeded in a Southerly direction.
(c) Twenty (20) degrees from the horizon the object took on a somewhat erratic motion, altering its course in a manner which would be very difficult for a conventional aircraft, and a slight angle toward the south and disappeared.
