SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

An unidentified flying object was reported observed at Marrakech, French Morocco on 25 August 1952 at approximately 15:40 hours. The object was described as circular in shape, between 5 to 10 meters in diameter, and gray in color. It was observed for approximately 2 to 3 minutes.

The object was first observed at an estimated altitude of 4,000 meters. It then descended to an estimated altitude of 1,000 meters, and then climbed back to 4,000 meters. No noise or exhaust was observed. The object maintained a stable position, and then accelerated and disappeared from sight at high speed.

Observer: Major Ralph A. Jacobsack, AC 72235.3. The observer was a captain, military observer, and a graduate of the Air Force Radar Technicians School.

This report was compiled from information furnished by 1st Lt. William C. Dansker, who was the observer. The data was verified by Major Ralph A. Jacobsack.
