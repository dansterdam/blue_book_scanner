AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

HARROLD, TEXAS, at approximately 2040, 23 FEB 53, observed visual contact with an unidentified aerial object while on an air patrol flight. The object was described as being white in color, pointed in the front, flat on the bottom, and about 40 to 50 feet in diameter. It was moving at a very high rate of speed and had a tail of blue-white flame. The object disappeared rapidly to the south. No unusual sounds or odors were reported.
