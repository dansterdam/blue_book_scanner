AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

REPORT NO. 53-28 INCIDENT OP, SHAW AFB AT THE SOUTH END OF RUNWAY OC.

1. On 2 Jul 53, at 2225, the pilot of an F-86 had a total of 1360 hours and a considerable amount of night flying experience. No description of the reported object is contained in the report.

2. There was a thin broken overcast condition at approximately 5,000 feet and a broken stratus layer at 10,000 feet. Surface winds were light and variable.

3. There were no known meteorological conditions to cause such a sighting.

4. There was no physical evidence on the sighting.

5. There was no interception action taken.

10. There were only two aircraft flying in the local area and both had radio contact with the control tower. The aircraft involved had no visible or electronic contact with the reported object.
