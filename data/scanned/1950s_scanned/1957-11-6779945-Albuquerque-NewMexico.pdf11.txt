On UFO Spotted in Casino

MARDON - C.D. DEFENSE OF U.S. AIR FORCE (USAF) MAJOR TOLD HIM THAT AN UFO WAS SPOTTED OVER A CASINO IN CASABLANCA, MOROCCO ON NIGHT OF NOVEMBER 6, 1957. NO OTHER DETAILS GIVEN. UFOS WERE OBSERVED WHICH VARIED IN COLOR AND NUMBER.

THE INCIDENT WAS REPORTED BY SHERIFF LEWIS MAYBERRY LAST MONTH TO THE AIR FORCE, AND DESCRIBED BY OTHER EYEWITNESSES. IT WAS STATED THAT THE OBJECT DIRECTLY IN FRONT OF THE DRIVER OF THE CAR HOVERED FOR SEVERAL MINUTES BEFORE SPEEDING AWAY. FURTHER INTERVIEWS WITH THE WITNESSES HAVE BEEN ARRANGED.
