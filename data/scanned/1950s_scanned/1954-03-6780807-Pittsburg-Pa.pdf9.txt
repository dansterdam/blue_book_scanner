SOUTH PARK MILITARY RESERVATION
PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

SUBJECT: Visual and Radar Sighting of an Unidentified Flying Object.

TO: Commanding Officer
509TH AAA BATTALION (GUN) (9003)
SOUTH PARK MILITARY RESERVATION
PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

1. At approximately 2300 hours, 15 January 1954, the following incident occurred:
a. It was observed by the personnel of the emplacement that a bright light, which appeared to be an aircraft, was sighted in the southern sky traveling in a northerly direction.
b. After the object had been observed for approximately 2 to 3 minutes, it appeared to stop and hover at an estimated altitude of 10,000 to 15,000 feet.
c. While the object was in a hovering position, a second similar object was observed approaching the first object from the northeast. The second object also appeared to stop and hover adjacent to the first object.
d. After the two objects had remained in a hovering position for approximately 5 to 7 minutes, they suddenly accelerated and streaked off to the northwest at tremendous speed, disappearing from sight in a matter of seconds.
e. The visual sighting was corroborated by radar contact at the same time and location. The radar plot indicated that the objects were traveling at an estimated speed of 1,000 to 1,500 miles per hour.
f. The duration of the entire incident was approximately 10 minutes.

2. This report is submitted for your information and appropriate action.
