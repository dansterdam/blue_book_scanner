HEADQUARTERS
3650TH MILITARY TRAINING WING
SAMPSON AIR FORCE BASE
Geneva, New York

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects Letters

TO: Chiefs, Air Technical Intelligence Center
ATTN: ATIAA-2a
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio

1. Three unrelated letters of widely different dates have been received by this headquarters as a result of publicity in national media.

2. None of the observations give the impression of significant accuracy, and some of them received in this time to make an interpretation.

3. Each letter was acknowledged briefly upon receipt answering questions which were asked.

4. Copies of the letters, in the form received, as well as adaptations on AF Form 112 are forwarded in compliance with APR 200-5, 29 April 1952.

FOR THE COMMANDING OFFICER:

H. O. DAMON
Major, USAF
Asst Adjutant

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVAL
