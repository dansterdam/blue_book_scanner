SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT REPORT
LOCATION: HAMLET, INDIANA
DATE: 8 SEPTEMBER 1954

On 8 September 1954, the undersigned received a report of the sighting of an unidentified aerial object near Hamlet, Indiana. According to the informant, the object was observed at approximately 1930 hours on 7 September 1954 traveling in a northeasterly direction at a high rate of speed. The object was described as circular in shape, approximately 50 feet in diameter, and emitting a bright bluish-white glow. 

At approximately 2000 hours on 7 September 1954, the object was reported to have crashed in a field approximately 5 miles northeast of Hamlet. Upon investigation, the object was found to be the remains of a heavily damaged aircraft. No survivors were located at the scene.

The aircraft wreckage has been secured and is currently under examination by technical personnel. A full report on the findings will be submitted upon completion of the investigation.
