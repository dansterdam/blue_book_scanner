23 JULY 1956
PROVO, UTAH

Case 193, Provo, Utah, July 28, 1956 - We quote from the Reports of Director of Intelligence: "Reports of a fiery light and a blastoff at the crest of Mt. Timpanogos. Fireworks caused the light and blast. Search craft followed flight to mountain's crest, but found no traces of any objects." They said the fire lit the sky at the top of the mountain. One said it appeared to be a missile which crashed into the mountain. The search found no traces of the flaming object.
