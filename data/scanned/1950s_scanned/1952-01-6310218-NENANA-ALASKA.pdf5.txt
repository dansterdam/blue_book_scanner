NENANA, ALASKA
6 January 1952 
Subject: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
To: Commander
Air Force Intelligence Center
United States Air Force
Washington 25, D.C.

1. The following information is submitted concerning an Unidentified Flying Object observed in the Nenana, Alaska, area on 6 January 1952.

2. At approximately 2105 hours on 6 January 1952, an unidentified object was observed approximately 10 miles west of Nenana, Alaska, traveling in a northerly direction. The object was estimated to be approximately 30 feet in diameter. It was observed to be traveling at an estimated speed of 1500 to 2000 miles per hour. The object was observed for approximately 30 to 60 seconds before disappearing from view.

3. The observer reported the object to be intensely brilliant, and pulsating. No sound was heard. The night was clear and the visibility was excellent at the time of the observation. 

4. This report is forwarded for your information and appropriate action.
