DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

In reply refer to: SD 27-21 7 April 1952

SUBJECT: Photograph of an Unidentified Aerial Object taken by Mr. Donovan A. Harvey, Riverside, California.

TO: Commanding General
Air Material Command 
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Dayton, Ohio
ATTN: ACIE

1. Attached hereto for your information are two copies of the report of investigation by Special Agent 2nd Lt. D. Eckert, Riverside, California District, dated 29 March 1952, File 5J-15, subject as above.

2. This investigation was conducted by this Headquarters under the direction of the Air Technical Intelligence Command, and includes pertinent details obtained from the witness concerning the sighting of an unidentified aerial object reported in a photograph.

3. The photograph was originally processed by the Air Technical Intelligence Center (ATIC) and returned to the witness, Mr. Harvey. He reported the object in the photograph as a luminous saucer-shaped object.

4. No further investigative action is contemplated and the file on this case is considered closed.

5. Although it is invited to paragraph 7, AEC 50-1, this letter is unclassified.

1 Incl
Lt Colonel, USAF
District Commander

UNCLASSIFIED
