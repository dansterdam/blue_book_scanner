RC CASE (INFORMATION ONLY)
SOURCE: DIR "World of Flying Saucers"

31 October 1955
Florida (Gainsville)

A flight of bombers refueling in mid-air at night can be a startling sight, more than once has been reported as a gathering of flying saucers. Such an incident occurred in Florida on October 31, 1955, when a disk jockey at Gainesville broke into his radio program about ten o'clock in the evening to announce that flying saucers had been sighted. Hundreds of persons hurried out of their homes to look at the Halloween visitors, clearly visible in the night sky. One reporter stated that he had seen four to six objects, where in glue, oblong in shape, traveling somewhat in a straight-line formation that later changed to a v [sic]. Both the radio station and the police station were swamped with telephone calls, most of whom were the people who had seen the objects and could not explain what they had witnessed. A flight of bombers had been refueling at an altitude of some feet, and the result was a startling sight to those unaccustomed to such a display.
