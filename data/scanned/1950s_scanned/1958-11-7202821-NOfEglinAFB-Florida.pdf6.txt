SUPPLEMENT TO AF FORM 112
To: AIR PROVING GROUND CENTER (AMC)
Eglin Air Force Base, Florida 
11-1-53
Observing Agency: A. F. 
Date: 9/18/58   Time: 2215 EST
Nature of Observation: A bright light was observed moving across the sky in a northeast direction from the observer's position on the Eglin Air Force Base. The light moved at a rapid rate of speed and left no trail. The light appeared to be disc shaped and was estimated to be about the size of a dime held at arm's length. The light was observed for approximately 10 seconds and then disappeared.
