AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
REPORT NO. IN-15-C-55K

2. Sighting of unidentified aerial object in the vicinity of Itazuke AFB, Japan on 7 January 1956.

3. At approximately 1910 hrs on 7 January 1956, the CAA controller at Itazuke AFB observed an unidentified object in the sky to the south of the air base. The object was at an altitude estimated to be about 15,000 feet. The weather was clear at the time.

The object was observed to move in a northerly direction at a high rate of speed, estimated to be in excess of 600 knots. The object appeared to be elliptical in shape and was emitting a bright light. After traveling a distance of approximately 10-15 miles, the object suddenly disappeared from view.

Personnel from the control tower at Itazuke AFB also observed the object and its movements. No other aircraft were known to be in the vicinity at the time.

The weather was clear, visibility was unlimited, and the moon was full at the time of the sighting.

4. Commanding General: CONFIDENTIAL.
