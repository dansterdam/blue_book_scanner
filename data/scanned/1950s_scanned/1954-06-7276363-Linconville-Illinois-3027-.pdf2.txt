REPORTED SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT NEAR LINCONVILLE, ILLINOIS, JUNE 7, 1954

On June 7, 1954, two observers reported seeing an unusual aerial phenomenon near Linconville, Illinois. According to their account, at approximately 8:30 PM, they observed a bright, glowing, oval-shaped object moving slowly across the sky. The object appeared to be approximately 25-30 feet in diameter and was surrounded by a halo of light. The object was observed for several minutes before it accelerated and disappeared from view.

The report was investigated by the United States Air Force as part of Project Blue Book, a program established to investigate reports of unidentified flying objects. The investigation concluded that the observed object could not be identified and was classified as an "unidentified" aerial phenomenon.
