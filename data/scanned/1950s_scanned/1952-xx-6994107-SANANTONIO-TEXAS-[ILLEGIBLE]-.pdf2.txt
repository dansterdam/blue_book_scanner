FLIGHT REPORT
South Texas  
30 July 1952  
Maj Marvin P. Thomason, Hq Intel Officer
Teletype: this headquarters, CIG 1273 S AIRCRAFT and AFT 200-5

1. References teletype this headquarters, 281230 July 1952, number 010 1273 S AIRCRAFT and AFT 200-5, dated 29 April 1952. A report is submitted on the attached incidents of an unidentified aerial object.

2. The incident was highly interesting and was seen by the control tower, tower officers, and observers. This object was approximately 30 feet in diameter, cigar-shaped, bright silver in color, and traveling at an extremely high rate of speed. This object was also seen by the crew of the fighter interceptor in the local SC and is attested. The observer of the ground control operator in the local RC and is attested. The incident occurred at approximately 8:10 pm on 28 July 1952.

3. This is a preliminary report. A more detailed documented report will be forwarded to this headquarters in the near future. 

APPROVED: 
Martin F. Tracey
Major, Hq Intelligence Officer

Through channels in accordance with AFR 200-2, dated 29 April 1952.
