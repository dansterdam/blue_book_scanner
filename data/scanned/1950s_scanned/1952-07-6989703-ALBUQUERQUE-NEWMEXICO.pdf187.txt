AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
Sighting of Unconventional Aircraft
Miami, Florida
30 July 1952
OBSERVER: William J. Praiser, "SAC"
Home: Miami, Florida
SUMMARY: (Under certain censorship of report. Omit significant data was contained in this paragraph. Last informant is listed as Lt. (Sig. )J.L. Driscoll)
On the nights of 24 thru 28 July 1952 unconventional objects were observed in the Miami area. The objects were described as flying at high speed and making abrupt changes in direction. They were reported to the Miami Filter Center. Detailed information and data on balloon flights, weather conditions, etc. were obtained, and which compiled into a report. This report serves the Chief of Naval Operations which could be associated with the Internal Distribution (See incl. /1/.)

FOR WILLIAM J. PRAISER, JR.
MAJOR, USAF
WING INTELLIGENCE OFFICER
