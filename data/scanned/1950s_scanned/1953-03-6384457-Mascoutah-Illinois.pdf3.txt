Object sighted like a dirigible flying towards Ft. Payne and St. Louis. Object was first seen at 5:20 p.m. CST on 3-8-53 by observers in Mascoutah, Illinois. The observers were civilian watch repairer and his wife. The object was moving at a high rate of speed and disappeared towards the northeast. It was seen for about 30-35 seconds.

1. Type or sighting: Dirigible-like object.
2. Time of sighting: 5:20 p.m. CST on 3-8-53.
3. Location of observer: Mascoutah, Illinois.
4. Location of object: First seen to the southwest moving towards Ft. Payne and St. Louis. Disappeared towards the northeast.
5. Description of object: Dirigible-like in appearance, high rate of speed, about 30-35 seconds in duration of sighting.
6. No physical evidence.
7. No interception or identification action taken.
