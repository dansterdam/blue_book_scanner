Air Intelligence Information Report
Report on Unidentified Flying Objects in the Storm, New York State
Date of Report: 2 Mar 53
Date of Information: 11 Feb 53 to 1 Mar 53
Evaluation: C-2 C-3
Prepared By: Robert V. Kurtz, 1st Lt., USAF
Subject is: Ground Observer Corps

SUMMARY: There were 8 sightings of unidentified flying objects reported to the Filter Center Station, Lackland Air Force Base, Buffalo, New York during the period 11 Feb 53 to 1 Mar 53. 
SOURCE of 7 reports was CGC posts in Olean, Cattaraugus County, New York (W2C205). Sightings were made in all but one case by qualified ground observers.
Source of one report was carried out by unqualified observer. Sighting was made approximately 2:25 p.m.
