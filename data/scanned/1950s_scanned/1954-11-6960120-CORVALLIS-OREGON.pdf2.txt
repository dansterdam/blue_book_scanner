SUBJECT: Report of Sighting of Unidentified Aerial Objects 

1. On 6 November 1954, at approximately 1720 hours, two unidentified aerial objects were sighted in the vicinity of Corvallis, Oregon. 

2. The objects were described as circular in shape and approximately 40 to 50 feet in diameter. They were observed to be flying at a high altitude and at a high rate of speed. The objects were silver in color and appeared to be reflecting sunlight. 

3. The objects were sighted by several persons in the Corvallis area and were observed for a period of approximately 2 to 3 minutes before disappearing from sight. 

4. No sound was heard from the objects and no evasive action was observed. The objects appeared to be traveling in a straight line and at a constant speed. 

5. The weather conditions at the time of the sighting were clear, with unlimited visibility.
