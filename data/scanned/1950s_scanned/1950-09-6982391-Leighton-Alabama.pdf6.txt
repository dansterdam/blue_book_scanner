REPORT OF INVESTIGATION 
OF UFO SIGHTING NEAR LEIGHTON, ALABAMA, ON 6 SEPTEMBER 1950

1. On 6 September 1950, approximately 1930 hours, the writer was notified of a UFO sighting in the Leighton, Alabama, area. Immediately, the writer, accompanied by two military policemen, proceeded to the location.

2. Upon arrival at the scene, it was learned that three civilian witnesses had observed a bright, luminous object in the sky at approximately 1900 hours. The object was described as circular in shape, approximately 30 feet in diameter, and emitting a bluish-white light. The object was observed to ascend rapidly and disappear from sight after several minutes.

3. The three civilian witnesses were interviewed separately. Their descriptions of the object and the events were essentially the same. All three stated that the object appeared to descend slowly at first, then accelerated rapidly and vertically disappeared from sight. No sound was reported associated with the object.

4. A search of the area was conducted, but no physical evidence or remnants of the object were found. Interviews with local residents revealed no additional witnesses to the incident.

5. This report is submitted for your information and any further action deemed appropriate.
