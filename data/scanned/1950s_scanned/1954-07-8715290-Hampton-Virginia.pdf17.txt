"In reply refer to
AFOIN AFTAIS 10073

Mountain View, Missouri 
Dear Mr.

Thank you for your letter of 2 October 1954, together with photo negative, reporting the sighting of an unidentified flying object.

To date, definite identification of your sighting has not been made; however, the information and photograph are being analyzed. The negative will be returned to you as soon as prints have been obtained.

Attached for your convenience is the "U.S. Air Force Summary of Events and Information Concerning the Unidentified Flying Object Program," which gives the official information established by the Air Force in this field. This summary is the means we use to keep the public informed.

You may be assured that your interest in this program is greatly appreciated.

Sincerely,
R. C. SCHNI
CAPT, USAF
