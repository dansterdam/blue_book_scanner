DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY
OFFICE OF INFORMATION
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

The Secretary of Defense has requested me to thank you for your letter of April 10, 1958, concerning a report of unidentified flying objects observed over Barbados, West Indies on April 6, 1958.

Your thoughtfulness in forwarding this information is greatly appreciated for the Department of Defense.

I am forwarding your letter and enclosures to the Office of Naval Intelligence for their evaluation and study.

Your interest in this matter is greatly appreciated.

Sincerely yours,

ERNEST C. SHERMAN
Commander, U.S. Navy
Public Information Branch
