SUBJECT: SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS NENANA, ALASKA 6 JANUARY 1952
SPECIAL INFORMATION COPY

DECKER, Lyle E. 1900 Lacey St, Fairbanks, Alaska
