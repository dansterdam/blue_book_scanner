SUBJECT: CHICAGO, ILLINOIS: OBJECT SEEN BY 28-31 PERSONS REPORTED TO LT. SANNER, WGN-TV 2 MAY 1955.

1. ON 2 MAY 1955, LT. SANNER, WGN-TV, CHICAGO, ILLINOIS, REPORTED THAT 28-31 PERSONS OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT IN THE SKY OVER CHICAGO ABOUT 2000 HOURS ON 1 MAY 1955.
