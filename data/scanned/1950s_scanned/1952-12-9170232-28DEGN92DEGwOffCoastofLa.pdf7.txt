WORLD AERONAUTICAL CHART INDEX
Scale of Series 1:1,000,000

CIVIL CHART SUPPLY
Authorized agents for the sale of aeronautical charts have been appointed at certain airports throughout the United States for the distribution of aeronautical charts. U.S. Air Force and U.S. Navy activities order these charts from the Director, U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey.

MILITARY CHART SUPPLY
1. Aeronautical charts, maps, and related material distributed by Aeronautical Chart Service are listed in current edition of the "Catalog of Aeronautical Charts and Related Publications," which is distributed in accordance to U.S. Air Force and U.S. Navy directives.
2. Obtain:
a. Within Continental United States: Aeronautical charts from the Aeronautical Chart Service designated for the theater of operations and distributed to supply facilities.
b. Outside Continental United States: From the appropriate U.S. Aeronautical Chart Depot or Aeronautical Chart Service as directed by the U.S. Air Force or U.S. Navy.
3. Within Continental United States: Requisitions from all activities within Continental United States are submitted to the appropriate Aeronautical Chart Supply System depots in accordance with U.S. Air Force and U.S. Navy directives.
