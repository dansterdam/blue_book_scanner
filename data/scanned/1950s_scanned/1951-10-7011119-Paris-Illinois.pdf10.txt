2D 1-11-103. SUBJ: UNKNOWN SUBJECT. Unidentified Aerial Object. Incident occurred in Paris, Illinois, 7 October 1951. 
The object was described as large, vague, and luminous, traveling in a slow, curious course. No vapor trails, sound, or no projectile characteristics. No visual size or other estimations were stated. The objects was observed for about 15 minutes, and several local citizens reported witnessing the maneuvers of the object. While maneuvering, the object would accelerate very rapidly, then stop, float, or hover, then accelerate again. The color was described as bright, and the size would change. The object traveled over this area and some other locales and then disappeared.  According to witnesses, this object appeared quite close to the ground at times, and it was estimated to be larger than a private plane. It was reported as not being like any aircraft known.
