UNCLASSIFIED
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

22 JULY 1952

SUBJECT: Unidentified aerial objects
TO: Commanding General
Air Technical Intelligence Center
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Ohio

1. Attached hereto for your information and any action deemed appropriate are the reports of unidentified aerial objects observed on 22 July 1952, subject as above. 

2. In an investigation, it was ascertained that the unidentified objects reported were not a star or planet.

3. In further investigation, it was stated that the film of the objects of the aerial investigations was considered closed in mistake.

4. Attention is invited to T. 2371-52, "Dan Armstrong et al", investigator's comments, wherein specific mention is made to the physical and scientific aspects of the unidentified aerial objects.

JOHN D. COLEMAN
Colonel, USAF
Director of Intelligence
