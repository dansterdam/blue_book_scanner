24 DISTRICT OSI INTELLIGENCE REPORT Subject: Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object Philadelphia. Pennsylvania, 17 April 1955

Time 18*. OFFICIAL DATE: 17 April 1955

SUMMARY: At approximately 1830 hours, 17 April 1955. observers in the Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania, area sighted an unidentified flying object for approximately 10-12 minutes. The object was described as circular in shape, silver in color, and emitting a bright fluorescent glow. No sound was heard. The object remained stationary for the duration of the sighting.
