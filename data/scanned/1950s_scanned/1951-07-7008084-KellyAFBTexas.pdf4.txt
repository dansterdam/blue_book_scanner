Ltr 120 31-27, 10th CIC DIS-SU (IO), Kelly AFB, Texas, 3 August 1951.

Subject: Sighting of Unidentified Aerial Object.

As for the above sighted object, it was observed in the sky, hovering approximately 300 yards distant from the observer, at 2205 hours and observed for 5 minutes.

a. Observation was made visually with aid of field glasses.

b. MINOR observed the object from the Control Tower at 2 miles distance, weather was good, visibility approximately 15 miles. Object had no outline or definite shape, however, it was observed as a round luminous mass approximately 40 feet in diameter, and visible for approximately 5 minutes.
