CASE NO. 6287
COBURN, VIRGINIA
4 October 59

1. AT APPROXIMATELY 1900 HOURS ON 4 OCTOBER 1959, MRS. VIRGINIA COBURN , LOCATED AT 112 MAGNOLIA AVE., COVINGTON, VA., OBSERVED A STRANGE LUMINOUS OBJECT IN THE SKY WHILE DRIVING HOME FROM WORK IN COVINGTON, VA.

2. MRS. COBURN STATED THAT AS SHE WAS DRIVING HOME SHE OBSERVED THE OBJECT IN THE SKY OVER COVINGTON, VA. IT WAS DESCRIBED AS BEING ROUND IN SHAPE AND LUMINOUS WHITE IN COLOR. SHE STATED THAT IT MOVED WITH A RAPID BUT JERKY MOTION AND CHANGED DIRECTION ABRUPTLY.

3. MRS. COBURN STATED THAT SHE STOPPED HER CAR AND OBSERVED THE OBJECT FOR APPROXIMATELY 5 MINUTES. WHEN SHE ARRIVED HOME SHE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED THE SIGHTING TO HER SON WHO ALSO OBSERVED THE OBJECT.

4. MRS. COBURN STATED THAT SHE DID NOT HAVE A CAMERA AVAILABLE AT THE TIME AND WAS UNABLE TO PHOTOGRAPH THE OBJECT.

5. THIS REPORT IS BEING FILED FOR INFORMATION PURPOSES ONLY. NO FURTHER ACTION IS CONTEMPLATED AT THIS TIME.
