AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
REPORTING AGENCY: 29TH AIR DIVISION
LOCATION: Rapid City, S. Dak.
DATE OF INFORMATION: 2 March 1957
PLOT ACFT SQ.: ELLSWORTH AFB, S. Dak.
PILOT:Capt. Thomas J. O'Hara
DATE/TIME GRP: 2 March 1957
A/C TAIL NO.: not provided

SUMMARY: Witnessed some strange lights in the sky. The lights were from the lights but appeared to be changing in color (white, blue and red). There was a halo of light around the object and it appeared to be rotating. They were either changing in size or brightness. The object was observed for several minutes, then disappeared.

What next? They were traveling at a rapid rate and as they changed color they appeared to be rotating. The object has disappeared.
