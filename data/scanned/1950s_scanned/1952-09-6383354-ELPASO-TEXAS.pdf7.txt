AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
IR-147-52
GOC, 4TH WEATHER SQUADRON (FTW) , Ellingtoh AFB, Texas
SUBJECT : UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
1. The following report of a sighting of unidentified flying objects was received from a private citizen consulting a GOC post:
a. Source reported on August 6, 1952, a sighting of 5 circular or disc-shaped objects observed at about 8:15 p.m., central daylight saving time, over El Paso, Texas. Observed for about 15 minutes. Objects were described as being round in shape, approximately 30 to 50 feet in diameter, flying at an altitude of about 10,000 to 15,000 feet, and traveling from west to east at a speed estimated to be 600 to 800 mph.
b. No further information on the behavior, maneuvers, or description of the objects was reported.
c. This report has been evaluated and it was determined that the objects observed could have been conventional aircraft, but further investigation is being conducted to determine the exact nature of the sighting.
