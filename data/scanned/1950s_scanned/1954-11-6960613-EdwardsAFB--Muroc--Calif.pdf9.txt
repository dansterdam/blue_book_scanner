GENERAL D-3 LIST
SUBJECT: DATA ON UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT ALLEGED TO HAVE BEEN SIGHTED AT EDWARDS AIR FORCE BASE, CALIF. ON 6 NOVEMBER 1954
1. On 6 November 1954 between 1000 and 1030 hours, LIEUTENANT EDWARD A. DALY, U.S. MARINE CORPS RESERVE, attached to EDWARDS AIR FORCE BASE, CALIFORNIA, observed an unidentified flying object while on routine flight duty at the base.
2. LIEUTENANT DALY reported that the object was sighted in the southeast sky, traveling in a straight line at a high rate of speed, and disappearing in the southwest sky. He estimated the object's speed at approximately 500 to 700 miles per hour. The object appeared to be round or oval in shape, and had no visible means of propulsion. No contrails were visible.
3. This report was made a matter of official record with proper military authorities on the base.
