UNITED STATES AIR FORCE

Dear Mr. ********,

This is in reply to a very brief report concerning some unidentified object in this area. A copy of the report is attached.

It would be appreciated if you can arrange to have some photographs taken of the item, a sooner detailed report, and forward to this office. Information as to the location and the circumstances under which the object was sighted will be helpful.

Identification is often made through study of photographs. In the interest of government economy and morale, it is requested that investigative personnel on orders to interrogate the subject make this trip.

This action is requested in accordance with paragraph 4c, AFR 200-2, dated 5 February 1953, copy attached. Your assistance in this matter would be appreciated.

Sincerely,

H. K. GILBERT
Colonel, USAF
Deputy for Science
and Components
