SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER THE WHITE ROCK LAKE, DALLAS, TEXAS

1. First report of UFOR was received by 1st Lt. William Applinghaum, Director on duty at the Duncanville, Texas Filter Center. It left no trail and emitted no sound. The object was under observation for about 20 to 30 seconds. The incident was passed to the Control Center at Duncanville. No further information was obtained and the object was not brought under control.

2. This report comes from civilian personnel. No other reports have been received to date.
