CONFIDENTIAL
AUGUST 28, 1954

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS SIGHTINGS

On 25 August 1954, this office was advised that a USAF radar unit located at Willow Grove, PA, had tracked a number of unidentified airborne objects on the night of 23 August 1954. The objects were stated to have been in excess of 200-4 mph, and one object was stated to have been tracked descending to the surface near Philadelphia, PA.

(a) The object was slightly longer that 20 ft.
(b) The object was oval in shape.
(c) The object was shiny and metallic in appearance.
(d) The object was not burning and did not produce any smoke or exhaust.
(e) The object was not illuminated.
(f) The object moved at a very high rate of speed.
(g) The object exhibited a very high rate of acceleration.
(h) The object was tracked by radar for a short period of time.
