AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: U.S.S.A.D.

THAT ON 11 JUNE 1952, ROBERT S. ALVAR REPORTED TO THE UNDERSIGNED THAT ON 9 JUNE 1952 AT 2150 HOURS, HE SAW AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT PASS OVER DETROIT, MICHIGAN. ALVAR REPORTED THAT THE OBJECT WAS DISC-SHAPED, APPEARED TO BE FALLING THEN LEVELED OFF AND DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT.
