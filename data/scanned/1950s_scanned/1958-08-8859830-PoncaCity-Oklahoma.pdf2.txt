Desk #26, 4715th Ground Observer Squadron, Joplin Air Defense Filter Center (47515), Undentified Aerial Object, 1958
1. Position, title, and comments of the reporting officer:
Paul M. Dudley, Deputy Director, United States Air Force Joplin Air Defense Filter Center, Joplin, Missouri. This officer observed the physical phenomena described and considered the performance characteristics as given.
2. Physical characteristics observed: The physical characteristics of the object are described as follows: The object appeared as a single, brilliant, intensely luminous object, glowing in a whitish-orange color, and estimating it to be approximately 10-15 feet in diameter. The object was observed to be moving at a very high rate of speed, traveling in a straight line, and appearing to be completely silent. The general direction of travel was from west to east. The object was observed for approximately 30 seconds.
3. Evidence of physical evidence, such as materials and photographs: None.
[Signed] J. E. SUTTON Captain, USAF Commander
