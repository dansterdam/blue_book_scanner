AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
FROM: DEPUTY CHIEF OF AIR STAFF, INTELLIGENCE
TO: 22 - 1
FILE: 1 OF 1

UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS SIGHTED BY MRS. 
OF SAN ANTONIO, TEXAS ON 27 MAY 1952 FROM 2000 TO 2045 HOURS.

MRS.  STATEMENT:
ON 27 MAY 1952, AT 2020 HOURS, I OBSERVED EIGHT BRIGHT LIGHTS IN THE SKY. THEY APPEARED TO BE MOVING VERY RAPIDLY IN A NORTH-SOUTH DIRECTION. FOR APPROXIMATELY 25 MINUTES THEY WERE VISIBLE, THEN DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. THEY DID NOT SEEM TO CHANGE POSITION IN RELATION TO ONE ANOTHER. THERE WERE NO DISCERNIBLE WINGS OR TAILS. THE LIGHTS WERE ABOUT THE SIZE OF A "50 CENT" PIECE WHEN VIEWED IN THE SKY. THEY WERE EVENLY SPACED AND MOVING IN A STRAIGHT LINE, FIRST FROM SOUTH TO NORTH AND ONE LATER ON THE WEST SIDE WHICH WENT IN THE SAME DIRECTION, NORTH AND ONE LATER ON THE WEST SIDE WHICH WENT IN THE SAME DIRECTION, NORTH AND WEST.

SAN ANTONIO, TEXAS

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
