SUBJECT : Flying Object Report - Korea
EVALUATION OF INFORMATION : Essentially reliable
On 11 August 1954 at 1225 hrs, a flying object was observed over Suwon, Korea. It was described as a round, glowing object, about twice the size of a star, which maneuvered in the sky for several minutes and then disappeared from view. The object was observed by a Chaplain and three enlisted personnel at the 8055th Air Base Squadron. The object was observed through binoculars and appeared to be about 1,000 yards distant. It made no sound, left no trail, and was not positively identified. ...
