AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
TR-95-52
16 MAY 1952
Subject: Balloon sightings, 16 May 1952
1. Balloon sightings were reported from Fifth Air Force units in Korea on 16 May 1952. An observation was made by some F-86 pilots that a balloon approximately 15-20 feet in diameter and 30-40 feet long was seen passing through their area. The balloon appeared to be going at a high rate of speed as the F-86s were unable to catch up with it. The balloons were observed at altitudes varying between 15,000 and 20,000 feet.
2. Friendly pursued the balloon using jet fighters. The balloon was still ascending when lost from sight.
3. D/I Comment: (5th AF) - Further investigation is being made by the South ATSS.
Comment - This report of lighted balloons may be a basis of explanation for many of the sightings reported over this area of Korea.
