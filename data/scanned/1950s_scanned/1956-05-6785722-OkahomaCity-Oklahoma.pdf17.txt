UNCLASSIFIED
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Unidentified flying objects
LOCATION: MCALLEN, OKLAHOMA
DATE OF INFORMATION: 15 May 1956
DATE OF REPORT: 15 May 1956
SOURCE: 745th AC&W SQUADRON, DUNCAN VILLE, TEXAS
SUMMARY: At approximately 1420Z, 15 May 1956 Permit APS (RADAR) called this station and reported that they had spotted 3 unidentified objects flying from east to west at an estimated speed of 600 to 800 miles per minute. The objects had an estimated altitude of 30,000 to 40,000 feet. Ground radar confirmed the objects and visual sighting was made at approximately 2022Z. Captain Rankin, the controller, division (Diploma) advised that the objects appeared to be approximately 200 yards in diameter, were illumined on the inside, had no visible means of propulsion, and were observed for about 15 minutes before they disappeared from sight. Fighters were then launched to attempt interception, but no further contact was made. Air Defense Command and ATIC have been informed of this incident.

(Signature)
ROBERT W. HOUSTON
INFORMATION OFFICER
Intelligence Officer
