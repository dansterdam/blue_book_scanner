1. In accordance with AFR Force Letter 200-5, 29 April 1952, the following information is submitted:

a. Subject sightings of unidentified flying objects occurred on 4 September 1952 between the hours of 1600 and 1604 Pacific Standard Time. The objects were first observed at approximately 1600 hours and were visible for approximately four (4) seconds.

b. The objects were observed from the surface with the naked eye. 

c. However, sighted objects while in a climbing turn near the ground light in color and in unusual formation approximately fifteen (15) miles east of Sacramento, California. The objects moved from the northeast toward the southeast and disppeared to the south in a matter of a few seconds, leaving a trail of apparent smoke or vapor behind them. 

d. Observer is 24 years old and is assigned to this organization. He has approximately 4 1/2 years of military intelligence experience and was trained as a flight observer.
