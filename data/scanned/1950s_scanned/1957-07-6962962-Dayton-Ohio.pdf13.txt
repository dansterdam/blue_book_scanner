UFO Follows Brazilian Airliner
On July 4, 1957, a Brazilian airliner plane was followed by a UFO, according to the report. The UFO was sighted by the crew and passengers of the airliner, which was traveling from Sao Paulo to Rio de Janeiro. The UFO reportedly followed the airliner for approximately 15-20 minutes at a distance of about 300 meters.

The report states that the UFO was a luminous object that was watched by the crew and passengers of the airliner. It also mentions that the airliner's captain reported the incident to the Brazilian government upon landing. The report indicates that this incident was investigated by the Brazilian government.
