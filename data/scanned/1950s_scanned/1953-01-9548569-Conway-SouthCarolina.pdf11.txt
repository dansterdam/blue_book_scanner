Report of Unidentified Flying Object
Horry County, South Carolina, 21 - 22 Feb 53

Investigator: EARL M CROUCH, Maj, USAF

Location: Conway, South Carolina, 33°50'34" N, 79°03'42" W (January), Three (3) Navy bombers were reported in the area of approximate sighting.

Sighting: On 21 Feb 53, while checking weather at Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio, T/Sgt William M. The object was first observed as a white or silver colored light, approximately 2000 yards in diameter, very bright and stationary. Later the object began to move, changing directions, speeding up, slowing down, and finally disappearing. Sgt Grouch reported the sighting to the Base Operations Officer, Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio.
