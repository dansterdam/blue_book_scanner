2D 49-0-7
SUBJECT: Unidentified Sightings at Sheaves Cove, Newfoundland

1. On 7 May 1953 Major JACK P. WHITE, Data Analysis Officer, contacted this writer and informed him that reports of unidentified flying object sightings during the period of March and April 1953 in the vicinity of Sheaves Cove, Newfoundland, had been received.

2. On 6 May 1953 Captain GORDON F. KEYHOE and Lieutenant JOHN T. MEEHAN interviewed CORRIGALL, Mrs. ELIZABETH CORRIGALL, and GRANGER, Mrs. ANNA GRANGER at Stephenville, Newfoundland. These individuals stated that they had observed strange lights in the sky during March 1953 as they were working on the CORRIGALL farm. They described the lights as brighter than stars, moving rapidly, changing color, and appearing to stop and hover in the sky.
