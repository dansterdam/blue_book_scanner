UNCLASSIFIED
AF FORM 112-PART II
REVISED 1 JUNE 1956
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
10000th AACS SITE: Roswell, Colorado
DATE OF SIGHTING: 11-6-57
I. SOURCE: Address: Levelland, Texas
Age: 15
Occupation: Officer's School Officer
DETAILS: The informant advised that the investigator had obtained a copy of his previous interview relating to his observation of an unidentified object being observed in the vicinity of Levelland, Texas on 11-6-57. He was again asked for additional details on this incident. He stated that the object traveled about 3,000 yards and disappeared from sight at a distance of approximately 1,000 yards from the point of observation. The object appeared to be about one-quarter the size of the full moon and was approximately 300 feet from the ground. It was described as glowing on the underneath portion and gave off a bright light. The object moved at a high rate of speed and was observed for a period of approximately 5 to 10 minutes by the informant and others before disappearing. SOURCES had no opinion as to the identity of the object.
