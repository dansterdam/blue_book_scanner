HEADQUARTERS
AIR MATERIEL COMMAND
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO

26 September 1952

SUBJECT: SIGHTING (UNID)

TO: Director of Intelligence, Headquarters USAF
Washington 25, D.C.

1. Flying Tiger A/C 9244, southbound at 12,000 feet, sighted an unidentified object at 12:00 noon, approximately 30 miles southeast of Boise, Idaho, on 23 September 1952. The object was about 1,500 feet above the aircraft, moving east to west, and appearing to be a saucer-shaped object of approximately 50 feet in diameter, traveling at an estimated speed of 420 knots. The object was described as silvery in color and reflecting sunlight. After several minutes, it disappeared from view at a very high rate of speed.

2. An ADIZ scramble was ordered, but the object was not seen again.

3. No additional details available.

FOR THE COMMANDING GENERAL:
