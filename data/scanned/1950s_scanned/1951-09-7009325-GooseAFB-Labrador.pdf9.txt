REPORT EVALUATING THE PHYSICAL ASPECTS OF AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT OBSERVED AT GOOSE AFB, LABRADOR, 23 SEPTEMBER 1951.
1. OBSERVATION: At approximately 2100 hours, 23 September 1951, the control tower at Goose AFB, Labrador, observed an unidentified aerial object which exhibited the following characteristics: 
(a) Object was luminous, reddish-orange in color, and estimated to be 25-30 feet in diameter.
(b) Object was observed to ascend from ground level, hover for approximately one minute, and then descend vertically to ground level.
(c) The total observation time was approximately 2 minutes.
2. COMMENTS: The observation was made by three reliable observers under good visibility conditions. The object could not be identified as any known aircraft or other aerial phenomena. The report is considered credible and merits further investigation.
