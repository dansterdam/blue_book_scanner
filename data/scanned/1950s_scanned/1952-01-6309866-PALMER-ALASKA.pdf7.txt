Dear Sir:

Corresponding in reply to your communication of January 14, 1952, regarding the sighting of unidentified aerial objects over Anchorage, Alaska, on January 12, 1952.

Your report of this incident has been carefully reviewed and considered by the staff of this Headquarters. The attached summary indicates that the observed phenomenon, while unusual, is not considered to be of hostile or unnatural origin and therefore not of operational concern to the Air Force.

However, your report has been appropriately recorded and filed with other similar incidents for future reference. This action is taken as a matter of routine procedure in the interest of maintaining complete records of all reported observations of this nature.

Your interest and cooperation in reporting this incident are greatly appreciated. Should you have any additional information or observations to submit, please do not hesitate to forward them to this office for consideration.

Sincerely yours,
Shirley Wittenbach
