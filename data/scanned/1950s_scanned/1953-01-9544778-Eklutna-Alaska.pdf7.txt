AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
HEADQUARTERS 
HEADQUARTERS OF ALASKA
23 January 1953
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Eklutna, Alaska
1. On 21 January 1953, Mr. Richard F. Prather, a civilian employee of the 6400th Air Intelligence Squadron at Elmendorf Air Force Base, Alaska, reported observing an unusual aerial object.
2. At approximately 1735 hours, 21 January 1953, Mr. Prather was riding in a government vehicle near Eklutna, Alaska, when he observed an unidentified aerial object at an estimated altitude of 200 to 300 feet. The object appeared to be about 20 to 25 feet in diameter and had an orange-red color. After observing the object for about 10 to 15 seconds, it rose vertically at a rapid rate and disappeared from view.
3. No weather or other type balloons were released in this area near the time of the observation.
4. The object was observed under clear weather conditions. 

FRANCIS R. NEIGHBORS
Colonel, USAF
Director of Intelligence
