Appendix D
Alleged Sighting of Unidentified Flying Objects at Langley AFB, Virginia, 
4 and 5 September 1959

1. On 4 September 1959, one green and one white light were observed moving up and down in the eastern sky over Langley AFB, Virginia, between the hours of 2130 and 2230. The objects appeared to be about the size of a star, but were brighter. They were observed by several military personnel at the base.

2. On 5 September 1959, at approximately 2130 hours, a reddish orange light was observed moving in the eastern sky over Langley AFB, Virginia. The object appeared to be about the size of a star and moved in a zigzag pattern. Several military personnel also observed this object.

3. All observations were reported to the 1st Fighter Interceptor Squadron, and an investigation was initiated by the Air Force. The observations could not be explained as any known celestial or aircraft phenomenon.

4. This incident has been entered in the files of Project Blue Book as case number 5524.
