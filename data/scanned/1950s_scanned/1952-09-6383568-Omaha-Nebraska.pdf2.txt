CHART SUPPLY AND DISTRIBUTION INSTRUCTIONS
Chart Sections are designated by letter and number on the Aeronautical Charts Published by the U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey.
CIVIL CHART SUPPLY
Aeronautical Charts for the sale to the general public are distributed by the Aeronautical Chart Service, U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey, Washington, D.C.
MILITARY CHART SUPPLY
Aeronautical charts, maps and publications distributed by the Aeronautical Chart Service are indexed or listed in the current edition of the USAF Catalog or Aeronautical Charts and Related Publications.
1. Requisitioning
a. Outside Continental United States:
(1) USAF activities are to submit requisitions thru the supply channels designated by the Theater
Commander for distribution and redistribution of aeronautical charts and related publications.
(2) All other activities request charts thru channels to Headquarters, Aeronautical Chart Service, USAF, Washington
b. Within Continental United States:
Requisitions for USAF activities will be submitted to the Chart Distribution Officer at the Air Force Base or Station to which the individual unit is assigned. This procedure will be followed when requesting charts emanating from Air Force, Navy, or other sources when available. Requests for charts and maps covering areas outside the continental United States will be submitted to U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey, Washington, D.C.
