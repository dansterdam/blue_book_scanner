REPORT NO.: 3

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: SALISBURY BEACH, MASS.
(4) INFORMATION:
(a) GENERAL: On the night of 3 Mar 54 at 1958 hours, a group of 8-10 persons observed an unidentified flying object over the ocean approximately 2 miles from the shore at Salisbury Beach, Mass. The object was observed for a period of about 15 minutes.
(b) High thin saucer-like object was observed.
(c) Size reported to be larger than a light aircraft, about the size of a large balloon.
(d) The number of witnesses cannot be clearly established prior to (e) negative.
(e) Positive-The witnesses claim that they have a good knowledge of most aircraft and meteorological phenomena and state that the object was unlike anything they had ever seen. One witness is a professional aircraft observer.
(f) No evidence of damage to aircraft or persons was reported.
(g) The location of the sighting is within a civil air patrol area (AMGERS 7 2) . The daily flight of the AMGERS 7 2 training flights did not report or observe any unidentified objects.
