SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Salem, Virginia,
           12 January 1958

TO:        Commander
           Langley Air Force Base
           Virginia

1. This communication confirms information furnished Mr. MAJOR W. --------, Intelligence Officer, 3/5th Bombardment
Wing, Langley Air Force Base, Virginia, on 3 February 1958, relative
to an alleged sighting of an unidentified flying object in the vicinity
of Salem, Virginia.

2. The 12 January 1958 edition of the Roanoke Times, a daily newspaper published at Roanoke, Virginia, contained the following article:
"Strange Objects Reported in Sky in ___ / This Section."

"Strange objects in the sky created concern in the Roanoke
Valley area early today", State Police at Salem received
a report from a motorist reporting a large object in the sky
with flashing lights. The motorist stated it was visible for
about 12:20 A.M. In addition, state police
said they had received reports that the object or objects had been seen by persons
in Floyd County and near Draper in Pulaski. Origin of the
strange object was not known early today."

3. On 15 January 1958, Special Agent JOHN R. McNEILL interviewed
Mr.------- House 3, Salem, Virginia. The following information concerning the unidentified flying object which
was allegedly sighted was furnished:

(1) Description of the Object:
    a. Appeared round, with a short cone trailing.
    b. Appeared 10-20 feet in diameter, the face of the
        object appeared granular or coarse in texture. According to the
        witness, the firing cone was fiery red. The cone appeared to be a cone of fire
        from the object itself. The cone was about the same length
        as the object itself.
