United States
Unusual Aerial Sightings
MacDill Air Force Base, Florida
13 August 1952
A. W. J. GRANT, CAPTAIN, USAF
Personal Interrogation

On 13 August 1952, MacDill Air Force Base, Florida, at 11:11 hours local time (19:11), approximately 20 airmen and two civilians made the following sightings:

From one to three flying objects were observed heading approximately 360 degrees and gaining altitude. One object changed course abruptly to the west moving in excess of 600 m.p.h. This was object then appeared to hover for about five minutes then resumed its original course. The other two objects did not alter course but disappeared in a northerly direction. The period of surveillance was about twenty minutes.

APPROVED:
A. W. J. GRANT
CAPTAIN, USAF
Intelligence Officer
