SUBJECT: Unidentified Aerial Object Report
Biloxi, Mississippi
6 April 1952

1. On 6 April 1952, the following report was received from Air Defense Command at Biloxi, Mississippi:

"At approximately 11:00 a.m., Central Standard Time, 6 April 1952, an unidentified aerial object was observed by Mrs. N. R. Burr, 3300 Delauney Street, Biloxi, Mississippi, while she was doing laundry in her back yard.

"Mrs. Burr stated that she observed the object for approximately one minute. She described it as circular in shape, about the size of a dinner plate, and metallic silver in color. The object was moving in a south to north direction at an estimated altitude of 1,000 to 1,500 feet and at a speed estimated at 150 to 200 miles per hour.

"The object was observed to make a sudden vertical ascent and then disappear from sight."

2. No other information is available at this time. This report is being forwarded for your information and possible further investigation.

FOR THE COMMANDER:

LAWRENCE J. TACKER
Major, USAF
Chief, Aerial Phenomena Branch
Directorate of Intelligence
