CORRECTION INSTRUCTIONS

1. The USAF Aeronautical Chart and Information Service is making every effort to furnish the user with accurate charts. This user will immediately advise the agency of any corrections or amendments of information, drainage and cultural features (roads, canals, power lines, etc.) on the face of the chart, or on an overlay of the area, or under "Remarks" below. In supplying correction corrections, the date and time of the observation used by observer in making the determination.

2. The corrected chart shall be forwarded to USAF Aeronautical Chart and Information Service, Washington 25, D.C. in accordance with established security regulations. When superseding regulations permit, mailing on unsecured chart, the following instructions for use of chart are to be followed:

a. Bank correction on face of chart or under "Remarks" below.
b. Hold chart along regular fold lines, but with back out.
c. Fold an extra fold along dotted line and staple in place so marked.
d. Place in an extra fold along dotted line and staple in place so marked.

3. If the chart cannot be mailed without wrapping, it shall be placed in a diplomatic pouch or wrapped and transmitted in accordance with security regulations.
