AKITA JAPAN 3 AUGUST 1958
Flying saucer seen in Akita
AKITA (Kyodo) -- Flying saucer was reported here by two residents of Yusawa, Akita Prefecture, and they said the luminous object at about 2,000 m. One witness said it moved slowly, the other said it moved rapidly and two black smoke-like trails followed it.
She said the circular-shaped object flew across the sky and disappeared in about one second.
