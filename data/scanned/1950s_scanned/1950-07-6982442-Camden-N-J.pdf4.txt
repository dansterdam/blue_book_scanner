SPECIAL INVESTIGATION REPORT - PROJECT BLUE BOOK
DATE: 6 July 1950
LOCATION: Camden, New Jersey
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
1. On 6 July 1950, this office received a report of an unidentified flying object observed on 3 July 1950 in the Camden, New Jersey area.
2. According to the report, at approximately 2100 hours on 3 July 1950, a large, glowing, disc-shaped object was observed hovering over the RCA Victor plant in Camden, New Jersey. The object was described as approximately 100 feet in diameter, luminous, and emitting a bluish-green glow. It was observed for approximately 10 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight.
3. No other details or witnesses could be obtained.
4. Based on the limited information available, this sighting is classified as UNIDENTIFIED.
