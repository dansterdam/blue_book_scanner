AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: FLYOBJECT

IN ACCORDANCE WITH AFLI 200-5, dated 29 April 1952, SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting (Short Title: FLYOBJECT), the following report is submitted:

On 26 July 1952 at 0025 MST, an Air Force Base, New Mexico, saw approximately 8 to 10 round objects at a high altitude which they described as travelling at an extremely rapid speed. They were golden in color and appeared to be travelling from west to east. They seemed to be travelling from west to east and were located approximately 30 miles north of the base. The reliability of the observer is considered excellent.
