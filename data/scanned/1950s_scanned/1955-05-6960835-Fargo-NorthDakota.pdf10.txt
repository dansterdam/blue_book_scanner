AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
21 NOV 1950
SOURCE: ADDRESS WITHHELD
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OBSERVED
REPORT: ON 4 NOVEMBER 1950, AT APPROXIMATELY 1815 HOURS, A CIRCULAR OBJECT WAS OBSERVED OVER FARGO, NORTH DAKOTA. THE OBJECT WAS CIRCULAR IN SHAPE, BRIGHT WHITE IN COLOR, AND APPEARED TO BE ABOUT THE SIZE OF A SILVER DOLLAR HELD AT ARM'S LENGTH. IT REMAINED STATIONARY FOR SEVERAL MINUTES AND THEN DISAPPEARED. THE OBJECT WAS ESTIMATED TO BE AT AN ALTITUDE OF APPROXIMATELY 1000 FEET ABOVE THE GROUND. WEATHER CONDITIONS WERE CLEAR AT THE TIME OF THE OBSERVATION. NO FURTHER DETAILS WERE INCLUDED.
