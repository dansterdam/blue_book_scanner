AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
DEC 29 1954 7:05
SUBJECT: UNKNOWN AERIAL OBJECTS OBSERVED OVER NEW YORK, N.Y.
PERIOD: 27 DECEMBER 1954
AREA: NEW YORK, N.Y.
1. AT 1750Z, 27 DECEMBER 1954, A PILOT FLYING OVER NEW YORK, N.Y., OBSERVED SEVERAL UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS. THE OBJECTS WERE FIRST OBSERVED OVER NEW YORK CITY AT AN ALTITUDE OF APPROXIMATELY 10,000 FEET, TRAVELING IN A SOUTHWESTERLY DIRECTION. THE OBJECTS WERE DESCRIBED AS ROUND OR CIRCULAR IN SHAPE AND APPEARED TO BE MANEUVERING AT A RAPID RATE OF SPEED. AFTER SEVERAL MINUTES, THE OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED TO ACCELERATE AND DISAPPEAR FROM VIEW.

2. INVESTIGATION IS CONTINUING. ANY FURTHER INFORMATION WILL BE REPORTED.

/S/ WILLIAM C. GARNER, MAJOR, USAF
