ALBUQUERQUE AIR DEFENSE AREA (LOS ALAMOS)
Effective Date March 15, 1950. In the interest of safety, all airmen proposing to fly within 150 nautical miles of Los Alamos, New Mexico are an altitude greater than 18,000 feet MSL or more than 4,000 feet above terrain, whichever is higher, are encouraged to file flight plans, preferably IFR, with the appropriate C&E and MISO or attach the existing flight identification by either airmen or MISO or more than 4,000 feet above terrain, whichever is higher.
