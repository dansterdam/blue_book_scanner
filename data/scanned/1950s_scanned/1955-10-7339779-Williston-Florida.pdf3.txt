AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

Flight No.: Williston FLBRID (B)

By the account of the observer, the following is a true report for the following incident:

On 01/09/55 at 01/01/52 over Williston, Florida, the witness observed an unidentified object in the sky.

The witness stated that he had observed the object to be circular in shape and larger than his aircraft with lights along the perimeter. The object was observed from 01/01/52 to 01/01/32 and appeared to be traveling at a very high rate of speed.

Information gathered from the 4602d Air Intelligence Service Squadron indicates that the sighting was of an aircraft from Eglin Air Force Base that was conducting a training exercise on the night in question. The possibility exists that it too may have been a refl re-refueling operation.

COMMENTS of Approving Officer:
Concur with the opinion of the preparing officer that the sighting was most likely caused by the SAC air-to-air refueling operation.
