(2) Size: Not applicable

(3) Color: Color of object could not be seen by observer, however observer took careful note that all lights were red.

(4) Number: One (1)

(5) Formation: Not applicable

(6) At first observation, observer stated he saw a horizontal row of red lights, with the rear slightly higher than the front. At last sight observer stated that he saw two rows of red lights as the object was moving away from him.

(7) Not applicable

(8) When he stopped and got out of his truck to observe the object the observer stated that, it was so quiet that you could hear a pin drop.

(9) Observer stated that as the object moved off slowly, he drove at approximately 80 MPH to get to the open country on the other side of town. He traveled approximately 1 mile, but lost all contact with it. Observer further stated, "it must have increased in speed immensely."
