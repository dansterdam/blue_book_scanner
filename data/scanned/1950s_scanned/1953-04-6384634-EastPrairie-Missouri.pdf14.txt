AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object
AREA REPORTED ON: East Prairie, Missouri
DATE OF INFORMATION: 23 April 1953
FROM (AGENCY): 3510 ATI, USAF
EVALUATED BY [DELETED]: [DELETED]
1. TWO UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED FOR 15 MINUTES ON 23 APRIL 1953, BETWEEN 10:30 AND 10:45 PM, CENTRAL STANDARD TIME, OVER EAST PRAIRIE, MISSOURI.
2. OBJECT ONE: SILVER, CIRCULAR, ABOUT 60 FEET IN DIAMETER AND SEEMED TO HAVE ROTATING LIGHTS AROUND THE PERIMETER. IT WAS FIRST OBSERVED AT 10:30 PM APPROXIMATELY 2000 FEET ABOVE GROUND LEVEL, MOVING FROM NORTH TO SOUTH AT A SPEED ESTIMATED AT 1000 MPH. IT DID NOT LEAVE ANY EXHAUST TRAIL. OBJECT TWO: SIMILAR IN APPEARANCE TO OBJECT ONE, EXCEPT THAT IT WAS SLIGHTLY LARGER IN SIZE. IT WAS OBSERVED AT 10:35 PM, APPROXIMATELY 3000 FEET ABOVE GROUND LEVEL, MOVING FROM WEST TO EAST AT A SPEED ESTIMATED AT 1500 MPH. IT ALSO DID NOT LEAVE ANY EXHAUST TRAIL.
3. BOTH OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED VISUALLY BY TWO CIVILIAN WITNESSES, JOSEPH B. FINN AND JAMES R. WILLIAMS, WHO WERE ENGAGED IN FARMING ACTIVITIES AT THE TIME OF THE SIGHTING. THERE WERE NO OTHER KNOWN WITNESSES.
4. THE SIGHTING WAS REPORTED TO THE UNITED STATES AIR FORCE BASE, SCOTT AIR FORCE BASE, ILLINOIS, ON 23 APRIL 1953.
5. OUR INVESTIGATION SHOWS THAT THE SIGHTING WAS GENUINE AND THAT THE OBJECTS OBSERVED WERE UNIDENTIFIED.
