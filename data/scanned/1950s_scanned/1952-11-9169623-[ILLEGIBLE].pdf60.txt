Florence, 9-6-Air Area:
A flying saucer spotted in Florence, 9-6 - area. According to an air force communication for the CAA tower, the object was on the west at about 10,000 feet and the witnesses said it was completely silent - just like a television set turned off. The CAA official described the object as glowing extremely brightly without any visible means of propulsion. He said he watched it hover and saw no smoke or flame. The object was observed for about 10 minutes and then disappeared from sight quickly. Witnesses at the CAA station at Lamb-Teton had reported sighting the object earlier. Marion police officers on travel were at the CAA office to say that they had observed the object in the sky toward Florence.
