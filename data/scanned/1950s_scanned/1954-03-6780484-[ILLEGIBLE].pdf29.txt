UNCLASSIFIED
HEADQUARTERS 
MIDDLETOWN AIR MATERIAL AREA 
Olmsted Air Force Base, Middletown, Pennsylvania

22 March 1954

SUBJECT: Investigation of Unidentified Flying Objects
TO: Commander
Air Technical Intelligence Center
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Ohio

1. In accordance with instructions set forth in paragraph 6, Air Force Regulations 200-2 and 200-2A, subject "Unidentified Flying Objects", dated 26 August 1953, the attached report at investigation is forwarded for consideration.

2. Since sighting and viewing of object/objects was reported by eight (8) individuals to have occurred (3) different locations in South Central Pennsylvania on the evening of 6 March 1954 (2200 hours) it was deemed appropriate to secure the nearest appropriate surveillance/phenomenon.

3. All witness named, from whom the attached statements were freely and willingly obtained, are considered in this report to be responsible, mature sources.

4. The original report of subject investigation was forwarded to the Director of Intelligence, Department of the Air Force, Washington 25, D.C. this date.

UNCLASSIFIED
