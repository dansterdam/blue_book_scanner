ALASKA
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
Sighting of Unidentified Flying
Objects Observed at Willow, Alaska
DATE OF REPORT: 17 Feb 53
PLACE: Willow, Alaska 
17 Feb 53 
Robert M. Gallant, 2nd Lt. USAF
Major, USAF
Dir. of Inst.
V29 R53/1320 Feb 53, Msg No. 0670 

1. Between the approximate hours of 0945Z and 1005Z, 17 February 1953, an unidentified flying object was observed in the general area of Willow, Alaska (CORDSAR A 50 5445). The object, which appeared to be a small light, was sighted from a C-47 aircraft (AS 1012) by two rated pilots.

2. Investigation has revealed no airborne or weather balloons known to be in the area at that time, and identification has not been possible.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
