UNCLASSIFIED

SOURCE: COMMANDER, 78TH FIGHTER INTERCEPTOR SQUADRON, LUKE

SUMMARY: ON 13 MAY 1952 AT APPROXIMATELY 1100 HOURS, AN UNIDENTIFIED
AERIAL OBJECT WAS SIGHTED OVER PHOENIX, ARIZONA, BY TWO PILOTS OF THE
78TH FIGHTER INTERCEPTOR SQUADRON, LUKE AFB, ARIZONA.
DESCRIPTION: THE OBJECT WAS INITIALLY OBSERVED AT AN ALTITUDE OF ABOUT
20,000 FEET OVER SOUTH PHOENIX. IT WAS MOVING IN A SOUTHEASTERLY
DIRECTION AT A VERY HIGH RATE OF SPEED, ESTIMATED TO BE ABOUT 20 MILES
PER SECOND OR APPROXIMATELY 40,000 MPH. THE OBJECT WAS ELLIPTICAL IN
SHAPE, SILVER IN COLOR, AND APPEARED TO BE ABOUT 30 FEET IN DIAMETER.
IT WAS OBSERVED FOR ABOUT 10 TO 15 SECONDS BEFORE IT DISAPPEARED FROM
VIEW. NO VAPOR TRAIL OR EXHAUST WAS OBSERVED AND NO NOISE WAS HEARD.
