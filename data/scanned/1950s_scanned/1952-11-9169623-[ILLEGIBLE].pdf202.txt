In accordance with AFR 200-2, dated 29 April 1952, subject: Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOB): On 30 November 1952, the U.S. Air Force Technical Representative, 4707th AAA and Guided Missile Sq was advised that a radar operator at White Sands Proving Ground had observed an unidentified flying object (UFOB) on the radar scope. The Technical Representative was directed to conduct an investigation. The object was observed on the radar scope for a period of 10 minutes traveling at an estimated speed of 6,000 - 7,000 mph. The object was observed to make abrupt turns and maneuvers that could not be accomplished by any known type of aircraft. No further details are available in this report.
