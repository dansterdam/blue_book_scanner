On 24 July 1952, a member of the Air Force Band based at Moody AFB, Georgia, reported having sighted several unidentified flying objects in the vicinity of Lakeland, Georgia. An interview with the airman disclosed that he had seen three (3) objects, at various altitudes and distances from his point of observation, and that because of their odd flying characteristics, he made special effort to follow their course and maneuvers. The following report contains information received from the SOURCE pertaining to the sighting of unusual flying objects.
