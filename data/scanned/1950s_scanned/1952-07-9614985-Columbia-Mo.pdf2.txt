AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
LOCATION: CENTRAL MISSOURI
DATE OF INFORMATION: 17 JULY 1952
PREPARED BY: CAPT. F. BAUSKE, USAF, INTELLIGENCE OFFICER
APR 200-S AND MESSAGE, THIS HQ, CASE 1028, DATED 10 JULY 1952
SUMMARY: SOURCE OBSERVED A BRIGHT LIGHT, ESTIMATED 4'X4' AT 1140 HOURS, GST ON 17 JULY 1952. OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR 8-10 MINUTES, MOVING EAST TO WEST AND IS ESTIMATED TO BE AT 20,000 FEET ALTITUDE. IT WAS ACCOMPANIED BY A SMALLER OBJECT, OF A BRIGHT, YELLOWISH COLOR, WHICH REMAINED IN FRONT OF THE MAIN OBJECT ONLY FOR A FEW MINUTES AND THEN DISAPPEARED. SOURCE WATCHED IT THROUGH BINOCULARS. OBJECT APPEARED TO BE CIRCULAR, ROUND IN SHAPE AND THE OBSERVED COLOR WAS A DULL, REDDISH ORANGE. NO INTERCEPTOR ACTION WAS TAKEN.
