Note, this is an initial report.
Investigation initiated by District Commander, based on information from Hal Field Office, San Antonio, Texas, that several persons had observed an unusual aerial phenomena southeast of Gonzales, Texas, on 20 May 1952. During the investigation, it was determined that while driving on Highway #3, approximately one (1) mile north of Gonzales, Texas, two (2) unusual aerial objects had been observed, and that one while driving on Highway #2 at that same location, a third unusual aerial object had been observed. The described objects were: one (1) circular object with a ball of fire, and the other because it had no markings or insignia. All other interviewees were considered reliable.
