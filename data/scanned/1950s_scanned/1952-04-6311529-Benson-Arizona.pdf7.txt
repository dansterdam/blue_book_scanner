50 539TH TACTICAL TRAINING SQUADRON (COMBAT FLYING) 30 MARCH AND 4 APRIL 1952 
BENSON, ARIZONA

519.1 SUBJECT: Unidentified Aerial Object sighted by C. L. Jasper on 28 April 1952, Benson, Arizona.

TO: Commanding Officer 
Air Technical Intelligence Center
Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio

1. Reference is made to your letter of 22 April 1952, the following additional information is submitted with reference to unidentified aerial object sighted on 28 April 1952.

2. The angle of the object from the horizontal position was from 70 to 75 degrees. The object bearing, taken from the observer's compass, was from 250 to 255 degrees which would be considered to be slightly east of south.

3. The manner in which the object disappeared is uncertain and stated in previous report, observation was discontinued for approximately fifteen seconds during which time the object took leave.

4. This office is unable to furnish more detailed information but efforts all possible assistance on any further developments.

FOR THE COMMANDING OFFICER
/s/ G. L. SCHAUB
1st LT., USAF
Adjutant
