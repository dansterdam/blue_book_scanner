U.S. Airmen Sighted Flying Object Sighted near Washington, D. C. 13 July 1952
Lieutenant-Colonel, USAF, Commanding, 1257th Air Transport Squadron, 3700 Broad Street, Washington 25, D.C.
Frank H. Allen, Lt Colonel, USAF - National Airlines flight crew
FYFQ-APF submitted by Olmsted Flight Service Center 1/ July (19)52)
Crew of a scheduled airliner aircraft in flight observed an unidentified flying object in the area of a ball or billows type Washington, D.C. jet trail on 13 July.
The object approached the observers aircraft, then maintaining a parallel course. on the same level, at the same speed, until the aircraft pilot turned on all 13 July.
This aircraft crew were apparently those previously experienced in observation of and from aircraft. weather was excellent for observation.

FRANK H. ALLEN LT COLONEL, USAF INTELLIGENCE OFFICER
