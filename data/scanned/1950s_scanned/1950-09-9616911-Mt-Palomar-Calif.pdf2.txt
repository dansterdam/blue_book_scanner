United States of America
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
Report of Unidentified Aerial Objects
Los Angeles, California
26 September 1950
Report No.: A-1
Summary: (As appears in the original report, dates and other identifying information have been redacted)
"AS SOME BOGY FLEW PAST (OBSCURED) 1ST AND 2ND LIEUTENANTS (NAMES OBSCURED) OBSERVING THE MOVEMENT OF THE CELESTIAL BODIES WHEN THEY SAW WHAT APPEARED TO BE A GLOWING STAR ABOVE THE HORIZON."
The objects, observed at a great distance, moved at a great rate of speed from approximately 310° to 100° horizontally, in two to three minutes, emitting a light, which was visible intermittently. The only part of the light appearing to be the size of a star.
UNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 15 YEARS.
DISTRIBUTION BY ORIGINATOR:
A-2, Head quarters, Fifteenth Air Force
