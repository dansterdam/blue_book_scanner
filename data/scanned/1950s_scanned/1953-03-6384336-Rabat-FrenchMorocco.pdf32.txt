AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

Headquarters of Intelligence Division
Strategic Air Command
23 March 1953

UNCLASSIFIED

The report describes an incident that occurred at Casablanca, French Morocco on 24 February 1953, where two unidentified aircraft were observed by the Casablanca Air Traffic Control. The report provides details about the observation, including the time, location, size, and movement of the objects. It also mentions that the objects were tracked on radar and that the incident was reported to higher authorities.
