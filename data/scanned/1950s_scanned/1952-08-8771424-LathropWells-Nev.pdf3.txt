AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
15 JUL 1952
1. Type of observation: First-hand observation of strange aerial object. The witnesses were a Shift Leader and an Airman at the Lathrop Wells Air Force Base in Nevada. They reported observing an unidentified aerial object near the base.
2. Time of sighting: 2250, 8 August 1952. Duration of the sighting was approximately 15 minutes.
3. Number of observers: Two.
4. Location: 36°19' N, 116°10' W. Approximate area of sighting was 25 miles north of Lathrop Wells.
5. Description of object: A bright, self-luminous object, approximately 25-30 feet in diameter, having a distinct brightness and color which changed from amber to green. The object maneuvered in a zig-zag fashion and accelerated to an extremely high speed, estimated to be over 1,000 mph, then disappeared from view.
6. Weather conditions at time of sighting: Witnesses stated the sky was clear and the object could be seen distinctly.
7. Activities or conditions which might account for sighting: No known Air Traffic in general area at time of sighting.
8. No interception or identifying action taken.
9. No known Air Traffic in general area at time of sighting.
