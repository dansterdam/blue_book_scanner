HEADQUARTERS
3575TH PILOT TRAINING WING (BASIC)
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
VANCE AIR FORCE BASE, OKLAHOMA

14 November 1958

SUBJECT: UFO Investigation

TO:   Commander
      2045th AACS Squadron
      ATTN: Flight Service Center
      Carswell Air Force Base
      Fort Worth, Texas

1. I am investigating an alleged sighting of unidentified flying objects in the vicinity of Fairfax, Oklahoma on the morning of 2 November 1958.

2. The observer states that he saw four orange colored discs in a fast formation headed due south, altitude unknown, at a terrific rate of speed. The discs were in sight through the front windshield for about five or six seconds.

3. I would appreciate a possible confirmation of any aircraft under your control that might have passed in the vicinity of ten miles directly north of Fairfax, Oklahoma, 0630 Zulu, 2 November 1958. If you have any additional information on this particular incident, please forward same.

FOR THE COMMANDER:

DONALD R. SHOVER
1st Lt, USAF
Investigating Officer
