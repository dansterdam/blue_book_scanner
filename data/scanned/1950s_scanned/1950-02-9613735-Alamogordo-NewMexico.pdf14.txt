This investigation is submitted in compliance with AFCSI Letter No. 85, dated 12 August 1949, to report all sightings of unidentified flying objects. CPL LERTIS E. STANFIELD, AF-35-37111, 302nd Air Police Sq., Holloman AFB, observed an unusual aerial phenomena at 1930 hours 24 Feb 1950 and at 0200 hours 25 February 1950. All pertinent information was obtained from STANFIELD and is reported herein. Photographs of the object which were taken by STANFIELD are herein enclosed, numbered 1 to 5 in the sequence in which they were taken.
