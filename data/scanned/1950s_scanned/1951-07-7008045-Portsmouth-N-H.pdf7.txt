UNCLASSIFIED
REPORT OF SIGHTING OF UNCONVENTIONAL AIRCRAFT
1. Description: Long tube with fin construction on rear end of tube. 10-20 feet in length with black or dark color. Overall color, almost colorless resembling fused quartz. Had a brilliant glowing light at front end. This glow was between one-half and one inch wide. This glow or corona was between our position and the sun, making a reflection impossible. Disappeared very rapidly 500-1000 feet. No sound involved. No answers, it was at level flight, traveling from east to west. It was clear and no clouds in sky.
2. Date: 2910Z, duration of observation was approximately twenty seconds. 2% July 1951
3. Manner of Observation: Visual observation from an automobile going in a northerly direction on a main highway.
4. Location of Observers: Proceeding north along main highway towards Portsmouth, N. H. about 4 miles from toll gate on bridge. The object passed over the bridge from east to west at approximately 1000-2000 feet. The object seemed to be slightly above the ordinary tree tops.
