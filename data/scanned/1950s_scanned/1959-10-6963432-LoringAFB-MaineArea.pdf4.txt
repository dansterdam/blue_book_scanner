Following is an extract from ATIC Duty Officers Report for your information:

1. Two (2) short reports to ATIC Duty Officer via SAC and BASE Hq Duty Officer were made early 23 Oct 59.

- Three (3) unidentified flying objects were sighted 23 04242, 11 miles south of Loring AFB by 2 MC and 1 airman. The objects were quite small and bright, just descmbled but only bright star seen. 2nd report - 2 bluish objects with red rings sighted 0600Z, 23 Oct 59, over Presque Isle, Maine. Witnesses - Mrs. and airman reported one hovering, radar picked up object, and determined their altitude to be 350 to 500 knots, according to radar.
