An ATIC received a TWX 26 September 1958 ATIC control no. 11-42-58 attaching a summary of an incident of airborne objects of an unusual and irregular nature observed in southern United States.

It revealed object, about the size of a dime held at arm's length, which appeared to have an irregular tail of light, was reported to have been seen from several locations in southern United States.

It was determined little or no investigation and analysis that the witness observed a high altitude meteor or its afterglow. This incident is comparable to the report in October 19-57 from the Western United States in article.
