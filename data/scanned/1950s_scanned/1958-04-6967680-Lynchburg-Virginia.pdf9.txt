ATTN: PROJECT SIGN
LYNCHBURG,
VIRGINIA
6 APRIL 1958
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
1. AT 2030 HOURS ON 5 APRIL 1958, PRIVATE JOHN DOE, U.S. ARMY, WAS OBSERVING THE SKY FROM HIS APARTMENT WINDOW IN LYNCHBURG, VIRGINIA. HE OBSERVED A BRIGHT, ROUND, WHITE LIGHT IN THE SKY THAT SEEMED TO BE MOVING RAPIDLY. THE LIGHT APPEARED TO CHANGE DIRECTION SEVERAL TIMES AND THEN DISAPPEARED.
2. PRIVATE DOE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED HIS OBSERVATION TO MILITARY AUTHORITIES. HIS REPORT WAS FORWARDED TO PROJECT SIGN FOR INVESTIGATION.
3. PRIVATE DOE IS A RELIABLE OBSERVER WITH NO KNOWN HISTORY OF MENTAL ILLNESS OR TENDENCY TOWARD IMAGINARY SIGHTINGS. HIS OBSERVATION IS CONSIDERED CREDIBLE.
4. NO EXPLANATION FOR THE OBSERVED OBJECT HAS BEEN FOUND AT THIS TIME. THE CASE REMAINS UNDER INVESTIGATION.
