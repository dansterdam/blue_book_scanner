September 18, 1963

Dear Sirs,

In response to your request of September 9, 1963, for some information on the subject of unidentified aerial phenomena, we think these information may be of interest to you.

There have been a number of sightings of unidentified aerial phenomena over the United States during the past years. These lights were later identified by the Air Force as being caused by the testing of aircraft in the area at that time.

Sincerely,

MARCH M. JACKS
Public Information Division
Office of Information
