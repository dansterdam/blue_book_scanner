SOURCE'S DESCRIPTION OF SIGHTING: SOURCE and Mrs. Tennessee (OJGF 0214) were sighting in front of a house on [Redacted], Tennessee on SEP 55 in air coming from the North and pointed out the object to each other. Object came from north and moved in a straight line motion. Almost overhead the object turned eastward, slowed down and suddenly turned and zoomed out of sight. The object was observed for only a short time through the binoculars as it was leaving.
SOURCE first stated object was a distant object, as it emerged from haze, appeared oval, was about the size of a large beachball and not as large as an ordinary air balloon. Later SOURCE gave some measurements that object was larger than basketball but not as large as an ordinary air balloon. SOURCE gave dimensions of about 15 ft long and 10 ft wide. Object appeared to be metallic, shiny and was revolving or spinning. SOURCE could find small stubby wings or airfoils taking off and no sound from object. SOURCE had no ideas what object might have been or what caused it.
