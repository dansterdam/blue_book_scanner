REPORT SUBJECT: Greenville, North Carolina
DATE: 6 February 1954
LOCATION OF INCIDENT: Greenville, North Carolina
TIME OF INCIDENT: 1600 hours

DETAILS OF INCIDENT:
On 6 February 1954 at approximately 1600 hours, an unidentified flying object was observed over Greenville, North Carolina. The object was described as circular in shape, approximately 40-50 feet in diameter, and was observed for approximately 10 minutes before disappearing from view.
Several witnesses reported the object was metallic in appearance and was traveling at a high rate of speed. No sound was reported by the witnesses. No evidence or physical traces were found at the scene.
The incident was reported to the US Air Force, which launched an investigation under Project Blue Book. A radar tracking record was obtained, which showed an unidentified target in the area at the time of the sighting. The radar track is provided as supporting evidence.
