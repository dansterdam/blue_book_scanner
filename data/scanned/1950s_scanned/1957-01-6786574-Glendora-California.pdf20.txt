EAST LANSING, MICHIGAN
48033
March 22, 1967

Gentlemen:

About ten years ago I saw a U.F.O.
It happened like this.

My family & I were up near Mount 
Pleasant visiting my now late father
& his family. Everyone was asleep except
Dad & I. Dad had to give his wife, 
a nurse in Mt. Pleasant and asked me 
if I wanted to come keep him company.
I went.

Well we had just topped a
hill when I saw it. It was so close
