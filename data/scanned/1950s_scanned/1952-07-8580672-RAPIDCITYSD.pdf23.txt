AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: FLYMART
AREA REPORT OF OR : RAPID CITY, SOUTH DAKOTA
DATE OF INFORMATION: 9 JULY 1952
DATE OF REPORT: 12 JULY 1952
SOURCE: CIVILIAN - PART-TIME AIRCRAFT OBSERVER
SUMMARY: On 9 July 1952 a report was received by the ACAD Defense Wing. Several unidentified flying objects were sighted by a civilian part-time aircraft observer near Rapid City, South Dakota on 22 July 1952. This report was referred to the USAF Technical Intelligence Operations Section for evaluation and interpretation.
The objects were observed to be traveling at an estimated speed of 1200 mph. The objects were described as aluminum-colored, circular in shape, and their size was estimated to be 40-50 feet in diameter. The objects were seen at an estimated altitude of 20,000 feet, and there were a number of them, from 4 to 10 in number. The objects were maneuvering in an apparently random pattern, and were observed in the sky for about 10-15 minutes.
