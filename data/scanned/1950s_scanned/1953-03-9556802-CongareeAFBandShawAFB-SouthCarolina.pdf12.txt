UNCLASSIFIED

DISPOSITION FORM

FILE NO.
SUBJECT: Unidentified Aerial Object Sighting

TO: AFLA~2 ATTN: Mr. James
1. Attached is an unidentified aerial object sighting from the South Carolina area on 29 Apr 53.
2. The object was first observed ground visually by the Assistant Director of Security at the Savannah River Project from 1545 to 1645 EST.
3. The observed object was 707 until 1635 EST, then the object was confirmed by two radar check on 767jb and 767za until 1707 EST.
4. Interception by F-80 and F-51 type aircraft was attempted with negative results.
5. We would appreciate your comments on the above sighting.
6. Airbatic charts are being requisitioned, and when they are received, the subject sighting will be returned to you for further analysis.
