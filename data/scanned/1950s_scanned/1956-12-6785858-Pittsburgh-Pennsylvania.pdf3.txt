AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
DATE OF INCIDENT: 4 December 1956
LOCATION: Greater Pittsburgh Airport, Coraopolis, Pennsylvania
OBSERVER: Captain Harold M. Holloway, USAF
SUMMARY: Captain Holloway observed an unidentified flying object at approximately 8:15 PM on 4 December 1956 at Greater Pittsburgh Airport, Coraopolis, Pennsylvania. The object was described as having a glow (surface), was 3-5 feet in diameter, and had a speed of 200-300 knots. It was observed for approximately 30 seconds before disappearing. The local GCA station and airport control tower were contacted, but they had no knowledge of the sighting. The sighting was considered a qualified technical observation.
