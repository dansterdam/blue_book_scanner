SUBJECT: Report of Fighter-Control Observer

1. It is hereby certified that the following statement concerning observations of an unidentified object made by me on 20 May 1952:

From my impression of this object, it was that of a well-defined light, which increased in size as it approached, and which was of a bright round white which became larger until it reached a size approximately that of a dinner plate. This object was observed for approximately 3-4 minutes.

2. I sighted the object at 14:53 EST on the date indicated for a period of about five minutes.
