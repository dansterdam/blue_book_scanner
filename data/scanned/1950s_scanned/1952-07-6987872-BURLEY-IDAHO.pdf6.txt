Unidentified Flying Objects Sighted Over Burley and Paul, Idaho 28 July 1952
State of Idaho, USA
28 July 1952
Gerald E. Tyler, Major, USAF
Teletype PRIORITY submitted by Hamilton Flight Service Center 2822USPT July 29.
Twenty-two objects were sighted at 1436, moving very fast from north to south at 7500-8000 feet altitude. The objects were round and dim, generally the size of a basketball. They were observed for approximately three minutes. The objects were at an estimated speed of 1000-1500 mph and were observed by several persons. No evasive action or unusual maneuvers were reported. The objects disappeared in a southerly direction. No explanation was ventured.
