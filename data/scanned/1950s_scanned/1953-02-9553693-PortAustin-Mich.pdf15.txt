Report of Unidentified Aerial Object

Commanding Officer 
524th AC&W Squadron 
Selfridge Air Force Base, Michigan

1. The 524th AC&W Squadron of Port Austin, Michigan, made a report to the Air Technical Intelligence Center concerning a confirmed visual and radar sighting of an unidentified aerial object occurring on 17 February 1953 at 2254 hours. EST. 

2. It is mentioned in this report that your squadron tracked this object on their radar for a period of 17 minutes.

3. AIC is attempting to gather all the facts surrounding the incident in order to determine the exact nature of the sighting, and to establish a possible triangulation on the object, if possible, and to obtain any additional information which might assist in completing the incident.

4. This information can be used to corroborate the Port Austin sighting and to establish a possible triangulation on the object, if possible, and assist in completing the incident.
