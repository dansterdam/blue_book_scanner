UNCLASSIFIED
FLYING OBJECTS SEEN NEAR JOLIET ILLINOIS
22 JULY 1952
THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION IS UNCLASSIFIED AND IS BEING SUBMITTED FOR PROJECT 10073 RECORDS.
ON 22 JULY 1952 AT ABOUT 2300 HOURS, MR. AND MRS. JOHN DOE BOTH OBSERVED SEVERAL BRIGHT LIGHTS IN THE SKY OVER CHICAGO HEIGHTS, ILLINOIS. THEY WERE STANDING IN THEIR BACK YARD WHEN THEY FIRST NOTICED THE LIGHTS.
DESCRIPTION OF OBJECTS:
THE OBJECTS WERE VERY BRIGHT AND APPEARED AT HIGH ALTITUDE. THEY WERE SEEN TO MOVE RAPIDLY AND IN VARIOUS DIRECTIONS. OBJECT APPEARED TO CHANGE COURSE ABRUPTLY, AND THEN RESUME THEIR ORIGINAL FLIGHT PATH. THEY WERE OBSERVED FOR ABOUT 15 MINUTES. THE OBJECTS DID NOT APPEAR TO BE AIRCRAFT. NO SOUND WAS HEARD.
THE OBSERVERS ARE CONSIDERED RELIABLE. THERE IS NO EVIDENCE FOUND TO DISCLAIM THEIR OBSERVATION.
SECRET UNTIL JUNE 30, 1975
