CASE RECORD NO. 5743
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 
1. AT 2302 HRS L GMT, 6 JULY 1954, A BRIGHT OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY PERSONNEL OF THE US AIR FORCE TO ASCEND RAPIDLY FROM THE GROUND NEAR BALTIMORE, MARYLAND, AND DISAPPEAR FROM VIEW.
ABOUT 5 MINUTES LATER THE OBJECT REAPPEARED, DESCENDING SLOWLY WITH A PULSATING GLOW OF LIGHT. IT WAS UNDER OBSERVATION FOR APPROXIMATELY 15 MINUTES BEFORE IT AGAIN ACCELERATED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE ROUND OR ELLIPTICAL IN SHAPE AND OF A DIAMETER ESTIMATED BETWEEN 30 AND 40 FEET. IT EMITTED A GLOW OR LUMINESCENCE OF YELLOWISH-WHITE COLOR.
NO SOUND WAS HEARD DURING THE OBSERVATION. THERE WERE NO METEOROLOGICAL DISTURBANCES OR AIR TRAFFIC IN THE AREA AT THE TIME.
THIS SIGHTING HAS BEEN EVALUATED AND IS CONSIDERED UNEXPLAINED.
