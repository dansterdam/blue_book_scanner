MEMORANDUM TO: D.I.D. UNIT, ATIC
SUBJECT: SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 
1. On 7 Nov 56, at approximately 1930 hours, Lt Col JOHN C. SNIDER, 0-9999, Marine Barracks, El Toro, California, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object.
2. Lt Col SNIDER stated that while on routine patrol, he observed a bright, round, luminous object in the sky. The object was estimated to be 1/4 to 1/2 mile in diameter and was traveling in a southerly direction at a very high rate of speed. The object was bright white in color and remained visible for approximately 10-15 seconds before disappearing from view. 
3. The weather at the time of the sighting was clear with unlimited visibility. No aircraft were in the area at the time and no other persons observed the object. 
4. Lt Col SNIDER has had extensive experience as a pilot and is considered a reliable and credible observer. His report is considered to be an authentic sighting of an unidentified flying object.
5. No further information is available at this time. Upon receipt of any additional data, a supplemental report will be submitted.
