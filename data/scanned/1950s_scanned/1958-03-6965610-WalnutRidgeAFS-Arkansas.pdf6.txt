WALNUT RIDGE AFS, ARKANSAS
6 MARCH 1958

OBSERVATION OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 

AT APPROXIMATELY 0100 HOURS ON 6 MARCH 1958, THE CONTROL TOWER OPERATOR AT WALNUT RIDGE AFS, ARKANSAS OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT IN THE SOUTHWESTERN SKY.  THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS GREENISH-WHITE IN COLOR AND EXTREMELY BRIGHT.  IT APPEARED TO BE MOVING IN A NORTH-EASTERLY DIRECTION AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED.  THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 20 SECONDS BEFORE DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.
