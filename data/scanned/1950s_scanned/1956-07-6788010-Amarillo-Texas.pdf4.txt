San Joaquin Valley, California

Case 173, San Joaquin Valley, Cal., July 22, '56 - Said the Los Angeles appeared Aerial Survey Department (Authority: US Case 173). Two mysterious flying objects were reportedly seen in the San Joaquin Valley, California, about 6:45 p.m. on July 22, 1956. The objects were described as circular in shape, about the size of a quarter held at arm's length, and had a slight bluish-green coloration. They were observed for approximately two minutes by two men, both of whom are described as being reliable persons. The objects were first seen at about 6,000 feet altitude, then descended to about 4,000 feet, at which point they accelerated at a high rate of speed and disappeared from view in a westerly direction. The objects were watched for approximately five hours before they "vanished". (Data 1956)
