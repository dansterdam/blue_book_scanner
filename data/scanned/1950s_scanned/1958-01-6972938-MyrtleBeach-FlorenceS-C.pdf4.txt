UNCLASSIFIED
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
10 and 13 Jan 58
UFO's sighted by sight (8) civilians in Myrtle City, S.C. about 12:20 hours (EST). 9-13 Jan 58. SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS (UFOS)
SUMMARY: 1. On 10 and 13 Jan 58 UFOS were sighted by 8 civilians in Myrtle City, S.C. at about 12:20 hours (EST).
2. The objects were described as round and white in color.
3. The objects were observed to maneuver at high speeds and change directions at will. 
4. AF Intelligence in this area has been alerted to this UFOS sighting throughout South Carolina on 12 Jan 58.
JAMES F. RACKLEY, JR.
Director of Operations
