AERONAUTICAL CHART SUPPLY
1. Aeronautical charts, maps and publications distributed through the U.S.A.F. Aeronautical Chart Service are intended as listed in the current edition of AF Regulation 95-12 "Aeronautical Publications."
a. Charts of the Continental United States.
(1) Supply requests are submitted through the supply channels as designated by the appropriate USAF Aeronautical Chart and Map Center.
(2) Supply facilities are to submit requisitions for replenishment of stocks as required.
b. Overseas Charts and Maps.
(1) Supply requests are submitted through the supply channels designated by the appropriate USAF Aeronautical Chart and Map Center.
2. Information regarding cloth reproduction of certain selected aeronautical charts and maps may be obtained from the Air Photographic & Charting Service, United States Air Force, Washington 25, D.C.
3. The U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey, Department of Commerce, Washington 25, D.C., is responsible for the distribution of certain aeronautical charts.

CORRECTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Military users of this chart are urged to mark thereon any errors or changes concerning: Corrections of radio and airway information, drainage, cultural features (roads, canals, power lines, etc.) should be marked on the face of the chart or on a separate sheet.
2. Folding chart along border fold lines, but with book mat. 
3. Correction or change markings on face of chart or under "Remarks," below.
4. Retired chart shown in folder with book not permitted. Retired charts are to be returned.
