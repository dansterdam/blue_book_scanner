HEADQUARTERS THIRD ARMY
FORT MCPHERSON, GEORGIA

17 March 1950

SUBJECT: Unconventional Aircraft

TO: Commanding General
Air Materiel Command
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Dayton, Ohio 

Attention: MCIT (Control No. A-1917)

1. Enclosed herewith is newspaper clipping re flying saucer.

2. According to report from CIC Agent, Birmingham, Alabama, it has been determined that the article was written by Capt. Preston, Public Information Officer, Birmingham Air Force Base. Lt. Preston said she would follow pertinent regulations in the matter. The newspaper waited approximately two days at the end of 'that time they heard nothing further from Lt. Preston and printed the story. After the article had been printed, Lt. Preston informed the local CIC office that he had been contacted concerning the story. Up to that time, no other action had been taken.

FOR THE COMMANDING GENERAL:

R. K. KEANE
