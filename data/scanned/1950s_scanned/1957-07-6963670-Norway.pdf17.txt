DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY

24 June 1959

Dear Mr. 

Your letter addressed to Secretary of Defense McElroy regarding your photograph is being referred to the Air Technical Intelligence Center for analysis and/or evaluation.

In order to complete the ATIC evaluation of this photo, the following additional information is requested:

1. Date photo was taken
2. Direction in which picture was taken 
3. Angle of elevation at which photo was taken
4. Compass bearing or with compass
5. Latitude.

As soon as this evaluation is completed, we will forward
the results to you and return your photo.

Sincerely,

LAWRENCE J. TACKER
Major, USAF
Executive Officer
Public Information Division
Office of Information Services
