Bluish-Green Object Explodes in Sky

SAN FRANCISCO, CALIF. - From a meteor. It was seen about 9:30 drop straight to earth. Several other persons reported seeing the same object. Witnesses said it appeared to explode and saw debris falling. Newspapers and radio stations from the area were flooded with calls. Western Airs Line said they had a report from a pilot who had seen the same object go past his plane, which was at 11,000 feet altitute and was in the vicinity of Lancaster, Pa. NEW ERA, FRIDAY, AUGUST 2, 1957.
