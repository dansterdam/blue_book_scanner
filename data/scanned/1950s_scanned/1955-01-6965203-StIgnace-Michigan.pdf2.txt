UNIDENTIFIED IN MICHIGAN

In accordance with the following report is
submitted:
1 ) Lt. Col. Starin
2 ) Address: Dept. of Investigations
3 ) Date: 5 January 1955
4 ) Place: St. Ignace, Michigan
5 ) Time: 6:30 P.M.

The object appeared to be round, dark color,

7 ) Action Taken: Investigated by local police
and State Police. No traces of landing found.
8 ) Narrative: At approximately 6:30 P.M., 5
January 1955, the police in St. Ignace, Michigan,
received a call reporting a strange object in the
sky. Several people had observed the object and
reported it to the police. One observer described
it as being round, dark colored, approximately
20-25 feet in diameter. It was traveling at a high
rate of speed and appeared to be going in a
downward trajectory. The object was observed
over the city for several minutes before disappearing. The local and State Police investigated the
area but were unable to find any trace of a landing.
No photographs were obtained.
