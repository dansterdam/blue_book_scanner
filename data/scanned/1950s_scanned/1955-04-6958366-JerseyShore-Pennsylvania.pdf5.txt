AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
ACTIVITY OF THE OBJECT
ON 6 NOVEMBER 54, A CALL WAS RECEIVED AT THIS HEADQUARTERS REPORTING THE SIGHTING OF TWO BRIGHT CIRCULAR LIGHTS IN THE SKY OVER THE JERSEY SHORE NEAR WILLOW GROVE, PA. INVESTIGATION OF THE INCIDENT WAS INITIATED AND THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION WAS OBTAINED.
THE OBJECT WAS FIRST OBSERVED APPROXIMATELY 7:30 P.M. ON 6 NOVEMBER 54 BY MR. AND MRS. JOHN DOE AND MR. AND MRS. WILLIAM SMITH, RESIDENTS OF WILLOW GROVE, PA. THE OBJECTS WERE REPORTED TO BE LARGE BRIGHT CIRCULAR LIGHTS OF VARYING INTENSITY, MOVING SLOWLY AT HIGH ALTITUDE AND DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW AFTER SEVERAL MINUTES.
ATTEMPTS TO LOCATE MILITARY OR COMMERCIAL AIRCRAFT, OR WEATHER BALLOONS IN THE AREA OPERATING AT THE TIME AND LOCATION OF THE SIGHTING WERE NEGATIVE. NO UNUSUAL WEATHER CONDITIONS WERE REPORTED IN THE AREA AT THE TIME.
THE SOURCE OF THE OBJECTS REMAINS UNIDENTIFIED.
