All that was seen was a series of flashes of light similar to that produced by a strobe light. However, the flashes occurred on a regular basis and a straight line could be drawn through the paths of each set of flashes. It was therefore convincing that one object had passed quickly from North to Southwest horizons, giving a flashes of light at regular intervals. Nothing was seen between the flashes as the object traveled to the Southwest horizon within less than a minute.
