AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
COUNTRY: U. S. A.
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: SHAW AFB, SOUTH CAROLINA
DATE OF INFORMATION: 13 JULY 1953
SUMMARY: On 13 July 1953, at about 10:10 PM, two observers at Shaw AFB, South Carolina, sighted an unidentified flying object. The object was observed through a telescopic sight on an M-1 Gun Sight for approximately one minute. The object was described as round, silver in color, and larger than a star. It was observed to be moving in a northwesterly direction at a high rate of speed.
The object was observed through a magnified telescopic sight and appeared to be approximately 5 degrees in diameter. The object was under observation for approximately one minute and then disappeared from view. No trail or exhaust was observed. The object was observed to be at an estimated altitude of 35,000 feet.
The weather was clear and the object was observed against a dark sky. No aircraft or other objects were observed in the vicinity at the time.
This report is forwarded for your information.
