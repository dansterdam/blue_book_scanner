DATE - JUNE 13, 1959

April 29, 1959, time 7:25 p.m., was located at location in Staunton Int. in Co-New Va. Object (radius 6 milez which was rotating) appeared with noise like high tension wire and was following route northeast for some distance. Was sighted by about two minutes. When it stopped and a tremendous flash of light occurred, then object disappeared rapidly to the northeast.
