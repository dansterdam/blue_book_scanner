OFFICE MEMORANDUM - UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT
TO: Mr. Perry
FROM: Mr. Francis
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object
1. Date of occurrence was Sunday evening, 3:43 to 9:00 P.M., E.S.T.
2. March 3, 1958, got March 1. Meteorological factors and pilot reports indicated severe, possibly extreme turbulence over Tampa and vicinity. Turbulence is varying extremes. Pilot's report on 3/3/58 states the object was a light like a landing light, reported "glowing," and continued approximately 2 hours before disappearing.
3. The stability index on the Tampa CCCCZ, March 3, R/S observation shows stability 3. This R/S stabilized approximately 2 hours before the objective was reported.
4. Objects observed were first reported to be by the Tampa tower. They wanted to know if we had released a balloon, as they had sited the size of the R/S balloon. When a slight light on the object was observed, the R/S balloon was ruled out, as the size was reported to be much larger than the R/S balloon.
5. P.S. Excuse O.I. typewriter.
