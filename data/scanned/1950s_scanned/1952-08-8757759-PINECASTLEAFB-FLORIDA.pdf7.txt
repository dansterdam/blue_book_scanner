The text of the report is as follows:

AT 0936 HOURS 7 AUGUST 1952 AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT WAS OBSERVED OVER PINE CASTLE AIR FORCE BASE, ORLANDO, FLORIDA.  

THE OBSERVER WAS ON HILLINS AIR FORCE BASE WHEN HE OBSERVED THE OBJECT. IT WAS VISIBLE FOR 15-20 SECONDS AND MADE NO AUDIBLE SOUND OR VISIBLE EXHAUST. NO ESTIMATE AS TO THE SIZE OF THE OBJECT CAN BE MADE.

AT FIRST SIGHTING OBJECT WAS MOVING NORTH-NORTHWEST AT A SLOW RATE OF SPEED. THEN OBJECT SUDDENLY MADE A SHARP CLIMBING MANEUVER AND DISAPPEARED AT AN ESTIMATED 25,000-30,000 FEET ALTITUDE.

WEATHER AT TIME OF SIGHTING WAS CAVU WITH SCATTERED CUMULUS CLOUDS. NO INTERCEPTIONS WERE MADE AND NO PHOTOGRAPHS WERE TAKEN.

AIRCRAFT FROM THIS BASE WERE AIRBORNE AT THE TIME BUT THERE WERE NO REPORTS OF UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS RECEIVED FROM THE PILOTS. THE AIRCRAFT RETURN SCHEDULES INDICATE THAT SOME AIRCRAFT WERE ON PATROL OVER THE PINE CASTLE AREA AT THE TIME OF THE SIGHTING. A BARRAGE BALLOON WAS RELEASED AT SANFORD NAVAL AIR STATION AT 0500 EST.
