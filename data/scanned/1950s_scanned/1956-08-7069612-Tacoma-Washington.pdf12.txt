11 August 1956

Case 186, Pittsburgh, Pa., Aug 11, 1956 - Said the Pittsburgh Sun-Telegraph Mrs. Florence Scalise of Emerson said she spotted an object shaped like a "ship" for more than two hours on Saturday morning.
