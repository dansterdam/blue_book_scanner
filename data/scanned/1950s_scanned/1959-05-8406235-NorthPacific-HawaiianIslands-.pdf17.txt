13 July 1959. B-52B - Hawaii Flight.
This report concerned a sighting of unknown light (s) seen by the crew of a B-52B aircraft on a training mission over the north Pacific off the Hawaiian Islands. 
The sighting occurred at approximately 1:00 a.m. (local time).
The crew reported that they observed a bright light, which appeared to be larger than a star and seemed to be accompanied by three smaller lights. 
The lights were observed for about 3 to 5 minutes before disappearing.
The crew estimated the object(s) to be at an altitude of about 20,000 feet, and the lights were said to be stationary and motionless in relation to the aircraft.
No definite conclusions were reached regarding the identity of the light(s). The report was forwarded to the Air Force Directorate of Intelligence for further analysis.
