PROJECT NUMBER
UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

ATTENTION: ASSISTANT AIR ATTACHE

1. ON NIGHT OF 19 MAR 53 AT APPROV.XMATFLY 2300 Z A BRIGHT UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WAS NOTICED BY NUMEROUS CIVILIANS IN WINSOR, NEWFO'JNDLAND WHO WERE PRESENT IN VARIOUS LOCATIONS. THIS OBJECT APPEARED TO BE VERY HIGH AND TRAVELING AT A TERRIFIC RATE OF SPEED IN A SOUTH CHANDLING DIRECTION. IT IS REPORTED TO HAVE LOOKED VERY BRIGHT AND CAST A REFLECTION ON GROUND WHICH LOOKED LIKE BRIGHT SUNLIGHT. DURATION WAS ONLY A FEW SECONDS.

2. INTELLIGENCE PERSONNEL AT GOOSE AIR FORCE BASE WERE CONTACTED AND THEY REPORTED THAT THEY HAD RECEIVED NO RADAR CONTACT OR VISUAL OBSERVATION OF ANY UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT AT THIS TIME.

3. IT IS REQUESTED THAT ANY ADDITIONAL INFORMATION AVAILABLE CONCERNING THIS OCCURRENCE BE FORWARDED TO THIS OFFICE.

T.J. CONNELLY JR.
MAJOR, USAF
