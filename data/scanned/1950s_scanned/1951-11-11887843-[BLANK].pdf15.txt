Department of the Air Force, HQ., USAF, Wash. 25, D.C. 
20 December 1949

TO: Commanding General, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, Ohio
SUBJECT: Establishment of a project within the Air Force Geophysical Research Program in order to further investigate and accumulate data on the "Light Phenomena".

1. Reference is made to the approval of the Research and Development Board, Office of the Secretary of the Air Force, of 21 November 1949 to include this project in the Sciences Budget. If will be necessary for you to prepare a project plan. This plan should include a statement of the problem, method of approach, scope, personnel required, and necessary measurements. The funds requirement should be stated by object classification.

2. When this necessary information has been received from your command, this headquarters will take appropriate action to secure Research and Development Board approval and to initiate the project.
