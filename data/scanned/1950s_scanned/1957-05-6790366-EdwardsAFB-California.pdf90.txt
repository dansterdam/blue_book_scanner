EDWARDS AFB, CALIFORNIA
27 MARCH 1957
DATA:
At 0800 hours on March 27, 1957, an object was observed moving in the sky over the Edwards Air Force Base, California. The object was first detected by radar personnel and subsequently observed visually. The radar personnel reported the object to be rectangular in shape, approximately 12 feet long and 3 feet wide, moving in a northeasterly direction at an altitude of 15,000 feet. The object was observed visually for a period of about 10 minutes by several witnesses on the ground. The object appeared to be metallic in nature and displayed no lights or other identifying markings. It moved at a uniform speed and in a straight line until it disappeared from view.
