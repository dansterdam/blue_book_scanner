1. Reference AIR 200-2, Subject: INTELLIGENCE: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting.
a. Passed, California 22 April 1957 - CIVILIANS: Residents of Los Angeles, California, reported that they had observed an unusual phenomenon and had submitted a letter to the Headquarters, U.S. Air Force, Washington 25, D.C. on 9 April 1957.

b. INTERVIEW: Special Agent GLENN T. COX interviewed SIMON at his office.
        (1) Description of the object:
            The object observed was described as circular, silvery-white in color, approximately 100-120 feet in diameter, surrounded by an elliptical light with a length radius of 100-120 feet. The elliptical glow was bright reddish-orange in color, while the object itself was silvery-white. The object was sighted and watched for approximately 15 minutes before it disappeared.
        (2) Description of course of object:
            Mr. SIMON related that his attention was directed to this object by its brilliant glow. He first observed it to be moving in a northwest direction at an angle of approximately 60 degrees above the horizon. The object made a very little upward-then-downward movement and then disappeared.
