"No Case Information Only   28 September 1956    Columbus, Ohio

Case 224, Columbus, Ohio, Sept. 28, 1956 - According to Columbus Division, I former member of the ground crew of a T-33 jet fighter witnessed eight cigar-shaped objects follow a T-33 at 11:20 a.m. The pilot of the T-33 was unaware of the objects. Pilot and ground crew identified the objects as aircraft, but said they were unlike anything the pilot had ever seen. They were very slow, almost hovering, and made no noise."
