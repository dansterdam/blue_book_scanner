OPERATIONAL IMMEDIATE
OBSERVATION OF TWO OR THREE UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS AT APPROXIMATELY 2285 EDST. OBJECTS WERE REPORTED TO BE LOCATED EAST OF THE AIRCRAFT ON A HEADING OF 271 DEGREES ON PITTSBURGH OMNI RANGE. AMERICAN 519 WAS LOCATED APPROXIMATELY TEN MILES EAST OF TYRONE PENNA., AT 15:22 WHEN THE SIGHTING OCCURRED. OBJECTS WERE DESCRIBED AS FLYING AROUND A THUNDERSTORM. A AIRLINE PILOT REPORTED THAT LATER THE OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED TO BE FLYING ABOVE HIS AIRCRAFT. THIS LATER OBSERVATION WAS PROBABLY TWO F-86 TYPE AIRCRAFT. FROM GREATER PITTSBURGH AIRPORT INVESTIGATING AIRLINE PILOTS REPORT. WEATHER AT TIME OF OBSERVATION WAS NINE MILES VISIBILITY.
