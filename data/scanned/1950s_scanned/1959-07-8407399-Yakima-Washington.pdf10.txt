AIR TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE CENTER
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE
OHIO

AFOTN-L22 21 Mar 1960

SUBJECT: UFO Sightings, State of Washington 

TO: SAFOI-3d (Maj. L. J. Tacker)

1. It was brought to the attention of ATFC by Congressman from the State of Washington, that during July and August of 1959, there were a number of unidentified flying objects sighted in the State of Washington in the area of Yakima.

2. These objects were described as red, green, white, blue, and yellow.

3. ATFC contacted the 25th Air Division, McChord AFB, Washington and requested information on these sightings. The Directorate of Intelligence of the 25th Air Division informed ATFC on 15 September 1959, that based upon investigation, they determined that these friendly sources were SAC aircraft conducting a refueling mission in the area.

4. It is requested that Congressman Tollefson be informed of our findings concerning these sightings. It is requested that the letter be addressed to the Congressman as requested.

PHILLIP D. EVANS
Deputy for Sciences and Components
FOR THE COMMANDER:
