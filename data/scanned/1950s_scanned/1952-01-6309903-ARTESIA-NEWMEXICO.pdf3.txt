REPORT OF INVESTIGATION

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS SEEN OVER 
ARTESIA, NEW MEXICO, ON 16 JANUARY 1952.

REFERENCE: Telephone conversation, Capt Randall and Lt Col Kelly, 22 April 1952.
WITNESS: Mrs. Randall, a civilian. 
DATE OF INCIDENT: 16 January 1952. 
PLACE OF INCIDENT: Artesia, New Mexico.

SYNOPSIS: Mrs. Randall, who lives in Artesia, New Mexico, reported seeing two unidentified objects in the sky over Artesia on 16 January 1952. According to her statement, the objects were circular in shape, metallic in appearance, and flew at a high rate of speed from west to east. They were observed for approximately 10 seconds. No sound was heard. Mrs. Randall stated that she has had previous experience with aircraft and that she is positive the objects were not conventional aircraft.
