AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

PLACE: TOKYO, JAPAN              DATE: 20 Mar 55 
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS TRACKED BY GROUND RADAR
SOURCE: USAF R-25B 1105-2 Ground Radar

This report forwards information obtained from the interrogation of USAF personnel who visually observed and/or tracked unidentified aerial objects with ground radar at Tokyo, Japan on 20 Mar 55. 

The objects were observed on ground radar and by visual observation by personnel operating the radar set.

The objects were observed for approximately 2 to 3 minutes and were tracked at an average speed of 500 mph at an altitude of 25,000 feet.
The objects appeared to be circular or elliptical in shape.
