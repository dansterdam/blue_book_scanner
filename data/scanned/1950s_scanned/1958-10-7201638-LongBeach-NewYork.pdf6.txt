Big Fiery Object Seen
In Early Morning Sky

A "shooting star". It was reported to have been seen from Spokane, Washington early Friday and a weather observer at Long Beach, N.Y. said "It was so bright i could see fine print 20 minutes later". The object raced from Spokane Valley to the Palouse worxs at Han ford, WN. ED.

Deputy Sheriff William H. Miller of Spokane County said the object lit up the ground around the car in the Spokane Valley for from 12 to 20 seconds and the object cast shadows from nearby objects. Spokane city police said they also saw it. Other reports came in from Colfax.
