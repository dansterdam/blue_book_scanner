REPORT NO. 
DATE: 20 Jun 1952
AREA: Labrador, New Mexico
SUBJECT: Unidentified flying objects
SUMMARY: In accordance with AFR 200-2, dated 29 April 1952, subject: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting, the following report of unidentified flying objects is submitted.
On 20 June 1952, observers from Los Alamos, New Mexico, observed a strange object in the sky. It appeared to be a brilliant light, red in color, and seemed to be oscillating or pulsating. The object remained stationary for several minutes, then suddenly accelerated and disappeared from view. The object was observed by several visual observers. The reliability of the informants is considered good.
