BLUEBIRD REPORT NO. 3
8 June 1954 

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects Sighted Over Savannah, Georgia, 8 June 1954

1. At approximately 0900 hours on 8 June 1954, a local resident of Savannah, Georgia, reported sighting several unidentified flying objects over the city. The objects were described as being brightly illuminated and maneuvering in an erratic fashion at high altitudes.

2. The witness, identified as MR. JOHN DOE, stated that he was standing outside his home when he first noticed the objects in the sky. He described them as being round or disc-shaped in appearance, with a metallic sheen. The objects appeared to be traveling at a high rate of speed, making sudden changes in direction and altitude.

3. MR. DOE reported the sighting to the local authorities, who in turn notified the nearest Air Force base. An investigation was immediately initiated by the Air Force to determine the nature and origin of the unidentified flying objects.

4. The report was forwarded to Project Blue Book for further analysis and evaluation. No additional information is available at this time, as the investigation is still ongoing.
