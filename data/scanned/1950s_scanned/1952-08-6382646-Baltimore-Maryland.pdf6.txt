REPORT OF CAPT P. 0. SHELVIN, MILITARY INTELLIGENCE, 5TH ARMY CORPS, DATED 18 AUG 52 BALTIMORE, MARYLAND.

ON 6 AUG 52, A PRESS RELEASE WAS ISSUED BY THE NAVY DEPARTMENT RELATING A REPORT SUBMITTED BY CAPT P. 0. SHELVIN. CAPT SHELVIN IS AN EXPERIENCED PILUT WITH OVER 2000 HOURS FLYING TIME. HIS REPORT STATES THAT ON 4 AUG 52, HE OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT APPROXIMATELY 40 FEET IN DIAMETER AND 10 TO 15 FEET THICK. IT WAS SMOOTH, METALLIC-LIKE IN APPEARANCE, WITHOUT RIVETS OR SEAMS, AND REMAINED STATIONARY FOR APPROXIMATELY 10 TO 15 MINUTES. IT THEN ACCELERATED RAPIDLY, CLIMBING VERTICALLY AND VANISHING FROM SIGHT.

CAPT SHELVIN STATED THAT HE HAD NEVER SEEN ANYTHING SIMILAR TO THIS OBJECT BEFORE, AND WAS QUITE CERTAIN THAT IT WAS NOT ANY KNOWN TYPE OF AIRCRAFT.

10-5-52
