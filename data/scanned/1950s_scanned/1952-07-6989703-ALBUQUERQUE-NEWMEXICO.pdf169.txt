1 AUGUST 1952
SUBJECT: Transmittal of File Telasri of Unusual Phenomena in the Sky

TO: Director of Intelligence USAF
ATTN: AFOIN-2A
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AFB, OHIO
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

1. In accordance with instructions via phone this date, transcript attached herewith as requested.

2. The individual in the case was interviewed 31 July and stated substantially as follows:

- This object was sighted 230 hours 29 July 1952. It was described as far exceeding in brilliance and size any aircraft he had ever seen. It exhibited a steady, but pulsating light coming from within the object in the background. This object appeared to be approximately 20,000 feet in altitude and of considerable size. The witness further stated that the object, after hovering in his vision for some time, disappeared at a speed which he estimated to be in excess of 1,000 miles per hour.

3. This information is released to the public to relieve queries and statements. This has been coordinated with Mr. A. C. Pratt, Major, USAF.

WILLIAM J. TRAINIS
Lt. Colonel, USAF
Intelligence Officer
