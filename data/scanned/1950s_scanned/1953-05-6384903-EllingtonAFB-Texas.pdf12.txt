SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT, ELLINGTON AFB, TEXAS, 4 MAY 1953
1. FORWARDED HERE WITH IS A REPORT CONCERNING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OBSERVED BY SEVERAL AIRMEN AT ELLINGTON AFB, TEXAS, ON 4 MAY 1953.
2. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS A BRIGHT LIGHT WHICH APPEARED TO BE HOVERING IN THE SKY. IT WAS FIRST OBSERVED BY AIRMEN STANDING OUTSIDE THE BASE OPERATIONS BUILDING AND LATER SIGHTED BY AIRMEN ON DUTY IN THE RADAR OBSERVATION TOWER.
3. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR A PERIOD OF APPROXIMATELY 45 MINUTES BEFORE IT DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
4. AN ATTEMPT WAS MADE TO TRACK THE OBJECT WITH RADAR, BUT NO SIGNAL WAS OBTAINED.
5. IT IS REQUESTED THAT THIS MATTER BE INVESTIGATED FURTHER AND THAT THIS OFFICE BE ADVISED OF THE RESULTS OF THE INVESTIGATION.
