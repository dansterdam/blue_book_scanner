SUBJECT: (UNCLASSIFIED) Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object near Birmingham, Alabama, 5 June 56

SPECIAL INQUIRY 
TO:
Commander
Air Defense Command
Mt. AFB
Colorado Springs, Colorado

1. The following is for your information and any action warranted:

Arnold B. Zimmerman, 12364345, and Major Robert L. Collier, AD 829639, and Major 
Collier, Maxwell AFB, Alabama, advised S/A Barnidge that at 01302 hours, 5 June 1956, they had sighted an unidentified object flying in the vicinity of Birmingham, Alabama. In this connection, the following information was furnished:

"a. Description
(1) The object was described as being nearly circular in shape and flat by Major Collier, and by Major Zimmerman as an ellipse shaped object.
(2) The size was described by both officers as being similar to a small American type fighter aircraft, approximately 50 to 55 feet in diameter and 6 to 8 feet in thickness.
(3) The color was indicated as aluminum or silver.
(4) Only one object was sighted and there were no tails or trail or exhaust. There was also no sound noted above the sound of the B-25 aircraft in which the officers were flying."

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS;
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
