This report describes progress on Project Stark, PPS-100, for the period from June 6, 1952, to July 7, 1952.

Panel of Consultants
Dr. J. Allen Hynek, Professor of Astronomy, Director of the Mc Millin Observatory, and Assistant Dean of the Graduate School at Ohio State University, was employed to consult on astronomical aspects of the work involved in this project. The Tentative Observer's Data Sheet, Exhibit 1, enclosed in the June 6 report, was studied by Dr. Hynek and some changes and additions were made in accordance with his suggestions.
On June 22, Dr. Hynek started a tour to interview several professional and amateur astronomer groups. The purposes of these interviews are:
1. To learn if any competent people in this profession have made sightings which have not been reported.
