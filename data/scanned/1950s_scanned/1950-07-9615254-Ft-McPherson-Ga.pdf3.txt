SUMMARY OF INFORMATION

At approximately 1025, 10 July 1950, WOJG of the 111th CIC Detachment, Headquarters Third Army, Fort McPherson, Georgia, reported an unidentified object in the direction of northwest. This object was sighted by the detachment and WOJG reported it was flying in a northeasterly direction. The object appeared small and had a bright metallic appearance. It was later obscured by low hanging clouds. Object was moving in an easterly direction at a slow rate of speed. It was at an unpredictable height, very high. OSI, Marietta, Georgia was notified immediately.
