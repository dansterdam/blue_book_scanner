SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT, 7 MAY 1957

1. On 7 May 1957, at approximately 2230 hours, the control tower operator at Edwards Air Force Base observed an unidentified object in the sky. The object was moving in a north-south direction at a rapid but constant speed. It was visible for approximately 30 seconds.

2. The object was described as round in shape, very bright, and white in color. It appeared to be at an altitude of approximately 20,000 feet. No sound was heard in connection with the object.

4. The control tower operator reported the sighting immediately to the base operations office. A search was made of the area, but no further observation of the object was made.
