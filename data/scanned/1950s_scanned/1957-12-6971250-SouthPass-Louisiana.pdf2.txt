FLYING OBJECT SIGHT WISE-PILOT OF PATTEN MOTOR COMPANY PLANE OWNED BY A-. W.. EWING OF-OLD FORTUNA, LA PASS

Sighting: Pilot for Patten Motor Company plane, owned by A.W. Ewing of Old Fortuna, Louisiana, reported sighting an unusual object while in flight at an altitude of 6,000 feet on December 6, 1957. The object was described as round and white in color, moving rapidly at an estimated speed of 500-600 mph. The object was observed for about 1/2 hour by the pilot and his copilot while en route from a VHF CG light station south of Pass-a-Loutre to Old Fortuna. The object was considered to be reliable and of unusual nature, but the report does not make any conclusions or identify the object.
