UNCLASSIFIED
AIR MAIL

File No: 11D 21-0 3 OCT 1952
REPORT OF INTELLIGENCE REPORT

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object at Little Rock, Arkansas

TO: Director of Special Investigations
Headquarters United States Air Force
Washington 25, D. C.

1. SYNOPSIS: Mrs. Silas Sims, Little Rock, Arkansas, reported she observed an unidentified object which appeared in the sky over Little Rock for approximately 1030 hours on 15 September 1952. She took a photograph of an unidentified flying object which appeared in the sky over Little Rock.

2. DETAILS: Mrs. Silas Sims, of the Jones Corporation, 2000 Martin Field, Little Rock, Arkansas, reported to the 11th District Office of Special Investigations, Little Rock, Arkansas, on 29 September 1952, that on 15 September 1952, she was standing on her front porch, facing south, when she observed a disc shaped object in the sky. The object, which had a silver color, was moving in a northerly direction at a rapid rate of speed. She stated that the object had no visible means of propulsion and that it made no noise. She immediately took a photograph of the object with her personal camera. The negative of this photograph, which is approximately 2 by 2 1/2 inches in size, shows an object which appears to be a disc shaped flying object. The photograph was shown to several persons who agreed that the object in the photograph was unidentified.

The original photograph has been reproduced as a 5 by 7 inch print. The original negative has been returned to Mrs. Sims per her request.
