At 1130Q on 8 April 1953, several persons stationed at Fort
Buchanan, San Juan Puerto Rico observed an unidentified flying
object variously described as a "bright star", "bright ball",
"bright ball of fire," at a great height.

Attached hereto are the statements of the various witnesses to
the sighting.

An investigation at Ramey revealed that two RB-36 aircraft
were in the area at the time of this sighting. The RB-36
were flying at altitudes of 12,000 and 21,000 feet.

Bradford Shunan
Major, USAF
