SUBJECT: Flash Report on UFO, Sharon, Georgia

The following information is reported to you because of the continued interest that has been shown in unidentified flying objects which have been reported in this area. Inquiries will be made to you.

As 1300 hours, 21 Oct 59, a mix-like substance continued to fall from the sky over Sharon, Ga. for almost 3 hours, according to UFO regulations, this substance was collected and delivered to the FBI office, Robins AFB, Ga. under case # 59-138.

In a telephone conversation between Maj. _ and Maj. _ it was reported that the substance (called "angels' hair") have been reported numerous times during the last few years.
