OBJECT Unidentified Flying Object

APR 18 1959 
The weather observer of the Freiburg Weather Bureau saw on Thursday evening at 2322 hours a shining, glowing disc-shaped object surrounded by a tremendous light from North North West to South South East. The light gradually faded from sight. Two American students - the husband and wife - who were studying physics at the Freiburg University saw the same strange object at the same time.
