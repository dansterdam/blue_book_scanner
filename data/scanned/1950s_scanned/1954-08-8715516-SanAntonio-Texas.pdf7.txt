AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
AT: SAN ANTONIO, TEXAS
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT 
DATE: 28 AUGUST 1954
OPERATION DIVISION, ARMORED SECTION, LACKLAND AFB, TEXAS
DESCRIPTION OF INFORMATION: This report states that on 28 August 1954, an unidentified aerial object was sighted near San Antonio, Texas. The object was described as a bright, almost white light which turned into an almost red light before the object disappeared into the distance. The object continued to be watched in the sky and was estimated to be traveling at a speed of around 700 to 750 miles per hour, appearing from the north and traveling to the south. The source of this information is an Intelligence Instructor with the USAF pre-flight school at this location.
