AIR TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE CENTER
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE
OHIO

16 September 1952

Commanding Officer
Air National Guard
Columbus Air Force Base
Columbus 7, Ohio

Dear Sir:

It would be appreciated if you would furnish the Air Technical Intelligence Center with the following information:

a. On 22 July 1952, between 2200 and 0100 the following morning, were any aircraft airborne which carried lights other than conventional navigation lights?

b. On 25 August 1952, between 2000 and 2230, any aircraft airborne with the same characteristics?

c. A description of the aircraft and the lights, if any, were airborne.

Your cooperation would be appreciated.

Sincerely yours,

/s/ Donald E. Dupuy
Colonel, USAF
Chief, Technical Analysis Division
