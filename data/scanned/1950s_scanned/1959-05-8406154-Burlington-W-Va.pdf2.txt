2-2 MAY 1959
report
1. This report was made on May 8, 1959, based on information received from Colonel John D. Whitted, QAC, Director of Operations, Headquarters Mid-Continental Air Defense Force, Ent Air Force Base, Colorado Springs, Colorado.
2. It was reported that on May 2, 1959, at approximately 2345 hours, a RAPCON radar operator at the Burlington, West Virginia Radar Site, observed an unidentified target on his radar scope. The target was tracked for approximately 10 minutes, during which time it covered approximately 13 miles. No visual observation of the target was made.
3. Colonel Whitted stated that this incident had been thoroughly checked and investigated, and that the radar site was a reputable and reliable one. However, no positive identification of the target could be made.
