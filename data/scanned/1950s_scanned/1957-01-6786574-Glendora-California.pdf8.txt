No Case (Information Only)                   2 January 1957
                                                      Warren, Connecticut

1957, Jan.2---Warren,Conn.---10:45pm est---A large,green UFO that changed from green to red, to yellow, then back to green! It whirled about and glided down from the sky softly- then disappeared! A clear, cold night. Reported by woman on her way to work.
