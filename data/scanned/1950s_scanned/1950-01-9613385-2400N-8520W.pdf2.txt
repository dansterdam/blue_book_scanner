UNCLASSIFIED
UNITED STATES (GULF OF MEXICO)
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
DATE OF INFORMATION: 29 January 1950
SOURCE: 307TH BOMBARDMENT WING (L)
SUBJECT: RADAR PHENOMENA
SUMMARY: Personnel aboard B-29 aircraft over the Gulf of Mexico noted in the radar "scope" three objects orbiting the aircraft from all quadrants with speeds estimated up to 3,000 miles per hour, no visual sightings were made.
