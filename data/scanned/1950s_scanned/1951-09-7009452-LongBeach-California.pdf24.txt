HEADQUARTERS 27th AIR DIVISION (DEFENSE)
NORTON AIR FORCE BASE
SAN BERNARDINO, CALIFORNIA

UNCLASSIFIED

DEC 21 1951

SUBJECT: (Restricted) High Flying Unidentified Object

TO: Commanding General
Air Technical Intelligence Center
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Dayton, Ohio

1. Reference is made to your letter ACIN-ATIAA-2c dated 23 November 1951. In compliance with your request, the following information is submitted in answer to the questions contained in paragraph (3) of your letter:

a. Original sighting of object was made visually at 0715 PST from position over Santa Monica Bay as indicated on attached map overlay.

b. Element making original sighting took off from George Air Force Base.

c. Reports that object was directly over Burbank and March Air Force Base at approximately 0720 PST, making a land fix at high altitude. (Based on later interrogation of pilots by DI of ATCC Fighter-Interceptor Wing, when reports indicated both sightings at the same time.)

d. Paragraph 1 c above applies.

e. Norton Air Force Base and March Air Force Base were queried as to the launching of any type balloon, or any other object that might have had the appearance of the object in question. From those queries it was determined that no balloons or other objects were launched from either of those bases not replying to date, and their reply will be forwarded to your headquarters.

UNCLASSIFIED
