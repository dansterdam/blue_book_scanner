HQ 4706th Defense Wing, Intel OOC.9 Subjects: Recovery of Unidentified Flying Object

Intel OOC.9 (31 May 52)  
Air Technical Intelligence Center, Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio

TO: Commanding Officer, Hq 4706th Defense Wing, O'Hare International Airport, Frank Ridge, Illinois

1. Reference is made to your Report 4706th-2-52 dated 29 May 52.

2. An analysis of the object shows that the object is a large glass marble covered with a tan-like substance. The origin of the object or the coating is unknown.

3. It would be possible to determine the energy necessary to propel the object as mentioned in the original report. However, it is not deemed advisable to do this due to time and financial factors.

4. Your cooperation in forwarding this object is appreciated.

DONALD L. BOWER
Colonel, USAF
Chief, Technical Analysis Division
