AF FORM 112--PART II
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

HEADQUARTERS TACTICAL AIR COMMAND
LANGLEY AIR FORCE BASE, VIRGINIA
6 NOVEMBER 1957

CLASSIFICATION: UNCLASSIFIED

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS

1. On 6 November 1957 at approximately 2330 hours, a police officer on patrol in Las Vegas, Nevada reported observing an unidentified flying object. The object was described as a yellow-orange light, approximately 30 feet in diameter, moving in a horizontal direction at a high rate of speed. The object was visible for approximately 3 minutes.

2. This is the only report received by this headquarters from the Las Vegas area on this date. No other pertinent information is available.

3. This report has been classified UNCLASSIFIED.

JAMES E. FUCHS 
2Lt, USAF
Assistant Intelligence Officer
