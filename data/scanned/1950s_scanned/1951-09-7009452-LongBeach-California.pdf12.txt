TRANSCRIBED - 22 September 1951

On 22 September 1951 at 0830 PST, an unidentified object was sighted over Long Beach, California. The object was described as spherical in shape, silvery in color, and estimated to be approximately 50 feet in diameter. The estimated altitude of between 50,000 ft. and 55,000 ft. The object appeared to be a static, high-altitude, flight-type aircraft, further described as having no visible means of propulsion.

Weather
Negligible at the time.

Description
Three disc-shaped balloons were seen, long beach and Santa Monica areas, at approximately the same time, traveling in a northerly direction at a high rate of speed. As the "object" passed over the area, the three balloons disappeared.

All of the major aircraft stations and installations contacted reported negative sightings, and no indications of unusual activity were observed.

Additional information has been requested as to additional details of the sighting and investigation of the incident by the Project Blue Book and other possible balloon launchings.
