ALEXANDRIA AFB, LA
6 NOVEMBER 1954
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLY-ING OBJECT SIGHTING
TO: COMMANDER 
AIR FORCE AIRDEFENSE COMMAND 
ENT AFB COLORADO
1. ON 6 NOVEMBER 1954 AT APPROX 1930 HOURS LOCAL TIME, A MASTER SGT AND 1ST LT WERE ON DUTY IN THE RADAR OPERATIONS ROOM AT ALEXANDRIA AFB LA. BOTH OFFICERS OBSERVED A BRIGHT WHITE LIGHT HOVERING AT AN ESTIMATED ALTITUDE OF 20,000 TO 25,000 FEET. THE OBJECT REMAINED STABLE FOR APPROXIMATELY 30 SECONDS, THEN CLIMBED TO AN ESTIMATED 40,000 TO 50,000 FEET AND ACCELERATED IN A NORTH EASTERLY DIRECTION TILL IT DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT IN APPROXIMATELY 10 SECONDS.
2. THERE WAS NO RADAR CONTACT ON THE OBJECT AT ANY TIME IT WAS UNDER OBSERVATION.
3. THE APPEARANCE OF THE OBJECT AND ITS BEHAVIOR WERE UNLIKE ANYTHING PREVIOUSLY OBSERVED BY EITHER OF THE OFFICERS. THEY WERE UNABLE TO OFFER ANY IDENTIFICA-TION OR EXPLANATION OF THE OBSERVED PHENOMENON.
4. THIS IS BEING REPORTED AS AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SIGHTING.
