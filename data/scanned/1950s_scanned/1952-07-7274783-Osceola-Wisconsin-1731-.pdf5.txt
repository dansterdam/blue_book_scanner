Osceola, Wisconsin July 7, 1952
ON 29 JULY 1952 MR VINCENT L RASMUSSEN OF OSCEOLA, WISCONSIN, REPORTED TO THE OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS (OSI), MALMSTROM AIR FORCE BASE, MONTANA, THAT ON 7 JULY 1952 AT APPROXIMATELY 8:45 PM HE AND SEVERAL OTHER PERSONS OBSERVED A STRANGE BRIGHT LIGHT HOVERING IN THE SKY OVER THEIR HOME IN OSCEOLA, WISCONSIN. THE LIGHT WAS ESTIMATED TO BE APPROXIMATELY ONE QUARTER TO ONE HALF MILE DISTANT.
RASMUSSEN STATED THAT THE LIGHT APPEARED TO BE ATTACHED TO A ROUND, DARK OBJECT OF UNDEFINED SIZE. HE DESCRIBED THE LIGHT AS BEING QUITE BRILLIANT AND CONSTANTLY CHANGING IN SIZE AND INTENSITY. AT TIMES THE LIGHT APPEARED TO BE EMITTING RAYS OR BEAMS.
RASMUSSEN STATED THAT HE WATCHED THE OBJECT FOR APPROXIMATELY 30 MINUTES BEFORE IT SUDDENLY DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. HE STATED THAT HE AND SEVERAL OTHER WITNESSES TRIED UNSUCCESSFULLY TO TAKE PHOTOGRAPHS OF THE OBJECT.
THIS OFFICE HAS NOT BEEN ABLE TO OBTAIN ANY PHOTOGRAPHS OR OTHER TANGIBLE EVIDENCE REGARDING THIS SIGHTING. FURTHER INVESTIGATION HAS FAILED TO DISCLOSE ANY ADDITIONAL INFORMATION.
