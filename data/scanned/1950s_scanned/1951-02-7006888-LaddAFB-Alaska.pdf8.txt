TITLE: UNUSUAL SIGHTING ON 25 FEBRUARY 1951
AT LADD AFB, FAIRBANKS, ALASKA
BY: 500th AIR BASE GROUP
PERIOD: 25 February - 1 March 1951
OFFICE OF ORIGIN: 
DD.52, LADD AFB, ALASKA 
STATUS: CLOSED
CHARACTER: SPECIAL INQUIRY - UNUSUAL SIGHTING
REFERENCE: None, this is an initial report.
SYNOPSIS:
Investigation initiated 25 February 1951 prompted on information from 1st Lt OLLIE D. RUBLE, AP-19/29570L, Operations Officer, 11th Air Division, that personnel of the 500th Air Base Group had sighted an unusual unidentified object on 25 February 1951.
Investigation discloses that on 25 February 1951, at approximately 2135 hours, 25 February 1951, allegedly sighted a gray object hovering over the Ladd AFB at an altitude of 10,000 feet for about 12 to 15 minutes and resembling a flattened football, showed to the north after emitting a single white smoke ring. Object was not observed by the weather observation balloons or aircraft in the area at that time. No photographic evidence or additional witnesses were located.
