AF FORM 112 - PART II 
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: 9 July 1953 report/Perrin AFB, Texas
On the night of 9 July 1953, at 2139 hours, the control tower at Perrin Air Force Base, Texas, received a phone call from the pilot of a T-33 jet aircraft who reported sighting an unidentified flying object. The object was described as being round and white in color, and was observed to be going straight up. The pilot stated that he had made two passes at the object but was unable to get closer than a mile. The light was said to be going straight up.
