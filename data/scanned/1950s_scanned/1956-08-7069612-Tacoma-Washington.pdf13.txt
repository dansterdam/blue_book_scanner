No Case (Information Only)	12 August 1956
Parkersburg, West Virginia

Case 187, Parkersburg, W. Va., Aug. 12, 1956 - According to the Parkersburg Sentinel the UFO was seen by a Sentinel reporter and his family had a 15-second view before the mystery object disappeared. The object looked round with a bright glow around the size and altitude could not be determined. It appeared to be traveling at a high rate of speed with no sound. The observer said it came from the north and looked like a shooting star. They tried to trace it into the horizon but it seemed to disappear suddenly. At the request by the Sentinel asking for verification of the sighting brought results. Said the newspaper, "As least five residents reported seeing the same object moving across the sky. All three witnesses said the object looked round and appeared to be orange-silver in color. One observer said at first she thought it was a plane but as it moved closer she could see it moving very fast. Still later reports confirming the object came from Vienna, Belpre, Williamstown and Harrisville."
