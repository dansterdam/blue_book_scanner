Air Intelligence Information Report
Wichita Falls, Texas, 9 July 1953
Subject was sighted by several people in the sky at Wichita Falls, Texas. The object was described as being silvery-white in color, shaped like a disc, and slightly concave on the underside. It was traveling in a southwest direction at a very fast speed, with no sound or trailing flames. The object was observed for approximately 2-3 minutes before disappearing from sight. The sighting was relayed to the local Air Force base.
