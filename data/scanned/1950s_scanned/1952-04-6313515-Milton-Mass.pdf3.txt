1. REPORT OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATION: 
Based on information received, this case has been thoroughly investigated by the Air Force Office of Special Investigations. The following results have been obtained:
ON 25 APRIL 1954, SPECIAL AGENT [REDACTED], OSAI [REDACTED], INTERVIEWED [REDACTED], TELEPHONE [REDACTED], MILTON, MASSACHUSETTS, REGARDING HIS REPORTED SIGHTING OF AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT OVER MILTON, MASSACHUSETTS, ON 25 MARCH 1954. [REDACTED] STATED THAT HE AND HIS WIFE WERE SITTING IN THE LIVING ROOM OF THEIR HOME ON THAT DATE WHEN THEY OBSERVED A BRIGHT, FLASHING OBJECT IN THE SKY. HE ESTIMATED THE OBJECT TO BE AT AN ALTITUDE OF APPROXIMATELY 10,000 FEET AND IT APPEARED TO BE TRAVELING AT A SPEED OF 300 TO 400 MILES PER HOUR. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 1 MINUTE AND 45 SECONDS, DURING WHICH TIME IT FLASHED INTERMITTENTLY.
