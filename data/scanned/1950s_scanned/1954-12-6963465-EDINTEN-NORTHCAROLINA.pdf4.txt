AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
DATE 6 DEC 54
SUBJECT UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT SIGHTED AT EDEN, NORTH CAROLINA ON 14 NOV 54
SOURCE AIR DEFENSE COMMAND, DIRECTORATE OF INTELLIGENCE, ATIC, WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO
CLASSIFIED DATA DELETED
1. REFER TO PREVIOUS REPORT SUBMITTED ON THIS INCIDENT.
2. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION OBTAINED FROM FURTHER INVESTIGATION OF THIS INCIDENT IS AS FOLLOWS:
A. WITNESS GERALD CROMER, 30 YEARS OLD, DRIVER FOR TOBACCO WAREHOUSE, EDEN, NORTH CAROLINA, HAS BEEN LOCATED AND INTERVIEWED.
B. CROMER STATES THAT HE SIGHTED THE UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT AT APPROXIMATELY 1815 HOURS ON 14 NOV 54 WHILE DRIVING ALONE ON HIGHWAY 14 APPROXIMATELY 4 MILES SOUTH OF EDEN, NORTH CAROLINA.
C. HE STATES THAT HE FIRST NOTICED THE OBJECT IN THE WESTERN SKY TO HIS RIGHT WHILE DRIVING NORTH. IT APPEARED TO BE HOVERING OR STATIONARY AT FIRST.
D. HE STATED THAT AS HE CONTINUED DRIVING, THE OBJECT BEGAN TO MOVE AND APPEARED TO BE MANEUVERING OR FLYING IN AN UNDULATING MOTION. HE WATCHED IT FOR APPROXIMATELY 15 TO 20 MINUTES AS HE DROVE NORTH ON HIGHWAY 14.
E. CROMER DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS BEING ROUND OR OVAL IN SHAPE, APPROXIMATELY 40 TO 50 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND HAVING A BRIGHT WHITE OR SILVER COLOR. HE STATED IT APPEARED TO HAVE NO VISIBLE MEANS OF PROPULSION AND MADE NO SOUND. HE DESCRIBED IT AS UNLIKE ANY AIRCRAFT HE HAD EVER SEEN.
F. CROMER STATED THAT WHEN HE ARRIVED HOME, HE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED HIS SIGHTING TO THE SHERIFF'S OFFICE IN EDEN.
