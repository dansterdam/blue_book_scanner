REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT SEEN ON 28 SEP 60

Sighting of a very bright light about 1 1/2 times size of full moon. Object appeared to be hovering and pulsating. It was observed for over 40 minutes. Three witnesses saw the object which made a definite westerly movement, then returned to its original position. The witnesses could not identify the object. It appeared to be higher than jet aircraft observed in the area at the time. The object was observed to be at a constant elevation and not coming closer or going further away. It was reported to the Air Force Intelligence Officer at Lowry AFB, who stated the Air Force had no information on this particular object.
