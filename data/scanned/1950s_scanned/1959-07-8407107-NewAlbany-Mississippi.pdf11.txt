New Albany, Mississippi
September 16, 1959

Dear Sir;

Please excuse this delay but after completing 
the form on the sixth of August I misplaced 
it and consequently thought nothing more about 
it. I nearly came across it in some 
old letters and am now forwarding it
to you.

Very sincerely yours,
