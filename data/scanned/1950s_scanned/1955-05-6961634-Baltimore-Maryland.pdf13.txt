DISTRIBUTION:
FROM: Capt AK	TO: 	CONTROL NO:

SUBJECT: FLYING SAUCERS OVER BALTIMORE, MD.

ON MAY 6 1955 AT 7:00 PM EDT, FOUR BRIGHT LIGHT OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED FROM THE EDGE OF BALTIMORE, MD. TRAVELLING IN STRAIGHT LINES AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED. OBJECTS WERE VISIBLE FOR ABOUT 5 MINUTES. THEY THEN DISAPPEARED IN THE DISTANCE BEHIND SOME CLOUDS. ALL FOUR OBJECTS WERE MOVING AT THE SAME SPEED AND ALTITUDE. THEY APPEARED TO BE DISC-SHAPED AND LUMINOUS, GIVING OFF A YELLOW-WHITE GLOW.

WITNESSES INCLUDED A RETIRED MILITARY OFFICER AND OTHER RELIABLE CITIZENS. NO SOUND WAS HEARD FROM THE OBJECTS. WEATHER WAS CLEAR WITH HIGH CLOUD COVER.

IT IS THE OPINION OF THIS OFFICE THAT THE OBSERVED PHENOMENON WAS OF A GENUINE UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT NATURE.
