DISPOSITION FORM
FILE NO.
SUBJECT Officers of the Day Report  
TO ATIC FROM EWGES DATE 12 May 1953 COMMENT NO. 1
The following is for your information and/or necessary action taken from the report of the Field Officer of the Day and Assistant Officer of the Day #1 for the period beginning 0830, 11 May 53 to 0830, 12 May 53:
A telephone call was received at 0410 EST, 12 May, from Mr. (deleted) (Tel) 275-2701, Dayton, Ohio, reporting a flying object moving slowly over Route 49 near the Miami Valley Golf Course. The object was observed for about 5 min. This object, oval in shape, was estimated to be about 50 ft in diameter. It was observed to be in level flight, at an estimated altitude of 1500-2000 ft. The object was observed to glow with a red light, white on the bottom, and remained in sight about 5 min. The object was then observed to accelerate very rapidly and disappear to the west within a few seconds. The report is submitted for your information.
Capt Wood, ATIC Duty Officer, was given the above information at 0416 EST.
(signed)
FRANCIS J. VENSEL, 1st Lt., USAF
ASST AI ADJUTANT GENERAL
