16 December 1958

Dear Mr. ***,

Major Robert J. Friend of the Air Technical Intelligence Center has asked me to write and thank you for the photographs you so graciously provided. Under separate cover, I am returning those photographs to you, as they would be viewed by Air Technical Intelligence Center personnel only.

Upon completion of the Air Technical Intelligence Center analysis and your evaluation, the original photographs will be returned to you.
