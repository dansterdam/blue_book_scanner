UNCLASSIFIED

SECRET INTELLIGENCE REPORT 

SUBJECT: Flying Discs
CHICAGO, Northwest Side 
2300 hours, 30 July 1952

TO: Director of Special Investigations 
Headquarters United States Air Force
Washington 25, D. C. 

1. SYNOPSIS: Flying discs seen by resident of Chicago about 2300 hours, 30 July 1952.

2. DETAILS: This office has been advised by the Federal Bureau of Investigation, Chicago, that on 30 July 1952 this agency was advised that a Chicago, Illinois, housewife had reported that she had seen what she believed to be "flying discs" at 2300 hours on 29 July 1952, while in the vicinity of Portage Park and Addison Street, Chicago. The woman stated that about six (6) or seven (7) objects appeared to be circular in shape, were gray in color, and moved in a diagonal line across the sky. The witness estimated that the objects were at an altitude of approximately six (6) or seven (7) thousand feet and that they were not in any formation but were independently flying and maneuvered on a vertical plane. She estimated that the objects were approximately six (6) or seven (7) feet in diameter and were quite large in appearance. No sound was reported to be associated with the objects.

3. ARTICLE: Two (2) copies of this report being transmitted through channels to the Director of Intelligence, United States Air Force, Washington, D. C., and to the Director of Central Intelligence, Central Intelligence Agency, with current data and information.
