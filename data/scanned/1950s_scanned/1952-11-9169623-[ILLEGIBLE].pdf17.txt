SUBJECT: Sighting of Unidentified Objects at your station 13 Nov 52

TO: Commanding Officer
7793th ACAW Squadron 
Orchard, Louisiana

1. It is requested that you complete the enclosed radar observation data sheet in connection with the sighting of unidentified objects over your station on 13 November 1952. In the event that you have no AP scope 11-23 at your station, it is requested that the additional information be submitted by letter.

2. Do you feel that there may be a connection between the visual sightings by the respective observers at Orchard and your radar observations? We would appreciate your opinion on this.
