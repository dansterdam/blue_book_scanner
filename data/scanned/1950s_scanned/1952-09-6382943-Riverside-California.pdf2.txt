AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
COUNTRY: United States 
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Sighting Reporting
DATE OF OBSERVATION: 2 September 1952
LOCATION: Riverside, California, U.S.A.
SUMMARY: On 2 September 1952 at 2345 hours PDST, four (4) civilians observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Riverside, California. The object was described as star-like in appearance, blue, red, and yellow in color, and moving erratically from side to side and up and down. The object was observed for approximately 2 minutes. No identifying marks or characteristics were visible and the reliability of the observation is unknown. Wind velocity and direction is also unknown.
