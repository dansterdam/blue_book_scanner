At 2013 C.S.T. the Minneapolis filter center reported that a Mr. Rittenhouse, a Ground Observer, reported sighting a cigar-shaped object, flying northwest of his home in Hopkins, Minn. Mr. Rittenhouse got in his car and went down the road to observe the object with powerful telescope and binoculars. He stated the object was 15 miles long, and was star like, and changing from brilliant to dull, the point-of-the fan shape object was pointed down and it looked like a pillar of light.
