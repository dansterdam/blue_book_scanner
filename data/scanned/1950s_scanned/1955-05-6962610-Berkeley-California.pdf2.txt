SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT - BERKELEY, CALIFORNIA
SUMMARY: ON 26 MAY 1955, RESIDENTS OF BERKELEY, CALIFORNIA, OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER THE CITY. DETAILS OF THE SIGHTING ARE AS FOLLOWS:
AT APPROXIMATELY 2200 HOURS ON 26 MAY 1955, SEVERAL BERKELEY, CALIFORNIA, RESIDENTS OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER THE CITY. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS BEING ROUND IN SHAPE, APPROXIMATELY 30-40 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND EMITTING A REDDISH-ORANGE GLOW. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 5-10 MINUTES, DURING WHICH TIME IT HOVERED ABOVE THE CITY AND MOVED ERRATICALLY AT HIGH SPEED BEFORE DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.
