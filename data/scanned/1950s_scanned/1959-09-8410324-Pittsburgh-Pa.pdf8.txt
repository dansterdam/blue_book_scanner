Following is an extract from ATIC Duty Officers Report dtd 14-15 September 1959 for your information:

0930 - The following message was received by telephone from L/Col Thos M Collins, Atlanta, Georgia: He stated that this message was passed to him from AFOIN Indications Center, Washington D.C. by A. D. Linder:

"At 0300Z, 15 Sep 59, a small cone-shaped object with bright lights attached to it was sighted over Atlanta, Georgia. Object moved at 500-600 knots 2,000 feet altitude, ground clutter around object is one shaped object, the lights across the horizon for a distance of several miles. No further information given. Object disappeared from sight into clouds, but does not burn or blink when object laid on. No noise; no trail. Object size of comet head. Cable attached to object 15 to 20 feet in possession of Squirrel Hill police. 04 Sept is in possession of Squirrel Hill Policeman.

2330 - Air Command Post called and reported receipt of following messages from various AF, USA, FAA. Air Force Command Post calls AFOIN that squirrel hill police demand the returned article found by them which they believe incident a hoax."

Did not call Major Rinehart and spare time because of short time remaining before duty hours. He should be notified however as he plans to take some positive action this morning.

/s/ RONDELL
Captain, USAF
