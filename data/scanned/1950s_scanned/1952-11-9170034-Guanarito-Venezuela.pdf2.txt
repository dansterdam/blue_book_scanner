According to press report flying saucers were seen by the people of GUANARITO, VENEZUELA on 19 November during evening hours. The whole population of this town watched for several seconds two misterious aircraft of an unknown type flying at a very fast speed in a north direction. Later they were joined by two other aircraft of a smaller size, then the four aircraft disappeared into the clouds.

COMMENTS of Preparing Officers:

- The last time the papers reported flying saucers (August 1952), Lt. Colonel Felix Romin Mousse, Acting Chief General Staff and Chief Venezuelan Air Force, advised the president that he had jet Venmues flying in the area. at the time of this Sighting reported by civilians on the grounds.
