United States
Intelligence Section, 3520th Flying Training Wing, Lowry Af Base
27 July 1952

Dear John J. Honeywell, Intelligence Officer,
AT THIS 20-20

SUBJECT: AF Report, Wichita, Kansas
Colonel Dunn of the 4450th Maintenance and Supply Group, Wichita, Kansas, reported sighting an unidentified object at 7:30 PM, CST, 26 July 1952.

Colonel Dunn stated that the object appeared to be a round, white, luminous object, approximately 25-30 feet in diameter, which hovered and then took off at a very high rate of speed. The object was observed through binoculars by Colonel Dunn and other personnel. There were no sound or vapor trail.

APPROVED:
JOHN J. FORD
Maj. USAF
Intelligence Officer
