AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: FLYOBJECT
DATA: On 13 January 1953, between 1548 hours and 1300 hours, air defense radar sites at George Airforce Base, near Victorville, California, observed an unidentified flying object while on a routine training mission. The object was tracked on radar and visually observed by the duty controller, who estimated the speed of the object to be in excess of 1000 miles per hour. The object was observed to make sudden maneuvers and changes in altitude, reaching an estimated altitude of approximately 60,000 feet. The object was tracked over a period of approximately 45 minutes.
