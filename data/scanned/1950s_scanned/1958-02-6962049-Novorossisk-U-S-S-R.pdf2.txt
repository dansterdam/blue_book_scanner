INFORMATION:
1. ON 26 JANUARY 1958 AT APPROXIMATELY 12:30 A.M., TURKISH TIME, A LUMINOUS OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY PERSONNEL OF THE TURKISH AIR FORCE NEAR NOVOROSSISK (44°44 'N-33°47'E), U.S.S.R.

6. AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT: THIS IS THE SECOND TIME (CASE 12-1-57) THAT AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT HAS BEEN REPORTED IN THIS AREA.
