Kirtland AFB, Massiesippi R3‑2‑52

Report of Sighting of Unidentified Object

Information Pertinent to Object Sighted:

1. Shape:

Inasmuch as the object was sighted by radar, a description of its shape is restricted to what appeared on the PPI scope. Observer stated that target appeared in a regular tear-drop shape.

2. Size:

The size of the object was estimated as being approximately that of a C-54 aircraft.

3. Color:

Not applicable due to sighting by radar.

4. Number:

One (1)

5. Trail or exhaust:

Not applicable due to sighting by radar.

6. Propulsion system:

Not applicable due to sighting by radar.

7. Speed:

The speed of the object was estimated as being forty (40) to forty-five (45) miles per hour.

8. Maneuver:

The target moved very slowly south for approximately two (2) minutes, moving only a mile or so; it then stopped for a period of four (4) minutes, it then began to move very slowly northward for three (3) minutes, stopped for two (2) minutes and disappeared. After approximately three (3) minutes, the target re-appeared and begun moving north at a more rapid pace and disappeared completely at a distance of fifty (50) miles from the site of the radar set.

9. Sound:

Not applicable due to sighting by radar.

10. Manner of Disappearance:

See 8 above.
