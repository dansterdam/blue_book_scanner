AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects
OBSERVER: USAF, Nebraska
DATE OF REPORT: 24 September 1952
DATE OF INCIDENT: 9 April 1952
SUMMARY: On 9 April 1952 at about 2020 CST, two witnesses, one airman of the 25th Air Division, USAF, and one civilian, observed three unidentified flying objects over Offutt Air Force Base, Nebraska. The objects were described as being in a straight line formation, bright white or silver in color, and traveling at a high rate of speed. The objects were observed for approximately 3-4 minutes before disappearing. This report was forwarded to ATIC on 29 September 1952.
