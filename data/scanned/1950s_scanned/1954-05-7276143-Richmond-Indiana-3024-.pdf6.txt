DURHAM AFB NORTH CAROLINA 27 MAY 1954

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS SIGHTED IN VICINITY OF RICHMOND, INDIANA ON 7 MAY 1954

TO: COMMANDING GENERAL
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

1. REFERENCE IS MADE TO TELEPHONE CONVERSATION BETWEEN COL O'MARA AND COL DUFFY OF THIS HEADQUARTERS ON 27 MAY 1954 CONCERNING UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS SIGHTED IN THE VICINITY OF RICHMOND, INDIANA ON 7 MAY 1954.

2. THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION HAS BEEN DEVELOPED FROM INVESTIGATION CONDUCTED BY THIS HEADQUARTERS:

A. ON 7 MAY 1954 BETWEEN 1900 AND 2000 HOURS EST, FIVE UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS WERE SIGHTED BY RESIDENTS OF RICHMOND, INDIANA AND VICINITY. THE OBJECTS WERE DESCRIBED AS BEING ROUND IN SHAPE, APPROXIMATELY 30 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND TRAVELING AT HIGH SPEEDS IN STRAIGHT LINE COURSES AT ALTITUDES ESTIMATED TO BE BETWEEN 5000 AND 10000 FEET.

B. THE OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED TO MAKE ABRUPT TURNS AND CHANGES OF DIRECTION WITHOUT APPRECIABLE LOSS OF SPEED. DURING SOME OF THESE MANEUVERS, SMALLER OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED TO BREAK AWAY FROM THE MAIN GROUP.

C. ATTEMPTS WERE MADE TO TRACK THE OBJECTS BY RADAR, BUT WITHOUT SUCCESS. THIS HEADQUARTERS HAS BEEN UNABLE TO IDENTIFY OR EXPLAIN THE OBJECTS.

3. IT IS REQUESTED THAT ANY INFORMATION AVAILABLE AT YOUR HEADQUARTERS CONCERNING THIS INCIDENT BE FORWARDED TO THIS HEADQUARTERS.

SIGNED
JOHN F. DUFFY
COLONEL, USAF
ASSISTANT FOR AIR INTELLIGENCE
