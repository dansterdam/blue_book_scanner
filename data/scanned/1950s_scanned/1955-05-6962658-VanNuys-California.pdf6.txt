SUPPLEMENT TO AF FORM 112

NAME: JERRY L. GAGNON

PLACE: VAN NUYS, CALIFORNIA

DATE: 23 APRIL 1955

INVESTIGATIVE OFFICER'S REPORT:

CASE SUMMARY: ON 23 APRIL 1955, THE REPORTING WITNESS, MR. JERRY L. GAGNON, OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER VAN NUYS, CALIFORNIA WHILE ENROUTE TO HIS PLACE OF EMPLOYMENT. HE DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS ROUND IN SHAPE, ABOUT 75 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND HOVERING APPROXIMATELY 300 FEET ABOVE THE GROUND. THE OBJECT WAS DULL SILVER IN COLOR AND MADE NO SOUND. IT REMAINED STATIONARY FOR ABOUT 30 SECONDS THEN ACCELERATED RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. WITNESS WAS UNABLE TO TAKE ANY PHOTOGRAPHS OF THE OBJECT. INVESTIGATIVE OFFICER HAS REVIEWED THE DETAILS OF THIS CASE AND CONCLUDES THAT THERE IS INSUFFICIENT EVIDENCE TO IDENTIFY THE NATURE OF THE OBSERVED OBJECT.
