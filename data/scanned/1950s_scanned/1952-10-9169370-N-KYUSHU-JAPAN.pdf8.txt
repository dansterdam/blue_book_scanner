AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: Aerological Observation, 29 September 1952, APC 929
IR-6-52
PAGE 5 OF 15

A surveillance balloon, with a light attached to the train, was released by personnel of the 20th Weather Squadron. The point of release was the Kumamoto Airfield at 1746 hours, 29 September 1952. The balloon was tracked by personnel on the ground and ascended to an altitude of about 26,000 feet. At that time, a light on the balloon burst and could no longer be seen. The balloon was tracked until lost from sight at an altitude of about 270 degrees.
