SUBJECT: Unidentified Objects
MEMO FOR THE RECORD:
REFERENCE: Unidentified Objects
SUMMARY: Captain RYAN, Assistant Chief, ATIC reported: On 13 May 1955, the attached memo for record, dated 13 May 1955, subject "Unidentified Objects" was received.
All applicable data having bearing upon the objects viewed, as reported, are incorporated into this report as follows:
The two (2) pieces gave evidence of having been joined, perhaps now for a second time, one piece was approximately 2 3/4 inches long, the other was approximately 2 1/2 inches long. The total length was about 20 inches. A hole (not seen in the center) had been cut or made in the material. The edges were slightly curled in appearance, and if original and the pieces had never been joined, would have presented a more circular shape. The pieces had more obvious been reheated, and if original would have been edged or finished. It is considered the pieces or objects were part of some experimental or test material and were discarded.
