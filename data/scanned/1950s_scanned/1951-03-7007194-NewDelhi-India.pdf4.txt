On Thursday morning 15 March 1951 an object was sighted by
MR.  and Mrs.  .
MR.  reported to have sighted this object in the sky from his residence at   NEW DELHI. The object was estimated to be about 3 feet in diameter and about 8 feet long and is reported to have been surrounded by a luminous glow. It was said to have moved in an erratic manner and finally disappeared from view after about 3 minutes.
MR.  who is employed as a Senior Mechanical Engineer in the Ministry of Technology and now Director of the Research and Development Laboratory of the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, had reportedly witnessed this incident.
It is estimated the object to be about 3 times greater than full moon size and was sighted at an altitude of about 4500 to 5000 feet. The color of the object is described to have been light blue in the center fading into yellowish white near the edges.
The object seemed to be revolving and its speed was estimated to be about 650 to 700 mph. It was observed from 9:55 AM to 10:10 AM. The object was sighted by about twenty other persons in the area.
The case was investigated by the Directorate of Air Intelligence and by the Director of the Research and Development Laboratory of the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.
