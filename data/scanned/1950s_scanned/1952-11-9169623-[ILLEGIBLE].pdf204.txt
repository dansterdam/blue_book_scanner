(Unclassified) Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object, Bethesda, Md. - 12 Nov 52

1. The enclosed documents relate to the sighting of one or more unidentified flying objects in the vicinity of the National Institute of Health, Bethesda, Md. on 12 Nov 52. This is a continuation narrative report to be used in conjunction with the drawing and overlays (incl 2).

2. The drawing, map, and two photographs of a lighted object were obtained by the National Institute of Health (Incl 3).

3. Questionnaires completed by the observers of the matter are attached hereto (Incl 4).

4. An aerial search which occurred in the following was attended results as Incl 15. On 14 Nov, the same paper declared that the "mystery" had been "solved". Upon inquiring, it was determined that no USAF helicopter had delivered a patient to the hospital as had been reported, and this office decided that the helicopter location does not rule out the view, and this fact is conveyed to the field.
