Case 211. Burlington, Wisc. Sept. 7, 1956 - Several residents called the sheriffs office after seeing an object traveling eastward at a high rate of speed. Coast Guard observers at the Wind Point Station also saw the object. They tentatively identified it as a weather balloon, but said they knew of no agencies in the area which launched such balloons.
