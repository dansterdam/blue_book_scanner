22 June 1954

Reports of Unidentified Flying Objects Seen in the Vicinity of Vero Beach, Florida, on 8 June 1954

1. On 8 June 1954, at approximately 2300 hours, EST, witness observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Vero Beach, Florida. The object was described as circular in shape, brilliantly lighted, and approximately the size of a man's hand held at arm's length. It was traveling at a very high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 2-3 seconds and then disappeared from view. No sound was heard.

2. At approximately 2315 hours, EST, another witness observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Vero Beach, Florida. The object was described as circular in shape, brilliantly lighted, and approximately the size of a man's hand held at arm's length. It was traveling at a very high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 3-4 seconds and then disappeared from view. No sound was heard.

3. At approximately 2330 hours, EST, another witness observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Vero Beach, Florida. The object was described as circular in shape, brilliantly lighted, and approximately the size of a man's hand held at arm's length. It was traveling at a very high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 2-3 seconds and then disappeared from view. No sound was heard.

4. All three witnesses were interviewed and their stories were found to be consistent. The witnesses were considered reliable and their observations were considered accurate. No explanation for the sightings has been determined.
