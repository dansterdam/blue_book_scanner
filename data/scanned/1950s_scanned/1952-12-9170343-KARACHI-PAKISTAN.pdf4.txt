Source: Wire from US Army Military Attache Karachi, Pakistan - wire dated 12 Dec 52
CAPT.RASHID, ROYAL PAKISTAN AIR FORCE, DOWN GRADED AT A 2 YEAR INTERVAL: DECLASSIFIED ON JAN 10, 1955. DOD DIR 5200.10

Karachi press reported that Thursday morning (varying times between 0730 and 1000 AM) four circular objects, GLOWING A BLUISH AND REPORTED BY SEVERAL WITNESSES TO HAVE MADE HIGH SPEED DIVES, were sighted and reported by 3 persons while metal watch bias (sic) were stopped by one of the objects. The circular objects were reported to have approached from the south-east, travel at a high altitude and maintain a constant elevation. The objects were reported to be visible for about 5 to 10 minutes in the clear sky and were observed by other persons at the same time.
