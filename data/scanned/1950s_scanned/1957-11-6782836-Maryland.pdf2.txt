HEAD QUARTERS
549TH AIRCRAFT CONTROL AND WARNING SQUADRON
PORT GEORGE G. MEADE, MARYLAND
14 November 1957
SUBJECT: Report of Incident
TO: Commanding Officer 
Headquarters
Port George G. Meade, Maryland
ATTN: 2LWGS
1. On 12 November 1957 this Headquarters received a call from the Civilian Security Police, University of Maryland to the effect that one of their Security Police had observed an unidentified flying object in the vicinity of College Park, Maryland. 
2. Upon receipt of subject telephone call a detail of two (2) Air Police investigators were dispatched to 15:35 hours to investigate the report of a UFO (Unidentified Flying Object) which had been observed in the sky approximately 15 minutes prior to call.
3. At approximately 15:45 hours the arrival of Military Police escort at Port Headquarters, the dispatched Air Police detail returned and reported to Commanding Officer.
4. Upon arrival at scene of reported incident, contact with Civilian Security Police was established and an investigation begun.
5. An object in the sky approximately 8 feet by 6 feet that appeared to be metallic in color and was hovering at an altitude of approximately 5 feet was observed by civilian witness. 
6. Subject matter was interrogated and nothing material was left at the scene. Contact was made with military authorities and the object was found not to be radio-active.
7. After the incident as described, the scene was secured and subject personnel were interrogated according to regulations of Second United States Army.

ALOTT P. STERN
2Lt, 21st Corp
Commanding
