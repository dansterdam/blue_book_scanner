UNCLASSIFIED
AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
USAF Air Crew Debriefing Requirements (SI)
AREA OF REPORT: North Korea
PERIOD COVERED: 22 August 1952 - 30 Apr 1952
SUBJECT: Korean Combat Crew Returnees
NO: AF50533-T
The report contained herein is an intelligence debriefing of combat crew returnees from North Korean Captivity as set forth in USAF Debriefing Requirements LR la - USAF AFOIP-C/CC-9 dtd 14 April 1952. Salient features of this report are namely:

(1) One encounter with unidentified enemy aircraft engaged in combat with friendly aircraft over the west coast of Korea. This operation resulted in the destruction of two enemy aircraft by USAF pilots.
(2) Observations of unidentified flying objects at Taegu AFB and Kunsan AFB, Chonanai, and (3) Recommendations and comments regarding this type of equipment procedures and training.

APPROVED:
THEODORE L. PASCO
Lt Col, USAF 
Director of Intelligence
