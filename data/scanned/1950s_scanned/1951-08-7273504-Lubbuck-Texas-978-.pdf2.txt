UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
ATIC PROJECT BLUE BOOK
SPECIAL REPORT NO. 14
DATE 7 August 1951
LOCATION Lubbock, Texas
TIME 9:45 P.M. CST
DATE 31 August 1951
OBSERVER 2/Lt. D. C.  Barker,A.C.
RELIABLY REPORTED BY: Lt. Col. L. E. Ireland, USAF

DESCRIPTION: Numerous luminous objects appeared in the sky, moving rapidly in a generally northeasterly direction. The objects appeared suddenly as a group, passed over the observers, then disappeared after a total elapsed time of 12-15 seconds. They were observed by several persons, appeared to be about 1/2 to 1 degree in size, and were bright enough to light up the surrounding terrain. Their movement appeared to be smooth and regular, without erratic motion. Several other sightings were reported in the same general area and time period.

CONCLUSION: UNIDENTIFIED
