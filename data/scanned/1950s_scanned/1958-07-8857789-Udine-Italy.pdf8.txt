20 JULY 1955

Flying Saucer-Like Object Reported Seen In N. Japan
Tokyo, July 19--Sightings of a flying object resembling a flying saucer were reported in northern Japan Thursday night.
The Defense Agency, Civil Aviation Bureau, and the Self-Defense Forces all received reports of the incident.
The Air Defense Command announced that the weather bureau had explained the phenomenon as being due to a natural phenomenon, called "earth light" which has been reported previously.
The announcement added that the weather conditions at the time were such as would generally be favorable for this natural phenomenon. (The agency emphasized that no unusual aircraft was involved.)
