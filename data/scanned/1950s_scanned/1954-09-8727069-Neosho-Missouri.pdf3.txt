SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object at Neosho, Missouri
DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS; DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS. 
DOD DIR 5200.10

1. DETAILS: A special agent in charge, Little Rock. Arkansas, Field District Office, OSI, USAF, appeared and submitted a written report to the Effect that at approximately 9:35 P.M. CST, on 8 September 1954, he observed an unidentified flying object over Neosho, Missouri.

2. The report states that the object was observed for approximately 5 minutes. It appeared and seemed to be suspended in the air, with its lights on. The object then moved in a northwesterly direction at a high rate of speed. The object was described as large. estimating its diameter to be 10,000 to 12,000 feet. The military personnel who observed the object were with the 81st Fighter Interceptor Squadron, and considered as experienced observers. The contents of this report are set forth below.
