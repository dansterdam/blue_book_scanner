KADENA AIR FORCE BASE 
OKINAWA, RYUKYU ISLANDS
7 OCTOBER 1951

This is a report of a sighting of unidentified aerial objects on 7 October 1951 at Kadena Air Force Base in Okinawa.

At approximately 1820 hours on 7 October 1951, four unidentified aerial objects were observed by several officers and airmen stationed at Kadena Air Force Base, Okinawa, Ryukyu Islands. The objects were first sighted by the Airborne Control and Warning Squadron on radar, and visual contact was then established by personnel on the ground.

The objects were described as bright white lights, round in shape, and traveling at an estimated speed of 600 to 900 knots. The objects were observed to make sudden 90 degree turns and other abrupt maneuvers. The sighting lasted for approximately 10 to 15 minutes before the objects disappeared from view.

No explanation for the identity or origin of the objects has been determined. The incident was reported to higher headquarters for further investigation.
