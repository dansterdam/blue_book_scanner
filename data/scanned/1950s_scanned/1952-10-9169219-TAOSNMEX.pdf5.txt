INCLOSURE: SUMMARY

In accordance with AFR 200-2, dated 29 April 1952, subject: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting (Short Titles: “FLYOBJREP”), the following reports of Unidentified Flying Objects are submitted:

On 17 October 1952, Charles E Colby, in USAF, Richard A Yoke, technician, USAF, William S Arrowood, 2nd Lieu., USAF, and Warren J Simonslie, 2nd Lieu., USAF, stationed at Kirtland AFB, Airforce Base, New Mexico, sighted an unusual aerial object north of the city of Albuquerque, New Mexico.

These sightings were made from different locations, which were as follows: Major Colby's sighting was made from approximately ten (10) miles south of Base, was low, and appeared to be approximately 25 miles northeast of the observers at an altitude of approximately sixteen thousand (16,000) feet MSL. The other observers were at the local football stadium and were watching the activities of the half-time break when sighting the object. All sightings were made from 2110 to 2115 MHR.
