(3) The object appeared to be stationary, hovering, and motionless.
(4) The object was observed to be round or disc-shaped, and was estimated

(5) No noise or exhaust was observed emanating from the object.
(6) The object was observed to have a metallic or aluminum-like surface
coloration.
(7) No other aircraft were observed in the vicinity of the reported

a. Attempts to locate and interview the civilian pilot who reported this

obtained regarding this incident.
