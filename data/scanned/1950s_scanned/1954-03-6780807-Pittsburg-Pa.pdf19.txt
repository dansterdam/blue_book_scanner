15 March 1954
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects Report (UFOB)

TO: Commanding Officer
509th AAA Bn (Gun) (90mm)
South Park Military Reservation
Broughton, Pennsylvania

1. The following information is in regards to an unidentified object sighted up to 12 March 1954 according to the report.

2. The object was first observed at approximately 2130 hrs on 11 March 1954. It was a single light moving in a straight line at a high rate of speed. The object was observed to disappear and reappear at irregular intervals. The object was observed to be approximately 30,000 feet in altitude and traveling at an estimated speed of 1200 mph. The object was observed by several individuals and a few attempts were made to track the object with radar but the attempts were unsuccessful.

3. The location of observation was at the 90th Azimuth of 6000 yards from the center of the South Park Military Reservation, Broughton, Pennsylvania.

4. The tracking indicator was clear, with an overcast of approximately 20,000 feet and the wind was 10 mph from the NW.

5. No further details could be determined from the report.
