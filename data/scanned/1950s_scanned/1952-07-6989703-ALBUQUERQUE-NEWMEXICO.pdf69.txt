Submitted in accordance with AF LTR No. 200-5, dated 20 April 1952. Sub: Unidentified Flying Objects.

As a result of publicity given,reported Unidentified Flying Objects in the vicinity of Great Falls, Montana on 29 July 1952. Mrs. & Mrs. ______ reported the following:

"At 0600 hrs, 5 November 1950, she and her husband were traveling in a car on Highway 91 near Choteau, Montana, when they observed an unidentified flying object moving from east to west passing within approximately 100 yards of their car. The object was disk-like and appeared to be about the size and shape of an automobile. It displayed a bright blue-white glow and appeared to be oscillating or wobbling as it moved through the sky. The object passed from sight rapidly."
