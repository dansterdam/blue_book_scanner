1. The Honorable ____ Floor Leader, Colorado State Legislature, dated 5 December 1955, stated that on or about 25 November 1955 he had seen an unidentified object in the vicinity of LA VETA, COLORADO.

2. The Denver Office, FBI, referred the matter to this office by letter, dated 9 December 1955. Attempts were made by this office to contact ______.

3. On 12 January 1956, Special Agent JAMES A. ROEERS, of this office, interviewed Senator ________ in the State Capitol Building. Senator ________ verified the date and location where he had seen the object as being approximately 15 miles southeast of LA VETA, COLORADO. Senator ________ stated that he saw the object when he was driving down the highway and it was in the sky to three-fourths of the diameter of the full moon. He said that the object was greenish-blue in color, appeared luminous, seemed to be of jelly-like consistency and had no distinguishable features. The object, according to the Senator, was approximately 15,000 feet elevation above sea level, traveled at a fairly high rate of speed and disappeared from view over Mt. Mestas. He estimated that the object was in his sight for approximately five seconds and that, estimated the speed of the object as being about one-half the speed of a meteorite. He stated that the object suddenly disappeared from view. He could not remember whether the disappearance was gradual or instantaneous. The Senator stated that the sighting line of the object would have been from approximately 7000 to 8000 feet. He said that the time of the sighting was approximately 1830 hours 25 November 1955, that there were no clouds or thunderstorms in the area and that there was no reflection or vapor trail connected with the object.
