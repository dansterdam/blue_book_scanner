UNCLASSIFIED
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT Sighted 15 Miles Southeast of Ocean City, Maryland, 2750 hours, 27 Jul 54.

TO: Commander
Air Technical Intelligence Center
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base
Ohio
ATTN: ATIAA-20

1. Transmitted herewith for your information is one (1) copy of the transcript of an investigation of Subject incident by District Office No. 32, dated 3 August 1954, file 55-24-7-11, subject as above.

2. Subsequent to writing this attached report, the District Commander, USAF, was advised by the District Intelligence Officer, that in view of his office manual and a directive just received from this Headquarters, he is requested to identify it as a smoke pot. The District Intelligence Officer is of the opinion that the smoke pot was used during some naval exercise in which no attempt was made to recover the pot, and the District Intelligence Officer advised that there is no indication of intensive naval activity in the area as subject incident indicates, and that a closer examination of the incident disclosed conditions that made it appear to be a smoke pot activity.

3. No further investigative action is contemplated and the files of the CPDES of this special investigation are considered closed.

4. Your attention is invited to the provisions of AFR 24-1, which specify the release of investigative information to unauthorized persons.

UNCLASSIFIED

ROBERTS P. NEILSON, JR.
Colonel, USAF
Commander
