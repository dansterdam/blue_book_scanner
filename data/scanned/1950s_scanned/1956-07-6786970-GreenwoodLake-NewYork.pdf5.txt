DETAILS:
Investigation predicated upon receipt of a telephone call from identified civilian, who had sighted an unidentified flying object in the aftersoon on 4 July 1956 at Greenwood Lake, N.Y. According to the information he provided, the object was observed for approximately 45 minutes and was described as a bright, silvery, disc-shaped object which appeared to hover and then to accelerate at a high rate of speed and disappear. No further details were obtained, as the witness was unable to furnish any additional information.
