1956, Sept. 6-Mainz, Germany-10:30pm(Central European Time)-A bell-shaped object was seen crossing the sky from Cassiopeie to Mars and there was a changing view of it, as though the sun was getting on it. It would change to orange, to half the size, to dark red, changing to orange again. It was seen for about 50 seconds, by Herr & Frau Roenicke.
