SUBJECT: Report of Sighting of UFO 

1. On 14 September 1950, at 1330 hours, local time, the undersigned observed a single, bright, rounded, metallic object in the sky over Nunivak Island, Alaska. It was observed for approximately 1 minute and 15 seconds before disappearing from view. 

2. The object appeared to be traveling in a straight line at a high rate of speed and at an altitude of approximately 10,000 to 12,000 feet. It was observed to be bright and metallic in appearance, but did not seem to reflect sunlight. The object gave the impression of self-luminosity. No trail or exhaust was observed, nor was there any sound associated with the object. 

3. Weather conditions at the time of the observation were clear, with scattered clouds at about 5,000 feet. Visibility was excellent. The undersigned is a competent observer of aircraft, and is familiar with the various types of aircraft and their characteristics. 

4. This report is submitted in accordance with existing directives. It is requested that any further information pertaining to this incident be forwarded to this headquarters.
