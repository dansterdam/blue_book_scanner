AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

On 17 January 1953 this office received information that a flying disk was seen in the sky near the Capital City of Panama Canal Zone observed as an object traveling at a high rate of speed. This information was not previously reported to this office and was withheld from publication until today as the official clearance of the image and data was withheld.

To Capt. James H. REASON, Director of the Project, a report from Julio R. HEUREUX, Director of the National Police, in regard to this incident has been received and it has been determined that the object observed was not a conventional aircraft and could not be identified. The incident is considered Important in Chile, was the observers. As the observer was considered reliable and responsible in security matters, and not subject to hallucinations or sensation, the report is considered of sefficient merit to warrant a request from this Agency for further investigation.
