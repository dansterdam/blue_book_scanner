26 MAY 1952 KOREA UNCLASSIFIED

AF FORM 112 - PART II

AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

ATLO. 9/L 3-sPAP 26-52-55

SUBJECT: Balloon sightings: At 2330-10, vicinity of CT 1038, an individual reported seeing a balloon-type object which was observed for approximately 5 minutes. The object was described as approximately 25 feet in diameter, silver in color, and suspended from the balloon was a lighted object approximately 5 feet in diameter which appeared to be glowing. The object was observed to descend to an estimated altitude of 10,000 feet, at which time it appeared to be the size of a silver dollar held at arm's length. Estimated speed was 25-30 knots. The object was tracked for approximately 15 minutes, during which time it was lost from sight. Additional observations of this nature have been made in this area of Korea. A further investigation is being made by ATLO Intelligence.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS; 
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.
