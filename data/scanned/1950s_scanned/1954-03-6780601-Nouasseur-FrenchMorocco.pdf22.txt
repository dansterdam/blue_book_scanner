The object was described as a bright, shiny, disc-shaped craft emitting a greenish-white glow. It was observed for approximately 10 minutes by numerous individuals and maneuvered at a high rate of speed. There was no noise associated with the object.
G-2 officer and the Base CIC officer submitted reports of this incident. THE REPORT STATES THAT AN ANALYSIS OF THE REPORTS INDICATED THAT THE OBJECT WAS OF A HIGHLY UNUSUAL NATURE AND UNIDENTIFIED. THERE IS NO FURTHER INFORMATION IN THE FILE AS TO THE FINAL DISPOSITION OF THIS INCIDENT.
