AREA REPORTED RO: CAMP EL TORO, CALIFORNIA, USA

SUMMARY: Unidentified flying objects were observed visually and by radar at Camp El Toro, California, on the evenings of 11 and 12 November 1956. The sightings were made from approximately 1229 AST to 1650 AST on 11 November 1956.

PART ONE: Description of sightings and radar observations by Lt LOUIS C. SADLER, USAF.

PART FOUR: Supplementary Investigative Efforts.
PART FIVE: Comments of Preparing Officer.

II. SUMMARY: Unidentified flying objects were observed visually and by radar at the Marine Corps Air Station, CALIFORNIA, on the evening of 11 November 1956 and the morning of 12 November 1956. The duration of these sightings was approximately 4 1/2 hours. Efforts to intercept and identify the objects were unsuccessful.
