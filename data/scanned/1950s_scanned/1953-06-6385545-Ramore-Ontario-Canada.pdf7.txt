912TH AIRCRAFT CONTROL AND WARNING SQUADRON

STATEMENT

As about 2325 EST 30 June 1953 I was called to see an object, it was orange colored and flat on top. It did not leave the area, it was visible about 10 minutes. The object came from the west and towards the east. It disappeared suddenly and no sound was heard. The weather was clear.
