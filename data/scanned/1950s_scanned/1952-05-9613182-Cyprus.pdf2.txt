AF FORM 117 - PART I
COUNTRY: USA-MALTA
AGENCY: AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object over Cyprus, CYPRUS
DATE OF INFORMATION: 19 MAY 1952
PREPARED BY: CAPTAIN CHARLES J. ROWLEY, USAF
INTELLIGENCE DIVISION, HQ USAF
SUMMARY: (Based on information received by letter, radio communication, and/or personal interview.)
Report #9 dated 15 MAY 1952 prepared by the USAF Liaison Officer, 1509-2 ATW, Detachment, ANDREWS AFB, states:
As approximately 2030 hours on 10 MAY 1952 in the city of Paphos, Southwestern Cyprus, USAF personnel noticed a brilliant light intensely bright, apparently from the level of the sea and disappearing into the sky. The object appeared to be of a circular shape and emitted a luminous light. It appeared to be moving at a very rapid rate of speed and there was no air traffic in the vicinity of Cyprus.
