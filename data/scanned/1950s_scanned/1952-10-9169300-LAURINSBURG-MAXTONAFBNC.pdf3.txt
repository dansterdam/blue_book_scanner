AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
UNITED STATES
LAURINSBURG-MAXTON AIR BASE, N.C.
24 October 1952
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
22 October 1952
1. ON 22 OCTOBER 1952, AT APPROXIMATELY 0314Z, AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WAS OBSERVED MAKING A BRIGHT LIGHT RADAR TRACK UNIT NO. 1, DESPITE STATED THAT HIS IS THE FIRST INSTANCE OF ANY STRANGE OBJECT BEING OBSERVED ON RADAR. HE DID NOT OBSERVE THE OBJECT VISUALLY AND AS IT PASSED OVER HIM, HE COULD SEE APPROXIMATELY 15 TO 20 STEADY LIGHTS ON THE OBJECT. THE OBJECT APPROACHED FROM THE EAST AT AN ESTIMATED ALTITUDE OF 15,000 FEET AND DEPARTED IN THE SAME DIRECTION AT A RAPID RATE OF SPEED.
2. ATTACHED AS INCLOSURE (1) IS A COPY OF ATX PREPARED IN ACCORDANCE WITH PAR 7B, AFR 200-2, 29 APRIL 1952.
