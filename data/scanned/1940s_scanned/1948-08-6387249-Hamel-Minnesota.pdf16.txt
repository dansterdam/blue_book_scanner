DEPARTMENT OF THE ARMY
OFFICE OF THE CHIEF, CHEMICAL CORPS 
WASHINGTON 25, D. C.

UNCLASSIFIED

FIELD OFFICE OCMLD
Army Chemical Center, Md.

29 October 1948

CMIVR-P 3801/8

SUBJECT: Unconventional Aircraft

TO: Director of Intelligence, G2USA
Washington, D. C.
ATTN: Chief, Intelligence Groups

1. This office has recently reviewed intelligence report by the Area Intelligence Officer, St. Paul, Minnesota, dated 16 August 1948, Serial 0406168, regarding the investigation of a reported "flying saucer" in the vicinity of Hamel, Minnesota.

2. The report has aroused the interest of some of our scientists who would like to have more details of the reported occurrence.

3. Photographs of the spot where the alleged "flying saucer" landed, and samples of the surface material of the dented spot are desired if practical to obtain them at this time.

4. Request photographs and samples be sent to this office for examination and analysis.

FOR THE CHIEF, CHEMICAL CORPS:

/s/ WILLIAM M. CREASY
Colonel, Cml C
Chief, Cml C Res & Eng Div
