SUBJECT: Information Pertaining to Flying Disc

On the morning of 18 July 1949, between the hours of 0800 and 0900, an object was called to my attention by Pvt ________. Its shape was round and its color was dull white. It was traveling in the air in an easterly direction at a high rate of speed. Soon after seeing it, I lost sight of it. The ceiling was unlimited. A C-82 was flying much lower.
