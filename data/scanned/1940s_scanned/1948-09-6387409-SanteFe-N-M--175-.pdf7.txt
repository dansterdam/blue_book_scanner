UNCLASSIFIED
HEADQUARTERS AIR MATERIEL COMMAND
WRIGHT FIELD, DAYTON, OHIO

OCT 18 1948

SUBJECT: Project "Sign"

TO: District Commander
17th District O.S.I. (IG)
Kirtland Air Force Base, New Mexico
ATTN: Lt. Jack Bolling

1. This Command is engaged in an analytical investigation of all reported sightings of unidentified aerial phenomena and has been authorized direct communication with all Air Force installations by Headquarters USAF letter dated 6 February 1948, subject "Report of Information on Flying Discs."

2. A report from Headquarters Fourth Army, attached as Enclosure 1, indicates that on or about 25 September 1948, a party at Sante Fe, New Mexico, saw an unidentified aerial object in Santa Fe on 23 September 1948.

3. It is requested your office interview all witnesses to this observation as indicated on Enclosure 1 or to obtain thenames of additional parties requested that their names be forwarded as a part
of this investigation. Much information as possible relative to the object as outlined in the Essential Elements of Information form, attached as Enclosure 2. Any additional evidence that may seem pertinent to the investigation, as indicated in section "General" of the form, should be included.

4. Interested personnel should be advised that the confidential nature of this matter precludes any publicity.

5. It is requested that all pertinent material be forwarded directly to the Commanding General, Air Materiel Command, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, Ohio, attention MCIAOX-3.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS; DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS DOD DIR 5200.10
