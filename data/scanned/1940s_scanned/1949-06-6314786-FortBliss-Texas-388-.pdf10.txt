UNCLASSIFIED
HEADQUARTERS
ANTIAIRCRAFT ARTILLERY AND GUIDED MISSILE CENTER
FORT BLISS, TEXAS

23 June 1949

SUBJECT: Unconventional Aircraft

TO: Commanding General
Fourth Army
Fort Sam Houston, Texas
ATTN: AC of S, G-2

In compliance with letter, Headquarters Fourth Army, file 432.1 NMSP,
subject as above, dated 4 April 1949, the following report is submitted:

1. Date of Observation: 22 June 1949 Date of Interview: 22 June 49

2. Exact time of observation: 1016 hours - 1228

3. Place of observation: South end of Bldg 4100 Fort Bliss, Texas
(See inclosure #1)

4. Position of observer: Standing on steps, one step up.

5. What attracted attention to object: Just happened to be looking in that direction.

6. Number of objects: At first observed four (4) objects; (See enclosure
#2). Then looked away to be sure that I was not imagining seeing
something and when I looked again about five (5) seconds later I could only
see three (3) objects; (See inclosure #2). Then looked away again for about
five (5) seconds and when looking at objects again I only saw two objects
for nearly two (2) minutes; (See inclosure #2).

7. Apparent size: Objects appeared to be about three (3) inches
wide and twelve (12) inches long and about twelve (12) inches deep as they
came up in a rocking motion. I would judge they were approximately a mile
and a half from me.

8. Color of object: Silver on the under side and a reddish
tinge on the top.

9. Shape: About square.

10. Altitude: 45 degrees.

DOWN GRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS
DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS
DOD DIR 5200.10

UNCLASSIFIED
