SYNOPSIS

Interviews were conducted with two persons who said they observed unidentified aerial phenomena between 0400 and 0400 hours on 7 April 1949 at Memphis, Tennessee. One person, who was quoted by a local newspaper as an observer of this phenomenon, could not be located for an interview. The Investigating Officer represented that no astronomical irregularities were observed by the Government's Weather Bureau, nor were any unusual aerial operations conducted or observed by the Naval air station, Millington, Tennessee.
