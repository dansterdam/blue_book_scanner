Liberal, Kansas Sightings

On August 6, 1962 a report was received at the Oiforte Air Force Base from a civilian resident of Liberal, Kansas. The report stated that at approximately 9:15 P.M. Central Standard Time on that date, a bright light had been observed in the sky from various locations in the Liberal area.

The light was described as about the size of a "basketball" and having a "brilliant whitish" color. It was observed to be moving at a high rate of speed in an erratic manner, suddenly accelerating, decelerating, and making abrupt turns. The light was visible for approximately 15 minutes before going out of sight. Several people observed the light, according to the report.
