UFO, 31 aug, Wichita, Kansas

The Weather Bureau at Wichita confirmed reports of radar sightings of unidentified flying objects over a wide section of south central Kansas between 10:30 pm and 11:30 pm, 2 Aug today.

Mr. Wellington said he and his wife saw one of the mysterious objects at 4:50 am today. He said "it was moving rapidly to the North".

He said a red vapor trailed behind the disc shaped object.

The Wellington Police Department said officers had been observing the objects most of the early morning hours. One was reported four miles east of Wellington.
