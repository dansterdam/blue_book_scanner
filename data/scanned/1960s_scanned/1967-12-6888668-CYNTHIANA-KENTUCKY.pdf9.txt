SUMMARY OF INFORMATION RECEIVED
CYNTHIANA, KENTUCKY
6 DECEMBER 1967

At approximately 6:05 PM on 6 December 1967, two unidentified flying objects were observed by several residents of Cynthiana, Kentucky. The objects were reportedly round in shape, silver in color, and approximately 30 feet in diameter. They were observed maneuvering at a high rate of speed at an altitude estimated to be 3,000 to 5,000 feet above the ground. No sound was reported by the witnesses. The objects were observed for a period of approximately 15 minutes before disappearing from view.
