PHOTO FOR THE RECORD: 25 Jul 65

Received a call from Dayton, Ohio regarding an unidentified object which he observed last night at 9:02 p.m. Object was viewed East-Northeast and looked similar to one of the satellites, however it was flying much faster. Observed for only 15-30 seconds. Asked him if he would be willing to filling out a form and said he would be all happy to fill one out. He said that there were several neighbors in the yard who also observed the object.

Additional Notes: ECHO I, ECHO II, PEGASUS I & II were not visible this time or evening.
