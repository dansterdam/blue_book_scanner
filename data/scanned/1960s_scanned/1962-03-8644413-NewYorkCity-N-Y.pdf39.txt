NO CASE (INFORMATION ONLY)
11 MARCH 1962
DEWSON, IOWA

A routine saucer sighting occurred at 8:10 p.m., March 11th. It was an unusual blue-green saucer-shaped object that hovered over Dewson for a period of about twenty minutes. The object had white lights blinking in an irregular pattern. It departed after the white lights went off completely visible for some time.
