BERGENFIELD, NEW JERSEY, SIGHTING REPORT (11583)
6 March 1967

On 6 March 1967, at approximately 7:15 PM, a local resident of Bergenfield, New Jersey reported observing an unidentified flying object in the sky. The witness stated that the object appeared to be a large, dark, triangular shape, hovering silently above the treetops. The object was observed for several minutes before it slowly drifted out of sight. No other details were provided in the report.
