ARRANGEMENTS FOR MEETING WITH AIR FORCE UFO OFFICERS
12 AND 13 JUNE 1967

Housing: Rooms (singles and doubles) for participants at the University of Colorado will be provided.

Prices: A "package" arrangement including room and board will be provided for the two days at a cost of $8.00 for a single room and $6.00 per person for a double room, both days.

Meals: Breakfast and lunch will be served on campus. Dinner arrangements will be made at local restaurants at participants' own expense.

Note: Participants are responsible for registering to Boulder on their own, checking into the respective dorms, and checking out when their sessions are over. If possible, please try to arrive by 5:30 p.m. on June 12 and 1:00 p.m. on June 13. All participants are requested to register with the secretary as to the location of their dorms, so that they can be reached if necessary. Check-in desk will have a schedule of the sessions and can assist to register for the meetings.
