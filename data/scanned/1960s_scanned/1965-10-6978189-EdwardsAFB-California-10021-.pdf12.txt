EDWARDS AFB 
CALIFORNIA 
10/21/65

SUBJECT: REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS

1. AT APPROXIMATELY 1745 HOURS ON 8 OCTOBER 1965 THE FOLLOWING INCIDENT OCCURRED:

2. MR. H. H. SMITH, CIVILIAN EMPLOYEE, EDWARDS AFB, OBSERVED 4 OR 5 BRIGHT LIGHTS MOVING ACROSS THE SKY FROM EAST TO NORTHWEST. THE LIGHTS APPEARED VERY BRIGHT AND WERE MOVING AT A VERY HIGH RATE OF SPEED. THEY WERE OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 30 SECONDS.

3. MR. SMITH STATES THAT HE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED THE INCIDENT TO BASE OPERATIONS. A POLAROID PHOTOGRAPH OF THE INCIDENT WAS TAKEN, BUT DUE TO THE GREAT SPEED OF THE OBJECTS, THE PHOTOGRAPH PRODUCED IS EXTREMELY INDISTINCT AND NO DETAILS CAN BE MADE OUT. 

4. THIS INCIDENT WAS PERSONALLY INVESTIGATED BY THE UNDERSIGNED AND THE ABOVE FACTS WERE OBTAINED FROM MR. SMITH. THE PHOTOGRAPH IS ATTACHED HERETO AS A MATTER OF RECORD.

JOHN F. HEMMER, CAPTAIN, USAF
PROJECT BLUE BOOK OFFICER
