Location: Lake Canton, Canton, Ill. 
Date: March 7, 1967
Case: 7467612
Observation: At approximately 6:00 p.m. on the evening of March 7, 1967, Mr. John Smith of Canton, Illinois, reported sighting a round, metallic object hovering over Lake Canton, located approximately 3 miles northwest of Canton, Illinois. The object was described as being approximately 30 feet in diameter, silvery in color, and appeared to be rotating slowly. Mr. Smith observed the object for approximately 5 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view. No sound was reported during the sighting. Weather conditions at the time were clear with good visibility.
