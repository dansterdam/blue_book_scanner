10. CONCLUSION: Astro(Arcturus) Aircraft(possible)
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
On several nights in succession the witness saw light in the night sky. The object(s) appeared to be stationary for a long period at least a half hour before below the horizon. This flashing light was observed several nights. The object looked like a rotating beacon. On one of the above nights the witness and others saw red lights in the objects. At the same time as the above sightings other nights similar lights were seen by the witness at an elevation of 20-30 degrees on the night of 27 Oct. 66. The times were at approximately 11:15 pm on the 27th. Other descriptions are consistent with that of Arcturus or an aircraft.
