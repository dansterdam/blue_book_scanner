Project Blue Book Minot AFB Sightings
8 November 1966

On 8 November 1959, in the afternoon, several calls were placed to Col Morich at Minot AFB at 897-4660 ext 3030 and 3231. However, we could not get an answer. Lt Hansel wanted to ask him about the weather radar.
