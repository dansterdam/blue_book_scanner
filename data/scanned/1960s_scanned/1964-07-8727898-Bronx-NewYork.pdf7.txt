Tacoma, Wash. 22 July 1964

July 22, 1964: Tacoma, Wash. A deformation bright object appeared in the sky toward the southeast. It resembled a balloon, but much larger, to some witnesses. It resembled a helicopter, to others. It was observed for some 6-8 minutes, and disappeared after changing direction, but no sound was heard.
