UFO sighted by Daylight - October 18, 1964
Mr. and Mrs. Robert Miles of Titusville, R.D. 3, saw the UFO hovering in the sky southwest of Titusville as seen from the 334 of Church Hill Road. The object was white and showed no details. It hovered for about a minute and then flew off rapidly.
