FRIDAY, AUGUST 8, 1962
PORT-AU-PRINCE, HAITI
AT APPROXIMATELY 2315 HOURS, LOCAL TIME, A RESIDENT OF PORT-AU-PRINCE REPORTED OBSERVING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE NIGHT SKY OVER PORT-AU-PRINCE.
THE WITNESS DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS CIRCULAR IN SHAPE, APPROXIMATELY 75 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND EMITTING A BRIGHT LIGHT FROM ITS UNDERSIDE. THE OBJECT WAS SEEN TO HOVER IN THE SKY FOR SEVERAL MINUTES BEFORE RAPIDLY ACCELERATING AND DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.
