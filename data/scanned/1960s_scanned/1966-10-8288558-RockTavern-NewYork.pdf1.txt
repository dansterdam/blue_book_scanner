PROJECT 10073 RECORD
1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 20 Oct. 66 21/0002
2. LOCATION: Rock Tavarn, New York
3. SOURCE: Civilian
10. CONCLUSION: Other (Locomotive brakeling)
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS: Observer watched a round, bright white light that appeared to project a spot of some hills. The light disappeared instantaneously behind the mountains. The observer compared the unknown light phenomenon to a locomotive headlight. The light never appeared to be overhead.
Investigating officers' conclusions are that the observer saw a light that, based on terrain features, and pinpoints the spot where the observer was, was probably a locomotive headlight. All traffic in the vicinity at midnight. The NY State police indicated that there were none located on the trestle near where the observer claims the light turned out to be a rail traffic on the trestle.
