DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
LITTLE ROCK AIR FORCE BASE, JACKSONVILLE, ARKANSAS, 72077

SUBJECT: (U) Investigation of Unidentified Flying Object

1. (U) The following information is the result of an investigation conducted by Captain Howard D. Leavey, AO044595, Combat Intelligence, Directorate of Intelligence, 1st Strategic Aerospace Division, Little Rock Air Force Base, concerning the report of an unidentified flying object (UFO):

2. (U) Physical evidence obtained: (1) Piece of black plastic bag approximately 8 x 5 inches in size. The plastic bag contained some foreign material and was moist on the interior. The bag is now a part of the permanent file concerning the report of a UFO.

3. (U) Investigation information: According to information received from Captain Leavey, a farmer near Fayetteville, Arkansas, reported seeing an unidentified flying object in the sky on 24 August 1965, at approximately 0350 hours. The farmer, who was not identified, stated that the object was cigar-shaped and appeared to be approximately 35-40 feet long. It was emitting a bright reddish-orange glow and was moving in a northeasterly direction at a high rate of speed. The object was observed for approximately 20-30 seconds before disappearing from sight.

4. (U) Other information: The information concerning the report of an unidentified flying object (UFO) has been forwarded to the proper authorities.
