AGTC 
28 July 68 
10:15a

Received a call from Mr. Ritron, Colilasnan, and wanted to report a 
sight he and Mr. Marano, about several sightings north from Leon, Iowa, 
12 mi E of Iowa City, Iowa. He stated it was observed at 
2 am on the 26th of July for several seconds and very bright. 
He wanted to know if Lt. Marano received any reports from this 
data. He asked for Lt. Marano to return his call to him.
