SOUTHAMPTON, NEW YORK
V. 12,418 July 6, 1968

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: SOUTHAMPTON, NEW YORK
DATE: JULY 6, 1968
TIME: APPROXIMATELY 10:30 P.M.

The informant stated that while looking out a window of his residence, he observed a bright, oval-shaped object in the sky which appeared to be approximately 20 feet in diameter. The object was moving slowly in a westerly direction and seemed to be approximately 300 to 400 feet in the air. 
The informant further stated that the object appeared to be metallic in nature and was emitting a bright, white light. He indicated that he observed the object for approximately 1 to 2 minutes before it disappeared from view. 
No other witnesses were located who could corroborate the informant's observation. No physical evidence was located at the site.
This case is being carried in the files of the U.S. Air Force as UNIDENTIFIED.
