Air Technical Intelligence Center 
Wright-Patterson AFB
Dayton, Ohio

Dear Sirs:

About a year and a half ago I reported seeing a white, partly transparent object in the sky with a red dot in the middle which my six-year-old daughter observed as well. It appeared to be hovering or moving slowly at an extremely high altitude. The object which was sighted by several people in the area was oblong in shape and seemed to have a metallic appearance. The sighting occurred on the night of August 25, 1951. I had collected several names of people who had also seen the object, but due to the time lapse, I am unable to recall the exact number.

The balloon-shaped object apparently hovered or moved very slowly at an extremely high altitude for over an hour. The object practically faded from view as the night progressed. My wife's observation was that it seemed to be a balloon of some sort, but the motion and altitude did not seem compatible with a balloon. In fact, a balloon could have caused quite a bit of noise, which was not the case. The object had a metallic, shiny appearance and did not seem to be illuminated from the interior.
