MEMO FOR THE RECORD

Subject: UFO Report, 19 October 1967

On 20 October 1967 received a call from [REDACTED] Petersburg, Va. 

On 19 October at 9:05 pm EDT at Williamstown, W. Va. he sighted a UFO. The total duration of the sighting was about 40 seconds. (20 seconds in car and 20 seconds outside of car). There was one other witness to sighting. [REDACTED] did not know his name but got his license plate number.

The object had wings like a horseshoe and had lights all along the wings. The lights on the wings were red and green and there were (pass.) white lights on body of the craft. None of the lights flashed but were on constantly. These wings met in front and joined; the height of the object 35-40 mile.

He saw object while driving, it seemed to be hovering. He then stopped, it was directly overhead. He heard a deep jet sound and the craft started to move slowly. It did a semi-circle and disappeared behind a hill. The observer was looking toward Williamstown.

The object disappeared and estimated his distance as 4 miles from a nearby airport.

There was a full moon that you could just barely see and it was a clear night.
