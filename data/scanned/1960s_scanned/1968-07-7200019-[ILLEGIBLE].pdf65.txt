DAILY WEATHER MAPS
WEEKLY SERIES JULY 8-14, 1968

The charts in this publication are a reduction of the original charts which are prepared and distributed daily by the U.S. Weather Bureau. They include the Surface Weather Map, the Constant Pressure Charts, the 1000-500 mb Thickness Charts, the 700 mb Height Chart, and the Daily Precipitation Chart. All of these charts are arranged from Monday through Sunday. They are copied from operational weather charts and facsimile weather maps used daily in the U.S. Weather Bureau's National Meteorological Center.

The Surface Weather Map and the Constant Pressure Charts are the same as those used on the Daily Weather Map. The Constant Pressure Charts are the same as those used on the Daily Weather Map.
