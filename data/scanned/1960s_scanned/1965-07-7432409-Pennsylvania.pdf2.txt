Memo for The Record: Call of 13 Jul 65
TDEW received a call ___ Bradford, OHIO, 45308 regarding an observation of his on 12 Jul 65 at approximately 9 pm for 15 minutes. Object was flying over Piqua, Bradford is straight west of Piqua, object was observed through 7 X 50 Binoculars. 
He felt that he could give more information in letter form where he could draw a picture. Sgt Moody asked him if it was alright to send a copy of FTD Form 16# for him to fill out explaining that it had additional space for comments and drawings. Form was sent to him on 13 Jul 65.
