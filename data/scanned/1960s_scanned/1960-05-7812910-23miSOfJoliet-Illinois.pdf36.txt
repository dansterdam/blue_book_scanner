No Case (Information Only)                                   24 May 1960
Ocumare Del Tuy, Venezuela

On May 24 at Ocumare Del Tuy, Venezuela a doctor, a topographer and a policeman watched three UFOs descending in formation with one of the objects landing on the ground. After the objects left the area the tall weeds were found flattened and scorched in a diamond-shaped pattern.
