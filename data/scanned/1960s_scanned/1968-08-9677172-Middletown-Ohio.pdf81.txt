U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service
DAILY WEATHER MAPS
WEEKLY SERIES AUG. 5-11, 1968

The charts in this publication are continuations of the Daily Weather Maps published by the Weather Bureau. These charts present the surface weather map, the 500 millibar chart, the station weather map, the surface weather map, and the Daily precipitation chart, all of which are arranged on a single page of this publication. They are prepared from original weather maps prepared by the Weather Bureau, and the symbols used on the Surface Weather Map are the same as those used in the Daily Weather Maps.
Copies of the Daily Weather Maps may be obtained without charge by writing to the Superintendent of Documents, U.S. Government Printing Office, Washington, D.C. 20402. Each copy will cost $.30 as long as the supply lasts. Checks should be made payable to the Superintendent of Documents.
