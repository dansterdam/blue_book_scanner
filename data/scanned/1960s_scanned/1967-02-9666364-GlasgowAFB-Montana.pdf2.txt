DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON, D.C.

SUBJECT: UFO Observation, 24 February 1967

TO: FTD (TDTR/UPO) 
Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio

Reference is your subject as above, dated 22 August 1967. The following is submitted:

1. The first object was observed for a few minutes. The second object was observed for about one hour by Sergeants Vaughn, Hansen, and others.

2. The first object was viewed on radar. The second object showed up on radar but in a different position. The objects showed intermittent activity.

3. FTD Form 165 is designed to cover sightings of only one object. Therefore the information was conflicting.

4. Tower personnel were contacted. Air activity in the area at the time the objects were sighted. Tower personnel did not observe the objects.

5. Sergeant Hansen was the only one that heard the sound of the engines. 

Added information: The sightings were of flashing lights.

FRANCIS C. PICKELL Colonel, USAF 
Commander
