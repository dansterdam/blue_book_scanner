SUBJECT: VISUAL SIGHTING — MANCHESTER, NEW HAMPSHIRE

1. The following information is submitted in accordance with the above references:
a. On the evening of 6 June 1967, at approximately 2145 hours, a private citizen observed an unidentified aerial object in the sky over the city of Manchester, New Hampshire. The citizen, Mr. John Doe, was standing outside his residence in the northwest section of the city when he witnessed the object.
b. Mr. Doe described the object as a bright, luminous, disc-shaped craft hovering in the sky approximately 800-1,000 feet in altitude and 1.5-2 miles distant. The object remained stationary for several minutes, then accelerated and rapidly disappeared from view.

c. The enclosed photograph was taken by Mr. Doe using a Polaroid camera. The quality of the image is poor due to the distance and atmospheric conditions at the time.

d. Project Blue Book has categorized this case as "Unknown" pending further investigation.

e. No further information is available at this time. Additional details will be forwarded upon completion of the investigation.
