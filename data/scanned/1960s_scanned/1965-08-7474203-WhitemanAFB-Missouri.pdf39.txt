Beaver Valley Times, Week of Aug 16, 1965
'Saucer' in Action
Just as it rose out of sight, underneath and to the sides of, right, the object in the photographs made its appearance. According to the boys the object had a very bright like silver glow and appeared larger than a Piper Cub airplane. It was difficult to judge altitude from the ground, as it was rather high, and had an elliptical shaped object and did not seem to give off which appears.
In addition to the reported events at North and South Park areas, sighting the Air Force said it has received many calls from residents in the Pittsburgh area indicating that they too had seen objects in the sky, confirming the first reports.
