RECEIVED 27 JUL 60 
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
R: AUGUST 7, 1960
FR: HQ 4347 COMBAT DEFENSE SQN, SIOUX FALLS, SOUTH DAKOTA
TO: DIRECTOR OF INTELLIGENCE, WASHINGTON, D.C.
SUBJ: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SIGHTING

AT 2022 HOURS LOCAL TIME, 7 AUGUST 1960, SERGEANT JAMES WALKER, USAF, WAS ON DUTY AT SITE SIERRA ALPHA ECHO OPERATING THE GCA RADAR WHEN HE OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED TARGET ON HIS SCOPE. TARGET APPEARED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 15 TO 20 MILES SOUTHWEST OF SITE AT AN ALTITUDE OF 12,000 FEET.

TARGET MAINTAINED A STEADY COURSE AND SPEED FOR APPROXIMATELY 15 MINUTES THEN ABRUPTLY CHANGED COURSE APPROXIMATELY 90 DEGREES AND INCREASED SPEED RAPIDLY.

NO KNOWN AIRCRAFT WERE OPERATING IN THE AREA AT THE TIME. SERGEANT WALKER HAS REPORTED THIS SIGHTING TO HIGHER HEADQUARTERS. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION WILL BE FORWARDED UPON RECEIPT.
