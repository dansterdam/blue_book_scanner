CASE OF INTEREST
REPORT ON UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT[S]
FOR 7 OCTOBER 1967
Tucson, Arizona

1. GENERAL:
On 7 October 1967, at approximately 1915 hours, an unidentified flying object was observed by several witnesses in the vicinity of Tucson, Arizona. The object was observed over a period of approximately 15 minutes.

2. DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT:
The object was described as circular in shape, with a diameter estimated to be approximately 25-30 feet. The object appeared to be luminous and gave off an orange-red glow. It appeared to be hovering at an altitude estimated to be between 500-1000 feet above the ground. No sound was heard associated with the object.

3. MOVEMENTS OF OBJECT:
The object was observed to make several sudden changes in direction, estimated to be 60-90 degrees, without any apparent loss of altitude or speed. The object was also observed to make a rapid, vertical ascent out of sight.

4. ANALYSIS:
No conclusive explanation for the unidentified flying object has been determined at this time. The object does not appear to conform to any known or conventional aircraft. Further investigation is ongoing.

5. CONCLUSION:
This case is classified as an unidentified flying object. Additional information and analysis will be provided as it becomes available.
