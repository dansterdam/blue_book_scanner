SUMMARY REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENA
1. The enclosed report is submitted in accordance with AFR 80-17. The incident occurred in the vicinity of Clyde, New York, on 16 July 1967.
2. The sighting was reported by a civilian who stated that he observed a bright light in the sky, which appeared to be maneuvering. The witness further stated that the light was observed for approximately 15 minutes.
3. Evaluation of this report indicates that the observed phenomena was most probably a satellite or other celestial object. No further investigation is deemed necessary.
