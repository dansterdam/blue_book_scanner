13 October 1961

Dear Mr.

Your letter of August 15, 1961 to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration has been referred to the Air Force. 

In order that we may attempt identification as it is requested that you provide the following information to us in the pre-addressed envelope:

a. Time of observation and elevation of the object when first and last seen.
b. The direction the object was moving.
c. The direction in which the object was moving.
d. The duration in which the object was moving.
e. Size (size held at arm's length - quarter at arm's length).
f. Did the object appear to blink or twinkle.

Your assistance and cooperation in this matter will be appreciated.

Sincerely,

WILLIAM T. COLEMAN, JR.
Colonel, USAF
Public Information Division
Office of Information

Point Pleasant, New Jersey
