TACOMIANS SEE WEIRD OBJECT

Tacoma, Washington, April 4 - A mysterious pink object passed directly over Tacoma at 7:16 p.m. Witnesses included Mrs. A.H. Porter and her daughter Frances, 21, who were reported driving east on 11th Street when they observed the object.

The UFO was described as appearing somewhat like an airplane when it first came into view. It was circular and a "pinkish-pinkish" color. The object suddenly stopped and was stationary for some time, then started moving again, apparently vanishing while in full view near the vicinity of the KTNT-TV towers.

Canyon Heights Junior High School students Pam Watson and Theresa Mazzano, both 14, had also observed the object above the television towers. They stated that the UFO was a reddish-purple color, and was traveling southward.
