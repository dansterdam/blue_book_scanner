PROJECT 10073 RECORD

BOYDTON, VIRGINIA 
JANUARY 7, 1969

1. On January 7, 1969, at approximately 9:00 p.m., the following information was obtained from a reliable source in Boydton, Virginia:

At approximately 8:30 p.m. on January 7, 1969, the source observed a peculiar configuration of lights in the night sky northwest of Boydton, Virginia. The lights were observed for approximately 10-15 minutes before disappearing.

The source stated that the lights appeared to be situated on an object that was triangular in shape and was moving at a high rate of speed. The lights were described as white in color and pulsating or flashing. No sound was heard in association with the object.

The source reported the sighting to the local police department.
