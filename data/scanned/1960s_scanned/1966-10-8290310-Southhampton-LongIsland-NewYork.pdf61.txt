Air Force Denies UFO Report
The Air Force has denied a UFO report from Long Island on November 1, 1966.

According to the report, the object was seen at about 5:00 p.m. by several people near Bridgehampton, New York. The object was described as glowing and changing colors, hovering over a field for about 20 minutes before rapidly accelerating and disappearing.

The Air Force stated that the object was probably a commercial aircraft reflecting the sun's rays. They said that without more specific information, they could not confirm the UFO report.
