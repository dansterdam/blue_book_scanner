PROJECT BLUE BOOK
20 August 1963

1. Reference is made to the report of an unidentified object sighted in the Melville Air Sabstation, Labrador, Canada, on 6 August 1963.

2. The object was reported to have hovered over the installation for approximately 45 minutes and to have emitted a beam of light which illuminated the area.

3. The report, which includes photographs of the object, is being forwarded to the Air Technical Intelligence Center for evaluation.

4. This report will be carried in the Project Blue Book files as Case No. 8489.
