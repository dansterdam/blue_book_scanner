ELECTROMAGNETIC SAUCER IN NEVADA: A sighting which gives further confirmation to John J. Robinson's article in this issue of SAUCER NEWS. Carlson City, Nevada, November 9, 1963. According to the report, at 5:20 p.m. the witness observed a disc-shaped object, about 30 feet in diameter, hovering in the southwestern skies. The object was tremendous in size, hovering at about 1,000 feet altitude. It had a golden cast and appeared to be made of a metallic material. The witness stated that it had "an odd green color with a golden cast," and was lighted from within. The witness runs an electronic repair shop and stated that he first noticed the object from a window of his home. It had "an odd green color with a golden cast" and was lighted from within. Some time later it dropped downwards into a gully outside. She added that a radio station had been intermittently blacked out while the object was in the sky. Other persons sighted police, the weatherman, and a radio station personnel while the sighting continued. The sighting lasted about 20 minutes, and the object was then lost to view. No sound was heard, and no trail was observed. The report states that the phenomenon could not be identified.
