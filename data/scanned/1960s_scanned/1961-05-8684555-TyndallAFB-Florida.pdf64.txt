TYNDALL AIR FORCE BASE 
FLORIDA
8 MAY 1961
REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
SUBJECT: OBSERVATION OF UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT
1. On 8 May 1961, at approximately 2000 hours, an unidentified object was observed by several persons at Tyndall AFB, Florida.
2. The object was first sighted by A1C JOHN DOE, 3300th Security Squadron, at about 2000 hours, while he was on routine patrol duties. He reported the object to the base Security Control Center.
3. The object was observed by the following persons:
A1C JOHN DOE, 3300th Security Squadron
A1C RICHARD ROE, 3300th Security Squadron
Capt JAMES SMITH, 4500th Air Base Wing
4. The object was observed from 2000 hours until approximately 2020 hours, at which time it disappeared from view.
5. The object was described as a bright, round, stationary light, hovering at an estimated altitude of 1,000 to 2,000 feet. It appeared to be approximately 40 to 50 feet in diameter. The object emitted a pulsating, reddish-orange glow.
6. No other physical or electronic data was obtained regarding this sighting.
