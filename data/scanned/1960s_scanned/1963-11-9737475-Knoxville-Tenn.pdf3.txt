The text of the letter is as follows:

Dear Mr. Olivier:

This is in response to your letter of December 12, 1963. This is because the Air Force may have received on two instances which individually had no special significance but collectively with and became of interest on November 29th.

The Foreign Technology Division advises us that they have received of information, the details of which they do not have at the present time. As a matter of information, the Foreign Technology Division has available a technical report on the subjects of UFO's. If you are interested in obtaining a copy of this report, please let us know.

Sincerely,

MAJOR H. JACKS
Assistant Chief, Public Information Division
Office of Information
