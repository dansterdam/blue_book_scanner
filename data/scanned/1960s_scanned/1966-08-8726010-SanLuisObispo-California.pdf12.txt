Project 10073 Record
1. Date - Time Group: 28 August 66 29/0245Z
2. Location: San Luis Obispo, California (2W)
3. Civilian Sighting of Objects
4. Number of Objects: One
5. Length of Observation: 20 - 30 minutes
6. Brief Summary and Analysis: The two different observers reported seeing an unusual object in the sky with a large weather balloon reported to be around 8 inches by one observer. The object was described as sparkling and blinking. Due to the altitude, the observers could not look up and notice a sparkle object as the object seemed to fade in and out of view.
7. Action Taken: From Holloman AFB, N.M. on August 26, 66. The case was forwarded to ATIC, Columbus, Ohio and it was tentatively identified as a weather balloon found by an observer near San Luis Obispo, Calif.
