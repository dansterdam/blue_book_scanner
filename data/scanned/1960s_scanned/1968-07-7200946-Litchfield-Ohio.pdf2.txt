DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS FOREIGN TECHNOLOGY DIVISION (AFSC)
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO 45433

REPLY TO
ATTN OF: TDPT (UFO)

SUBJECT: UFO Observation, 20 August 1963

TO: Litchfield, OHIO 44253

Your name has been given to the Aerial Phenomena Office (Project Blue Book) as being a witness to an unidentified flying object. If you were the person who made this sighting report, your testimony is valuable to us. The purpose of this letter is to request your assistance in the envelope provided. If you were not a witness to this sighting, could you please make a statement to that effect and return the envelope provided. 
Thank you for your assistance in this matter.

EDWARD J. RUPPELT, Colonel, USAF
Chief, Aerial Phenomena Office 
Aerospace Data Division
Director, Aerial Phenomena Office
