DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS 19TH AIR DIVISION (DEFENSE)
HOMESTEAD AIR FORCE BASE, FLORIDA

28 September 1967

SUBJECT: UFO Report of 20 July 1967

TO: FTD (TDTR)

1. The following information is forwarded in accordance with AFR 80-17:

a. Command report of the UFO was reported to the 19th Air Division, Homestead AFB, Florida, on 20 July 1967. The object was described as a bright light moving in an erratic manner and was observed by two security policemen at 1950L to complete the report and obtain distribution as indicated in Attachment A.

b. The investigation of this report has been completed and the results are forwarded in this report. For some time this officer did not forward the report due to delay in obtaining witnesses and clarifying some questionable details.

c. The newspaper article dated 29 Aug 67 did not include statements from these witnesses and recognized there had been wide coverage and recognition as a potential UFO sighting in the Homestead area. It is recommended the newspaper article dated 24 Aug 67 be used as a reference to this report as it may be necessary.

2. If more information is required please call Autovon extension 7010/8310/7611.

FOR THE COMMANDER

ROBERT H. CLOAR, 2d Lt, USAF
Assistant Information Division
Base UFO Project Officer
