North Devon visitor

The North Devon Journal on August 31st 1962 includes a report of a mysterious object which was said to have been seen in the vicinity of Hartland Point, North Devon, on August 27th 1962. It was seen by Mrs. Weston of Combe Martin who was standing outside her home when she noticed the object. It appeared to be silvery-grey in color, orange and was moving brightly north, then changing direction and moving west over Lundy and Hartland Point.
