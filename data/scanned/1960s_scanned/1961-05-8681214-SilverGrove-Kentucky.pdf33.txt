SILVER GROVE, KENTUCKY 

8 MAY 1961 

REPORTING OFFICER: WILLIAM KOENIG, USAF

1. ON 8 MAY 1961 AT APPROXIMATELY 2200 HOURS, A UFO WAS SIGHTED OVER SILVER GROVE, KENTUCKY. THE UFO WAS OBSERVED BY SEVERAL WITNESSES FOR A PERIOD OF APPROXIMATELY 15 MINUTES.  

2. THE UFO WAS DESCRIBED AS A BRIGHT, WHITE LIGHT THAT HOVERED AND MANEUVERED IN THE SKY. IT WAS SILENT AND APPEARED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 100 FEET IN DIAMETER. 

3. SEVERAL ATTEMPTS WERE MADE TO PHOTOGRAPH THE UFO BUT THE RESULTS WERE UNSUCCESSFUL DUE TO THE POOR LIGHTING CONDITIONS.

4. LOCAL LAW ENFORCEMENT AND CIVIL DEFENSE AUTHORITIES WERE NOTIFIED OF THE SIGHTING. NO OTHER UNUSUAL ACTIVITY WAS REPORTED IN THE AREA.

5. THIS REPORT IS SUBMITTED FOR YOUR INFORMATION.
