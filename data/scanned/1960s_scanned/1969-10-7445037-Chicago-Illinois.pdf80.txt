U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service

DAILY WEATHER MAPS

WEEKLY SERIES OCTOBER 13-19, 1969

The publication of this material is authorized by the Superintendent above sea level. The contours are shown as dashed lines, and are labeled in thousands, feet. The heavy solid line shows the wind direction and speed at the 500 millibar level.

The highest and lowest temperatures are analyzed and shown for the principal cities. They include the Surface Weather Maps the 850 millibar, 700 millibar, 500 millibar Charts, the 300 millibar and the Daily Weather Map. They are used by government agencies, private weather services, and the general public.

These weather maps are prepared by the National Meteorological Center of the Weather Bureau. The symbols used on the maps are those explained in the Weather Bureau's Technical Paper no. 21.
