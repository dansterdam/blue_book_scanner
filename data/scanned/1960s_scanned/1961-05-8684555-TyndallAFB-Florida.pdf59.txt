TWO ROUND OBJECTS, ABOUT 20-25 FT IN DIAMETER, SILVER IN COLOR, HOVERING AND MANEUVERING AT LOW ALTITUDES.

OBJECTS MANEUVERED ERRATICALLY AND CHANGED SPEEDS, RANGING FROM HOVERING TO APPROXIMATELY 400-500 MPH.

OBJECTS WERE DEFINITELY NOT CONVENTIONAL AIRCRAFT. CAPTAIN RANDALL IS EXPERIENCED PILOT WITH OVER 5000 HRS FLYING TIME.

RADAR SCOPE PHOTOGRAPHS TAKEN. OBJECTS DISAPPEARED FROM VISUAL AND RADAR CONTACT AFTER ABOUT 20 MINUTES.
