"HONDO 29 
NO MISSION 
9-14-60
TO COL. HYNEK, DIRECTOR, PROJECT BLUE BOOK
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AFB
OHIO

SUBJECT: UNKNOWN OBJECT OBSERVED OVER INDONESIA 
REFERENCE: OUR RADIO MESSAGE NR. 0001 OF 13 SEP 60 

1. THE OBJECT OBSERVED WAS A CIRCULAR, METALLIC APPEARING VEHICLE. IT APPEARED TO HOVER FOR ABOUT 40 MINUTES AND THEN ACCELERATED TO A HIGH RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY PERSONNEL OF THE U.S. NAVAL FORCES AT SURABAJA, INDONESIA.

2. DETAILS OF OBSERVATION ARE AS FOLLOWS: THE OBJECT FIRST CAME INTO VIEW AT ABOUT 0945 LOCAL TIME AND WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 40 MINUTES. IT APPEARED TO BE HOVERING OVER THE CITY OF SURABAJA AT AN ESTIMATED ALTITUDE OF 2,500 TO 3,000 FEET. THE OBJECT WAS METALLIC IN APPEARANCE, CIRCULAR IN SHAPE AND ESTIMATED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 30 TO 35 FEET IN DIAMETER. THERE APPEARED TO BE A HAZY OR MISTY VAPOR EMANATING FROM THE OBJECT. AFTER HOVERING FOR APPROXIMATELY 40 MINUTES, THE OBJECT ACCELERATED RAPIDLY TO A HIGH RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW IN A WESTERLY DIRECTION. 

3. AUTHENTICITY: THIS REPORT IS CONSIDERED AUTHENTIC AS THE PERSONNEL MAKING THE OBSERVATION ARE OF THE HIGHEST CALIBER AND THEIR IDENTIFICATION VERIFIES THEIR CREDENTIALS. FURTHERMORE, OTHER REPORTS FROM VARIOUS SOURCES TEND TO CORROBORATE THIS INCIDENT. THEREFORE, IT IS CONCLUDED THAT THE SIGHTING IS A GENUINE UNKNOWN OBJECT AND NOT A HOAX. 

4. RECOMMENDATIONS: IT IS RECOMMENDED THAT A SPECIAL INVESTIGATION BE MADE BY PROJECT BLUE BOOK TO OBTAIN ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON THIS INCIDENT. IT IS ALSO RECOMMENDED THAT ANY FURTHER REPORTS OR INFORMATION CONCERNING THIS INCIDENT BE FORWARDED IMMEDIATELY TO PROJECT BLUE BOOK FOR THEIR ANALYSIS.

RICHARD W. YOUNG
MAJOR, USAF
OPERATIONS OFFICER"
