REPORT ON UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON OBSERVED AT SALEM, VIRGINIA, ON APRIL 6, 1967.

On the evening of April 6, 1967, at approximately 9:10 P.M., a local citizen telephoned the Salem Police Department to report an unusual aerial phenomenon over the area of the Roanoke River near the Salem Bridge.

The caller stated that he had observed a large, luminous object hovering over the river approximately 500-600 feet in altitude. He described the object as circular in shape, approximately 50-60 feet in diameter, and emitting a bright white light. The object was said to be silent in operation and to have made no sound.

Police officers were dispatched to the scene immediately. Upon arrival, the officers also observed the luminous object hovering over the river. The officers stated that the object appeared to be elliptical in shape and to change colors from white to orange to red during the observation period. The object was observed for approximately 15 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight.

No further details or information were obtained regarding this incident. The case was reported to the United States Air Force as an unidentified aerial phenomenon.
