AFR 80-17(C1)

IN THE EVENT SUCH AN OB JECT IS PLACED ON THE COMPASS, THE CENTER OF THE COMPASS SHOULD BE AT THE SAME POINT ON THE GROUND AS THE OBJECT, AND THE NORTH-SOUTH LINE ON THE COMPASS SHOULD BE ALIGNED WITH THE NORTH-SOUTH LINE ON THE GROUND TO INDICATE THE DIRECTION OF THE OBJECT'S MOTION.
