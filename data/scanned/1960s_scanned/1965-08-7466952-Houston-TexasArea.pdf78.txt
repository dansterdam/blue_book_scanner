INFORMATION SHEET

1. Answers to the following questions will be obtained from persons calling in regard to the observance of an unidentified flying object (UFO). If person refuses to give answers, do not complete report.

e. Formation, if more than one

i. What angle and elevation was the object on first observance

k. How did the object disappear (instantly, to the north? no direction, etc.)
l. How long was the object visible (5 min, 1 hour, etc.)
m. How was the object observed (naked eye, binoculars, etc.)
n. Time and date of sighting 
o. Light conditions (night, day, dusk)
p. Geographical position of object (north of Houston, South of Pasadena, directly overhead, etc.)
q. Weather (clear day, hazy, cloudy, broken clouds, etc.)
r. Temperatures of day (80°, 90°, etc.)
s. OTHER INFORMATION not answered above

5. Thank caller for information, but do not promise that a return call will be made to confirm the observance of the object.
