Rann Lake lodge
August 10, 1966
At about 6:35 p.m. central standard on august 10, 1966 [was observed] an unidentified flying object by several residents of rann lake, minnesota. the object was first observed by mrs. barbara losey [name redacted] as she was mowing her lawn. she immediately called her husband and several other residents of the area to observe the object. the object was observed for approximately 30 minutes by mr. and mrs. losey, mr. and mrs. frank nelson, and several other residents. the object was described as round or spherical in shape, a dull grey or silver color, and emitted no sound. it appeared to hover over the lakeshore, a few hundred feet off the ground. it then moved away slowly to the southwest and disappeared from view. after it had disappeared, the witnesses said they could see a trail of smoke or vapor for several minutes in the sky. the witnesses were unable to determine the size or exact altitude of the object, but felt it was approximately 50-75 feet in diameter and at an altitude of 500-1000 feet. they were unsure of the exact time the object was visible, but estimated it to be about 30 minutes. no other explanations for the sighting could be determined at the time. this report was filed by major hector quintanilla, project officer, project blue book.
