CAPE GIRARDEAU, MISSOURI
29 May 1964

1. On the night of 28 May 1964, at approximately 2100 hours, Miss XXXX and her sister XXXX were driving south on Route 61 approximately 3 miles north of Cape Girardeau, Missouri, when they observed an unusual light in the sky. 

2. According to the witnesses, the light appeared to be round and bright, approximately 30-40 feet in diameter. The object was hovering above the highway at an altitude of approximately 500 feet. 

3. As the witnesses were driving, the object descended to an altitude of approximately 100 feet and followed their automobile for a distance of approximately 1 mile. The object then accelerated at a high rate of speed and disappeared from view.

4. Attempts to obtain additional information from the witnesses have been unsuccessful as they have refused to discuss the incident further.
