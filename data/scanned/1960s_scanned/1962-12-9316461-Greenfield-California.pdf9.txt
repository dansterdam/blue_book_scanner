SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

TO: DC SF

THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION HAS BEEN RECEIVED BY THE FRESNO, CALIFORNIA REGION OFFICE, REGION 1-15TH CIC, UNITED STATES ARMY, AND FURNISHED TO THIS OFFICE:

AT 0927, 14 DECEMBER 1962, THE KING CITY UNIT OF THE MONTEREY COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE RECEIVED A CALL FROM A RESIDENT OF GREENFIELD, CALIFORNIA, REPORTING THE OBSERVATION OF AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT VISIBLE IN THE EAST. THE GREENFIELD UNIT ANSWERED THAT THEY DID NOT SEE ANY OBJECT WHICH COULD BE IDENTIFIED AS UNUSUAL AND ALSO ADVISED THAT THEY COULD NOT DETERMINE ITS DIRECTION OF TRAVEL. THIS INFORMATION WAS ALSO OBSERVED BY THE MONTEREY UNIT OF THE MONTEREY COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE AND WAS ALSO CONSIDERED UNUSUAL ENOUGH TO BE REPORTED TO THIS OFFICE.

IT WAS DESCRIBED AS VERY BRILLIANT AND APPROXIMATELY 10,000 FEET IN ALTITUDE. THE GREENFIELD UNIT OBSERVED IT AT 0927, 14 DECEMBER 1962, AND IT WAS DESCRIBED AS BEING SIMILAR TO A BALL OF FIRE. AT 0932, THE OBJECT DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.

HAMILTON AFB, CALIFORNIA, CHECKED ALL STATIONS TO ASCERTAIN IF THERE WERE ANY SIGHTINGS OF THIS NATURE IN THIS AREA. THERE WAS ALSO OBSERVED A HALO WHICH WAS ASSUMED TO BE A VAPOR TRAIL. AT 0917 THE LAST TRANSMISSION WAS RECEIVED BY THE MONTEREY COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE.
