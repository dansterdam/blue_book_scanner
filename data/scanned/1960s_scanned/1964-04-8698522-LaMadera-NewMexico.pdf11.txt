SANTA FE (U.P.) - Two members of the San Juan Coun-ty sheriff's department reported seeing a flying object over the San Juan mountains near La Madera, N.M. recently.
The sighting was made about 7:30 p.m. near the foot of La Madera mountain, according to Deputy Sheriff Orlando Gallegos, who reported the incident to the State Police.
Gallegos and Deputy Sheriff Cenobio Gallegos said they saw the object give off a bright light and then disappear.
The two officers said they had been patrolling the area near a school when they saw the object in the sky. They said there were no flashing lights or other distinguishable features on the object and that it simply gave off a bright light and then vanished from sight.
The matter was reported to the State Police, to the Air Force Office of Special Investigations and to the Federal Aviation Agency. No other reports on the incident have been received so far.
