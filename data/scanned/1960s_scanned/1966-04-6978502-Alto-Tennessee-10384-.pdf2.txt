The object appeared to be circular in shape, approximately 50-60 feet in diameter. It was hovering over a field approximately 200-300 yards from the witness's vehicle. The object had a metallic appearance and was illuminated from within, producing a soft glow.

III
ACTIONS OF OBJECT:
The object was first observed hovering over the field. It then began to ascend slowly, maintaining its position over the field. After several minutes, the object accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view.

IV

The witness, a local resident, was driving along a rural road when he observed the object. He stopped his vehicle and observed the object for approximately 10-15 minutes before it departed.
