MEMO FOR RECORD
SUBJECT: Report of 3 Feb 69
Smithsonian received the photos but Dr. McCrosky is in Mexico and won't be able to look at them for a while yet. The Mexican meteorite impacted at 0705 GMT 5 Feb 69. The Smithsonian has four scientists in the area and have recovered 7 pieces of it which weigh over 12 kgm. The scientist said that the meteorite was typical of an L6 type and that 532 photographs of the impact crater have been taken.
