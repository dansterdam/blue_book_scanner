SAP-01CC/Miss Turner/76326/August 20, 1968 

AUG 20 1968

Dear Miss Turner, 

This will reply to your letter of August 13, 1968, in which you requested information concerning an unidentified flying object (UFO) sighting and your daughter's report.

Without additional information, we cannot offer an explanation of your sighting. However, if you will complete the attached questionnaire, we shall be able to investigate further and make an evaluation.

Should you ever sight another UFO, please report it as soon as possible to the nearest Air Force Base in the United States. This type of on-the-spot investigation, which usually results in a more accurate analysis.

In the meantime, you might be able to identify the sighting by looking at the categories of objects often reported as UFOs in the material we are including.

Sincerely,
JAMES H. ATKINSON
Major, USAF
Chief, Civil Branch
Community Relations Division
