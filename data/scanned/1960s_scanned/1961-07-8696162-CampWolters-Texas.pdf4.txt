SUBJECT: UNEXPLAINED AERIAL LIGHT OBSERVED AT CAMP WOLTERS, TEXAS 
1. On 8 July 1961, an unexplained aerial light was observed by several individuals at Camp Wolters, Texas. 
2. At approximately 2030 hours, Warrant Officer O. B. F. observed a bright white light in the northern sky. The light appeared to be stationary and was observed for approximately 15 minutes. 
3. At approximately 2045 hours, Warrant Officer F. and Captain H. also observed the bright white light in the northern sky. The light remained stationary and was observed for approximately 10 minutes. 
4. At approximately 2100 hours, Lieutenant G. and several enlisted personnel observed the bright white light in the northern sky. The light remained stationary and was observed for approximately 15 minutes. 
5. No radar contact was made on the object and no aircraft were observed in the vicinity at the time the light was sighted. The weather was clear with good visibility at the time of the sighting. 
6. The light was described as being approximately the size of a baseball held at arm's length and of a bright white color. It remained stationary throughout the sighting. 
7. This incident is considered to be an UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT.
