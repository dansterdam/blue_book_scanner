DETAILED REPORT - PROJECT BLUE BOOK
ORIGINATOR REPORT NUMBER - 1967 - 1968
CASE NUMBER 6888374 THE NEW ALBANY, INDIANA INCIDENT
LOCATION : NEW ALBANY, INDIANA
DATE - TIME GROUP: 6 JUNE 1968
SUMMARY:
ON 6 JUNE 1968 AT APPROX. 2115 HRS. LOCAL TIME, MRS. LUCY HOPKINS, RESIDENT OF NEW ALBANY, INDIANA REPORTED OBSERVING SEVERAL UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS IN THE NIGHT SKY ABOVE HER HOME. MRS. HOPKINS DESCRIBED THE OBJECTS AS BRIGHT LIGHTS MANEUVERING IN AN UNUSUAL MANNER. ONE OBJECT IN PARTICULAR WAS REPORTED TO HAVE HOVERED OVER HER HOME FOR A FEW MINUTES BEFORE ACCELERATING RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW. MRS. HOPKINS STATED THAT SHE IMMEDIATELY CONTACTED THE FLOYD COUNTY SHERIFF'S DEPARTMENT TO REPORT THE INCIDENT. LOCAL POLICE INVESTIGATED AND WERE UNABLE TO EXPLAIN THE SIGHTINGS. THE CASE WAS THEN FORWARDED TO PROJECT BLUE BOOK FOR FURTHER ANALYSIS.
