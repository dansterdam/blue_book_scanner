Summary of 1 July Sighting

1. The sighting described below is forwarded with further details as the sighting was so positively identified as Case L.

2. Mr. G. Gray of Richmond, Va., made the following sighting. He observed a red light which hovered and maneuvered at an approximate altitude of 10,000 feet over downtown Richmond for about 15 minutes, approximately 1930 - 1945 hours on 1 July 1962. The object then took off at a magnetic bearing of approximately 330 degrees.

3. An initial check with NASA was made and this sighting coincided with the passing of Echo I.

4. As this sighting is considered obviously resolved, no further information is furnished.
