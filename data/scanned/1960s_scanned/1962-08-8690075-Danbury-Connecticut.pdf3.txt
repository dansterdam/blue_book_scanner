AUG 2 1962

Danbury, Connecticut

Dear Mr.  ,

We are unable to confirm the sighting you reported as an incidence of an unidentified flying object. Sightings of this nature frequently occur and are difficult to assess accurately. The phenomena mentioned in the inclosure that you describe are indeed quite ordinary and characteristic of the day to day activities in the area.

Fishings of the nature that you encounter are numerous and it is presumed that your sighting was of a similar character. In any case, we are unable to conclude that this instance was of an unusual nature closely attendant to the data contained in the inclosure.  

Thank you for your interest in NASA, Goddard Space Flight Center. 

Sincerely,

James E. Lowry, Jr.
Director of Public Information
