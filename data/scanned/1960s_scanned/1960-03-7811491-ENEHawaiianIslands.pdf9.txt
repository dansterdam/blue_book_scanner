No Case (Information Only)
9 March 1960
Beverly, Mass.

On March 9 at Beverly Dr. and their daughter sighted a large circular UFO at 7:15 P.M. The object hovered, divided in two, and sped away. The object, which first hovered, had star-like rays around the perimeter that turned on when the object divided in two. After the object divided it moved northward across the eastern sky and disappeared.
