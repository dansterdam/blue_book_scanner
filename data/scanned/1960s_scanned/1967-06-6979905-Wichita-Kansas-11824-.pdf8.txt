SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
DATE OF OCCURRENCE: 11 June 1967
TIME: 2130 hours
LOCATION: Wichita, Kansas

1. On 11 June 1967 at approximately 2130 hours, the reporting individual observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Wichita, Kansas. The object was described as round in shape, silver in color, and approximately 80-100 feet in diameter. The object appeared to be hovering and made no sound.

2. The reporting individual observed the object for approximately 20 minutes before it suddenly accelerated and disappeared from view at a high rate of speed.

3. No other witnesses were identified, and no other reports of the sighting have been received. The Air Force investigation is ongoing.
