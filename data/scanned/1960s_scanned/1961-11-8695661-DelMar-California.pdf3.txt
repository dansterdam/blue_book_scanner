The UK's Leading Festival Organisers will be making a visit again to Stratford-upon-Avon on Tuesday 10th October to invite all forms of local musical talent to audition for O2 presents Stratford-upon-Avon Live Summer Music Festival 2018. The two hour free open mic style event will take place at the Music Room at Stratford ArtsHouse from 6pm - 8pm. All those interested are invited to turn up on the night armed with a 3-5 minute performance piece. A panel of judges will then select the most exciting and talented acts to perform at next year's festival. The final line up will represent an exciting mix of musical styles and genres including pop, rock, folk, indie, country, blues and more. The festival aim is to celebrate the diversity of musical talent in the local community and surrounding areas.

The O2 presenting Stratford-upon-Avon Live Summer Music Festival was a huge success in 2017 with over 10,000 people attending the two day event on the banks of the beautiful River Avon. The festival featured a stellar line up of both local and nationally renowned acts including Scouting for Girls, The Hoosiers, The Feeling, Lawson, Toploader, The Feeling, The Farm and more. O2 are delighted to be supporting the festival again as the headline sponsor in 2018.

Local events manager Jef Todd said "We are thrilled to be partnering with O2 again to bring this exciting festival back to Stratford-upon-Avon in 2018. The inaugural event was an outstanding success and we're confident that next year's festival will be even bigger and better. The open auditions on October 10th are a fantastic opportunity for local musicians to showcase their talents and potentially perform in front of thousands of people next summer. We'd encourage anyone with a passion for music to come along on the night and give it their best shot."

O2 presents Stratford-upon-Avon Live Summer Music Festival 2018 will take place on 20th and 21st July 2018. For more information on the festival and the audition event please visit www.stratfordlive.co.uk or follow @O2StratLive on twitter.
