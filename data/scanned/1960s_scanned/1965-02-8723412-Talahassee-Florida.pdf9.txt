OFFICIAL FILE COPY
TO: Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio 45433
6 March 1965
EASTERN AIRLINES
MIAMI - TAMPA - CENTRAL AIRPORT
Dear Sir:
The Air Force is conducting an investigation of an unidentified object over Daytona Beach, Florida on 1 February 1965.
The initial report indicates that the pilot of Eastern Airlines Flight 704 observed this object over Daytona Beach, Florida. We would appreciate it if you could have the pilot and/or other crew members who were on board furnish us with any additional information they may have regarding this incident.
Sincerely,
HECTOR QUINTANILLA, Jr
Major, USAF
Aerial Phenomena Branch
