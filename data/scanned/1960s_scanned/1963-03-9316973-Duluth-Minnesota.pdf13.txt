22 MARCH 1963
AKRON, OHIO

LOCAL SIGHTING:
WITNESS: MRS. MARTHA CRAIG, MARCH 22, 1910 P.M. WIFE OF SURVEYING THE YARD WHEN SHE LOOKED UP AND NOTICED WHAT APPEARED TO BE A MEDIUM SIZE: OBJECT. THE LIGHT WAS FIRST NOTICED AS BEING BRIGHT WHITE, PULSATING LIKE A STAR. THE OBJECT REMAINED STATIONARY OVER A PERIOD OF FIFTEEN TO TWENTY MINUTES, THEN SUDDENLY DISAPPEARED. MRS. CRAIG REPORTED THAT SHE WAS COVERING HER HEAD AT LEAST FIVE DIFFERENT TIMES, AS THE OBJECT BECAME VERY BRIGHT, AND THEN WOULD DIMINISH IN INTENSITY. AFTER WATCHING THE OBJECT FOR SOME TIME, IT SEEMED TO MOVE SLOWLY UPWARD AND THEN DISAPPEARED COMPLETELY. MR3. CRAIG STATED SHE WAS BLINDED BY THE BRIGHTNESS AT TIMES AND WAS GIVING OFF A SPARKLISH LIKE A SPARK, AND WAS MOVING OFF SLOWLY. SHE SAID SOMETHING BLACK SEEMED TO APPEAR AROUND THE EDGES OF THE OBJECT, AND THEN IT WAS GONE. SHE SAID THEN SHE SHIELDED HER EYES FOR ABOUT 15 MINUTES. NO SOUND WAS HEARD.
