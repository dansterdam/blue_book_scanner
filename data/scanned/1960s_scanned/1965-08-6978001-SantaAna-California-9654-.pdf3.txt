CASE OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OBSERVED AT SANTA ANA, CALIFORNIA, ON 6 AUGUST 1965

1. On 6 August 1965, at approximately 2255 hours, the undersigned was informed of an unidentified flying object sighting which had occurred in the Santa Ana, California, area.

2. The sighting was reported to the Coast Guard Station in Santa Ana, California, by an 18-year-old male observer. The observer stated that while driving south on MacArthur Boulevard in Santa Ana, he observed a bright light hovering in the sky at an estimated altitude of 500 feet. The light appeared to be elongated, approximately 50 to 60 feet in length. After observing the light for approximately 3-4 minutes, it suddenly disappeared.

3. A report of this incident was forwarded to the Air Force through normal channels. The Air Force has determined that this sighting can be classified as unidentified.
