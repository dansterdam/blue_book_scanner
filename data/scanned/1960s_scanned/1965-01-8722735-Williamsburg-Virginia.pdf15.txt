Langley Team Investigating UFO But Report Unlikely
Early this morning, this newspapermen received information from the United States Air Force that a team from Langley Air Force Base was investigating a reported sighting of a UFO in the Williamsburg, Virginia area.
The object, which was first observed around 1:30 AM on January 14, 1965 by several witnesses, reportedly hovered for a few minutes and then sped off at a high rate of speed. Air Force officials stated that the team from Langley would conduct a thorough investigation and attempt to determine the nature of the object. They said that a report on the incident would be made available as soon as the investigation is completed. No other details were provided.
