UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
APRO-WP-1 (3-57)

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
TO: CO, AIR TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE CENTER, WPAFB, OHIO

AT 0330Z, 28 FEB 66, WHILE APPROACHING PITTSFIELD, MASS, ON FLIGHT STANFORD CONN, THE USAF FACILITY (BRADLEY FIELD) OBSERVED ON RADAR AN UNIDENTIFIED FAST-MOVING TARGET APPROACHING AIRCRAFT AT ABOUT 12 MILES. TARGET CLOSED TO ABOUT 3 MILES AND PULLED AWAY. AIRCRAFT CREW OBSERVED WHITE AND FLASHING GREEN LIGHTS OF TARGET. IT APPEARED BRILLIANT AND SEVERAL TIMES BRIGHTER THAN STARS. ALTITUDE UNKNOWN.

PIR: (1) OBSERVATION VISUAL AND RADAR.
(2) LOCATION: NEAR PITTSFIELD, MASS. ON FLIGHT STANFORD CONN.
(3) 0330Z 28 FEB 66.
(4) WITNESS: USAF FACILITY (BRADLEY FIELD).
(5) BRIEF OBJECT DESCRIPTION: WHITE AND FLASHING GREEN LIGHTS, BRILLIANT, SEVERAL TIMES BRIGHTER THAN STARS. ALTITUDE UNKNOWN.
(6) RADAR DETECTION.

REMARKS: AN ATTEMPT BY TELEPHONE OFFICER TO OBTAIN A CONTACT WITH USAF FACILITY THAT MAY HAVE OBSERVED THIS INCIDENT WAS UNSUCCESSFUL AS THE FACILITY IS LOCATED IN CONN AREA COULD HAVE PROMPTED THIS RESPONSE.

LT. COL USAF
PROJECT OFFICER, BLUEBOOK
