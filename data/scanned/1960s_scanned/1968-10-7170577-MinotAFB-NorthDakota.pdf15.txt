OBJECT WAS FIRST SIGHTED AROUND 2100 HOURS 7 OCTOBER 1968 BY A MALE WITNESS WHO WAS DRIVING NORTH ON HIGHWAY 83 APPROXIMATELY 10 MILES SOUTH OF MINOT AFB, NORTH DAKOTA. OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS BRILLIANT WHITE LIGHT, ROUND IN SHAPE, AND SLIGHTLY LARGER THAN A BASKETBALL AT ARM'S LENGTH. OBJECT WAS ACCOMPANIED BY TWO SMALLER OBJECTS GIVING OFF PULSING RED AND WHITE LIGHTS ACCOMPANIED BY A DISTINCT WHITE-YELLOW LIGHT AT THE CENTER. WITNESS STATED OBJECT MOVED ERRATICALLY IN VARIOUS DIRECTIONS AND EXPERIENCED RAPID CHANGES IN SPEED RANGING FROM HOVER TO VERY HIGH SPEED.
OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 15 MINUTES. WITNESS STATED THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE UNDER INTELLIGENT CONTROL. THE OBJECT SEEMED TO REACT TO AND RESPOND TO HIS VEHICLE'S HEADLIGHTS. WITNESS STOPPED HIS VEHICLE AND WATCHED THE OBJECT FOR SOME TIME, THEN CONTINUED ON HIS WAY.
THE MILITARY AUTHORITIES WERE NOTIFIED AND INITIATED AN INVESTIGATION. THIS INCIDENT IS CURRENTLY UNDER FURTHER INVESTIGATION BY BLUE BOOK STAFF.
