McClellan AFB, California. 1966-12-82863
TARGET : Unknown
1. On 8 December 1966, McClellan Air Force Base, California, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object.
2. The observer, Mr. George Aiken, a communications technician at McClellan AFB, observed the object at approximately 1700 hours while driving on the base.
3. Mr. Aiken stated that the object was round, silvery in color, and about 30 feet in diameter. It was moving at a slow rate of speed and at an altitude of approximately 1000 feet.
4. Mr. Aiken further stated that the object was observed for approximately 2 minutes before it disappeared from sight.
5. This case has been investigated and it is the opinion of this office that the object observed was probably a weather balloon.
