REF : 1-1995-4/59

A report of a responsible witness in Fiji was received at the Consulate SUVA on April 22, 1963 (G.M.T.).
The witness, a prominent business man, observed an unidentified object over Fiji at 1320 G.T. on April 21, 1963. The object was saucer-shaped, silver in color, and estimated to be approximately 75 feet in diameter. It was observed for about two minutes before disappearing in a westerly direction at a high rate of speed. No sound was heard.

The witness is considered reliable and has provided the Consulate with detailed information regarding the incident. The report has been forwarded to the Department of State for any further action deemed appropriate.
