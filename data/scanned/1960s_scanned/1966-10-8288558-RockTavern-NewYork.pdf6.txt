REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
17 October 1966
ROCK TAVERN, NEW YORK
Investigator: Mr. James McLeod
Date of Incident: 17 September 1966
Time of Incident: Approximately 0200 hours
Location of Incident: Rock Tavern, New York
On 17 September 1966, at approximately 0200 hours, Mr. Jacob Freeborn, a resident of Rock Tavern, New York, reported observing a strange object in the sky over his home. Mr. Freeborn stated that he was awakened by a bright light outside his bedroom window. Upon looking outside, he observed a large, disc-shaped object hovering approximately 100-150 feet above the ground. The object appeared to be approximately 30-35 feet in diameter and was emitting a bright, pulsating light. 
Mr. Freeborn stated that the object hovered silently for several minutes before suddenly accelerating and disappearing from view at a high rate of speed. He immediately called the local police department to report the incident.
An investigation was conducted by local law enforcement officials, who were unable to determine the nature or origin of the object. No other witnesses were identified in the immediate area.
This report has been forwarded to Project Blue Book for further investigation and analysis.
