Dear Mrs. [Redacted],
Reference your letter of 11 August 1966 regarding your observation on 30 April 1966. We have had a chance to research your case and I find that you have been referring to the case we received from you on 30 April which states that you did not see any object on 30 April.
In order that we may avoid confusion and any misunderstandings on your sighting of 30 April, we would appreciate your completing a new AFD Form 164 to explain what you observed. Since we have your original report on the sighting of 30 April, we ask that you confirm your sighting carefully and scientifically. Therefore, request you complete a new AFD Form 164 and return it to us as soon as possible.
Sincerely,
