17 August 1961

Dear Mr. --

I have received a courtesy copy of your letter of August 7th addressed to the Editor of the Post-Intelligencer concerning photographs you made of an unidentified flying object.

It would be greatly appreciated if you could forward us a copy of the photographs along with any additional details regarding the sighting. We would be happy to return the material to you within several days if you forward it to us.

Thank you for your interest in this matter.

Sincerely,

WILLIAM T. COLEMAN, JR.
Major, USAF
UFO Project Officer
Public Information Division
Office of Information
Seattle 2, Washington
