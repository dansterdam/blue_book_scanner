U.S. AIR FORCE TECHNICAL INFORMATION SHEET
This questionnaire has been prepared so that you can give the U.S. Air Force as much information as possible concerning the unidentified aerial phenomena that you have observed. Please try to answer as many questions as you possibly can. The information that you give will be used to evaluate the significance and validity of your observations. Your name will not be connected with any statements or observations without your permission. We request this personal information so that, if it is deemed necessary, we may contact you for further details.
