9 April 1961

Dear Mr. [Redacted],

Your letter of March 29th addressed to the Civil Aeronautics Administration has been referred to this office.

The Air Force is assisting in the responsibility for the determination of the identification and origin of any objects observed flying in the airspace under its control, including those may have been labeled unidentified flying objects. The selected scientific personnel involved in these analyses are only a small part of the personnel engaged on the project of investigating such incidents involving UFOs. The selected scientists involved in these analyses are only a small part of the personnel engaged on the project of investigating such incidents involving UFOs and their conclusions.

The information supplied in your letter is not sufficient for a conclusion to be reached regarding this sighting. Enclosed for your information is a pamphlet entitled "How to Report a UFO Sighting." Should you have any further data to submit, we shall be pleased to consider it.

Your interest in this matter is appreciated.

Very truly yours,

WILLIAM E. COLEMAN, JR.
Public Information Division
Office of Information
