PHOTO ANALYSIS REPORT
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: Old Bridge, New Jersey

PHOTOGRAPHY: One original print (8"x10")
1. PURPOSE: This report is in reply to Work Order Number 67-111, submitted by Major David L. Gaines, 10 March 1967, requesting photo analysis be conducted on an "Unidentified Flying Object" photographed in Old Bridge, New Jersey, 5 April 1967.
