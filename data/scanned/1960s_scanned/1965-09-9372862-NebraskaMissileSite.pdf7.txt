HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON. D.C.

20 SEP 1965

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects (UFO)

TO: AFSC (FTD)

1. Reference APR 200-2, as amended. The following described unidentified objects were added to our reports of 
6 August 1965.

2. Description of Objects: The reported objects are plotted on the attached map which corresponds to the locations described below:

a. Reported by civilian guard at Sioux Army Depot to Golf Launch Control Officer. Two bright lights, approximately 1/2 to 3/4 mile in size, observed with binoculars. The first sighting began at 22:35 local time, and the second sighting occurred at 22:48 local time. The objects appeared to be hovering, ascended, and then disappeared in the north-east direction.

b. Reported from home by two enlisted men and observed by nine military personnel. The objects appeared as bright lights, approximately 5-10 degrees in size, and flashing orange, blue, and white lights. The objects were observed from 23:30 to 23:45 local time, moving rapidly above 30 degrees to the west.

3. Objects appeared to be the size of a pinhead, appeared in the middle of the sky, and moved on course as indicated. When such objects grouped, they formed into a circular formation. Three white objects grouped and then disappeared.
