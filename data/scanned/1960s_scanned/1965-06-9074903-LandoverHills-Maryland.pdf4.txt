2. Description: 
On the night of 27 Jun 65, at approximately 2330 hours, a married couple observed a bright light in the sky over Lanover Hills, Maryland. The object was described as circular in shape, approximately 50 feet in diameter, and hovering about 100-150 feet off the ground. The object remained motionless for several minutes, then suddenly accelerated and disappeared from sight. The couple reported the incident to the DISTRICT OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS.

3. Evaluation: The USAF evaluation of this incident is that the object observed was possibly an unidentified flying object (UFO). The speed and maneuverability of the object are consistent with the characteristics of a UFO, and the visual observations by reliable witnesses lend credence to the report. No satisfactory conventional explanation for the sighting has been determined.
