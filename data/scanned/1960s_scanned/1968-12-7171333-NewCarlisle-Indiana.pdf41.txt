U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/ Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service
DAILY WEATHER MAPS
WEEKLY SERIES DEC. 2-8, 1968
The charts in this publication are a continuation of the principal charts of the Weather Bureau publication, Daily Weather Maps. They include the Surface Weather Map, the 500 Millibar Chart, the Highest and Lowest Temperature Chart, and the Daily Precipitation Chart. These charts are arranged on a single page of this publication. They are copies of operational weather maps prepared by the National Meteorological Center/Weather Service. The Surface Weather Map and the 500 Millibar Chart are the same as those used previously in Daily Weather Maps, an explanatory text is printed below each chart in charge by writing to: Environmental Science Services Administration, Public Inquiries Office, Silver Spring, Maryland 20910 or 20852. Bulk copies may also be ordered, at a cost of $2.30 per 50 copies. Checks should be made payable to the Superintendent of Documents.
