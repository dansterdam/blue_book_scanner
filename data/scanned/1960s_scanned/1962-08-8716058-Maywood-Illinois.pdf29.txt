No Case (Information Only)
6 August 1962
Pasco, Washington

On August 6th, a resident "phish" of Pasco, Washington, reported
that at about 11:00 p.m. on the night of August 4th, he and a
neighbor observed what appeared to be a dark, cigar-shaped object
in the sky over Pasco. The object was approximately 100 feet long
and was moving in a northwesterly direction at a moderate rate of
speed. The resident stated that the object had no visible markings
or lights, and made no sound. After observing the object for
approximately 45 seconds, it disappeared from view. The resident
stated that the weather was clear at the time of the observation.
