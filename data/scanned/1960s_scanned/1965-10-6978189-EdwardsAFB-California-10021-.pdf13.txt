REPORT NUMBER 10021-65
DATE: 6 OCTOBER 1965
LOCATION: EDWARDS AIR FORCE BASE, CALIFORNIA
DETAILS:
On 6 October 1965, at approximately 1900 hours, a civilian employee at Edwards Air Force Base, California, observed and reported sighting a "flying saucer" or unidentified flying object (UFO) over the base. The witness stated that the object was metallic and disc-shaped and hovered over the base for a short period of time before accelerating rapidly and disappearing from sight.
The witness was unable to obtain any photographs of the object. No other witnesses to the event have been identified at this time. The Air Force investigation into this incident is ongoing.
