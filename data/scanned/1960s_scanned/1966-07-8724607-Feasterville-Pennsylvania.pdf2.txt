Reference your unidentified observation of 25 July 1966. For information, a report was made by an observer in your area concerning a bright light which was observed on this date at a distance of approximately one-half mile. The object was said to be hovering about 20-30 feet above the ground. The observer noted it for approximately 2 minutes and it then accelerated and disappeared. This information is for your file.
We wish to thank you for reporting your observation to the Air Force.
Sincerely,

WALTER QUINTANILLA, Jr. Major, USAF
