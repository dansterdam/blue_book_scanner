CIVIL CHART SUPPLY
The charts are for civil use only and are distributed to outside parties and principal offices through the 6th District United States. The charts may be purchased from the Civil Aeronautics Administration, Washington 25, D.C.

MILITARY CHART SUPPLY
Military Aeronautical Charts, Maps and Photographic Prints for Aeronautical Purposes are included in the current edition of the USAF Catalog of Aeronautical Charts and Aeronautical Information Publications.
The Directional Air Wing and other agencies concerned are the only authorized distributors of military charts and information. The USAF will not distribute directly to outside parties.

Requisitioning:
Requisitions for special United States (USAF) editions are to be made directly to the appropriate Air Force commands as specified in the USAF Catalog of Aeronautical Charts and Aeronautical Information Publications.
