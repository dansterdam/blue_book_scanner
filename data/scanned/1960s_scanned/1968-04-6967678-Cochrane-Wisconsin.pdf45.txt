HEADQUARTERS USAF TECHNICAL SCHOOL (ATC)
CHANUTE AIR FORCE BASE, ILLINOIS 61868

TSXR (Capt. 232/61)                                                     20 May 1968

SUBJECT: Investigation of Unidentified Flying Object

TO: XPO

1. Basic Reporting Data and Format:

a. Description of the Object:
(1) Shape: Like a pointed boomerang.
(2) Size: Bigger than a car, smaller than an airplane, bigger than the road was wide.
(3) Color: Orangish.
(4) Number: One.
(5) Formation: N/A.
(6) Discernible features or details: None.
(7) Tail, trail, or exhaust: Cloudy stuff around it that looked like fog, smoke, or much hair.
(8) Sound: None.
(9) Other pertinent or unusual features: None.

b. Description of Course of Object:
(1) What first called the attention of observer to the object? An automatic panned observer and after some distance ahead, it appeared to stop and its lights went out. This is when observer saw it.
(2) Angle of elevation and azimuth of object when first observed: Estimate from observer's report the elevation to be approximately 15° and azimuth 180°.
(3) Angle of elevation of object upon disappearance: Appeared to go straight down until out of sight.
