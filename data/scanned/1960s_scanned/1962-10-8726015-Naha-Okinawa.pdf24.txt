1 October 1962
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object
1. On 6 October 1962 at approximately 1930 hours, an unidentified flying object was sighted in the vicinity of Naha Air Force Base, Okinawa. The object was observed by two security police patrolmen on duty.
2. According to the report, the object was observed for approximately 30 minutes. It was described as round, reddish-orange in color, and approximately 20-30 feet in diameter. The object was observed to hover, make sharp turns, and accelerate to a high rate of speed and disappear.
3. No sound was heard during the sighting. The area was overcast at the time with light wind conditions.
4. The object was not detected on radar.
5. The report indicates that the two security police patrolmen were interviewed and their report was considered credible.
6. No further information is available.
