MEMORANDUM FOR CHIEF, COUNTERINTELLIGENCE DIVISION, USAF

SUBJECT: Reference Your Case UB109

The following is a recommended reply for your proposed inquiry, based on information received regarding the sighting of an unidentified aerial object on 24 October 1950:

Dear Congressman Beckworth:

We have received your inquiry on behalf of Mr. [REDACTED] regarding the sighting of an unidentified aerial object on the evening of 24 October.

Also attached are quite certain that Mr. [REDACTED]'s report, based on information received from Carswell Air Force Base, indicates the object sighted was a USAF balloon. While we regret the inconvenience to your constituent, we trust this clarification of the facts underlying his report is responsive to your inquiry.
