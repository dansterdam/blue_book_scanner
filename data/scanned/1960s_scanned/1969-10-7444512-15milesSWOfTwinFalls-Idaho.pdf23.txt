SUBJECT: Telephone to Vandenburg AFB, California Regarding UFO Observation of 21 October 1959

On 29 December 1969, Lt Warano called Vandenburg AFB, California and talked to Sgt Mansfield at SAC scheduling (ext 65313). Sgt Mansfield stated that they had a Minuteman launch from Vandenburg at 1939 hours PST on 21 October 1959. This is equivalent to 2139 hours EDT or only 6 minutes from the reported time of sighting.
