1. Reference letter, HQ Foreign Technology Division, Air Force Systems Command, 15 July 1965, subject: UFO sightings, EARLY AUGUST. 

2. Airman 2c DONALD HAD SEEN and an UFO incident on 12 August to the 29th NORAD Region. The incident was reported to the United States Air Force. However, three other witnesses to the incident have been contacted.

3. In compliance with paragraph 14, AFR 200-2, the reported data is submitted:

a. Description of the Object:
(1) Varied in shape from round to oval; scarlike.
(2) The size was estimated as that of a pea.
(3) The objects varied in color and appeared to change color from white, rouge red, blue, green, white.
(4) Witnesses differed. One witness saw only one object. Two witnesses saw from 3 to 5, one of which was oval in shape and remained a dull red color.
(5) No definite formation but objects appeared to be randomly scattered in a small pattern.
(6) No trails were observed.
(7) The objects made no sound audible to the observers.

b. Description of Course:
(1) The objects were called to the attention of the available witnesses by the control tower operator who has been separated from the service.
(2) The objects were initially observed in an east-northeast direction from the base operations building at Whiteman Air Force Base.
