CANBERRA, A.C.T.

UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS

Sighted On 12th June, 1961, an unidentified flying object was sighted by M.P. and Mrs. J.P. Chamberlain, of EAST CAPE PLANTATION, MILNE BAY DISTRICT, New Guinea.

"This sighting was seen by M.P. and Mrs. J.P. Chamberlain, of EAST CAPE PLANTATION, MILNE BAY DISTRICT. Object was reported as follows:-
It was a bright, star-like object, emitting a bluish-grey light, without sound, and remained stationary for some time. It then moved off rapidly towards the North East and disappeared. Examination through binoculars showed it to be an elongated object, delta wing shape, about 30-40 feet in length. No noise was heard and only the bright object was seen. No further particulars are known. It moved to the North East and disappeared.

You may care to pass this report to the appropriate authorities.

(D.M. Cleland)
