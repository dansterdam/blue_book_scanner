PRIORITY
3/21/1967

THE FOLLOWING IS A REPORT OF A SIGHTING OF AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT:

AT APPROXIMATELY 1950 HOURS, 1 MARCH 1967, THE CONTROL TOWER OPERATOR AT MINOT AIR FORCE BASE, NORTH DAKOTA, OBSERVED A BRIGHT LIGHT IN THE NORTHWESTERN SKY. HE ESTIMATED THE OBJECT TO BE APPROXIMATELY 500 FEET IN ALTITUDE AND TRAVELING AT AN EXTREMELY HIGH RATE OF SPEED. IT TRAVELED IN A STRAIGHT LINE, MADE A SHARP TURN, AND THEN DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. THIS SIGHTING WAS CONFIRMED BY THE STAFF DUTY OFFICER ON BASE.

THE OBJECT DID NOT APPEAR ON THE RADAR SCOPE AND MADE NO SOUND. IT WAS DESCRIBED AS A BRIGHT, WHITE LIGHT, AND WAS VISIBLE FOR APPROXIMATELY 1 MINUTE.

THIS REPORT IS BEING FORWARDED FOR YOUR INFORMATION AND REVIEW.
