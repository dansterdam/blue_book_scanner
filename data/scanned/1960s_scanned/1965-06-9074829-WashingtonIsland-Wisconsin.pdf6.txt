MICHIGAN LAKE
CASE 64 - 10
DATE RECEIVED 26 JUN 1965
SYNOPSIS: Information reported concerning unidentified flying object sighting in the vicinity of Washington Island, Wisconsin.
DETAILS:
1. On 4 JUNE 1965, at approximately 2300 hours, a USAF patrol car on routine patrol observed an unidentified flying object hovering over the water approximately 1 mile off the shoreline of Washington Island, Wisconsin. The object was first observed to the east of the patrol car and was approximately 1/2 mile out over the water. It remained stationary for a period of approximately 1 minute and then rapidly accelerated in a westerly direction and disappeared from sight in approximately 3 seconds. The object was described as round or oval in shape and emitting a bright white light. No sound was heard emanating from the object.
2. The patrol car commander immediately notified the Commanding Officer, 21st Air Division, USAF, who in turn notified higher headquarters. An immediate investigation was conducted by the 21st Air Division. No further information has been received from the reporting agent concerning this sighting.
3. This case is being forwarded to Project Blue Book for analysis and evaluation.
