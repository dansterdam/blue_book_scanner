Radio operator at Airport spotted object that changed position constantly outlined by steady light in fact, changed shape from elongated to half round to square and finally moon-shaped. Believed to be 30 miles away at 1,500 ft. Clouds covered object after 23 minutes. Lights was steady, Lost from view 10 minutes later. Air Visual of object at that direction identified as a star 4 minutes after disappearance. Area over which object assumed to be by ground observer is a square.
