MEMO FOR THE RECORD 
West Milton, Ohio

On 22 Oct 62 five individuals, including the witness, were watching what looked like a bullet with no wings. There was a trail following directly behind it. It was going southwest. It was so shiny that it hurt the eyes to look at it. Centrally was 30-40 ft. away for about 5 minutes.
