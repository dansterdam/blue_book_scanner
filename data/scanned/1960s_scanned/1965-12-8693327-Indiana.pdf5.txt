MEMO FOR THE RECORD 10 DEC 65
DAYTON DAILY NEWS
Mr. Baxter wanted to know the latest news on the sighting seen on ACME, Pennsylvania location. Major Quintanilla told him that there were no local reports, only telephone calls. He told him that we just contacted Oakdale Radar Site and there was no additional information. Major told him that the highway patrol went out to the area, but found nothing.
