DEPARTMENT OF STATE
UNCLASSIFIED
Department of State
ANALYSIS AND DISTRIBUTION
BRANCH DISTRIBUTION
MAY 27 1966
DATE: MAY 25, 1966
FROM: Embassy RIO DE JANEIRO
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects
REF: DEPCIR telegram 2116
The information contained in the reference telegram was transmitted by the Embassy to the Department to substantiate information appearing in the press on May 3, 1966 (Journal do Brasil) from its correspondent in Curitiba, Parana, to the effect that thousands of persons in Ponte Grossa had seen a great number of false and rapid flying objects in the sky.
The embassy's communication advised that we were attempting to obtain more information on the sightings and to confirm the data and time of the report. It added that press reports indicated the phenomenon was considered by the Brazilians to be worthy of official investigation.
The Globe of May 3 quoted its correspondent in Curitiba, Parana, that hundreds or persons in Ponte Grossa had seen a great number of false and rapid flying objects in the sky.

UNCLASSIFIED
