3. On 1 August 1965, Harry P. Addrili, Meteorological Technician and Supervisor, Civil Engineering Division, was interviewed regarding his UFO sighting. Addrili stated his organization maintains a visual watch for UFO's. Around 0735 Zulu on 31 July 1965, Addrili and several other persons observed a bright shiny white light object emitting a red pulsing light which was attached to a parachute or balloon. Addrili stated the object moved rapidly in a southerly direction and disappeared during his observation which lasted approximately 5 minutes. Addrili further stated the radio sound equipment would normally begin to oscillate, although this did not occur. He said the pulsing light and the manner in which the object moved were different from the normal balloon or parachute appearances. Addrili noted the object was a bright shiny white in color which is the predominant color displayed by other UFO sightings in this area.
