REPORT UNCLASSIFIED

RAVENNA, OHIO (MANTUA) 7 APRIL 1966

1. GENERAL INFORMATION:

On 7 April 1966, at approximately 7:40 p.m., EST, Mrs. Helen Sallaska, age 44, of Ravenna, Ohio, reported sighting an unusual aerial object over Ravenna- Mantua Road, Mantua, Ohio.

Mrs. Sallaska stated that as she was driving home from her job in Ravenna, she observed a large, round, silvery-white object hovering in the sky over Ravenna-Mantua Road at a point about one-half mile south of the Mantua-Shaw Road intersection. Mrs. Sallaska indicated that the object appeared to be approximately 40 to 50 feet in diameter. She described the object as having a dull, silvery-white color and appearing to rotate or spin, and pulsate or throb.

Mrs. Sallaska further stated that the object remained in a stationary position for approximately two to three minutes. As she drove closer to the object, it suddenly accelerated at a high rate of speed and disappeared from sight in a northerly direction.

2. CONCLUSION:

Based on the description provided by the witness, the object sighted appears to be an unidentified flying object. No explanation for the sighting has been determined.
