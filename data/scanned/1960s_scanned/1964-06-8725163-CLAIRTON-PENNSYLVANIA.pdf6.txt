ON 8 JUNE 1964, THE FOLLOWING REPORT WAS RECEIVED BY PROJECT BLUE BOOK FROM CLAIRTON, PENNSYLVANIA.

AT APPROXIMATELY 2200 HOURS ON 8 JUNE 1964, A LOCAL RESIDENT REPORTED OBSERVING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE SKIES OVER CLAIRTON, PENNSYLVANIA. THE OBJECT WAS DESCRIBED AS ROUND IN SHAPE AND EMITTING A BRIGHT LIGHT. IT WAS OBSERVED FOR SEVERAL MINUTES BEFORE DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.

INVESTIGATORS WERE DISPATCHED TO THE AREA TO CONDUCT INTERVIEWS AND GATHER ADDITIONAL INFORMATION. THE ATTACHED PHOTOGRAPH WAS OBTAINED FROM ONE OF THE WITNESSES. HOWEVER, DUE TO THE POOR QUALITY OF THE IMAGE, NO SPECIFIC DETAILS CAN BE DISCERNED.
