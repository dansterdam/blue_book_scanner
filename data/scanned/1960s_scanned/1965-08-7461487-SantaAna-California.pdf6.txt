ON 7 AUGUST 1965 THE SANTA ANA POLICE DEPARTMENT RECEIVED A REPORT FROM A MR. ERNEST L. NORMAN, 2932 WEST ST., SANTA ANA, CALIFORNIA, THAT HE HAD OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE SKIES OVER SANTA ANA EARLIER THAT EVENING.

MR. NORMAN STATED THAT HE WAS OUTSIDE HIS HOME AT APPROXIMATELY 2030 HOURS WHEN HE OBSERVED A BRIGHT OBJECT MOVING RAPIDLY IN THE SKY FROM EAST TO WEST. HE SAID THAT THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE CIRCULAR IN SHAPE AND GAVE OFF A BRIGHT LIGHT. HE STATED THAT HE WATCHED THE OBJECT FOR APPROXIMATELY 3 TO 5 MINUTES BEFORE IT DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.

THERE WAS NO RECORD OF ANY OTHER REPORTS RECEIVED BY THE SANTA ANA POLICE DEPARTMENT OF THIS INCIDENT, AND NO OTHER CALLS WERE RECEIVED FROM CITIZENS REPORTING A SIMILAR OBSERVATION.
