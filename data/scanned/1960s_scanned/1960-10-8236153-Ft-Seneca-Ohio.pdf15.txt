Space Creek, Ohio
10-8-60
On Sept. 25, 1960, 8:30 pm, three disk-shaped objects passed over the observer, moving in an easterly direction. The objects appeared to be white in color, and were estimated to be 50 to 75 feet in diameter. They were observed for approximately 2 minutes. No sound was heard. The sky was clear and the stars were visible. The objects moved rapidly.
This report is respectfully forwarded for your attention and investigation.
