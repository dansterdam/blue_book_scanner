UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT SIGHTED OVER CALIFORNIA
CORONA CITY, CALIF., July 31 - A bright, round, reddish-orange object was reported to have been sighted over Corona City on July 22. The object was described as being about 20 to 30 feet in diameter, and it was said to have moved at a fast rate of speed. The object was first sighted at about 10:30 p.m. and was visible for about 5 minutes before it disappeared from view. Several persons reported seeing the object, and their descriptions of it were said to be consistent.
