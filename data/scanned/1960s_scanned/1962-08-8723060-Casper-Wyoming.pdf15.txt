Text extracted from the document:

NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

SEP 20 1962

Technical Division
Dayton Air Force Base, Ohio

Gentlemen:

We have received a letter from Senator Gale W. McGee, United States Senate, Washington 25, D.C.

The Senator is forwarding to us a report of your investigation, in duplicate, to a sighting of an unidentified flying object made by a witness in Casper, Wyoming.

Please submit any report of your investigation, in duplicate, to the Senator.

Thank you for your cooperation in this matter.

Sincerely yours,

/s/ Paul G. Dembling
Director
Office of Legislative Affairs
