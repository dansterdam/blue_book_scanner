Northwestern University scientists officers investigate Tintic area
Professor J. Allen Hynek (chairman of the astronomy) Dept. at Northwestern Univ.) and two of his associates are from Dayton, Ohio, and are making observations in the Tintic area of Utah, Southwest of Salt Lake City. They are investigating reports that there are flying objects being seen there, They are offering a $1,000 reward to anyone who can provide them with definite information leading to the identification of these alleged "tinitic area meteors", which is the term used for the objects. Professor Hynek is carrying on the investigation as a follow-up of the sighting that occurred in Eureka, Utah, in the Rocky Mountain area.
