REPORT OF INVESTIGATION

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS

DATE OF OCCURRENCE: 6 November 1967

LOCATION: Covington, Indiana

NAME OF WITNESS: Walter Knapp

1. On 6 November 1967, Walter Knapp, a resident of Covington, Indiana, reported to the Air Force that he had observed an unidentified flying object on the night of 6 November 1967.

2. Mr. Knapp stated that at approximately 8:30 p.m. on 6 November 1967, he was outdoors at his residence when he observed a bright light in the sky. He described the object as round in shape, about the size of a basketball, and silvery-white in color. The object was moving at a high rate of speed and made no sound.

3. Mr. Knapp observed the object for approximately 2 to 3 minutes before it disappeared from view. He did not attempt to photograph the object.

4. An investigation was conducted by the Air Force, but no further information was obtained. The object remains unidentified.
