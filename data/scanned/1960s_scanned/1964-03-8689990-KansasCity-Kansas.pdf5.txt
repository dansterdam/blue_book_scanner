AIRLINE PILOTS REPORT EXPLODING UFO
March 19 -- most extraordinary UFO report was issued by Pan American Airlines pilots and captain flying their N7797U jet near R.A. Bothtow.
Captain Morris was piloting an aircraft containing 42 passengers bound from London to New York, and Captain Botthoe was co-pilot on a flight of 25 passengers on an route from New York to Frankfurt.
Both observed a strange object traveling at high altitude on an apparent north to south trajectory, which unexpectedly exploded with an immense brilliance, parts of which were obscured with an immense brilliance, parts of which were obscured with columns of smoke.
Neither pilot has been able to identify the phenomenon.
