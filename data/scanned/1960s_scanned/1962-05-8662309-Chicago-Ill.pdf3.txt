Green flash over Cincinnati
On 3 May 1963, at 8:55 p.m., a bright green flash streaked across the Cincinnati sky. Several witnesses reported seeing the light which traveled from north to south in a matter of seconds. The light was described as a "bluegreen puff" hues in color.
