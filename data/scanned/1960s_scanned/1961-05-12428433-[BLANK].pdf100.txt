LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL

HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES,
COMMITTEE ON SCIENCE AND ASTRONAUTICS,
Washington, D.C., May 22, 1961.

Hon. OVERTON BROOKS,
Chairman, Committee on Science and Astronautics.

This report on this country's only man-in-space program, "Project Mercury," is forwarded herewith for committee consideration a report entitled "Project Mercury - Second Interim Report."

This report has been prepared on the basis of briefings and materials provided by the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. The bulk of the information was provided by Mr. Howard J. Silverstein, who conducted the report. The report was reviewed by the committee prior to its submittal.

The report has been given proper staff review prior to its submittal to you for consideration.

CHARLES F. DUCANDER,
Executive Director and Chief Counsel.
