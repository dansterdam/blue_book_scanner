The Lansing, Michigan Area

On 8 May 1964 at approximately 9:00 p.m., EDT, the witness was driving west on US-127, approximately 7 miles south of Lansing, Michigan. The witness observed an object in the sky to the north. The object was described as round or oval in shape and appeared to be glowing with a yellowish-white light. The object was moving slowly and silently. The witness observed the object for approximately 5-10 minutes before it disappeared from view to the northeast.

The witness was an adult male. No other witnesses were reported. The weather at the time was clear with few clouds and the visibility was good. No photographs of the object were obtained.

This sighting was investigated and evaluated by the United States Air Force under Project Blue Book. The sighting was determined to be "Unidentified".
