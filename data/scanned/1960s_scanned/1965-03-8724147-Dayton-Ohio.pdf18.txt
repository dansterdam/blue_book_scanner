FFD (ANEW)
TO: A.F. PETERSON AFB, OHIO 45433
18 APRIL 1965

Dayton, Ohio 45433 
Dear Mr. Peterson:

On 3 April 1965 you reported an observation of an object on the night of 30 March 1965. This object was associated with other observations on or about the same time in the Dayton + Xenia area.

Would you please complete the attached AFTO Form 164 and return it to this office. This information is provided to assist us in our investigation. 

We assure you that your name will not be released to the news media regarding this matter.

Sincerely,

HECTOR QUINTANILLA, JR.
Major, USAF 
Chief, Project Blue Book
