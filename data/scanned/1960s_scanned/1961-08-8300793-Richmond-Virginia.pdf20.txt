17 Aug 1961
Stillwater, Minnesota

Aug. 17, Stillwater, Minn. Five citizens sighted an unidentified object over the city at about 10:20 p.m. The object was described as a "Lubback Lights" type, about 50 feet in diameter, with a white light on top and in the center, with a red light blinking on the bottom. Two witnesses reported the sighting as a red-glowed Triangular object with body.
