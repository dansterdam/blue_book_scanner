RECORD OF INVESTIGATION

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

DATE OF OBSERVATION: 10 OCTOBER 1961

LOCATION: JENSEN, UTAH

WITNESS: MR. JAMES JENSEN

1. This case was reported to the United States Air Force through normal civil channels. The initial report was made to the Commanding General, Ogden Air Materiel Area, Hill Air Force Base, Utah, and forwarded to Project Blue Book, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio. The following is a summary of the pertinent facts as contained in the official report.

2. On 10 October 1961 at approximately 7:25 A.M. MDST, Mr. James Jensen, a resident of Jensen, Utah, observed an unidentified object in the sky over his home. At the time, Mr. Jensen was standing on his front porch preparing to go to work. The object was first sighted at an estimated altitude of 10,000 to 15,000 feet above the ground and was moving in a straight line from southwest to northeast.

3. Mr. Jensen described the object as round in shape, silver in color, and approximately the size of a basketball at arm's length. It appeared to be rotating or spinning as it moved through the sky. The object was in view for approximately 30 seconds before disappearing from sight.

4. No sound was heard and no trail or exhaust was observed. The sky was clear at the time with a few scattered clouds.

5. Mr. Jensen immediately reported the sighting to the local authorities and later to the Ogden Air Materiel Area, Hill Air Force Base, Utah.

6. The report was carefully investigated by Project Blue Book, United States Air Force. All factors considered, the object could not be identified and was classified as "Unidentified" in the Project Blue Book files.
