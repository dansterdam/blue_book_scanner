Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio 45433
7 June 1966

Mr. and Mrs. [Redacted]
Toledo, Ohio #3608

Dear Mr. and Mrs. [Redacted],

Reference your recent unidentified observation of 28 March 1966. The information in your letter was not sufficient for evaluation. Request you complete the attached PID Form 164 and return it to the address provided.

I wish to assure you that we do not release the names of individuals without their permission. We are enclosing a copy of the brochure which describes the Air Force's program on unidentified flying objects.  

I wish to thank you for reporting your observation to the Air Force.

Sincerely,

HECTOR QUINTANILLA, JR., Major, USAF
