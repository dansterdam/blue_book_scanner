[Argentina-may 1962]
A flying sauger landed on an isolated farm in the province of Buenos Aires. The saucer hovered a short distance above the ground and several small beings exited the object, which they then re-entered and took off.

[may 31-june 5, 1962] the Argentine Government, on may 31, officially reported that a "flying saucer" had been sighted by two businessmen, a rancher and his wife. The woman, who had a closer view, said two round people were in the object. An Argentine Air Force officer said experts confirmed that the object displayed the characteristics of a flying saucer.
