10001 ME AIRDRM CER

WASHINGTON, D.C.

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Report

TO: DOGR

1. The following UFO report was received by the Officer of Air Force DOGR from the Command Post on 5 Jun 64, and is forwarded for appropriate reference.

2. A woman called stating she had seen a UFO 31 May 64, between 2300 and 2330 hours. Her husband and 3 children also witnessed the same object. It was coming from the SE moving to the NW at a moderate speed. The rounded object made an abrupt, rapid upward turn and proceeded to move rapidly in a southward direction out of sight. The color of the object was reddish. The size was that of a small aircraft and it made no sound.

3. Further information in the nearby area confirmed that no aircraft were flying in the area. The color of the object was also confirmed by other witnesses. No other information was received on either of these objects.

PAUL B. RICE
Major, USAF
Base Operations Officer
