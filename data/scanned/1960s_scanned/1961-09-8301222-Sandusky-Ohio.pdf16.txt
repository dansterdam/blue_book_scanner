REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
On 8 September 1961 the undersigned received information from Mr. Gertrude Martin, 901 West Liberty Street, Sandusky, Ohio, that an unidentified flying object had been sighted over Sandusky, Ohio on the night of 6 September 1961.
The report was forwarded to this office by the Commanding Officer, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio.
Investigation of the incident was conducted by the undersigned on 11 September 1961.
DETAILS OF SIGHTING
Mrs. Martin stated that on the evening of 6 September 1961, at approximately 7:15 p.m., she and her husband were sitting on their front porch when they observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Sandusky, Ohio. The object appeared to be oval in shape and yellowish-orange in color. It was observed for approximately two minutes before disappearing from sight.
Mrs. Martin further stated that she had taken a photograph of the object, but that she did not have the film developed at the time of the interview. She stated that she would forward the photograph to this office as soon as it was developed.
CONCLUSION
This report is forwarded for your information. No further action is contemplated at this time pending receipt of the photograph from Mrs. Martin.
