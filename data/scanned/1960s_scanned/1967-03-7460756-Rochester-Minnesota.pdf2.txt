MEMORANDUM FOR: Director of Technology and Systems
SUBJECT: Sighting of UFO (Case) H. Quintanilla/70916/mh 23May) 24 May 1967

REFERENCE: Telephone conversation of 23May 1967. The report indicates that you took pictures of the UFO. In order to aid in our evaluation, we would appreciate your forwarding the original negatives.

For your information, the minimum camera data required are:
a- Type of camera.
b- Type focal length, and make of lens.
c- Brand and type of film.
d- Shutter speed used.
e- Lens opening used; that is, f/ stop.
f- Was tripod or solid stand used.
g- Pointing direction camera was in relation to north, and its angle with relation to the ground.

2. If supplemental information is unobtainable, the minimum camera data required are the type of camera, the smallest and largest f/ stop and shutter settings of the camera.

Upon receipt of the above, an evaluation of the analysis will be undertaken and your negatives a comprehensive examination.

Upon completion of the analysis, we will return your negatives along with our findings.

James C. MARATT, Colonel, USAF
Director of Technology and Systems
