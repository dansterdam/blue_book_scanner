SUMMARY:
1. On 16 April 1962, a brilliant mid-day sighting of an unidentified flying object was made over the Lynndyl and Eureka areas of Utah. The object was described as a brilliant light source which appeared to hover and move around erratically. It allowed brilliant illumination. At pilot in a Cessna 150 observed the object at close range, following it in an attempt to identify it. The pilot stated the object appeared to be a bright light and appeared much larger than a normal aircraft. Observations and radar trackings confirm the presence of a metallic aircraft over this area at the time of the sighting.

2. At 0530Z on 19 April 1962 Captain JOHN G. SHIELD, 5th Tactical Control Squadron, Hill AFB, Utah, observed a brilliant light near Gould, Arizona which flashed and changed color. This object was observed on radar at Hill AFB and tracked intermittently for an appreciable length of time.
