MEMO for the record: 1 Feb 67
Contact with SPADATS revealed that on 25 Jan 67 there were several observed objects. The one most likely responsible for the object 2658 number is object #2659. SPADATS said that object # 2659 was the rocket body of Cosmos 139, while object # 2658 was some additional space debris. SPADATS said that object # 2658 had no time for decay since it is decayed shortly after launch. No definite data was obtained on the object since it had such a short life.
