REPORT ON 8293154 - NORTH DAKOTA
FROM MAJOR BAZINET, BLUE BOOK, DAYTON, OHIO
DATE: 8 SEPTEMBER 1966
SUBJECT: SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. On 3 September 1966, this office received a report from GWINNER, North Dakota, concerning the sighting of an unidentified flying object over that area.

2. The report was received from GWINNER who stated that on the evening of 2 September 1966, at approximately 2040 hours, he and his family were outside their home when they observed a circular, luminous object in the sky. The object appeared to be approximately 30 feet in diameter and was hovering at an estimated altitude of 300 to 400 feet above the ground. The object remained visible for approximately 5 minutes before disappearing behind some trees.

3. GWINNER stated that the object was completely silent and had no visible means of propulsion. He further stated that the object appeared to have a sort of glow or luminescence about it, but that it was not reflecting any light.

4. This office has forwarded the report to Project Blue Book for further analysis and evaluation. No further action is contemplated by this headquarters at this time.
