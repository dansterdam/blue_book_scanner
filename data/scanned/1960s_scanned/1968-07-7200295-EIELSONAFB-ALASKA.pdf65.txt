MEMORANDUM FOR RECORD
22 JULY 1968
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object Sighting
1. On 11 Jul 68, this office received a telephone call from Mr. [redacted], a civilian employee at Eielson AFB, Alaska, concerning an unidentified flying object which he observed on the evening of 10 Jul 68 between the hours of 1100-1130.
2. Mr. [redacted] stated that while driving approximately 30 miles northwest of Fairbanks, Alaska, he observed a strange light in the sky. The light appeared to be round in shape, emitting a bright white light. The object moved in a straight line at a high rate of speed, then stopped suddenly, hovered for a few seconds, and then moved off at a high rate of speed in a different direction. The object was observed for approximately 1-2 minutes.
3. Mr. [redacted] reported the incident to the Eielson AFB Command Post, who in turn notified this office. No other witnesses were identified.
4. This office contacted Mr. [redacted] and obtained the above information. No further action is contemplated at this time. This case is considered closed.
