OHIO CHILDREN WITNESS NEAR-LANDING OF UFO 
During the early evening hours of 22 Nov 54, a number of children in the Leroy, Ohio neighborhood reportedly observed a UFO which almost landed on a farm. The UFO was described as being round and silver in color, appearing to be about 40 feet in diameter. It hovered over a field for several minutes before departing at a high rate of speed. The children reported hearing a whirring sound emanating from the object. No landing traces were found. The incident was reported to Project Blue Book.
