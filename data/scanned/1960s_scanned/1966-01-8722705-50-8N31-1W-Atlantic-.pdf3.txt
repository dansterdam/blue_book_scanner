PHOTO ANALYSIS REPORT N, 66-23
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: SEATTLE, WASHINGTON

PHOTOGRAPHY: 1 print, 1 negative

PURPOSE: This report is in response to Work Order Number 66-36 from Major R. J. Quintanilla, USAF UFO, who asked for an analysis of a negative and print depicting an Unidentified Flying Object.  
ANALYSIS: This analysis was completed jointly by the Photo Analysis and Photo Processing Sections. The object in the photographs appears to be a metallic, slightly elongated, circular shaped object. It appears to reflect sunlight and have been observed at a relatively short distance. No evidence of structure, wings, or a wake has been caused. The object appears to have been observed in level flight.
