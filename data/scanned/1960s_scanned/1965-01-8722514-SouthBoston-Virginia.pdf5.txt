South Boston, Virginia - January 31, 1964
On January 30, 1964, at approximately 6:45 a.m., Mr. and Mrs. Johnson were awakened by an unusual sound outside their home. They looked out and saw an object hovering over a nearby field. The object was described as cigar-shaped, about 50-60 feet long, and about 20 feet wide. It was a dull metallic color and appeared to give off a reddish glow. The object hovered for a few minutes, then moved away at a high rate of speed.
Mr. and Mrs. Johnson were the only witnesses to this incident. The case was investigated by the Air Force and was classified as "Unidentified".
