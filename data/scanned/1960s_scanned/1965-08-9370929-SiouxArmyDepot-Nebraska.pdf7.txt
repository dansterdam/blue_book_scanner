SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects (UFO)
TO: AFSC (FTD)
1. Reference AFR 200-2, as amended. The following described unidentified flying objects are an addition to our report of 6 August 1965.
2. Description of Objects: The reported objects are plotted on the attached sketch map as referenced in subparagraphs below:
(1) Reported by civilian guard at Sioux Army Depot to Golf Launch Control Facility personnel. Objects were observed initially over the site and later drifted SW and the site disappeared. The 220 local time. The latter sightings consisted of two objects each time. 
(2) Reported by military personnel with the aid of binoculars. Nine military personnel observed the objects the size of a star, which appeared to the southwest and traveled westward, disappearing in the distance. Very faint, and of short duration.
(3) Objects reported to be twice the size of a pinhead, appeared in the middle of the big dipper and moved on a level course as indicated. When they reached the Pleiades, they threw white objects grouped as they moved on.
Observed by three airmen of a Geodetic Survey Team, who observed them full of the Geodetic (equivalent to a 40 power telescope).
