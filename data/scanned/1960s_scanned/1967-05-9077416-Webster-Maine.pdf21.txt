REFERENCE: TDPT (UFO)
Your letters dated December 28, 1967 and January 2, 1968

Dear Major Quintanilla:

In answer to your first letter of December 28, 1967, although Ronson Helicopters, Inc. has not been used for about two years and it is not contemplated that it will again be used. As a matter of fact, we are attempting at this time to sell this particular sign.

I think the above answers your letter of January 2, 1968. We in Atlantic City at that time.

We will be pleased to be of any additional service for you if possible, and we would also like to request for our own purpose the names and aerial sign operators in the United States.

Sincerely,
RONSON HELICOPTERS, INC.

(Signed)
Andrew Astrud
Vice President
