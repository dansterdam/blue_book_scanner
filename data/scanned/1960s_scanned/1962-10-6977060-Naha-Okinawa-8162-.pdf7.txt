RECORD OF INVESTIGATION INTO A FLYING OBJECT INCIDENT 

1. DATE OF OCCURRENCE: 5 OCTOBER 1962

2. LOCATION OF INCIDENT: NAHA AIR BASE, OKINAWA (22°53'N, 125°56'E)

3. TIME OF INCIDENT: 1905 LOCAL

4. DESCRIPTION OF INCIDENT: AT 1905 LOCAL TIME ON 5 OCTOBER 1962, SSgt JOSEPH F. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 60 SECONDS AND TRAVELED IN A NORTHERLY DIRECTION ACROSS THE SKY. IT APPEARED TO BE LARGER THAN A STAR AND BRIGHTER THAN THE SURROUNDING STARS. IT MOVED IN A STRAIGHT LINE AND THEN MADE A SHARP 90 DEGREE TURN BEFORE DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.
