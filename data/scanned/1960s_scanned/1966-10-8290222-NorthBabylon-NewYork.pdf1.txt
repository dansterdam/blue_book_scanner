PROJECT 10073 RECORD
1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 25-26 Oct. 66
2. LOCATION: North Babylon, New York
3. SOURCE: Civilian
4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: Three
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: Varied
6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Ground Visual
7. COURSE: Varied
8. PHOTOS: No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No
10. CONCLUSION: Astro(S/P) Satellite (Echo I)
11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS:
Observers watched three objects in the sky for several hours and thirty minutes. The objects moved in a triangle pattern except in a square pattern. The main body of light was flashing and changing colors. The objects moved across the sky from the S to the NE. It had a 25-30 foot apparent size. Observers compared the objects to stars, but they were flashing.
