UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
REPORT
DATE OF OBSERVATION: 6 September 1963
LOCATION: Oxford County, MAINE
TIME OF OBSERVATION: Unknown
REPORT BY: Camillus Carabine
TELEPHONE REPORT
SUMMARY: At approximately 2300 hours on the night of 6 September 1963, Camillus Carabine, a resident of Rumford, Maine, called the local Sheriff's Department to report an unidentified flying object. Carabine stated that he was driving near Rumford when he observed a bright light in the sky. He stopped his car and watched the object for several minutes. Carabine described the object as being disc-shaped, hovering, and giving off a brilliant white light. He also stated that the object suddenly accelerated and disappeared from sight. The Sheriff's Department notified the U.S. Air Force, who dispatched personnel to investigate the sighting. This report is the result of that investigation.
