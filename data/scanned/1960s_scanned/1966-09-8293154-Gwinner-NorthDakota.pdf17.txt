NORTH DAKOTA U.F.O. SIGHTING REPORT, CASE NO. 8293154
1. Date and Time of Sighting: 8 September, 1966, approximately 7:00 p.m. Central Standard Time
2. Duration of Sighting: approximately 10-15 minutes
3. Location of Sighting: Approximately 4 miles southeast of Gwinner, North Dakota
4. Number of Witnesses: 1
5. Description of Object(s): Circular, glowing object observed hovering and maneuvering in the sky.
6. Description of Course of Object(s): The object was observed to hover and make several quick left-to-right movements before disappearing.
7. Weather Conditions During Sighting: Clear skies, no clouds.
8. Witness Comments: The witness, a local resident, reported seeing the object while driving home from work. He stopped his vehicle to observe the object more closely. The object was silent and appeared to be 30-40 feet in diameter.
9. Investigation and Analysis: Project Blue Book was unable to identify the source of this unidentified flying object.
