UFO 31 March 1966
Two persons, Mr. and Mrs., called to give account of sighting.
1. Mr. Columbus, Ohio, reported that he and his wife were driving between 2300-2330 E. about 6 miles west of Columbus on the old county road between 2300-2330 E. and sighted a bright light in the sky. They further information given.

2. Mr. Columbus, Ohio, reported sighted, preliminarily, gem with a reddish bright light and a silvery outer ring which was not blinking. Couldn't definitely make out the shape, but that the light was clearly visible. Claims that appeared to be slightly larger than the full moon and noiseless. No color.
