6 Oct. 66 
Several calls were received regarding a streak of fire in the sky 
about 1015 EST 6 Oct. 66. One observer reported that the fire 
seemed to be following an aircraft. Another reported only the fire 
was seen.
Contact was made with the Base Operations WP AFB, Ohio at 1105 EST 
6 Oct. 66. A B-111 took off from Patterson Field at 1006 EST 
6 Oct. 66 and shortly thereafter kicked in his after-burner. They 
had received several calls regarding this incident also. They 
reported that an unusually large streak of fire was a result of 
his after-burner being kicked in.
CONCLUSION 
F-111 with after-burner
