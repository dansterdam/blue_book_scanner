CLASSIFIED TECHNOLOGY DIVISION ACT

FOREIGN TECHNOLOGY DIVISION USAF

INFORMATION REPORT
DATE: 9 SEPTEMBER 1968
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT (UFO) SIGHTING

1. On 6 September 1968, at approximately 2030 hours, a security guard observed an unidentified flying object (UFO) over the Naval Air Station, Key West, Florida.

2. The object was described as bright white in color, triangular in shape, and approximately 50-75 feet in diameter. It was observed hovering at an estimated altitude of 3000-4000 feet above the airfield for approximately 15 minutes.

3. At approximately 2045 hours, the object suddenly accelerated and departed the area at a high rate of speed, estimated to be in excess of 1500 mph.

4. The sighting was reported to the UFO Reporting Center, and the incident is being investigated by the Foreign Technology Division, United States Air Force.
