January 7, 1962, Auburn, California -
A civilian, whose name is not given, reported to the Air Force that he had observed an unidentified object in the sky over Auburn, California, at approximately 9:05 P.M. The object was described as being circular in shape, emitting a reddish-orange color, and estimated to be about 50-75 feet in diameter. The object was observed for about 1 to 1-1/2 minutes before it disappeared.
The report was forwarded to the Air Force for further investigation.
