TO HUMAN HEALTH SERVICE
WASH DC

AT APPROXIMATELY 0635 AM ON 6 MARCH 1966 OFFICERS OF THE TRENTON NEW JERSEY POLICE DEPARTMENT RECEIVED A CALL FROM A RESIDENT REPORTING A UFO SIGHTING IN THE AREA OF TRENTON NEW JERSEY.

OFFICERS WERE DISPATCHED TO THE AREA AND OBSERVED A LARGE LUMINOUS OBJECT HOVERING ABOVE A FIELD OFF OF ROUTE 29 SOUTH OF TRENTON NJ.

THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE CIRCULAR IN SHAPE WITH SMALL BLUISH LIGHTS ON THE EXTERIOR. IT REMAINED HOVERING FOR APPROXIMATELY 1 1/2 HOURS AND WAS VISIBLE TO A LARGE NUMBER OF CITIZENS. 

OFFICERS FAILED TO MAKE CONTACT OR COMMUNICATE WITH THE OBJECT. NO NOISE OR EXHAUST GASES WERE OBSERVED EMITTING FROM THE OBJECT.

UPON LEAVING THE AREA THE OBJECT ACCELERATED AT A TREMENDOUS RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.
