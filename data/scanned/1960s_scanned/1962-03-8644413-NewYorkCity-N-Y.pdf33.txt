11 MARCH 1962
DAWSON, IOWA

A routine saucer sighting from Dawson, Iowa, dated March 11th, has one unusual feature and that is a perfectly round spot in the middle of the object, black in color, which appeared to have a blinking light at its center. The object was observed for about 10 minutes. It was round, silver in color, and completely silent.
