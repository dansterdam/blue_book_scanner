NO CASE (INFORMATION ONLY) 
9 August 1962
Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio

EYE DET. Falls,O. 8/9/62
Sighting as reported by father & observer as a bright light in the sky at an estimated altitude of 1,000 to 1,500 feet. It was at first hovering then went into a circular movement and shortly thereafter moved straight up out of sight at a very high rate of speed. The duration was about 5 minutes. No sound was heard. Witnesses indicated that the duration was longer than 5 minutes.
