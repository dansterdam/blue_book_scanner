SUBJECT: SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. On 7 October 1969, at approximately 1730 hours, Mr. C. A. Johnson, a civilian employee of the US Army Aviation Systems Command, St. Louis, Missouri, reported sighting an unidentified flying object (UFO) over Chicago, Illinois.

2. Mr. Johnson stated that while driving north on the Kennedy Expressway in Chicago, he observed a circular, metallic-appearing object hovering over the expressway. He stated the object appeared to be approximately 30 to 40 feet in diameter and approximately 100 to 150 feet above the ground. Mr. Johnson further stated that the object hovered for approximately 2 to 3 minutes, then accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight.

3. An investigation of this incident was conducted by Special Agent R. H. Quintanilla of the Office of Special Investigations (OSI), Chanute Air Force Base, Illinois. The results of this investigation are as follows:

a. Mr. Johnson's description of the object he observed was consistent with reports of similar sightings in the Chicago area.

b. No other witnesses to the sighting could be located.

c. No radar contact of the object was established by any air traffic control facility in the Chicago area.

d. No aircraft or other known aerial vehicles were operating in the vicinity at the time of the sighting.

4. Based on the available information, this incident is classified as "Unidentified."
