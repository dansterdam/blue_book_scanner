REPORT
SANTA FE (UPI) - New Mexico State Police reported today they had received reports of a strange flying object seen over a remote area near La Madera in northern New Mexico.
According to the reports, the object appeared to be about 100 feet in diameter. It was first seen hovering over the area at about 8:30 p.m., then it took off at a high rate of speed, the reports said.
Several persons in the area called the State Police and reported seeing the object. The State Police said they had notified the Air Force, but an Air Force spokesman said they had no reports of any unidentified flying objects in the area.
According to the reports, several cars in the area were disabled momentarily when the object passed over them, but there were no other details on the nature of the disabling. The reports indicated the object made a loud noise as it took off.
The remote area where the object was seen is near the Rio Grande River in northern New Mexico.
