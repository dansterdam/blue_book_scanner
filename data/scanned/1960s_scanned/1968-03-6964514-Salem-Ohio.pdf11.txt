TPPT (UFO) Lt Col Quintanilla/70916/EPHA/12 Jun 68
UFO Observations, 13 Mar 68 & 2 Apr 68

1. The Aerial Phenomena Research Organization, Kentucky 40217, is in receipt of an unidentified flying object report from near Neville, Ohio which occurred on 2 April 1968 at 2015 hours.

2. The observer sighted an "object" that appeared as a series of gliding lights in a fixed position. Some of the lights went off and on, in a regular rhythmical manner while several others remained constantly on.  

3. We also have another report from Mt. Healthy, Ohio, which occurred on 13 March 1968 as approximately 1925 hours. The description is given on the attached Xerox portion of his report.

4. Request information on whether your electrical advertising may have been the cause for this observation. Thank you for your assistance.

RALPH QUINTANILLA, Jr., Lt Colonel, USAF
Deputy Chief, Aerial Phenomena Office
Air Force Systems Command Production Directorate
