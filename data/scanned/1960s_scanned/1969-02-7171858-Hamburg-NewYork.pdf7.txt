PROJECT BLUE BOOK
JANUARY 1969

MEMORANDUM FOR RECORD

SUBJECT: SIGHTING REPORT - HAMBURG, NEW YORK 
DATE: FEBRUARY 7, 1969

On the evening of February 7, 1969, at approximately 7:00 p.m., a resident of Hamburg, New York reported sighting a brilliantly lighted unidentified flying object over the Hamburg area. The object was observed for approximately 10 minutes.

The object was described as round in shape, approximately 40-50 feet in diameter, and emitting a brilliant white light. It was reported to be hovering and maneuvering at an estimated altitude of 1,000-2,000 feet. No sound was heard associated with the object.

This report was investigated by the United States Air Force, Project Blue Book, and classified as "Unidentified." No further information is available.
