BRIEF SUMMARY:

On June 6, 1968, at approximately 2015 hours, a local citizen reported observing an unidentified aerial object in the sky over Davenport, Iowa. The object was described as a dark, oval-shaped object hovering in the sky.

The witness reported that the object remained stationary for several minutes before rapidly accelerating and disappearing from view.

Attempts to identify the object through visual observation and radar tracking were unsuccessful. The case remains unresolved and is classified as an unidentified aerial phenomenon.
