Mysterious Explosion 
At Tacoma

At approximately midnight on December 10, 1953, what sounded like an explosion was heard over a wide area of Tacoma, Washington. City and county officials were unable to determine the cause. However, the usual number of inquiries were made by local citizens. No Air Force planes were reported in the area at the time.
