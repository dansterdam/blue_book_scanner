The Flip Low Altitude High Speed Training Route Chart -Eastern U.S. was used to try and determine if any aircraft on a low level training mission could have been responsible for the sightings in the area of the sighting. The most logical route was looked at but according to the Military Training Route Fighter Information Publication it is only used during daylight hours.
