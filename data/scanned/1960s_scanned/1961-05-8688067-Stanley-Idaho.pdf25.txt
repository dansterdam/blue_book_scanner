REPORT ON UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON 
WITNESSED AT STANLEY, IDAHO 
ON MAY 8, 1961

This object was observed by several individuals on the evening of 8 May 1961 near the town of Stanley, Idaho. The object was described as appearing to be a bright, circular light, approximately 50 feet in diameter, and hovering at an altitude of approximately 1000 feet. After several minutes of observation, the object suddenly accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight.

No photographic evidence of the object was obtained. This incident is recorded in the Project Blue Book files as case number 8688067.
