HEADQUARTERS
FOREIGN TECHNOLOGY DIVISION
AIR FORCE SYSTEMS COMMAND
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO

9 APR 1962

TO: TD-E (Colonel Wynn)

1. On 6 April 1962 Lt. Colonel Friend, TD-E, visited DDI to participate in a briefing on the UFO program for Mr. Edward R. Trapnell, Assistant for Public Relations to the Secretary of the Air Force. Present were Dr. J.Allen Hynek, consultant to the Air Force on the UFO problem.

2. After his interest in the matter was whetted by a statement by Dr. Robert Calkins, Assistant Chief of Scientific Intelligence, stating that the recent publicity associated with unidentified flying objects, with a suggestion that some private sources might deliver findings of the program.

3. During the briefing Mr. Trapnell was informed of the origin of the program, when it was founded, the conclusions of the Air Force after 15 years of study, current problems, and recommendations concerning the UFO program.

4. The UFO program will be transferred to some agency such as NASA, Army, and Smithsonian and pursued under as entirely different title such as Atmospheric Physics.
