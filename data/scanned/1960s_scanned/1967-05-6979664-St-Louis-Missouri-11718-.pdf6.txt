PROJECT BLUE BOOK
Case No. 1767B
Date: 2 May 1967
Location: St. Louis, Missouri

SUMMARY OF INVESTIGATION:
On 2 May 1967, a report was received of an unidentified aerial object sighting over St. Louis, Missouri. The witness, a private citizen, reported observing a silver, disc-shaped object hovering in the sky for several minutes before it accelerated and rapidly departed the area.

DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT:
The object was described as approximately 20-30 feet in diameter, silver in color, and disc-shaped in appearance. It was observed to hover silently for several minutes before accelerating and disappearing from sight at a high rate of speed.

CONCLUSION:
After a thorough investigation, no definitive identification could be made regarding the nature of the object. The witness was deemed to be credible, and the sighting remains classified as an unidentified aerial phenomenon.
