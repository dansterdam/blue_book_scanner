More objects over
Southampton

On March 17 the Southern Evening 
Standard reported that objects, described
as discs with bright lights, had been seen
over Southampton in the past few weeks. 
According to reports, Rollright Caravans,
a Southampton firm, stated that about 20
objects had been seen every night in the 
area, making rapid and abrupt maneuvers. 
The objects were reported to be visible 
from 6.30 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.
The Bournemouth and Southampton
groups of the British Flying Saucer Bureau 
are investigating these reports.
