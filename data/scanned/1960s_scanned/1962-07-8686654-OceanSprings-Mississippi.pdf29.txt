OCEAN SPRINGS, MISSISSIPPI
8 JULY 1962
SIGHTING REPORT

Subject reported a bright silvery object, approximately 40-45 feet in diameter, hovering in the sky at an altitude of 1500-2000 feet. The object reportedly had a "shimmering" effect and was observed for approximately 10 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view. The observer was a local resident and the sighting occurred at approximately 6:45 PM.
