MEMO FOR THE RECORD
22 June 1968

Subj: UFO Observation, 22 June 1968

Dr. Academic Director, AFIT, Ext 54808 observed an UFO on the night of 22 June 1968 at approximately 0200 hrs while driving to Cincinnati. 
Just before the Turnoff at Middletown he sighted an object between two and three foot high. The object was lighted and appeared to be 6 - 8 ft thick, due shaped, 60 - 75 ft long, well lighted, not brilliant. Seemed to be rotating clockwise at a fast pace. The pattern was repetitious, as he watched the object for twenty minutes. Object was heading west. Another thing it seemed to be rotating counter clockwise. He quit watching the object while it was over Franklin. Felt the approximate speed was 60 - 80 mph.
