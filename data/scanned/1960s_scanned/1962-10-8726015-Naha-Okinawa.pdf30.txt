NAHA, OKINAWA
8 October 1962
1. At approximately 1315 hours on 8 October 1962, an unusual aerial phenomenon was observed in the vicinity of Naha, Okinawa. The object was first sighted by personnel of the 29th Tactical Fighter Squadron, Kadena Air Base, Okinawa.
2. The object was described as an elliptically-shaped body, approximately 20-25 feet in diameter. It was observed to emit a glowing, reddish-orange exhaust trail approximately 15-20 feet in length.
3. The object was observed to ascend vertically from the ground and then accelerate rapidly in a southerly direction, disappearing from view in approximately 5-10 seconds.
4. No sound was associated with the object. Weather conditions at the time were clear with good visibility.
5. The observations were reported to the 6922nd Security Wing, which forwarded the information to the Commanding General, Fifth Air Force, and to the Director of Intelligence, PACAF.
