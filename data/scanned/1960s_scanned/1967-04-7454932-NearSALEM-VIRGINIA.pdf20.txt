Subject: Unidentified Flying Object Report

1. On 7 April 1967, this office received a report of a sighting of an unidentified flying object near Salem, Virginia. The report was forwarded to Project Blue Book for analysis.

2. The object was described as a dome-shaped, disc-like craft approximately 40-50 feet in diameter. It was observed moving slowly at an altitude of approximately 1000-1500 feet. 3. Interview of the witness indicates that the object was not an aircraft, satellite, or any known natural phenomenon. Project Blue Book has been unable to identify the object.

4. Based on the information available, this office is unable to provide a conclusive explanation for the sighting. The report has been forwarded to Project Blue Book for further analysis and inclusion in their files.
