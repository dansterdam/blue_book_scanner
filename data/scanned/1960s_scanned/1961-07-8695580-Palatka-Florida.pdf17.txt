DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY

3 August 1961

Dear Sir:

Thank you for your information concerning the unidentified flying object you sighted.

The Aerospace Technical Intelligence Center at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base conducts investigations of such sightings in an effort to determine their nature.

As your experience, it you will complete the attached questionnaire and return it to this office, we will be glad to investigate your sighting.

Sincerely,

BLAINE WEBB
Counselor Branch
Public Information Division
Office of Information
