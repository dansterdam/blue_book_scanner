DIXON, ILLINOIS
9 DECEMBER 1963 

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

1. This office received a report of an unidentified flying object observed over Dixon, Illinois on 9 December 1963. The following is a summary of the information obtained:

2. At approximately 2100 hours on 9 December 1963, Mr. Robert Dixon, age 42, was driving his automobile on Route 26 near Dixon, Illinois when he sighted an unidentified flying object in the sky. Mr. Dixon pulled his car to the side of the road and observed the object for approximately 15 minutes.

4. After approximately 15 minutes, the object began to move in a southwesterly direction at a high rate of speed and disappeared from sight. No other witnesses to this incident have been located.
