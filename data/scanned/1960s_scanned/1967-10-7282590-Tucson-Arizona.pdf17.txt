SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 

1. ON 7 OCTOBER 1967, AT APPROXIMATELY 2200 HOURS, A WITNESS OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE VICINITY OF TUCSON, ARIZONA. THE WITNESS WAS DRIVING ALONE ON ROUTE I-10, APPROXIMATELY 10 MILES WEST OF TUCSON, WHEN HE NOTICED A BRIGHT LIGHT IN THE SKY TO THE SOUTH. HE STOPPED HIS VEHICLE AND OBSERVED THE LIGHT FOR SEVERAL MINUTES. THE LIGHT APPEARED TO BE HOVERING AND STATIONARY. IT WAS LARGER AND BRIGHTER THAN A STAR. THE WITNESS REPORTED THAT HE COULD HEAR NO SOUND FROM THE OBJECT.

2. AFTER OBSERVING THE LIGHT FOR SEVERAL MINUTES, IT SUDDENLY ACCELERATED HORIZONTALLY, MOVING AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED, AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW. THE WITNESS THEN CONTINUED ON HIS WAY.

3. THIS REPORT HAS BEEN FORWARDED TO PROJECT BLUE BOOK FOR FURTHER EVALUATION.
