Officers Report Seeing Unidentified Flying Object

The officers from the different departments stated that they watched an unidentified flying object for about 15 to 20 minutes this morning. The object was described as bright and brilliant. The officers stated that the object appeared to be hovering over Highway 25 and was following a straight line route. Both the Highway Patrol and Police Department were notified of the sighting, and the object was tracked by both departments. 

According to the county sheriff's radio log, officers of the Police County Sheriff's department and U.S. Defense Command investigated the incident. The officers stated that the object appeared to be hovering over Kimberly Park in that area. According to local authorities, attempts to define the object's presence on radar were unsuccessful.
