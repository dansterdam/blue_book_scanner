M/Sgt. Received a telephone call on 18 May 64. Witness called Lockbourne AFB. Witness did not give name. Refused to identify himself. Flight reported as straight at all times. Obj believed to be 3,000 - haltitude. There were no green lights. Flight to ESE. Canton Akron area. All the time 6-7 minute duration. Slower than blimp. Flight to ESE.
Additional reports of lights over house that looked like two headlights with other lights close behind that looked like windows. Obj cruising for 15-20 minutes. 100-150 people playing BINGO saw obj. Obj maneuvered.
Witness noted that object did NOT have a green light as "expected" as a possible aircraft.
