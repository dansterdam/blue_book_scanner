REPORT
Davidsonville, Maryland 
February 9, 1967

1. On 9 February 1967, this office received a report from Mr. Edwin C. Piratzky, of Davidsonville, Maryland, concerning an unidentified flying object (UFO) which he had observed.

2. Mr. Piratzky stated that at approximately 2100 hours on 8 February 1967, he was walking his dog near his home in Davidsonville. He observed a bright light in the sky which appeared to be moving in an erratic manner. The object had a reddish-orange color and seemed to dart back and forth across the sky. 

3. After observing the object for several minutes, Mr. Piratzky returned to his home and retrieved a camera. He was able to obtain several photographs of the UFO before it disappeared from view.

4. The photographs have been forwarded to the Air Force for analysis. The results of this analysis will be forwarded to this office upon completion.

5. This case is being filed as Unidentified Pending Further Investigation.
