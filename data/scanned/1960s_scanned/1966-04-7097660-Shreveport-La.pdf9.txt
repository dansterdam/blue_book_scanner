MEMORANDUM FOR: Chief, Technical Information Division
SUBJECT: UFO Sighting - Shreveport, Louisiana, 7 April 1966

1. The following information has been obtained concerning a UFO sighting which occurred on 7 April 1966 in the Shreveport, Louisiana area.

2. At approximately 1815 hours on 7 April 1966, Mrs. Joan Wingate, 3445 Grover Place, Shreveport, Louisiana, reported sighting an unidentified flying object in the western sky. Mrs. Wingate stated that the object appeared to be round, silver in color, and was approximately 40-50 feet in diameter. The object was observed for approximately 15 minutes, during which time it moved slowly across the sky in a westerly direction, maintaining a constant altitude of approximately 1,000-2,000 feet. Mrs. Wingate stated that the object was visible for a period of approximately 15 minutes before it disappeared from view.

3. This sighting was reported to the Barksdale Air Force Base Control Tower, who in turn notified the Shreveport Police Department. In response, two Shreveport police officers were dispatched to the reported location. However, upon their arrival, the object had already disappeared from view and the officers were unable to sight the object.

4. Please advise if any further information is required concerning this incident.
