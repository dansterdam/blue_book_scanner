NON-SCHEDULED NEWSLETTER #22
On the night of January 25th, 1965, several persons watched a twinkling object, shaped like a plate and looking like a pair of plates, altitude over the Rappahannock River, at Fredericksburg, Va. The object was observed for a period of ten minutes.
