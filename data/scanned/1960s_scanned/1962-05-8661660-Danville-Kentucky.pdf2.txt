DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object
TO: FTD (TDBTL)

As 1235 hours, 23 May 1962, S/A WILLIAM T. TILLER, Civilian, Danville, Kentucky, telephone 792-7111, advised that at 0200 hours, 23 May 1962, he was driving along U.S. Highway 150, approximately 4.5 miles east of Danville, Kentucky, and observed a bright object hovering the ground. He stated the object suddenly shot straight up and disappeared in a valley. He could not determine the exact size or shape of the object, but estimated it was approximately 20 feet in diameter.

Tiller further stated he was excited over the incident and showed no interest when he attempted to question him further.

R. G. STUTSMAN
Lt Colonel, USAF
Commander
