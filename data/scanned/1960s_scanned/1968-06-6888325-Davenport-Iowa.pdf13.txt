THE SIGHTING

On June 6, 1968, the Davenport, Iowa police received a report of a UFO siting from a citizen. The witness, a resident of Davenport, stated that at approximately 9:50 P.M. he observed a large, oval-shaped object with a bright light moving across the sky from the north to the south. The witness further stated that the object appeared to be hovering momentarily before disappearing from sight. A photograph of the object was obtained by the witness.
