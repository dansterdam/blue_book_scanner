DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON
OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY
12 July 1960

Dear Mr. Holliday:

This is to acknowledge and thank you for your letter of 5 July together with clippings from the Fayette Tribune concerning unidentified flying objects seen near Oak Hill, West Virginia on 1 July 1960.

I have forwarded this information on to the Aerospace Technical Intelligence Center for their possible use.

Thank you very much for your interest in this matter.

Sincerely,

LAWRENCE J. TACKER
Colonel, USAF
Public Information Division
Office of the Secretary

Mr. Robert K. Holliday
Publisher, Tribune Press
Oak Hill, West Virginia
