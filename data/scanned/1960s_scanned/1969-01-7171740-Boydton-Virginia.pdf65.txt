U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service

DAILY WEATHER MAPS

WEEKLY SERIES JAN. 6-12, 1969

The charts in this publication are continuations of the original charts of the Weather Bureau publication. Weather of the United States. The surface weather map, the 500 millibar map, the highest and lowest temperatures observed, and the 1200-500 millibar thickness charts are included. All of the charts are the ones regularly published on 5 sidles per day of the U.S. daily weather map. They are copied from operational weather maps prepared by the National Meteorological Center, Weather Bureau.

The 500 millibar Chart is the same as previously issued in Daily Weather Map.

Supplementary sheets listing station and hourly data, may be obtained without charge by writing to: Environmental Science Services Administration, Public Information Office, Silver Spring, Maryland 20910. All orders, requests, and inquiries should be addressed as a cost of $2.50 per 50 copies published.
