U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service
DAILY WEATHER MAPS
WEEKLY SERIES MAR. 10-16, 1969
The charts in this publication are a continuation of the principal charts of the Weather Service Bulletin Daily Weather Map. They include the Surface Weather Map, the 500-Millibar Chart, the Weather Service Upper Air Chart, and the Daily Precipitation Amounts chart, all arranged on a single page of this publication. They are prepared from official weather maps prepared by the National Meteorological Center, Weather Bureau.
The symbols used on the Surface Weather Map and the 500-Millibar Chart are the same as those used previously in Daily Weather Maps. An explanatory sheet is available, and copies can be obtained without charge by writing to: Environmental Science Services Administration, PUBS, A45 Rockville, Maryland 20852. Bulk orders may also be ordered, at a cost of $2.30 per 50 copies. Checks should be payable to the Superintendent of Documents.
