UFO Observation, 3 April 1963

Winona Police Department
Winona, Minnesota 55987

1. The Aerial Phenomena Office is in receipt of an unidentified flying object (UFO) report from four miles south of Cochrane, Wisconsin. The observation occurred on 3 April 1963 at 5:15 pm CDT.

2. The observer stated that she saw a large orange boomerang-shaped object that seemed to be surrounded by smoke or fog. The object hovered over her car and the car's engine and radio went dead. The object was uncertain of its color, lights and radio went dead. The object was in sight but it appears to have been visible for only a long time.

3. Did you receive any reports of unusual objects for this date? If so, we would appreciate your comments as to a possible cause for this sighting.

4. Thank you for your assistance on this matter.
