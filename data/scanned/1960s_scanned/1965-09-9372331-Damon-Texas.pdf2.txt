THE REPORTED SIGHTING
1. (a) Time: 1905 to 1915 
(b) Date: 9 September 1965
(c) Location: Damon, Texas

2. DETAILS OF OBSERVATION:
At approximately 1905 hours on 9 September 1965, the reporting witness, who was driving on the Damon-Danbury road southeast of Damon, Texas, observed a bright light in the sky. The light was stationary and approximately 20° above the horizon in the southwest. The witness observed the light for about 10 minutes.
At approximately 1915 hours, the light began moving slowly to the northwest, maintaining its altitude of about 20° above the horizon. The light then accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view.
The witness described the light as brilliant white in color, larger than a star, and very bright. No other lights or objects were observed in the vicinity. The sky was clear and the visibility was excellent. No sounds were heard.

3. INVESTIGATOR'S COMMENTS:
The investigator was unable to identify the object observed. The location and description of the object are consistent with the observation of an unidentified aerial phenomenon.
