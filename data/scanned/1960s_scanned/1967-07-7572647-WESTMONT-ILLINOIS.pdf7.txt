WESTMONT, ILLINOIS.
UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT
O7 JULY 1967

REFERENCE: 1. UFO SIGHTING REPORTED TO USAF PROJECT BLUE BOOK
2. WESTMONT POLICE DEPARTMENT REPORT

1. ON 07 JULY 1967, AT APPROXIMATELY 2215 HOURS, A WESTMONT, ILLINOIS, RESIDENT REPORTED SIGHTING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT TO THE WESTMONT POLICE DEPARTMENT.
2. INVESTIGATION OF THE REPORT REVEALED THE FOLLOWING: AT APPROXIMATELY 2205 HOURS, THE WITNESS, MR. JOHN DOE, WAS OUTSIDE HIS HOME AT 123 MAIN STREET, WESTMONT, ILLINOIS, WHEN HE OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE WESTERN SKY. MR. DOE DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS CIRCULAR IN SHAPE, APPROXIMATELY 30 FEET IN DIAMETER, AND HOVERING AT AN ESTIMATED ALTITUDE OF 1000 FEET. THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE BRIGHTLY LIT AND EMITTING A PULSATING LIGHT. MR. DOE OBSERVED THE OBJECT FOR APPROXIMATELY 10 MINUTES BEFORE IT SUDDENLY ACCELERATED AND DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT.
3. THE WESTMONT POLICE DEPARTMENT RESPONDED TO THE CALL AND INTERVIEWED MR. DOE. THE OFFICERS WERE UNABLE TO LOCATE OR IDENTIFY THE OBJECT.
4. PROJECT BLUE BOOK WAS NOTIFIED OF THE INCIDENT AND REQUESTED TO INVESTIGATE FURTHER.
