Mrs Frank McGann called. Her mother wrote from Wisconsin, stating that on Feb 5th at 3 am in Janesville, Wisconsin, she saw an object. It was beautiful and surprising, described as a blue ball, it went across the sky, had gold rays, was long and oval shape, it was low in elevation as though it was going to land. Its flight was East to West, she was looking South. She called WCTN radio and TV. The speed was about ECHO'S. She has also observed meteorites. Her mother's address is Mrs Floyd C Perk, 416 Locust St, Janesville, Wisconsin.
