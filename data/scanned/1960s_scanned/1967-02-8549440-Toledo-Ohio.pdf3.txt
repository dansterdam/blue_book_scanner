MEMO FOR THE RECORD: 1 MARCH 67
Received call from Mrs. S-- G--, who said she saw a bright blue-green object, this Mrs. G-- stated. The object was seen for several minutes. The witness stopped her car and got out and looked. There was a humming sound noted during the sighting. The object was over the witness' car and was descending. The witness said that she would complete any forms sent to her.
