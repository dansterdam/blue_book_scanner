REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
1. AT 2120 ON 7 AUG 68, THIS HEADQUARTERS RECEIVED A CALL FROM THE YELLOW SPRINGS POLICE DEPARTMENT ADVISING OF A REPORTED UFO SIGHTING IN THE YELLOW SPRINGS AREA. ARRANGEMENTS WERE MADE FOR AN INVESTIGATOR FROM THIS HEADQUARTERS TO CONTACT THE WITNESSES.
2. MRS. MARTHA SHEPPARD, 704 LIVERMORE ST. , YELLOW SPRINGS, OHIO, REPORTED OBSERVING A BRIGHT, ROUND, ORANGE-COLORED LIGHT DIRECTLY OVERHEAD AT APPROXIMATELY 2025 ON 7 AUG 68. SHE STATED THE OBJECT HOVERED FOR ABOUT 5 MINUTES AND THEN MOVED RAPIDLY TO THE NORTHWEST.
3. MR. CARL THOMAS, 718 SUNRISE AVE., YELLOW SPRINGS, OHIO, CORROBORATED MRS. SHEPPARD'S REPORT. HE ALSO OBSERVED THE BRIGHT ORANGE-COLORED LIGHT DIRECTLY OVERHEAD AND STATED IT WAS ABOUT THE SIZE OF A QUARTER HELD AT ARM'S LENGTH. MR. THOMAS SAID THE OBJECT HOVERED FOR ABOUT 5 MINUTES AND THEN MOVED RAPIDLY TO THE NORTHWEST.
4. CHIEF NORMAN E. DAVIS, YELLOW SPRINGS POLICE DEPARTMENT, WAS ALSO CONTACTED. HE STATED THAT HIS DEPARTMENT HAD RECEIVED SEVERAL CALLS REPORTING THE SIGHTING OF THE UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT. CHIEF DAVIS SAID THAT HIS OFFICERS HAD BEEN UNABLE TO LOCATE OR IDENTIFY THE OBJECT.
