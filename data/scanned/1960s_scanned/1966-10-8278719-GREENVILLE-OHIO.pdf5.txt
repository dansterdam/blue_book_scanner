28 Oct. 66

Observer called back stating that he got up the next morning and saw Sirius as Dr. Hynek had asked him to. The observer stated that this star looked like the object he had seen on 7 Oct. Dr. Hynek had explained how the star had been misidentified as a UFO. The observer said he was a witness on the preceding morning and asked the witness to get up the next morning and compare his sighting with Sirius was rising in the morning at about 0:00.
