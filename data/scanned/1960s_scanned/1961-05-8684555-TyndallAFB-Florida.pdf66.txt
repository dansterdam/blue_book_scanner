TYNDALL AIR FORCE BASE

8 MAY 1961

REPORT OF INVESTIGATION

1. This report is submitted in accordance with the provisions of AFR 200-2.

2. On 8 May 1961, at approximately 2000 hours, an unidentified flying object was sighted over the Tyndall Air Force Base, Florida area.

3. The object was observed by Colonel XXXX XXXXX, Commanding Officer, 4756th Air Defense Wing, Tyndall AFB, Florida, and also by several other personnel on the base. The object was described as being round in shape, about the size of a large automobile, and emitting a bright reddish-orange glow. It was observed to be moving at a high rate of speed, estimated to be in excess of 1,000 miles per hour, and at an altitude of approximately 5,000 to 10,000 feet.

4. The object was observed for a period of approximately 3 to 5 minutes before disappearing from sight. No peculiar sounds were heard, and no unusual effects were noted on nearby aircraft or electronic equipment.

5. An immediate search of the area was conducted by security and intelligence personnel, but no trace of the object could be found.

6. In the opinion of the investigating officers, this incident appears to be a valid sighting of an unidentified flying object. The object was observed by highly reliable and trained personnel, and the characteristics of the object are not consistent with any known aircraft or natural phenomena.

7. This report is being forwarded to Project Blue Book for further analysis and evaluation.
