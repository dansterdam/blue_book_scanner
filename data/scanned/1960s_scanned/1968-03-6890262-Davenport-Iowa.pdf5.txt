AFR SD-17(C)

TO INDICATE THE DIRECTION TO THE PHENOMENON WHEN LAST SEEN. PLACE AN "X" ON THE COMPASS TO INDICATE THE DIRECTION TO THE PHENOMENON WHEN FIRST SEEN, AND A "O" TO INDICATE THE DIRECTION TO THE PHENOMENON WHEN LAST SEEN.

THE SKETCH BELOW. PLACE AN "X" AT THE POSITION OF THE PHENOMENON WHEN FIRST SEEN, AND A "O" TO INDICATE THE POSITION OF THE PHENOMENON WHEN LAST SEEN, CONNECT THE "X" AND "O" WITH A LINE TO APPROXIMATELY INDICATE THE COURSE OF THE PHENOMENON. ALSO SKETCH ANY OTHER DETAILS, SUCH AS THE SHAPE OF THE PHENOMENON, WHICH MAY BE PERTINENT. USE THE LARGER SKETCH.
