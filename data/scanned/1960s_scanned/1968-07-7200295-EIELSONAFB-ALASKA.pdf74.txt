DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
PROJECT BLUE BOOK
SPECIAL REPORT NO. 14

1. On 7 July 1968, at approximately 0115 hours, ZD, an unidentified aerial object was sighted at Eielson AFB, Alaska.
2. The witness, an Airman 1st Class, stated that he observed a large, disc-shaped object with a bright orange glow. The object hovered for approximately 60 seconds at an altitude of approximately 100 feet, then rapidly accelerated and climbed straight up into the cloud cover.
3. The object was observed visually and through 7 x 50 binoculars. No further information is available.
