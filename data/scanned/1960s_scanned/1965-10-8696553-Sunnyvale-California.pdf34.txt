PROJECT SUMMARY REPORT
EXG REPORT: 26/8/65

NAME-ID: Many unidentified triangular objects
LOCATION: Sunnyvale, California

PRECIS: On the night of 26 August 1965, a number of triangular shaped objects were observed by several witnesses over the Sunnyvale, California area. The objects were observed between 2130 and 2300 hours. The objects were reported to be approximately 100-150 feet in length, and were seen to hover and maneuver at a high rate of speed. The objects were observed by two separate groups of witnesses, and were reported to the USAF.

FOLLOW UP COMMENT:
BACKGROUND:
The witness, a military officer, stated that he and several other persons observed a triangular object over the Sunnyvale area on the night of 26 August 1965. The object was estimated to be approximately 100-150 feet in length, and was observed to hover and then accelerate at a high rate of speed to the northeast. The witness stated that the object was brightly illuminated, and that it displayed red, green, and white lights. The object was observed for approximately 30 minutes.

CONCLUSION: Further investigation is warranted to determine the nature and origin of the triangular objects observed over Sunnyvale, California on the night of 26 August 1965. This case remains UNRESOLVED.
