REPORT ON UNCORRELATED TARGET OBSERVED 6 NOVEMBER 1967 OVER TREYSA, GERMANY

The aerial phenomena observed over Treysa, Germany on 6 November 1967 was reported to the United States Air Force. The sighting occurred at approximately 1810 hours local time.

The observer, an unnamed German civilian, observed a group of 5-6 luminous, disc-shaped objects moving across the sky in a north to south direction. The objects were observed for a period of approximately 10 minutes.

The objects were described as being about 6-8 meters in diameter, glowing white in color, and moving at an estimated speed of 600-800 kilometers per hour. No sound was associated with the objects.

Weather conditions at the time of the sighting were reported as clear, with a few scattered clouds. No other aircraft were observed in the vicinity.
