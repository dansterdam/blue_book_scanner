MEMO FOR THE RECORD
26 Feb 63
Subj: Observation of Meteor, 25 Feb 63

On 26 Feb 63, Chanute AFB, Illinois called to inform us of a meteor observation by a TWX Pilot, near Joliet, Illinois on 25 February 1963, also house, local time. They were quite sure it was a meteor observation, however, if additional information proved them wrong they would forward this report to Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio.
