SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object
DATE OF OCCURRENCE: August 7, 1968
PLACE OF OCCURRENCE: Ottsville, Pennsylvania

DETAILS:
At approximately 10:15 PM on the evening of August 7, 1968, the witness, a Mr. John Doe, observed an unidentified flying object in the sky over Ottsville, Pennsylvania. Mr. Doe reported that the object was round in shape, approximately 50 feet in diameter, and was hovering silently at an altitude of approximately 500 feet. The object was illuminated from within and emitted a bright, pulsating light. After several minutes, the object accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view.

The case was investigated by the United States Air Force as part of Project Blue Book. No further details or conclusions were provided in the report.
