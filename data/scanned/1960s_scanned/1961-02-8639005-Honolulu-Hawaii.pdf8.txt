No Case (Information Only)
22 February 1961
Cockrell, Hemphill &
Hilliton Field, Texas

MYSTERIOUS OBJECT STUNS LAW OFFICIALS - Four apparent to be about 200 feet off the ground near Houston, Texas. The object was about 50 feet in diameter and about 20 men observed it near Conroe. The object was described as brightly-lighted, emitted a pulsing white light, and traveled across the sky in a horizontal fashion. It was approached by a Highway Patrol Unit, but the object quickly accelerated at a high rate of speed and disappeared. The officials also said that their radar would not pick up objects of low intensity. They said that the object was not making sound and that they could not identify it.
