SUBJECT: Case Summary U.F.O. Incident, Spencer, Indiana
1964 June 8 - 25 June 1964

The 4 page report by James F. Alvarez, east mansfield, Indiana, was travelling in his car when he observed an object in the sky. He described the object as being about 100 feet in diameter, oblong in shape, with a row of lights along the outer edge. The object was approximately 100 feet off the ground and moved silently. After about 5 minutes the object moved away rapidly and disappeared.
