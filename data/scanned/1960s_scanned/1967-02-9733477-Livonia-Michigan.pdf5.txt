UFOP Reporting Data (continued)

c. MANNER OF OBSERVATION:
(1) Use one or any combination of the following items: Ground-radar, visual, air-ground visual, air-electronic. (If electronic, specify type of radar.)

(2) Statement as to optical aids (telescopes, binoculars, etc.)

(3) If the sighting occurred while air-borne, give type of aircraft, identification number, altitude, speed, and home station.

d. TIME AND DATE OF SIGHTING:
(1) Exact time-date group or sighting.
(2) Light conditions. (Use one of the following terms: night, dawn, day, dusk, or dark.)

e. LOCATION OF OBSERVER(S): Give exact latitude and longitude or describe location and/or geographical position.

f. IDENTIFYING INFORMATION ON OBSERVER(S):
(1) Civilian - Name, age, mailing address, occupation, and estimate of reliability.
(2) Military - Name, grade, organization, duty and estimate of reliability.
