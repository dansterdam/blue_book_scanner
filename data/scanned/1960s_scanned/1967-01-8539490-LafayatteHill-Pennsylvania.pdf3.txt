22 February 1967

Branchville Hill, Pa. 19044

Dear Sir:

Receipt of your 13 February letter relative to your having a sighting of unidentified flying object on 3 January and other matter's having sighted a strong object on 3 February 12-7, is acknowledged.

The United States Air Force has established a central data collection as a result of these sightings. Many of the public are contacting the Air Force in increasing numbers. Your report has been forwarded to the proper agency for study. Your interest and cooperation are appreciated.

The interest you have evidenced by providing this information is encouraging. It is probable that the activities of the International Air Force Base will continue you in the near future. Additional efforts for the future.

Sincerely,

M. R. GARNER
Contract Director
