MEMO for the Records  20 Jan 67

Contact was made with Vandenburg APB, in regards to any missile activity that could have accounted for an alleged UFO observation. There was no missile activity from either the Vandenburg facility on or about 1315 hour local 13 Jan 67. 

Contact was made with Bob McClean at White Sands Missile Range. He advised in regards to any missile activity that White Sands fires on the average of one rocket a day but these rockets only rise to about 50 miles and then eject a parachute. There are no artificial cloud releases from white Sands that fire, regular knew about. Also the Navy launches AERO-test rockets from White Sands.
