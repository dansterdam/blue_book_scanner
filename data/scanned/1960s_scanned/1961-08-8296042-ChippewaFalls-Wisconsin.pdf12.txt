Oct. 23

Dear Sir:

This letter you will find is an answer to your general Miller, Ballston and Argyraeus systems. I regret that I missed your call. I received an answer from which a digest of the department follows.

I have read with considerable interest your letter of September 6 which you presented many strange things that occur in the sky in recent years. The Aerial Mail Company has received many reports, like yours, of peculiar objects in the sky, it seems likely that the observations are authentic and possibly merits investigation.
