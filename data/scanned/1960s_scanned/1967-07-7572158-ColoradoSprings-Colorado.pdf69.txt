MEMO FOR THE RECORD 19 July 67

Contact with SPADATS revealed that object number 2379 appeared somewhere over the Atlantic Ocean at approximately 0010Z. This was an unconfirmed visual report from NORAD's Saginaw, MI, over Europe and was responsible for many reports. SPADATS is contemplating its final decay message.
