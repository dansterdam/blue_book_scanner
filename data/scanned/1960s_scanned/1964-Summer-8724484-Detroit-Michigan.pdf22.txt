Information Circular 
Subject: American UFO Committee Review, Vol 4, No 3, Sep 64 
    
Mauldin, WYOMING: A county sheriff and several citizens reported seeing a strange object in the sky over Mauldin, Wyo., on 11 Dec 63. The object was first observed at about 8:15 p.m. and was watched for about 20 minutes. It was described as being triangular or diamond-shaped, glowing red, and moving at a constant speed and altitude. No sound was heard. The object disappeared into the distance. NICAP investigated this report.
UFO 12-28: This case was later reported by NICAP in the UFO Investigator, January 1964 issue.
