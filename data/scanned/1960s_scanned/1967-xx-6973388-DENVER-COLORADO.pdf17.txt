Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, OHIO
Dear Major Quintanilla:
On the night of October 31st there were no unidentified lights or other aerial phenomena reported to this office. This being a large hub center for the airlines and for general aviation, we would have been advised of any such sightings. To our knowledge, there were no balloons anywhere in the Denver area.
Gordon L. Poulson
Assistant Director of Aviation
