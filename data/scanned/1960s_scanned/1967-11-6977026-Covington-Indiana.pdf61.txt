SIGHTING REPORT

Covington, Indiana, 6 Nov 1967

F-4D Interceptor aircraft, pilot & RIO, Procedure no 56-1, observed a bright luminous object at 1654 EST that appeared to be on a collision course. Pilot made sharp turn but object continued to close. Pilot fired two bursts of 20mm cannon fire but object continued to close. At 300 yards range, object went into rapid climb and disappeared.
