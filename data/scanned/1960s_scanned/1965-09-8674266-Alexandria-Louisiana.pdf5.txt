J. THERE WERE WEATHER BALLOONS LAUNCHED FROM JACKSON, MISS., MOBILE, ALA, AND NEW ORLEANS, LA. THE DIRECTION IN WHICH THE WIND WAS BLOWING MAY HAVE CARRIED THE BALLONS FROM JACKSON, MISS., OR MOBILE, ALA INTO THE AREA OF THE SIGHTING. THESE BALLONS WERE LAUNCHED AT 1822 7 SEP 65.

K. RUSSELL J MANERING JR, 1ST LT, USAF, INTELLIGENCE OFFICER, 3RD TACTICAL FIGHTER WING, BOTH REPORTED THAT THEY SEEMED COMPETENT AND SINCERE ABOUT WHAT THEY SIGHTED. THE WEATHER WAS CLEAR WHICH HELPED TO MAKE GOOD IDENTIFICATION. BOTH OBSERVERS STATED THAT SEVERAL AIRCRAFT WERE SPOTTED ON THE SAME EVENING AT LOWER ALTITUDES. THE OBJECTS MAY HAVE BEEN A CRAFT OR HIGHLY REFLECTIVE SATELLITE.

NOTE: ADVANCE COPY DELIVERED TO DIA.
