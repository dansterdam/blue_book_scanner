PHOTO ANALYSIS REPORT
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: SEATTLE, WASHINGTON
DATE: JANUARY 1966
PURPOSE: This report is in response to Work Order Number 66-23 from Major K. Quintanilla, 4602nd AISS. This analysis was made pursuant to an investigation of an unidentified flying object.
ANALYSIS RESULTS: This analysis was completed jointly by the Photo Analysis and Photo Processing Divisions. The images on the negatives appear to be an object on the water or between the water and the sky and does not show any internal aircraft structure. A hazy deposit on the lens or an internal camera reflection cannot be ruled out as the cause. No evidence of any physical damage or alteration to the film exists.
PHOTO ANALYZED BY: GERALD A. SCHERMAN, Intelligence Research Specialist
