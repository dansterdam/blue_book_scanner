No Case, Information Only
Source: BLUE BULLETIN, Vol 2, # 1

4 November 1964
Bath Township, Ohio

On that same morning, in Bath Township, Ohio, a woman on the Bath Road, where she noticed a shiny object in the western sky which she felt was unusual. The object was at about 10 degrees elevation and moved slowly in a southerly direction. It was visible for approximately 40 seconds. The witness is a reliable person.
