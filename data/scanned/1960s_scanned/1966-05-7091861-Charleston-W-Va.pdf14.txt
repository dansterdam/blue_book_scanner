We are in receipt of an unidentified flying object report which you observed on 5 May 1966.
The information which we received was not sufficient for evaluation.

The information which we have received states that the American D projects are making an approximate investigation of a sighting on 5 May 1966 near Lynch Radar Site and Charleston, West Virginia.

In this is our consideration, we would appreciate your completed the attached PTD Form 164 and returning it in the envelope provided.

I wish to thank you for any information which you are able to provide.

Sincerely,

VICTOR QUINTANILLA, Jr. Major, USAF
Chief, Project Blue Book
