THE FOLLOWING IS A REPORT OF A SIGHTING AS PER STAFF DUTY OFFICER'S REPORT:
ON 6 OCT 65 AT 1650 I GOT A CALL AT OST 55:
A REPORT OF UFO SIGHTING. AS REPORTED BY   REPORTED BY CAPTAIN XXXXXXX
AT XXXXX (military base) STATED THAT HIS OBSERVATION WAS MADE UNDER THE FOLLOWING CONDITION:
"WHILE PERFORMING ROUTINE DUTIES ON THE EAST SIDE OF THE BASE, CAPTAIN XXXXXX NOTICED AN UNUSUAL OBJECT IN THE SKY ABOUT 5 MILES EAST OF THE BASE PROPER. HE OBSERVED THIS OBJECT THROUGH 7X50 BINOCULARS FOR APPROXIMATELY 2 1/2 TO 3 MINUTES. CAPTAIN XXXXXXX DESCRIBED THE OBJECT AS CIRCULAR IN SHAPE, SILVER IN COLOR, AND APPEARED TO TURN OR ROTATE AS IT HOVERED IN THE SKY. HE REPORTED THAT THE OBJECT SEEMED TO BE APPROXIMATELY 60 FEET IN DIAMETER. AFTER HOVERING IN ONE POSITION FOR A FEW MINUTES, IT SUDDENLY ACCELERATED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW WITHIN A FEW SECONDS. CAPTAIN XXXXXXX HAD NO FURTHER INFORMATION TO ADD TO HIS REPORT."
