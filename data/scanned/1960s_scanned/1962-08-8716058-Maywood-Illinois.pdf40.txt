16 Aug 52
Dear sir:
I gave your inquiry to an investigator who is familiar with the sighting and he advises me that the information requested has not been accurately reported. The information which he has is as follows:
The sighting was made on 16 Aug 52. The object was reported to be of a cigar shape, silver in color and of great size. It was observed in a stationary position for several minutes by a number of people in Maywood, Illinois, during daylight hours. The object was observed to take off at a high rate of speed and disappear.
No further information is available at this time.
Sincerely yours,
