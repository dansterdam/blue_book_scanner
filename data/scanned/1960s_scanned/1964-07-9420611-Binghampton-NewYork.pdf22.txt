CASE BRIEF

TITLE: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT
INCIDENT DATE: 9 JULY 1964
LOCATION: BINGHAMPTON, NEW YORK
INVESTIGATORS: MAJOR HECTOR QUINTANILLA, USAF, DIRECTOR PROJECT BLUE BOOK

DETAILS OF INCIDENT:
On 9 JULY 1964, at approximately 0730 HOURS, several citizens reported sighting an unidentified flying object over the Binghampton, New York area. Two photographs were taken of the object by an unnamed witness. The photographs show an elongated, cylindrical object in the sky.

The object was observed for approximately 10-15 minutes before disappearing from view. No further details on the size, speed, or altitude of the object are available in this report.

CONCLUSION: 
Based on the available evidence, including the two photographs, this incident remains UNIDENTIFIED. No satisfactory explanation for the reported object has been determined.
