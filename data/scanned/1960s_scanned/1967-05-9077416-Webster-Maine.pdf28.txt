REPORT OF INVESTIGATION
CASE NO. 9077416
1. NAME OF INVESTIGATOR: WEBSTER, MAINE
2. LOCATION OF INCIDENT: WEBSTER, MAINE
3. DATE OF INCIDENT: 9 MAY 1967
4. TIME OF INCIDENT: APPROX. 8:00 PM
5. TYPE OF INCIDENT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
6. DESCRIPTION OF INCIDENT: AT APPROXIMATELY 8:00 PM ON 9 MAY 1967, MRS. JANE DOE REPORTED OBSERVING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER HER HOME IN WEBSTER, MAINE. SHE STATED THE OBJECT WAS HOVERING APPROXIMATELY 500 FEET ABOVE THE GROUND AND APPEARED TO BE CIRCULAR IN SHAPE AND METALLIC IN COLOR. THE OBJECT REMAINED STATIONARY FOR SEVERAL MINUTES BEFORE ACCELERATING RAPIDLY AND DISAPPEARING FROM VIEW.
7. INVESTIGATION AND FINDINGS: INTERVIEWS WERE CONDUCTED WITH MRS. JANE DOE AND SEVERAL NEIGHBORS WHO ALSO REPORTED OBSERVING THE UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT. THEIR DESCRIPTIONS WERE CONSISTENT. WEATHER CONDITIONS AT THE TIME WERE CLEAR. NO KNOWN AIRCRAFT WERE REPORTED IN THE AREA. FURTHER INVESTIGATION COULD NOT IDENTIFY THE NATURE OF THE OBSERVED OBJECT.
8. CONCLUSION: THE OBSERVED OBJECT REMAINS UNIDENTIFIED.
