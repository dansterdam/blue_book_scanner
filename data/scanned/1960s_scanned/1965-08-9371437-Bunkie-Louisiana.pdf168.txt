CASE OF POLICE OFFICERS BURTON ROCKIE, DARRY, ROY LAFOUNDAIRE 
29 AUGUST 1959

SUMMARY:
On 29 August 1959, at approximately 2:30 A.M., Officer Burton Rockie and two other officers of the Bunkie, Louisiana Police Department observed a bright light, described as a "searchlight-type beam" coming from the "Manchester Union Leader" newspaper office. The light was described as being approximately 75 feet in diameter and was observed for about 10 minutes. No further details are provided.
