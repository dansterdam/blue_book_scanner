25 NOV 1959
SUBJJCT: Unidentified Flying Object Sighting
1. On the evening of 28 October 1959 at approximately 7:30 PM, the witness reported observing an extremely bright source of light approximately 10 degrees above the horizon located North-Northeast of his position.
2. On the subsequent letters, the witness described the object as having a disc-like appearance, bright and glowing, and that the object was approximately 30-40 feet in diameter. The witness also stated that the object appeared to have emanatifions from the top and bottom of the object and that the object appeared to rotate or oscillate as it was observed.
3. Telephone contact with witness on 30 November 1959 did not add any additional information to the original report. The witness could not furnish any additional details regarding the sighting and reiterated his previous statements.
4. The witness, who had been in the service, stated that he had observed a similar object approximately 2 years prior while in the service. The witness further stated he was in a vehicle at that time and was traveling when he observed the object for a brief period of time.
5. It appears the witness, a retired Air Force officer, reported the sighting to the local authorities in an effort to ascertain any additional information regarding the object observed. To this date, the witness has received no additional information from the authorities concerning the sighting.
