CASE # 9733735 - OXFORD COUNTY, MAINE
REPORTED: 9 SEPT 1963

ON 9 SEPTEMBER 1963, MR. A. T. SMITH, PARIS, MAINE, REPORTED OBSERVING A BRIGHT, CIRCULAR OBJECT IN THE SKY TO THE SOUTHWEST OF HIS RESIDENCE AT APPROXIMATELY 5:25 P.M. THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 30 SECONDS AS IT APPEARED TO TRAVEL IN A STRAIGHT LINE AND THEN ACCELERATED OUT OF SIGHT. MR. SMITH STATED THAT THE OBJECT APPEARED TO BE A BRIGHT METALLIC COLOR AND ESTIMATED ITS SIZE TO BE APPROXIMATELY 10 - 15 FEET IN DIAMETER.

REPORTING OFFICER: COLONEL JOHN F. MCCLOSKEY, USAF
