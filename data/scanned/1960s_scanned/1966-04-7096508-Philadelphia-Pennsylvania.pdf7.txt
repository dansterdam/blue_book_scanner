Throughout the observed period, it was moving but seemed to stay in the same general area. The movement was not a straight line, but was more of a circular motion, at times increasing and decreasing in speed. It's height was estimated to be about 500 feet above the ground.

This object did not have the appearance of a conventional aircraft. It was silent and there was no navigation lights of any kind visible. The wing span appeared to be as great as that of a Piper Cub but the overall size of the object was larger. It was observed for approximately 4 to 5 minutes and then disappeared abruptly.
