EQUAL'S SIGHTING - 
Guyahoga Falls, Ohio 
6/28/62
They saw a brilliant blue object about 300 ft. in diameter fly very fast over their house. They said it was driven without making any noise. It is reported that this occurred Saturday night about 11:30 P.M. 
The people reported to be very upset, shaken and alarmed about the incident. They said it was hundreds and hundreds of feet above the ground and not making any noise. It was observed for a short while and then disappeared in the direction of the North. 
There was no further action taken by the police or military according to the report, and no further investigation was conducted. No pictures or other evidence was obtained from the observers.
