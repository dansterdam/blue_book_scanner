SUBJECT: (U) UFS (S), Observation of 15 September 1959

1. The following information received from OSI District Office 28, Chicago, Illinois, is published in accordance with AFIR 200-2, dated 14 September 1959.

a. On 25 September 1959, the air force letter was addressed to the Washington Daily News concerning an incident which occurred on 15 September 1959 near Gary, Indiana.

b. The District Office at Chicago, telephonically advised the Assistant Director of OSI at Washington to report this incident, prompted by a request received from Air Force Intelligence in UFO's, prompted them to recognize this as a significant case and requested their cooperation in the investigation concerning Mr. Force.

2. The following information received from OSI Report 15, APR 200, dated 15 September 1959.

a. Description of the Object(s):
1. A group of white spots or lights.
2. Appeared to be side of large, white, round objects, approximately 1/2 mile in distance, and the size of a common balloon or sphere.
3. Static in position - independent, rather than fluorescent, light.
4. Color - reddish-white.
5. No systematic formation or spacing except that it appeared approximate to a football.
6. Disappeared details together with sudden extinction of light.
7. No trail, trail, or smoke observed.
8. Lights appeared to be at 3/4 to 1 mile in distance from the observer.

b. Description of Course of Object(s):
1. Observer was driving automobile and observed the lights through the windshield of the automobile. He recalls the lights, but only that he was aware of their presence actually.
