UFO Observation, 31 July 1967

1. The Aerial Phenomena Branch is in receipt of an unidentified flying object (UFO) sighting for 31 July 1967, from Barstow, California. The observer first saw the object at 7:15 pm PDT, as an extremely bright light, initially static, and then observed it maneuver erratically, appearing to move in a southwesterly direction, with sudden stops and starts. The object disappeared at 11:30 pm PDT in the south.

2. Request information on any balloon launches from your base that may have been in progress during this period, or any other aerial objects in the area, which may account for the sighting.
