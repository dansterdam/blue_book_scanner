Memo for the Record
18 April 1966
Called the wing intelligence officer at Otis AFB Automaton 522-1290 ext 4276
Intelligence officer said that at the time sighting (07:20 / 31 Mar 66,
Rehoboth, Mass.) the weather was deteriorating rapidly and there was a haze in the
area. The observers were close to the Hillgrove R.I. Airport and in a direct line
to the approach of the airport. There was heavy aircraft traffic in the area at
that time. He believed that the cause of the sighting was the landing or navigation
lights on an aircraft approaching said airport.
Call made by A3C Elmer:
