No Case (Information Only)
16 July 1962
Loerie, South Africa

SOUTH AFRICAN DIE POSKAETYL (newspaper) of 16 July 1962 reports that at approximately 5 pm on 15 July 1962, a farmer was working on his farm near Loerie, South Africa and noticed a strange object in the sky. The object was circular in shape and appeared to be composed of a metallic material. It hovered silently over the farm for several minutes before swiftly accelerating and disappearing from sight. The farmer reported the incident to the authorities but no further investigation was conducted.
