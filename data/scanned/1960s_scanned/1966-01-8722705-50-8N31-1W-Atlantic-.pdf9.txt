PHOTO ANALYSIS REPORT NR 66-89
LOCATION: SEATTLE, WASHINGTON
DATE: January 1966
PHOTOGRAPHY: 2 print, 1 negative
PURPOSE: This report is in response to Work Order Number 66-26 from Major H. Quintanilla, SAFOTO, who asked for an analysis of a negative and prints depicting an unidentified flying object. 
ANALYSIS: This analysis was completed jointly by the Photo Analysis and Photo Processing Sections of the Central Document Section. The object in the image could have been caused by a reflective light from a small water droplets on the lens. A water droplet on the lens could produce a light reflection that might be mistaken for an unidentified flying object.
PHOTO ANALYSIS BY: GERALD L. SCHMALTZ, Intelligence Research Specialist
APPROVED BY: THOMAS D. BARNES, JR., Major, USAF, Chief, Photo Exploitation Division
WALTER P. FRIO, JR., Photo Exploitation Directorate
