SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

AMOUNK OP DAMAGE: NONE REPORTED

CIRCUMSTANCES:

THE WITNESS AUGUSTA, GA., REPORTS THAT HE OBSERVED A DISC-SHAPED OBJECT, HOVERING ABOVE HIGHWAY ROUTE #1 ON THE AIKEN, S.C. SIDE, IN THE VICINITY OF COUCHTON, S.C.

WITNESS REPORTS OBJECT WAS BRIGHT WHITE IN COLOR, BRILLIANT IN APPEARANCE, HOVERING ABOUT 7 TO 10 FEET ABOVE THE ROAD, AND ILLUMINATING THE ENTIRE AREA WITH A BRIGHT GLOW.

OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY WITNESS FOR ABOUT 3-4 MINUTES, THEN SUDDENLY AND SILENTLY ACCELERATED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.

STATE HIGHWAY PATROL IN AIKEN, S.C. ALSO RECEIVED A CALL REPORTING THE SIGHTING FROM ANOTHER WITNESS IN THAT AREA.

A DISTANCE OF APPROX 20 MILES SEPARATE WITNESS LOCATION AND HIGHWAY PATROL REPORT.
