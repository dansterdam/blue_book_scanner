TIPO (UFO) LE COL QUINTANILLA/79216/MHA/4 OCT 63 
UFO OBSERVATION, 13 SEPTEMBER 1963

OCALA CITY POLICE DEPARTMENT 
OCALA, FLORIDA 32670

1. The Aerial Phenomena Office is in receipt of an unidentified flying object (UFO) report from a pilot who was flying near Ocala, Florida, sometime between 9:30 and 10:00 PM, EDT, on the evening of 13 September 1963.

2. The pilot reported that he was flying southeast at about 5000 ft when he sighted a bright light that appeared to be hovering over the ground about 1 mile in front of him, and when he got closer, the object flew off to the west about 6 miles in front of him, and when last observed, appeared to start descending to the ground.

3. Did you receive any similar complaints for the night of the 13th? Would appreciate your comments as to a possible cause for this sighting.

4. Thank you for your assistance on this matter.

VICTOR QUINTANILLA, JR., LT COLONEL, USAF
