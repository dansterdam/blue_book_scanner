July 20, 1964

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. Reference is made to your report on the observation of an unidentified flying object over Cambridge, Massachusetts on June 29, 1964.

2. The Air Force project office is unable to provide an identification for the object you reported seeing. However, we would appreciate any further details you can provide regarding this sighting.

3. Your cooperation in reporting such observations is sincerely appreciated, as it provides the Air Force with information that may be of value in the study of unusual aerial phenomena.

Sincerely,

MARION M. JACKS
Colonel, USAF
Chief, Civil Information Division
