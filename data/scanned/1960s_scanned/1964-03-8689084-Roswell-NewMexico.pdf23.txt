SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LOCATION: Roswell, New Mexico 
DATE: Spring 1953
PHOTOGRAPHY:
AF NR: One 35 MM film strip and 4 prints provided by TDEW
PURPOSE: This report answers work request #64-82 submitted by Major H. Quintanilla, USAF. The request forwarded one 35 MM film strip and prints for evaluation of image on the film strip and prints.
ANALYSIS: Foreign Technology Division Photo Analysis and Photo Processing personnel viewed the subject film strip and prints with the following results:
a. We do not feel that the image is caused by an object observed at the time of photography.
b. The image was probably caused by a drop of some type of processing chemical on the film emulsion during processing.
