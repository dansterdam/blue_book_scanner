Middletown, Ohio
August 9, 1968

A. Capt. Walter 0. Kaupa
B. SID

1. On 9 Aug 68, this office received a telephone call from Mr. Albert Radel, Box 1053, Middletown, Ohio, who reported sighting an unidentified flying object.

2. Mr. Radel stated that on 9 Aug 68, at approximately 1900 hours, he and his wife were driving on State Route 63, approximately 5 miles west of Middletown, Ohio, when they observed what appeared to be a small light in the sky. As they continued driving, the light appeared to grow larger and brighter. Mr. Radel stopped his car, got out and observed the object more closely. He described the object as round in shape, approximately 20-30 feet in diameter, and surrounded by a very bright light. The object appeared to hover approximately 100-200 feet above the ground and was silent. Mr. Radel stated the object remained in the area for approximately 10 minutes, then accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight. 

3. No other witnesses were located.

4. No physical evidence was obtained.

5. This report is being forwarded to your office for your information and appropriate action.
