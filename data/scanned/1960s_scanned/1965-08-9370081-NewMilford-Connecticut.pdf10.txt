NEW MILFORD, CONN.
22/9530 - DATEN: 9/1965
This is to report that an unidentified aerial object was observed over New Milford, Connecticut on August 15, 1965 at approximately 10:00 p.m. The object was described as a very bright luminous object which moved at a very high rate of speed. It was observed for approximately 15 to 20 seconds. The object appeared to be gyrating or wobbling as it moved across the sky. It then disappeared over the horizon.
