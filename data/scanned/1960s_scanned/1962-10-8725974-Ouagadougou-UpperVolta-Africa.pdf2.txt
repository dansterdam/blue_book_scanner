INCOMING TELEGRAM
AP IN: 42351 (13 OCT 62) M/db
FROM: Ouagadougou  
TO: Secretary of State
NO: 115, OCTOBER 12, 5 P.M.
PASS TO APGINSUSAF, USAFE AND 1127TH PA GROUP FT. BELVOIR

HAVE EXAMINED TWO PIECES OF MATERIAL FOUND NEAR LATITUDE WEST 3 DEGREES 20 MINUTES AND LONGITUDE 2 DEGREES 25 MINUTES AS INSTRUCTED. IN MY MESSAGE FROM ABIDJAN 10 OCTOBER, I REPORTED THAT ITEMS AND BELIEVE THESE TO BE PARTS OF SAME SEGSTATE.

SHAPE AND CONFIGURATION INDICATES THAT OBJECT MAY HAVE DISINTEGRATED IN HEAT AND BURNING. APPARENTLY NOT A MISSILE. APPEARS TO BE SIMILAR TO PIECE RECOVERED FROM SOACAUVRE AS PER IATRIC PIPE RESEMBLES A GUIDANCE CONTROL ELEMENT. ATTEMPTING TO GAIN POSSESSION OF COMPLETE ARTICLE AS INSTRUCTED. DECISION WILL BE REACHED AS TO FURTHER DISPOSITION.

DOWNGRADED AT 3 YEAR INTERVALS, DECLASSIFIED AFTER 12 YEARS.

TRT

NOTE: RELAYED APGINSUSAF, USAFE, 1127TH PA GROUP
