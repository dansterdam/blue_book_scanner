Discs over Stonehenge

Mr. and Mrs. P. Wilkins of Stonehenge-Amesbury were driving near Stonehenge when, according to the Flying Saucer Review of August 26, Mrs. Wilkins saw "Four shiny objects" coming over a hill. We find the two sides of the cars were level with the tops of these shiny objects, which were no sound in the air above. She drove her husband's attention to them.
