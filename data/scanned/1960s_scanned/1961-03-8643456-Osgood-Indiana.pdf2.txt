REPORT-38 (S/Sgt Packer)

1. On 27 March 1961 Mr. XXXX the report of a UFO sighting which occurred at Osgood, Indiana on 25 March 1961. Information regarding his sighting:

Witnesses: The UFO was reported as traveling from low in the south toward the north at high speed. It was sighted for approximately 15 minutes. The object had a bright light in the center and appeared to be bluish-green in color. There was no noise or trail observed.

As the object was sighted above the general level of the tree tops, it suddenly increased speed and disappeared from sight.

This coordination with the National Space Surveillance Center revealed that an object was tracked on 25 March 1961. The object was tracked over Osgood, Indiana and disappeared from sight at approximately the same time that the reported sighting occurred.

The witness's account of the sighter is considered to be authentic.
