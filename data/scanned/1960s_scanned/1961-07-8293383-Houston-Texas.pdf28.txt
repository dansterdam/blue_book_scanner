2010 hours, accepted collect call from Houston, who reported that an unidentified light, 3-5 miles high, was observed and tracked.
This light was tracked on 13 Jul 1961 from 2010 hours to 2300 hours when it disappeared from view. One of these jets was dispatched to investigate but did not sight the object. This phenomenon has been referred to 2AD.
