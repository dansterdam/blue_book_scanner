JEBEL LEBANON
On the morning of 9 April 1963, at approximately 0630 hours, a round, metallic disc-shaped object was observed for about 1 minute near Jebel, Lebanon.
The object was described as being about 10 to 15 meters in diameter, silver in color, and making a humming sound. It was observed at an altitude of approximately 100 meters, and was moving in a northwesterly direction at a speed estimated to be about 300 to 400 km/h.
The object was observed by several local inhabitants who notified the Lebanese military authorities. No further details are available at this time.
