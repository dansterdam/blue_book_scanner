CASE 679282
REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
BAIR 11365

1. WITNESSES:
Mrs. Jeanette Matthews, 3 Hills Drive, Davidsville, Md. At approximately 7:30 p.m. on 6 February 1967 Mrs. Matthews was outside her home and observed an unidentified luminous object in the sky. The object appeared to be moving in a south to north direction at an altitude of approximately 1000 feet. The object suddenly accelerated and disappeared from sight.

2. DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT:
Mrs. Matthews described the object as round in shape, approximately 30 feet in diameter, and luminous in appearance. The object appeared to be brighter than the surrounding daylight sky.

3. OTHER OBSERVATIONS:
Mrs. Matthews indicated that the object did not make any sound. She further stated that the object accelerated at a very high rate of speed and disappeared from sight within a few seconds.

4. CONCLUSIONS:
Based on the information provided, this incident appears to be a valid sighting of an unidentified flying object. No satisfactory explanation for the observed phenomenon has been determined.
