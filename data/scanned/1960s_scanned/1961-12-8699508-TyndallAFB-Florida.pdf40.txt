SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT (UFO) REPORT

1. REFERENCE IS MADE TO TELEPHONE CONVERSATION BETWEEN CAPT THOMPSON, TYNDALL AFB, AND CAPT HARRIS, ATIC, ON 8 DECEMBER 1961.

2. AT APPROXIMATELY 1515 HOURS, 8 DECEMBER 1961, A WHITE DISC-SHAPED OBJECT WITH A REDDISH GLOW AROUND THE PERIPHERY WAS OBSERVED OVER TYNDALL AFB, FLORIDA, BY CAPT THOMPSON AND SEVERAL OTHER WITNESSES. THE OBJECT DISAPPEARED WITHIN ABOUT 15 SECONDS.

3. WEATHER CONDITIONS AT TIME OF SIGHTING WERE CLEAR SKIES.

4. COORDINATES OF SIGHTING AREA: 30 DEG 04 MIN N, 85 DEG 34 MIN W.

5. NO PHOTOGRAPHS OR OTHER DATA WERE OBTAINED.

6. CAPT THOMPSON IS CONSIDERED A RELIABLE OBSERVER.

7. THIS REPORT IS SUBMITTED FOR YOUR INFORMATION AND ANALYSIS.
