ENGLAND
The sighting occurred about 10:30 P.M. on 11 March 1964 in the area to the north-west of the city of Wakefield, according to the report. The object was described as a red-orange glow with light striations, hovering over the area for a period of time before moving off in the direction from which it came. The report mentions that the object was observed by many people in the vicinity, including some airfield personnel at the Wakefield R.A.F. Station. Mr. Alfred Booth (49), of Langset, Crigglestone, Yorkshire, is cited as one of the witnesses who observed a half-moon-shaped object with an orange glow in the vicinity.
