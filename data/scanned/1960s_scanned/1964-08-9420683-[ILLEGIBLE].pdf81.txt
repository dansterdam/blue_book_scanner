PROJECT BLUE BOOK SPECIAL REPORT NO. 14 84th CONGRESS, 2nd SESSION HOUSE REPORT NO. 129

INTRODUCTION
This Special Report contains information on the scientific study of unidentified flying objects conducted by the United States Air Force under the code name "Project Blue Book." The report presented here was prepared in response to Senate Resolution 139,84th Congress, 2nd Session, and House Resolution 139, 84th Congress, 2nd Session, authorizing and directing the Committee on Armed Services to conduct an investigation and study of unidentified flying objects.

The purpose of this report is to present the results of the Air Force investigation of unidentified flying objects, including the conclusions and recommendations reached. The report covers the period from the inception of Project Blue Book in 1947 through April 1954.

The material in this report represents the combined efforts of many individuals and organizations, both military and civilian, who have contributed to the investigation and study of unidentified flying objects.
