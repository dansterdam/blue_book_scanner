RADISSON WEST 9-68 FRIDAY AUGUST 9, 1968
MIDDLETOWN, OHIO - AT APPROX. 1550 HRS EST ON 8-9-68 OBSERVER NOTICED A ROUND, WHITE OBJECT HOVERING IN THE SKY APPROXIMATELY 25 TO 50 FEET IN DIAMETER. IT WAS OBSERVED FOR ABOUT 10 MINUTES BEFORE IT MOVED RAPIDLY AWAY.
