PROJECT 10373 RECORD
1. DATE - TIME GROUP: 6 NOV 66 1145Z
2. LOCATION: LA PORTE, TEXAS
3. SOURCE: Civilian
4. NUMBER OF OBJECTS: One
5. LENGTH OF OBSERVATION: 15 minutes
6. TYPE OF OBSERVATION: Visual
7. COURSE: Westward
8. PHOTOS: No
9. PHYSICAL EVIDENCE: No

The object was observed to be about 10 feet in diameter and 150 feet long. The object was metallic silver in color and appeared to have bright blue lights on each side. The object had two fins or stabilizers in the back and was traveling at a high rate of speed. The object was tracked for about 10 minutes by the witnesses before it disappeared from sight. Other watchers had observed similar objects in the past, but this was the first time a balloonlike object was launched from Victoria, Texas at 0515. The observer stated that it looked like a balloon of some type.
