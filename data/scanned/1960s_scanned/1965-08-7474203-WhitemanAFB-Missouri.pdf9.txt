Subject: Unidentified Flying Object Report

1. On 26 July 1965, at approximately 0300 hours, the Radar Approach Control Operator at Whiteman AFB, Missouri, observed on the scope an unidentified target. The target was tracked for approximately 35 minutes, during which time it traveled a distance of approximately 30 to 35 miles at an average speed of 1500 to 2000 knots.

2. The object was observed to make a sharp turn and accelerate rapidly as it departed the area at an estimated speed of 3000 knots. It was further observed to climb to an estimated altitude of 50,000 feet and then descend rapidly in a vertical descent.

3. The object was tracked from 0300 to 0335 hours by the Radar Approach Control Operator. Weather conditions at the time were clear with excellent visibility.

4. Upon completion of the tracking, the Radar Approach Control Operator reported the sighting to the Command Post. The sighting was further reported to this headquarters.

5. An investigation was conducted, and a detailed report submitted. A copy of the report is forwarded herewith.
