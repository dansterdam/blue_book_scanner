ENGLAND
6 Jun 63
Survey Sighting . Saucer-like
The sighting occurred in England and was observed by 7 people. The object was described as a saucer-like object with a central dome and there was a bright light emanating from underneath. It was initially observed at an estimated altitude of 2,000 feet and then descended to an estimated altitude of 500 feet where it hovered briefly before accelerating and disappearing rapidly. The witnesses described the object as being larger than a large aircraft and having a metallic appearance. Some strange effects were reported, such as a brief disturbance in radio reception, but no other details are provided.
