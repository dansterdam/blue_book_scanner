Source: American UFO Committee Review, Vol 1, No 2, Sep 64

June 21 Sunnyvale, Calif.: A married couple sighted a brightly lighted UFO about at 10:30 pm. The object only 200 feet in size and had a brilliantly illuminated bluish-white light. Several other witnesses also observed the UFO. The object moved rapidly across the sky and disappeared suddenly, leaving no trace.
