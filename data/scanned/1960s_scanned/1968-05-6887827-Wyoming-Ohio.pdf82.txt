MEMO FOR RECORD
SUBJECT: Telegram from H. A. Miller Concerning a UFO Sighting of 13 May 69
They were flying in a helicopter from Mayaguez (Coast Guard) to Ponce, Puerto Rico on 13 May 1969 at 0930 hrs. Their object suddenly turned bright red, shooting up extremely fast, and disappeared from view. They watched this object for approximately 15 miles. At this point it became invisible. They proceeded to Ponce.
