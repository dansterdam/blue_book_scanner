May 24, 1961
First observed: 23:29 - 23:30
Object was a white, elongated, cigar-shaped craft moving in a southerly direction. Craft made no sound and left no trail. Estimated speed was 1,800-2,000 mph. After moving south for about 2 minutes, it disappeared from sight in the direction of the Gulf of Mexico. The observer could give no apparent explanation for the object observed, and stated the sighting could not have been any type of conventional aircraft.
R. Marshall, Capt, USAF.
