MEMO FOR THE RECORD
Sub j : UTO Rpt, Auburn, Washington

29 July 1968

Captain Shea, SAFGIP, called Lt Marano at approximately 0830 hours, 29 Jul 68 regarding the news release given to the local base. Capt Shea said he had coordinated with the press desk there and they coordinated with NASA. The only thing that had been changed in the proposed release was the following sentence: "Therefore, it seems probable that this rocket body is the stimulus for the sightings."

Lt Marano was informed that the release had been given to McCord AFB on Friday afternoon, 26 July 1968 for release.
