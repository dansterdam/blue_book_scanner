"No Case (Information Only)                                 8 September 1962
Duncanson, Robert:

Report on a report of a bright light seen over the Pacific Ocean near 37-20N 124-08W on 8 September 1962. The light was seen by the crew of a commercial jet aircraft at about 9:00 p.m. local time. The light was described as being larger than a star and stationary for about 5 minutes, then it disappeared. The location of this sighting is in the Pacific Ocean, approximately 300 miles west of the California coast. The Air Force was unable to identify the light object."
