EAGLE RIVER - WISCONSIN
APRIL 8, 1961
Between 1800 and 2000 hours, Mr. Theodore Bublitz reported observing a bright light moving rapidly in the sky in the Eagle River area. 
The object, he stated, appeared to change color from white to red to blue, and was accompanied by a loud humming noise. The object was visible for approximately 10-15 minutes. 
No physical evidence was discovered. The sighting was investigated and evaluated by Project Blue Book. Project Blue Book concluded the sighting was unidentified.
