NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
WASHINGTON 25, D.C.

APR 6 1965

Dear Mr. Bowers,

Your letter dated March 25, 1965, and enclosures have been received and carefully noted.

We have considered the information contained in your letter and enclosures concerning the recent sighting of an unidentified flying object observed by the U.S.S.R. on February 21, 1965, at about 17:57, C.S.T., of which detail and photographs are said to have been obtained. The material you have submitted has been added to the records of Project Blue Book at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base and will be included in the study of this sighting.

Your interest in this matter as well as your excellent cooperation are sincerely appreciated.

Sincerely yours,

Original signed by
Leo V. Abernathy
Chief, Scientific Monitoring
Apollo Flight Operations Office
Office of Manned Space Flight
