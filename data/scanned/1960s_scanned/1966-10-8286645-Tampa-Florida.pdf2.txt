DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON 25, D. C.

REPLY TO
ATTN OF: 15 DOT            24 Oct 1966

SUBJECT: UFO Report

TO: AFSC (FPD)
Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio 45433

1. In accordance with AFR 200-2, the following information regarding UFO's is submitted:

2. UFO's were sighted by Mrs. [redacted] from 2325 on 17 Oct 66.

a. Description of the objects:
(1) Ten of the UFO's were reported as being "donut-shaped." However, the observer said that she could not see through the objects, but they appeared to be hollow out in the center. The eleventh UPO was described as a single bright light.
(2) The objects were larger than the lights on passing airliners.
(3) The donut-shaped UFO's were alternately red, yellow, and green. The sphere of light was white.
(4) Eleven UFO's were sighted.
(5) (See attached diagram drawn by Mrs. [redacted] for flight paths of the UFO's.)
(6) See item (1).
(7) The first UFO, referred to by Mrs. [redacted] as the "mother ship", reportedly shot out a flash of green light.
(8) No sound was heard.
(9) None.

b. Description of course of objects:
(1) The lights.
(2) 10°.
(3) 45° to 65°.
