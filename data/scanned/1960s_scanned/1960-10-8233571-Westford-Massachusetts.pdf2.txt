DETAILS:
Information was received from a reliable source that an unidentified flying object was observed over the town of Westford, Massachusetts, at approximately 0200 hours EDT on 8 October 1960. The object was described as being from 40 to 50 feet in diameter and having a bright orange color. It was observed to hover over a wooded area for a period of approximately 15 minutes, then it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight. Two other witnesses, in separate locations, also reported observing the same object at the same time. 
REMARKS:
This unidentified flying object sighting is being forwarded to your office for further investigation and evaluation. The above information is based upon a report received from a reliable source.
