Pancake Shape Over Michigan
Mrs. Wayne Elliott, while driving her automobile on the outskirts of Grand Rapids, Michigan, sighted an unusual UFO on Monday, December 2, 1963.
According to her description, the object was shaped like a pancake and its altitude of about one half mile and was motionless. Mrs. Elliott said the size of this UFO was about the "same as a woman."
As the object moved, it tipped on edge and quickly disappeared from sight. The total observation time was about three minutes.
