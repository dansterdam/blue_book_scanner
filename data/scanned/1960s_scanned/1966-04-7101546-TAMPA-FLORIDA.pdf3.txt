DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT REPORT
25 April 1966

TO: AFSC (STIG)
Wright Patterson AFB, Ohio 45433

1. In accordance with AFR 200-2, the following information regarding reported UFOs is submitted: 

2. Three different UFOs were sighted on three different occasions by the observer in Tampa, Florida.

a. First sighting:
(1) Six (6) objects approximately the length of four (4) head-lights were sighted by the observer on 7 FEB 1966 at 12:02 while walking her dog in the back yard.
(2) The color of the objects was chrome or silver.
(3) There were no discernible features or details.
(4) The objects made no sound.
(5) The observer said objects were moving in a westward direction.
(6) The objects were hovering (approximately) 50 feet above the ground 300-500 feet from the observer. They remained motionless, hovering above the ground for approximately 5 to 10 minutes. The objects then headed west and disappeared over the horizon.
(7) The manner of observation was ground-visual without the use of optical aids.

b. Second sighting:
(1) Object was the size and shape of a Volkswagen car, and was covered with a white about the size of a softball can top.
(2) Object was sighted by observer outside her residence while walking her dog. The sighting occurred approximately 06002 on 11 Apr 1966.
(3) Object appeared to be made of glass, but the observer was unsure of this feature.
(4) Object appeared over treetops heading in a southerly direction.
