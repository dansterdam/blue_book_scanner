DEPT (UFO) Lt Col Quintanilla/70916/mns/29 May 68
UFO Observation, 20 April 1968
31 MAY 1968

Cincinnati, Ohio 45227

1. Reference your unidentified observation of 20 April 1968, which you reported to the Air Force at Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio.

2. Analysis indicates that you were probably observing the planet Jupiter. At 7:25 p.m. on the 20th of April, Jupiter was due south at an angular elevation of approximately 65 degrees. At 9:30 p.m. it was approximately 55 degrees above the horizon, and was the brightest star-like object in the sky at the time of your observation.

3. Once again, thank you for reporting your observation to the Air Force.

VICTOR QUINTANILLA, Jr., Lt Colonel, USAF
Chief, Aerial Phenomena Office
AEROSPACE TECHNOLOGY DIVISION
PRODUCTION DIRECTORATE
