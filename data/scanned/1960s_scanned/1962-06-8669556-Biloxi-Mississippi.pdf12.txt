KEEBLER TECHNICAL TRAINING CENTER (ATC) 
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE 
KEEBLER AIR FORCE BASE, MISSISSIPPI

REPLY TO
ATTN OF: 03

SUBJECT: Report of Unidentified Flying Object (UFO) 

TO: ATIC 
Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio

1. The following information concerning the report of an unidentified flying object observed at Keebler Air Force Base, Mississippi. Reference paragraph 13, AFR 200-2, 18 September 1959:

a. Description of Object:
(1) Shape - Oval or oblong. 
(2) Size - Length of an office desk.
(3) Phosphorescent or pale white.
(4) Number - One.
(5) Formation - N/A.
(6) Discernible features - Two rows of three lights at each end and light in between - Lights were very bright.
(7) Tail, trail or exhaust - None.
(8) Sound - None.
(9) Speed between 7,200 and 35,000 MPH.

b. Description of Course of Object:
(1) While fishing off pier near Keebler Air Force Base, the object was first observed approximately three degrees above the horizon and proceeded to approximately forty-five degrees above the horizon in two seconds. Azimuth was two-hundred eighty degrees. 
(2) Object moved on a westerly course rising. From the portion to be approximately forty-five degrees above the horizon, it disappeared in a cloud.
