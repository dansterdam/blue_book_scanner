Report on Investigation of a U.F.O. Sighting
On the evening of February 7, 1958, around 9:30 p.m. a volunteer Ground Observer Corps member reported seeing a bright light moving rapidly in the sky from east to west over Hudson, Wisconsin. The observer stated that the object was traveling at a high rate of speed and appeared to be at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet. The object was observed for approximately 30 seconds before disappearing from view. No other details were reported.
