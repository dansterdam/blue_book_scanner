TYNDALL AIR FORCE BASE FLORIDA

OFFICE OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS 

INTELLIGENCE REPORT
1 JUNE 61

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

1. On 8 May 61, this office received a report from Airman First Class James M. Hilton, 3417th Technical Training Squadron, Tyndall Air Force Base, Florida, concerning the observation of an unidentified flying object.

2. Airman Hilton stated that on 8 May 61, at approximately 1045 hours, he was working on the pavement of the flight line as a member of the base fire department when he observed a bright, silvery, disc-shaped object hovering in the sky at an estimated altitude of 3,000 feet. He stated the object appeared to be about 25 to 30 feet in diameter and was moving very slowly in a northeasterly direction. Airman Hilton further stated that he watched the object for approximately 2 to 3 minutes, after which it accelerated to a very high speed and disappeared from sight.

3. This office has conducted an investigation concerning this incident and has determined that no other personnel at Tyndall Air Force Base reported observing the object in question. The Weather Bureau and the FAA have also been contacted and neither organization had any knowledge of the object.

4. This report is forwarded for your information.
