BRIGHT LIGHT SEEN
BY SOUTHAMPTON BEACH
RESIDENTS
SOUTHAMPTON, N.Y., Oct. 24 (AP) - Residents of Southampton Beach reported seeing a bright light in the sky which was so intense that it illuminated the area for a distance of several miles. The light was of a reddish-orange color and appeared to be moving in a southwesterly direction. It was sighted about 8:15 P.M. and was observed for about 30 seconds. The object was described as about the size of a basketball and was estimated to be about 500 feet in the air.
