MEMO FOR THE RECORD
24 SEPTEMBER 1968

20 September 1968
6:55 AM EDT
10-15 min duration

Sighted 7 white lights about mag. of 1st mag. stars due west at about 20 elevation. The lights traveled from SE to NW and disappeared over the horizon in about 10-15 min. There was no noise and observer decided they weren't high flying aircraft because he could see no vapor trail. The angular velocity was fairly rapid. Witness at Ft. Detrick, Md.

Sunrise occurred at 6:55 AM EDT. (JAB)
