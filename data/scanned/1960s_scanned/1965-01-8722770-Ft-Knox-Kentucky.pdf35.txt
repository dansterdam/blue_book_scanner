January 13, 1965...The Lieutenant Commander Family of the 3rd Armored Division, stationed at Ft. Knox, Kentucky, reported an unidentified object of 60-90 feet in diameter hovering over his residence for approximately 15 minutes on the night of 7 January 1965. The object was described as round, glowing in color, and making no sound. It illuminated the area and cast reflections. The object was observed by the witness, his wife, and two children, and was seen to depart at a very high speed.
