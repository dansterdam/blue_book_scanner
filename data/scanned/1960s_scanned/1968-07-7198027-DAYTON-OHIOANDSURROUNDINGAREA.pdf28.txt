TDET (UFO) Lt Col Quintanilla/70916/de/28 Jun 68
UFO Observation, 22 June 1968

Dayton, Ohio 45440

1. Reference your Unidentified Flying Object (UFO) observation of 22 June 1968 which you reported to the Duty Officer at Wright-Patterson AFB.

2. The stimulus for your sighting has been identified as an aircraft that has been determined to be a C-121 aircraft which was in the area on the night of 22 June 1968. This aircraft, when sighted, can be seen as far as 15 miles away. The sign that was observed following the aircraft was a normal light-reflected from the leading edge of the wing and was operating in the normal manner.

3. We have checked and found that there were no other unidentified aerial objects operating in the Dayton, West Carrollton, and Kettering on the night of 22 June.

4. I hope this information is of interest to you. Thank you for reporting your observation to the Air Force.

VICTOR QUINTANILLA, JR., Lt Colonel, USAF
Chief, Aerial Phenomena Branch
Production Directorate
