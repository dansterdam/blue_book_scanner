TYNDALL AIR FORCE BASE, FLORIDA

8 DECEMBER 1961

SUBJECT: UNUSUAL AERIAL PHENOMENA REPORT

1. At approximately 1712 hours, 7 December 1961, the undersigned observed a bright, highly luminous metallic-appearing object moving in a northwesterly direction through the sky.

2. The object was observed for approximately 30 seconds. During this time it maintained a constant speed and altitude, and exhibited no erratic maneuvers. The object disappeared from view behind some high clouds.

3. No sound was heard, and no trail or exhaust was visible. The object appeared to be metallic in appearance and was extremely bright and luminous.

4. This report is submitted for information and record purposes.
