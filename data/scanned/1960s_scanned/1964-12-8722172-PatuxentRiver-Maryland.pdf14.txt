Patuxent Naval Air Station, Maryland, 29 December 1964 
Operators at Patuxent Naval Air Station, Maryland, reported an observation of an unidentified object which came onto their scope at 4,000 yards, came to a full stop, executed a tight turn, and disappeared from the scope. The object was first observed to be traveling east-west along the west coast and has a "very high speed".
