DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
WASHINGTON

OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY

MEMORANDUM FOR AESG
ATTN: LT COL C. A. WIGGERS

SUBJECT: UFO Photographs, Argentina

Reference is made to telecon between you and Dr. Hynek on 22 July 1965. Dr. Hynek advised that as follows: Published in the 19 July Boston Sunday Globe were front page pictures of two UFOs allegedly photographed by newspapermen in Buenos Aires, Argentina. These pictures were reported to have been seen by some hundred people. General reports stated that pictures
 were widely disseminated in Argentine press. Following information is needed:
(1) Information on certain circumstances of the pictures, i.e., what were the lighting conditions; what was the elevation in the sky of the objects.
(2) It is desirable to obtain either the originals or good copies of the pictures.
(3) What kind of camera was used in making pictures.
Information may be sent to Dr. J. Allen Hynek, Department of Astronomy, Northwestern University, Evanston, Illinois.

FRANK WEILER
Lt Col, USAF
Community Relations Division
Office of Information
