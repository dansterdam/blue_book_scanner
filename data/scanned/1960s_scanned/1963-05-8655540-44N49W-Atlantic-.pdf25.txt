7 May 1963
Liverpool, England

Source: Flying Saucer Review (Jul-Aug 63)

ENGLAND
Liverpool: On the evening of 7 May 1963 a Mr. D. was standing in his back garden when he saw a light in the sky which appeared to be approaching him. As it came closer he realized it was a saucer-shaped object. It was bright yellow in color and was rotating and giving off sparks. The object passed over his house emitting a humming sound as it went. Duration of sighting: about 15 seconds.
