MEMO FOR THE RECORD
23 Sept. 1967

Called Sgt. O'Donnell at Cannon AFB, New Mexico. Asked him to call both Melrose AFB Range and White Sands and find out if any missile was fired between 1815 - 1855 hrs on 23 Sept. 1967 as he was notified of a bright light seen in the area. He said he would check and advise if any tests or firings could be disclosed.
