DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE - INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

1. COUNTRY: Chile
2. SUBJECT: Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object
3. DATE OF INFORMATION: 3 Sep 1963
4. PLACE AND DATE OF OBSERVATION: Valparaiso, Chile, 28 Aug 1963
5. DATE OF EVALUATION: 8 Oct 1963
6. EVALUATION: 1
7. SOURCE: X

SUMMARY:
This report forwards translation of a sighting of an unidentified flying object which was seen near the city of Valparaiso on 28 August 1963.

The following is a translation of a report made by the Astronomical Society of Valparaiso on 28 August 1963:

"On 28 August 1963, around 23.00 hours local time, on the Eastern side of the Observatory of Pago Hondo (latitude 33.03'S, longitude 71.35'W), there was observed a luminous object which is close to the top of one of the mountains between the Cerro La Campana and the Cerro Alegre up to an altitude of approximately 20 to 30 degrees. This object was seen for about 30 minutes between 23.00 and 23.30 hours, when it suddenly shot upwards and disappeared in a fraction of a second."
