NO CASE (Information Only) 23 June 1963 
Source: FSYC (July 1963) Medina, Ohio
A lady in Medina, Ohio, which is near Cleveland, called the USAF and reported that she had seen an unidentified object at about 3:10 AM on 6/23/63. This object was observed by Mrs. M.M. Marvin Hall. Mrs. Hall described the object as a bright white color with pulsations or and bands rotating around the central body. The object was like a luminous band with two extension lights revolving about the central body. The object moved WNW and was last seen fading in intensity.
