Investigation of Reported U F O
5 July 1960

Captain Albert J. Marcinko
354th Air Control and Warning Squadron (ACWS)
L G Hanscom Field, Bedford, Mass.

1. You are directed to conduct an immediate investigation of a reported sighting of a U.F.O. in the vicinity of Wilmington, Massachusetts on 27 June 1960. A preliminary investigation has been conducted by the local authorities and the file is submitted to this office for Special investigations which will be made available to you.

2. The investigation will be conducted in accordance with AFR 200-2.
3. Reports of findings will be submitted to this office not later than 18 July 1960.
4. This letter is issued as authority to conduct this investigation in lieu of published special orders which are precluded by the time element involved requiring immediate investigations.

THOMAS G. A. WALSH, Major, USAF
Executive Officer
