REPORT (UD):
REQUEST FOR UFO INFORMATION
28 Jul 65
SUBJECT: Report on UFO Observation, 25 Jul 65, Cuming County, Nebraska.
REFERENCE: Your letter of 4 August 4, 1965, with regard to an unidentified flying object (UFO) sighting that occurred on 25 July 1965 in Cuming County, Nebraska. This letter contains a summary of the information I have gathered concerning this incident.
The observer, whose name has been deleted, reported seeing an unidentified flying object on 25 July 1965, at approximately 12:30 a.m. The object was first observed approximately 2 miles north of Bancroft, Nebraska, and was said to be approximately 20-25 feet in diameter, round, and metallic in appearance. The observer stated that the object appeared to be hovering and did not make any noise. The object was observed for approximately 2-3 minutes before it accelerated at a high rate of speed and disappeared from view.
You may find the enclosed map useful in providing the general location where the sighting occurred.
