Source:* PROJ Bulletin, Vol 2, # 1

Olmsted Falls, Ohio: November 8, 1964 - 6:00 P.M.
The two principal witnesses were Mrs. Alfred Haag, Luanne Haag 14.
They observed a bright object in the sky which they described as cylindrical in shape. The object had pulsating lights and seemed to be hovering, then it moved away at a high speed. They said it was unlike any aircraft they had seen before.
