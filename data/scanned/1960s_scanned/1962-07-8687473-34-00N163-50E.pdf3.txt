No Case (Information Only)
29 July 1962
Northampton, Ohio

Northampton, O. 7/27/62
10130 - 11:30 P.M. A. number of persons gathered outdoors, observed a very slow-moving, light-colored object to the southwest. It was described by the observer as a dark, just visible in the light, seemed to be round or oval, and was at an estimated altitude of about 1000 to 1500 feet. It moved very slowly and disappeared into the distance. No sound was heard. The object was observed for about 10 minutes. The witness stated that he had seen similar objects on several previous occasions but this was the best observation he had made to date. The sighting was reported to the Air Force.
