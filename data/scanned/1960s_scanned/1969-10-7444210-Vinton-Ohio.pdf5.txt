Observation of unidentified aerial phenomenon.

Extremely bright light source observed in sky near ground. Light source did not have discernible shape but appeared to be emitting brilliant white light. Light source was stationary for approximately 2 minutes then accelerated rapidly and silently out of sight. Witness reported feeling a heavy, oppressive feeling during observation, as well as a tingling sensation.
