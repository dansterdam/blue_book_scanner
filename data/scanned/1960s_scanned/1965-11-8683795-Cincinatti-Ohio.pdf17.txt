MEMO FOR THE RECORD
18 November 1963
Major Hance from Air Force Command Post called to report a balloon that was launched from Washington, Pennsylvania. The balloon will reach a height of 60,000 ft. and follow a Northwest-Southeast course.
The balloon is a 20 ft aluminum-type balloon. For additional information, contact Johns Hopkins University at Baltimore, Maryland, Astro Physics Department.
Major Hance received the call at 1500.
