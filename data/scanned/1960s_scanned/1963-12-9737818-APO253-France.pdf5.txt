APO253
9 December 1963
FRANCE

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object

1. On 28 November 1963, at approximately 1800 hours, an unidentified flying object was sighted by several witnesses in the vicinity of VIEUX BOUCAU, France. The object was observed for a period of approximately 10 minutes.

2. The object was described as disc-shaped, approximately 15-20 meters in diameter, and emitting a brilliant white light. The object was observed to hover briefly at an estimated altitude of 300-400 meters, then to accelerate rapidly and disappear from view.

3. No sound was reported associated with the object. No physical evidence or residue was found at the site.

4. The sighting was investigated by the French Air Force and the case is being forwarded to Project Blue Book for evaluation.

5. Recommend that this case be filed as "Unidentified" pending further analysis.
