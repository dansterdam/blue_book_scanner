MEMO FOR THE RECORD
OUSJ: MOTOR CONVENTION, 22 October 1963
22 October 1963

Mr Citroen's office at the Smithsonian Institute called the Aerial Phenomena Research Branch on the afternoon of 22 October 1963. They would like to know if we have received any reports from PIAR, Mt New Hampshire for the night of 21 October 1963. They replied that as of this afternoon we have not received any reports. However, if we do receive any reports from this area we will notify them.
