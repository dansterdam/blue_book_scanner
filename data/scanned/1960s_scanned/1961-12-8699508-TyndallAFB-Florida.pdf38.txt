ENCOUNTER REPORT
8 DECEMBER 1961
TYNDALL AFB, FLORIDA

Tyndall AFB, Florida
8 December 1961

1. On 8 December 1961, at approximately 2020 hours, the Tyndall Air Police Squadron received a call from the Tyndall Base Operations Officer reporting a UFO sighting over Tyndall AFB, Florida.

2. The sighting was made by the Base Operations Officer and the Senior Air Traffic Controller. They observed the object for approximately 10 minutes. The object appeared to be a bright white light, approximately 1/4 the size of the full moon, and was moving in a northwest to southeast direction at an estimated speed of 500-600 mph.

3. The object was tracked on the Tyndall AFB radar scope for approximately 10 minutes. The radar confirmed the visual sighting and the object was tracked for a distance of approximately 40 miles.

4. No further information is available at this time. This report is forwarded for your information and any action deemed appropriate.
