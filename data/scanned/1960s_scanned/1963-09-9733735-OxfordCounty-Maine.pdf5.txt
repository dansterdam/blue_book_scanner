MEMORANDUM FOR THE RECORD

SUBJECT: Unidentified Aerial Object, Oxford County, Maine.

1. On 9 September 1963, at approximately 2215 hours, a civilian in Oxford County, Maine, reported sighting an unidentified aerial object.

2. The object was described as a brilliant, bluish-white light source, approximately 25 feet in diameter, hovering at an altitude of about 500 feet. The object was observed for approximately 5 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from sight.
