REPORT OF INVESTIGATION

On 2 January 1967, special agent investigated the alleged sighting of an unidentified flying object in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, on 6 January 1967. 

The object was first observed at approximately 2100 hours by Mr. Howard Snyder, a resident of Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. The object was described as a dark, rectangular-shaped object, approximately 30 to 40 feet in length, which appeared to hover or move slowly over the Lehigh University campus.
