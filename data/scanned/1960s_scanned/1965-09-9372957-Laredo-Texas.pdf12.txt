DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE 
HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE (AFSC) 
LAREDO AIR FORCE BASE, TEXAS 78041

DCO-OP 20 Sep 65
SUBJECT: UFO

AFSC (FTD)
1. In accordance with AFR 200-2 and our conversation of 7 September, the enclosed reports are for your information and action where appropriate.
2. Our analysis here, of these and other similar reports so forwarded indicates that those we are inclined to be considered of natural origin phenomena such as astronomical sightings, aircraft, satellites, balloons, etc.
3. Reports which we receive which indicate observations of an unusual or otherwise unknown nature will be so treated and forwarded as per basic regulation by expeditious means.
4. Our offices will continue to process each report as objectively as possible; and any information or further instructions from your office we will be glad to entertain.

FOR THE COMMANDER

JOHN P. BLUMER, Major, USAF
Chief, Operations Plans Branch
