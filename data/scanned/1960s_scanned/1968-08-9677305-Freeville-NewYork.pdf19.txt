AFR 80-17(C)
THIS SYSTEM OF EVALUATION OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS IS REQUIRED TO BE USED BY ALL COMMANDS AND REPORTING AGENCIES TO INDICATE THE DEGREE OF CONFIDENCE TO BE PLACED IN OBSERVATIONS MADE. AN "A" RATING INDICATES A HIGH PROBABILITY THAT THE SIGHTING WAS OF A FAMILIAR OBJECT; "E" INDICATES THAT THE OBJECT IS COMPLETELY UNIDENTIFIED.
