MEMORANDUM FOR THE RECORD
6 NOVEMBER 1963
SUBJECT:  Unidentified Flying Object, New Paltz, N.Y., 9 November 1963
1. On 9 November 1963, this office received a METICULOUS from the Office of Special Investigations, SAC New York, which reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object near New Paltz, N.Y., on 9 November 1963.
2. The METICULOUS stated that at approximately 1915 hours on 9 November 1963, a local resident of New Paltz, N.Y., observed an unidentified flying object in the sky near his residence. The object was described as being round or circular in shape, of a brilliant white color, and approximately the size of a large automobile tire. The object remained stationary for several minutes, then moved rapidly off to the southwest and disappeared from sight.
3. The METICULOUS further stated that no other witnesses to the sighting were located, and that no other details were available.
4. This case is being filed without further investigation, as the limited information available precludes any meaningful analysis or evaluation.

CHARLES C. SAPP
Major, USAF
