UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
3 August 1965
Sacramento, Calif.
May Meislin, a carpenter, was proceeding near the Salt Springs Reservoir on the evening of 3 August 1965 when he observed an object, color and size of object unknown, hover approximately 75 feet above the ground for about 2-3 minutes. The object suddenly became intensely bright and appeared to be surrounded by a glowing red light. There were no other objects observed in the area. Meislin stated that he watched the object for approximately 30 seconds when it suddenly accelerated and disappeared, leaving no trail. There was no sound or traffic visible at the time of sighting.
