CASE 12258 - DAVENPORT, IOWA - MARCH 1968 

On 11 March 1968, Mr. Virgil O. Yeager, 603 W. 10th St., Davenport, Iowa, reported sighting an unidentified flying object to the Davenport Police Department. Yeager told police that the object was first observed at 10:30 P.M. on 10 March 1968, and that it had been observed in the sky for over two hours. 

Yeager stated that the object hovered, then moved rapidly off to the south and disappeared from sight. He described the object as oval-shaped, about 40-50 feet in diameter, and reddish-orange in color. Yeager said the object made no sound.

The Davenport Police Department received the report and contacted the U.S. Air Force. The Air Force responded and initiated an investigation through Project Blue Book. No further details of the investigation are available in this case file.
