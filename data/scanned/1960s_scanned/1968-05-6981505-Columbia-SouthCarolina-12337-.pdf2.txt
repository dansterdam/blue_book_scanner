COLUMBIA, SOUTH CAROLINA
12 MAY 1968

1. AT APPROXIMATELY 2130 HOURS, 11 MAY 1968, THIS OFFICE RECEIVED A TELEPHONE CALL FROM MRS. [REDACTED], TELEPHONE [REDACTED], WHO REPORTED THAT SHE AND HER HUSBAND, [REDACTED], HAD OBSERVED SEVERAL UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS (UFO'S) IN THE SKY OVER WEST COLUMBIA, SOUTH CAROLINA, AT APPROXIMATELY 2100 HOURS ON 11 MAY 1968.

2. MRS. [REDACTED] STATED THAT SHE AND HER HUSBAND WERE RETURNING HOME FROM A SHOPPING TRIP WHEN THEY OBSERVED THREE BRIGHT LIGHTS HOVERING IN THE SKY. SHE DESCRIBED THE OBJECTS AS ROUND, BRIGHT LIGHTS, APPROXIMATELY THE SIZE OF A QUARTER WHEN VIEWED FROM THE GROUND.

3. MRS. [REDACTED] STATED THAT THE OBJECTS APPEARED TO BE MOTIONLESS FOR APPROXIMATELY 10 TO 15 MINUTES, AND THEN SUDDENLY ACCELERATED AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED AND DISAPPEARED FROM SIGHT. SHE FURTHER STATED THAT NO SOUND WAS ASSOCIATED WITH THE OBJECTS.

4. THIS OFFICE IMMEDIATELY NOTIFIED PROPER AUTHORITIES AND CONDUCTED AN INVESTIGATION. HOWEVER, NO FURTHER INFORMATION OR EVIDENCE WAS DEVELOPED.
