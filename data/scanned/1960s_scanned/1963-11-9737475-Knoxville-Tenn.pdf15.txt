FLYING SAUCER REVIEW
Jan-Feb 56

La Plata
According to the newspaper "La Razon" of Buenos Aires, on the night of 5th September 1963, between midnight and 1 a.m., a luminous flying object was observed hovering over the city of La Plata. The object, which was powerful light, was spotted by a number of people who were driving through the area at the time.
