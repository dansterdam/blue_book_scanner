Information Report - Project Blue Book

Subject: Reported Sighting of Unidentified Flying Object, Biloxi, Mississippi, June 8, 1962

1. On June 8, 1962, at approximately 10:25 P.M., CST, a report was received from Mrs. George D. Miller, 2208 Marywood Drive, Biloxi, Mississippi, concerning a sighting of an unidentified flying object.

2. Mrs. Miller stated that she was standing in her backyard when she observed a bright light moving across the sky from east to west. She described the object as round and bright, with a reddish color. Mrs. Miller stated that the object moved rapidly and appeared to be at an altitude of approximately 5,000 feet. She stated that the object was in view for approximately 2 to 3 minutes before disappearing from sight.

3. A check of the area weather conditions at the time of the sighting revealed clear skies with good visibility and light winds from the south.

4. No other reported sightings of unidentified flying objects in the Biloxi area were received by the 4770th Air Base Wing, Keesler Air Force Base, on June 8, 1962.

5. This report is being forwarded to Project Blue Book, ATIC, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio, for evaluation.
