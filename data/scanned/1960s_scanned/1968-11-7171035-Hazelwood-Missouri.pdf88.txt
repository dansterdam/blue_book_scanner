U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE/Environmental Science Services Administration/Environmental Data Service
DAILY WEATHER MAPS
WEEKLY SERIES NOV. 11-17, 1968
The charts in this publication are a continuous series of principal charts from the Daily Weather Map. They include the Surface Weather Map, the 500 Millibar Chart, the Highest and Lowest Temperatures Chart, and the Daily Precipitation Chart. These are arranged on a single page of this publication. They are copied from operational weather maps prepared by the National Meteorological Center, Washington, D.C. These maps are called the 'Surface Weather Map' and the '500 Millibar Chart' and are those used previously in Daily Weather Map. An explanation is given in a caption and the legend. Copies may be ordered by writing to: Environmental Science Services Administration, Public Information Office, Rockville, Maryland 20852. Bulk copies may also be ordered, at a cost of $2.50 per 50 copies. Checks should be made payable to the Superintendent of Documents.
