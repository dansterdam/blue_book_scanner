MEMO FOR THE RECORD

Mr. Grander,--A Liaison Officer---Point Pleasant, California
Called 5 Oct 65

Statement: Califorina Drive
Cupertino, California 

Mr. --- was driving south on Budd Road in Cupertino at 2:10 a.m. PST.
Saw a very bright light in sky. This object was brighter and faster than anything he had ever seen. The object was descending toward earth and had a duration of about 4 minutes. Had a very long elliptical tail with many colors. Object was about a black long. Appeared to be a rectangular vehicle. Man turned left and object also appeared to turn left and some toward him. Thereafter it was gone and disappeared. There was no sound.
