REPORT ON UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
LANSING, MICHIGAN AREA

1. On 8 May 1964, at approximately 1930 hours, a resident of Lansing, Michigan contacted the Lansing Air Force Station and reported that he had observed an unidentified flying object over the Lansing area.

2. The observer stated that he was outside his residence cutting the lawn when he observed the object. He described the object as being disc-shaped, silver in color, and approximately 80 to 90 feet in diameter. The object was said to have been hovering at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet for several minutes. The observer stated that the object then moved in a westerly direction at a high rate of speed and disappeared from view.

3. The Lansing Air Force Station was unable to confirm this report through other sources. No other witnesses were located. No aircraft were reported in the area at the time of the sighting.

4. The report has been forwarded to Project Blue Book for further investigation.
