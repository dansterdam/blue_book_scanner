AEROSPACE TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE CENTER
UNITED STATES AIR FORCE
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE
OHIO

28 SEP 1955

TO: AA

SUBJECT: REPORT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

THIS HEADQUARTERS HAS RECEIVED THE FOLLOWING REPORT OF AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT:

LOCATION: OFF NORTH COAST OF LABRADOR
DATE-TIME GROUP: 24 SEPT 1955

DESCRIPTION: TWO ELLIPTICAL SHAPED OBJECTS WITH BRIGHT RED COLOR OBSERVED. OBJECTS WERE APPROXIMATELY 75 TO 100 FEET IN DIAMETER AND TRAVELED AT TREMENDOUS SPEED, ESTIMATED BETWEEN 1,500 TO 2,000 MILES PER HOUR. OBJECTS WERE OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 2 TO 3 MINUTES.

COPY PARA: NAJ CONKEY - LA
