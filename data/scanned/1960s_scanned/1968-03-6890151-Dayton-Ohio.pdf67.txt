AFR 80-17(C1)

WHAT WAS THE ANGULAR SIZE HELD A MATCH AT ARM'S LENGTH IN FRONT OF A KNOWN OBJECT, SUCH AS A TREE OR OTHER COMMON OBJECT? SUCH AS THIS WOULD INDICATE APPROX THE SIZE OF THE COVERED OBJECT. FOR EXAMPLE, IF THE ANGULAR SUBTENSE OF THE PHENOMENON WOULD HAVE BEEN COVERED BY THE MATCH HEAD, THEN THE PHENOMENON WOULD HAVE BEEN COVERED BY THE MATCH HEAD.
