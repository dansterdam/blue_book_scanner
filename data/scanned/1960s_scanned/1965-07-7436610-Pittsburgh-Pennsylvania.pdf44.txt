REPORTED SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT OVER PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA ON 7 JULY 1965.

1. TIME: Approximately 9:45 P.M., EST, Wednesday, 7 July 1965.

2. LOCATION: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, near the West End Bridge.

3. OBJECT DESCRIPTION: The object observed was about 50-60 feet long, about 12-15 feet thick, shaped like a cigar, and had a dark color. There was no noise associated with the object as it moved slowly and steadily at an estimated altitude of about 1,500-2,000 feet above the ground. The object was observed for about 10-15 minutes.

4. OTHER DETAILS: The object was first observed by Mrs. Ida S. Rodgers and her children. They called the attention of their neighbors, Mr. and Mrs. Raymond McGlothlin, who also observed the object. Several other neighbors also saw the object. The object appeared to be under intelligent control as it maintained a steady course and speed. It disappeared from view by moving behind trees in the area.

5. OFFICIAL CONCLUSIONS: No official conclusions or explanations have been made regarding this sighting. The report was forwarded to Project Blue Book for investigation.
