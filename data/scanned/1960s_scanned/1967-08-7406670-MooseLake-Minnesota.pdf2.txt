1 1-2-67
TO: PROJECT BLUE BOOK

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT

On 1-1-67 at approx. 1940 hours, a sighting of an unidentified flying object was observed by the following persons: 
1. Mr. John Strom, a resident of Moose Lake, Minnesota.
2. Mrs. Sue Strom, Mr. John Strom's wife.

The object was observed to be hovering at an approximate altitude of 1,000 feet. The object was described as round in shape, and had a pulsating light. The color of the light was yellowish-orange. The object was under observation for approximately 15-20 minutes before it accelerated rapidly and disappeared from view.

Lt. John Allen of the local police department was notified of the sighting, and a report was filed with Project Blue Book.
