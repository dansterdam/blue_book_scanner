SOURCE; AERO BULLETIN, SEPT-OCT 65
25 Jul 65 
Diamante, Argentina

At Diamante, Argentina, in the early morning of 25 July 65, a disc-shaped object, appearing like a car headlight and giving off red, yellow and green lights, was seen hovering over the area for 10 min. It then headed south and disappeared.
