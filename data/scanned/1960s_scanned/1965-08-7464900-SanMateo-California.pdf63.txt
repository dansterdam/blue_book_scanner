DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
329TH AIR BASE SQUADRON (COMA)
ELLINGTON AIR FORCE BASE, TEXAS 77230

EDO-A 24 August 1965

SUBJECT: Transmittal of UFO Reports

TO: Foreign Technology Division
ATTN: PPL-6(Sightings) ASD, Ohio

1. Copies of all UFO reports received from personnel in the area of Houston, Texas, and transmitted by our telephone conversation on 23 August 1965.

2. I have conducted preliminary investigations on all reports which did not conform with the description of familiar or known objects related in paragraph 1. None of the investigations developed information on all reports which could not be satisfactorily explained.

FOR THE COMMANDER

LAWRENCE E. LEACH, JR., Major, USAF
