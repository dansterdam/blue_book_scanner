REPLY TO
ATTENTION OF: 
UFO Sighting 

TO: Foreign Technical Division, ATIC/Major Quintanilla

1. At approximately 1310 hours on 8 October 1965, Mrs. Kathryn B. Haaagap, Willow Grove, Penna. telephone 1-215-MI-6-3131, stated that both she and her husband observed a strange object in the area over Wayne, Penna. at approximately 0930 a.m. She stated that the object appeared to be round in shape and resembled a bright meteor with a tail. Several lights appeared to separate into two lights and some time later the lights appeared to come back together again. She also stated that the object emitted a flash like lightning in the vicinity of the light.

2. This office contacted the Navy Flight Center at the base. Records indicated that a Navy flight inspection mission was being flown in the vicinity of Wayne, Penna. at the time of the sighting. The results of the flight mission indicate no unusual activity or contacts.
