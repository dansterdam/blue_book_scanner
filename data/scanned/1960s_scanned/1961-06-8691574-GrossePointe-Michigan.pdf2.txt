HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object (Sighting Report)
TO: 1. The following information is reported by the Director, Office of Special Investigations, concerning an unidentified flying object sighting near Grosse Pointe, Michigan on 8 June 1954.
a. Description of the object:
(1) Shape: Circular
(2) Size: Estimated 30-50 feet in diameter
(3) Color: A bright white light, or white or bluish white color
(4) Action: Hovered and disappeared
(5) Sound: None
(6) Duration: 3-5 minutes
b. Description of Course of Object:
(1) Observed through window that faced the east. Brightness of the object attracted attention.
(2) Object was stationary for a few seconds, then started to move rapidly, going straight up and disappearing from sight.
(3) Object just faded out, did not seem to pass out of sight, or disappear - simply faded out.
(4) Object just faded out and left no physical evidence.
