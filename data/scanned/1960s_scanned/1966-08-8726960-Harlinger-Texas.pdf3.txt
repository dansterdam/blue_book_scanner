AF IN: 13059 (9 Sept 66) Pg 2 of 4

LIKE A DOOR OPENING. THREE CHILDREN AGES 6 TO 9 STATED A SMALL PERSON APPEARED IN THE SQUARE OF LIGHT, THEN "DOOR" WAS CLOSED. THE PERSON WAS APPROXIMATELY 3 TO 4 FT TALL AND DRESSED IN BLACK BUT CLOTHING REFLECTED YELLOW OR GOLD COLOR

PAGE 2 RUNNING SIX UNCLAS

IN THE "DOOR" LIGHT, OBJECT HOVERED ABOUT 15 FT ABOVE GROUND NEAR AN ORANGE TREE APPROXIMATELY 35 FT FROM THE HOUSE BUT DID NOT LAND. ON DEPARTURE, A LOW HUMMING SOUND WAS HEARD. OBJECT TRAVELED NORTHEAST TOWARD HARLINGEN AT HIGH RATE OF CLIMB BUT "AVERAGE" SPEED. AT SECOND SITTING, 3 SEP 1966, 2888-2182Z.
ANBS YEAR OLD GIRL WITH A FRIEND OBSERVED OBJECT, TELEVISION SHOWED. THEN WENT OUT, HOUSE WAS LIT UP WITH EERIE RED AND YELLOW LIGHT WHICH SEEMED TO BE CIRCLING OR TWIRLING. ON LOOKING OUTSIDE, OBJECT WAS NOTICED HOVERING IN SAME SPOT AS AT FIRST SITTING NEAR THE SAME ORANGE TREE ABOUT 15 FT ABOVE GROUND.
OBJECT APPEARED TO BE COVERED WITH LIGHT. DEPARTURE DIRECTION AND SPEED UNKNOWN.

C. THE ABOVE SITTINGS WERE REPORTED TO KELLY AFB ON 6 SEP 1966 BY MR. JOHN DRIVE, HARLINGEN, TEXAS. HOME PHONE - BUSINESS PHONE -
