The text of the letter is as follows:

Honorable Lindley Beckworth
Member of Congress
Washington 25, D. C.

My dear Mr. Beckworth:

I have been requested by reply to your letter of January 22, 1953 concerning certain matters referred to therein. 

This matter is receiving the attention of the Department of the Air Force. The details you request are not immediately available, however, and it will take some time to compile the necessary data and give you the information you desire. We will give this matter our prompt attention and will advise you as soon as possible as to the results of our investigation.

With the highest esteem,

Sincerely,
T. F. RODEN, JR.
Colonel, USAF
Deputy Director, Installations
