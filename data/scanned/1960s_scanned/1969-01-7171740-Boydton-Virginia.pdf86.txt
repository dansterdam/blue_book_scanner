REPORT

SIGHTING OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 
BOYDTON, VIRGINIA
JANUARY 7, 1969

On January 7, 1969, at approximately 9:35 p.m., Mr. John Smith, a resident of Boydton, Virginia, reported observing an unidentified flying object in the night sky while driving home from work. 

Mr. Smith stated that the object was round in shape, approximately 50 feet in diameter, and emitted a bright, pulsating light. He further described the object as hovering silently for several minutes before accelerating at a high rate of speed and disappearing from view.

Local law enforcement was notified, but a search of the area yielded no further evidence of the reported sighting. This report has been filed with Project Blue Book for investigation.
