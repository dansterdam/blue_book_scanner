March 7, 1965 -- Sydney, Australia -- Newspaper and Radio Station Switchboards were jammed 345. night "4-5 calls from many persons complaining of 'unusual object' over New South Wales. The Civil Aviation Branch and Royal Air Force said there were no known aircraft in the area where the sightings reportedly occurred. Fairfield Observatory Director Gordon Radford said he counted "at least 1 per minute" at times that night. He said: "From a quarter to midnight, I made five attempts to estimate the light from the mass of each object." AIRHART Section January 1963, Massachusetts.
