THE SUNDAY REVIEW

SPEED FLYING
OBJECT SEEN
Pastrangle

WESTCHESTER REPORT:

What appeared to be a flying object was seen near Patrangle at 2:23 A.M. on October 1, according to reports received by the control tower at the Westchester County Airport. The object was reported to have left a trail of blue-green sparks in its wake as it streaked across the sky at a high rate of speed.

Persons at the airport said they were unable to identify the object. Westchester County police and the Federal Aviation Agency have been asked to investigate.
