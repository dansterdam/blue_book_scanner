OBSERVING A LARGE ELLIPTICAL OBJECT LOOKING WWN2 OVER HUDSON RIVER (APPROX POSIT 40-45N 74-007) AT 0845452. OBJECT OBSERVED WITH NAKED EYE FOR 5 OR 6 MINUTES.
