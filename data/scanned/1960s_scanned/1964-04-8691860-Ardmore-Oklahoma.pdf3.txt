ARDMORE, OKLAHOMA
APRIL 8, 1964
INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: OBSERVATIONS OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS

1. On 8 April 1964, at approximately 2100 hours, the undersigned was contacted by Mr. Sam Dunlap, a resident of Ardmore, Oklahoma, who reported observing an unidentified flying object in the sky over the city.

2. According to Mr. Dunlap, he was standing in his backyard when he noticed a bright light in the western sky. He stated that the object appeared to be hovering or moving slowly in an easterly direction. Mr. Dunlap reported that the object was elliptical in shape and emitted a bright, pulsating light. He further stated that the object suddenly accelerated and disappeared from view within a matter of seconds.

3. The undersigned immediately proceeded to the location and interviewed Mr. Dunlap. His description of the object was consistent with the initial report. No other witnesses were located in the immediate area.

4. This information is provided for your review and evaluation. No further action is recommended at this time.
