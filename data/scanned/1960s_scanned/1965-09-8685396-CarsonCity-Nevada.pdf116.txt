THE DAILY OKLAHOMAN
Bright Objects in Sky Set Phones, Nerves Jingling

As the night continued, so did the UFO reports. Chickasha, Guthrie, Stillwater, Tulsa, Norman and Oklahoma City all reported UFO sightings. The Oklahoma Highway Patrol troopers reported four of them in the area. One trooper saw a UFO which coincided with a call reporting one. The UFO, after two hours, faded out or shot off.
