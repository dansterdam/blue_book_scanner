AERIAL OBSERVATION OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT 
MESA, ARIZONA
8 JUNE 1963

SUBJECT: Aerial Observation of Unidentified Flying Object, Mesa, Arizona, 8 June 1963

1. On 8 June 1963 at approximately 2045 hours, an unidentified flying object was observed in the vicinity of Mesa, Arizona. The object was observed visually and with binoculars and motion picture camera by the reporting individual. The pertinent facts are as follows:

a. The object was first observed at approximately 2045 hours in the west-southwest sky at an elevation of approximately 30 degrees.

b. The object appeared to be circular or elliptical in shape, with a defined outer edge or rim. The object appeared to be reflective or metallic in nature, with a faint glow or luminosity.

c. The object remained stationary for approximately 2-3 minutes, and then began to slowly ascend and move in a northerly direction. The object's rate of ascent and movement appeared slow and steady. 

d. The object was observed visually with the naked eye and with 7x50 binoculars. In addition, approximately 30 seconds of motion picture film was obtained.

e. The weather conditions at the time of the observation were clear with light winds.

f. No other objects were observed in the vicinity of the unidentified flying object.

2. The motion picture film has been forwarded to the appropriate authorities for analysis. The reporting individual is considered to be a credible observer with no known history of misidentification of aerial phenomena.
