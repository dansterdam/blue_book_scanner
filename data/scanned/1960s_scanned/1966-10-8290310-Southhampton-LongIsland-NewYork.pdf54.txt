October 8, 1966 - 2210

Initial report - Mr. X, resident of Southhampton, Long Island, New York, reported sighting a bright, circular light in the sky at approximately 2210 hours on October 8, 1966. The light was observed for approximately 10-15 minutes. 

According to the witness, the object was first observed in the west-southwest sky. It was white in color and approximately the size of a basketball when viewed from the ground. The witness described the object as being completely silent. There was no trail or exhaust visible. He further stated that the object seemed to be maneuvering in an uncontrolled or erratic manner.

There were no other witnesses to this sighting. The weather was clear at the time with good visibility.
