"CO CASE (INFORMATION ONLY)
SOURCE: FLYING SAUCER REVIEW, JUL, AUG 62

More objects over Southampton

On March 31, the Southern Observation Group claimed to have seen some unfamiliar objects In the sky over Southampton. The objects, described as being 'saucer-shaped', flew in a southerly direction at approximately 7,000 ft. altitude. There were four of them, and they were first sighted at about 10.15 p.m. GMT. They were under observation for about 15 minutes.
The objects were described as being quite bright, with a 'silvery' appearance, and they were certainly not aircraft in the normal sense of the word. According to the observers, the objects were travelling at a very high speed, and appeared to be under intelligent control.
The area of observation was immediately east of Southampton, and the objects were travelling in the direction of Portsmouth. That night, there were also several reports of similar objects in the skies over Kent, Surrey and Sussex."
