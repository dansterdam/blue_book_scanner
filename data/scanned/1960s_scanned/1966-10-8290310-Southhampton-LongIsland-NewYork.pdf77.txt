SIGHTINGS IN WINGSBORO, NEW YORK

On 22 October 1966, a student observer reported seeing a bright, elongated object with a glowing reddish-orange color hovering over the area of Wingsboro, New York, at approximately 2030 hours. The object was observed for approximately 5-10 minutes before disappearing from view. No other witnesses were identified at the time of the sighting. The object was reported to have appeared to be approximately 50 feet in length and about 15 feet in diameter. The source of the report stated that the object did not make any sound and did not appear to have any visible means of propulsion. Further investigation is pending to determine if any other observers can be located to corroborate this sighting.
