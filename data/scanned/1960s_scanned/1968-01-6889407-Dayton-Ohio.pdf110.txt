DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS AIR FORCE EASTERN TEST RANGE (AFETR)
PATRICK AIR FORCE BASE, FLORIDA 32925

17 June 1968

REPLY TO
ATTN OF: ETT

SUBJECT: Object Identification

TO: Dr. Joseph H. Purser
Polk Junior College
Winter Park, Florida 32789

Dear Dr. Purser:

1. The material which you sent me arrived in good condition. I plan to obtain guidance on the best method to be used for evaluation and will advise you when it has been returned to Mr. Vidotto or yourself as directed.

2. Your interest and assistance in this matter is greatly appreciated. I will try to keep you informed on the progress of the evaluation and also the findings, if permitted.

Sincerely,

DANIEL E. WILLIAMS, Lt Colonel, USAF 
Chief, Data Base Management Division
Directorate of Foreign Technology
