"A REPORT ON AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECT
Sighted over Arkansas
8 October 1960
At approximately 9:15 PM, on the evening of 8 October 1960, an unidentified aerial object was sighted over the town of Magnet Cove, Arkansas, by several witnesses. The object was observed for a period of approximately 10 to 15 minutes.
Witnesses:
Mr. and Mrs. Clarence Fry, of Magnet Cove, Arkansas. Mrs. Fry is a teacher in the Magnet Cove school system.
Chief Deputy Sheriff John F. Carroll, of Malvern, Arkansas.
Mr. Connie Etter, of Magnet Cove, Arkansas.
Descriptions of the Object:
The object was described as being approximately 40 to 50 feet in diameter, circular in shape, and having a brilliant white light in the center which illuminated the entire object. The object appeared to be hovering at an altitude of approximately 500 to 1000 feet above the ground. It was observed to execute several rapid changes in direction and speed, moving from a stationary position to a rapid rate of speed, and then back to a stationary position. No sound was heard emanating from the object.
The object was observed to descend to an altitude of approximately 300 feet above the ground, at which time it appeared to increase in speed and height, and then disappear from view. During the time the object was under observation, it was not observed to leave any trail or exhaust.
REPORTED BY: Chief Deputy Sheriff John F. Carroll, Malvern, Arkansas.
INVESTIGATED BY: Special Agent, United States Air Force"
