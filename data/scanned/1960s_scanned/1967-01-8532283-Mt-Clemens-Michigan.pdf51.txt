 "TELEPHONE CALL RECEIVED BY USAF AT MT. CLEMENS, MICHIGAN FROM MT. CLEMENS POLICE DEPARTMENT ON 8 JANUARY 1967 CONCERNING UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT SIGHTING.

TELEPHONE CALL RECEIVED AT 7:40 PM EST ON 8 JANUARY 1967 FROM MT. CLEMENS POLICE DEPARTMENT.  ONE OFFICER STATED HE HAD RECEIVED A CALL FROM A CITIZEN REPORTING AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT IN THE AREA.  THE OFFICER STATED HE HAD OBSERVED THE OBJECT HIMSELF AND IT WAS DESCRIBED AS ROUND IN SHAPE, BRIGHT WHITE IN COLOR AND HOVERING IN THE SKY.  THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED FOR APPROXIMATELY 20 MINUTES BEFORE IT SUDDENLY ACCELERATED AND DISAPPEARED FROM VIEW.  THE OBSERVER WAS UNABLE TO ESTIMATE THE SIZE OR ALTITUDE OF THE OBJECT. NO OTHER DETAILS WERE AVAILABLE.  THE MT. CLEMENS POLICE DEPARTMENT STATED THEY WOULD CONTACT THE USAF AGAIN IF ANY FURTHER INFORMATION WAS OBTAINED."
