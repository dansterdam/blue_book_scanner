U. S. NAVAL OCEANOGRAPHIC OFFICE
WASHINGTON 25, D. C.

Mr. F. X. Aller
New Orleans 16, La.

Dear Mr. Aller:

This is to acknowledge with thanks your letter of 10 August 1962, reporting the unidentified celestial object you sighted on 29 July 1962.

The object remains unidentified, but have forwarded your report to the National Aerial Phenomena Agency, which is the best position to furnish the information you seek.

We appreciate the effort and interest you have shown in our marine reporting program and look forward to the continued cooperation of observers like yourself. Additional information as may come to your attention during future observations will be welcomed.

Sincerely yours,

WILLIAM G. WATT
Director, Safety Division
Navigation of the Commander
