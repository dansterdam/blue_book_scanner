CASE OF REPORTED UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT
1. Date and Time of Sighting:
April 8, 1961, 2000 to 2015 hours.

2. Location of Observer:
Eagle River, Wisconsin.

3. Description of Object:
Circular object with rotating lights around the edge. Object appeared to be 30 to 40 feet in diameter.

4. Course of Object:
Object approached from the southwest direction, hovered briefly, then moved rapidly in a northeasterly direction.

5. Observations:
Object was observed by two witnesses, a married couple. It was first sighted at an elevation of about 40 degrees and was in view for approximately 15 minutes.

6. Weather Conditions:
Clear sky, wind calm.

7. Further Comments:
The witnesses reported that the object made no sound. They stated that the rotating lights around the edge of the object appeared to be either blinking or rotating. When the object moved rapidly away, it appeared to gain altitude quickly.
