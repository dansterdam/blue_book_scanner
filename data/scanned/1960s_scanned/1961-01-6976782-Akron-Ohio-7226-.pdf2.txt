REPORT SUBJECT SIGHTINGS OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS
7 January 1961
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS SIGHTED OVER AKRON, OHIO
1. On 6 January 1961, at approximately 2030 hours, several unidentified objects were sighted over the Akron, Ohio area. 
2. The objects were described as being round or oval shaped, emitting a bright orange-yellow color, and traveling at a high rate of speed. The objects were observed by a number of witnesses over a period of approximately 15 minutes.
3. Attempts to identify the objects through visual observation and by tracking with radar were unsuccessful. 
4. No aircraft were reported in the area at the time of the sighting. The weather was clear with good visibility.
5. This incident is being reported in accordance with Air Force Regulation 200-2.
