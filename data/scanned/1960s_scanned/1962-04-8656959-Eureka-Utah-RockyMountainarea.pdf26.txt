POLICE OFFICER SIGHTED A VERY BRIGHT OBJECT IN THE SKY OVER THE ROCKY MOUNTAIN AREA NEAR EUREKA, UTAH. THIS OBJECT WAS FIRST OBSERVED AT ABOUT 8:50 P.M. ON APRIL 7, 1962.
