DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE

SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Object sighted by
Springfield, Ohio, 13 November 1967

TO: FTD (TDBT)

1. Reference is made to telephone conversation between 15 CINS,
Springfield, Ohio, and this office on 13 November 1967, concerning the subject.

2. On 13 November 1967, at approximately 1900 hours, a resident of Springfield, Ohio, advised that at approximately 1900 hours on 13 November 1967, he observed an unidentified flying object (UFO) in the vicinity of Springfield, Ohio. This object was approximately 40 feet in diameter and was flying at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet and moving in a southeast direction towards him. Then the object came towards him, hovered, and then travelled in an easterly direction. He reported this incident to the Police Department of this incident.

3. The above information was furnished to Major QUINTANILLA by a telephone conversation. Major QUINTANILLA said that this thing Tuesday morning, 14 November, and he said it would be handled as he advises.
