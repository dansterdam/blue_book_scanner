AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
American Sector, Berlin, Germany
15 August 1952 - 12 August 1952
SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects Reporting, "FLYOEPS"
DISTRICT: #3, CGI, Berlin, Germany
SUMMARY: Information relative to Unidentified Flying Objects has been obtained from a/c VEACH, CHRISTODOLOU and PFY, assigned to 14649th AACS Squadron, Tempelhof Air Base. On 12 August 1952 they observed round, flat, disc-like objects in the sky directly south of the Tempelhof Air Base. These objects were clearly visible and were observed for approximately 30 minutes. No sounds were audible in connection with objects. No trails were visible. The objects disappeared without any discernible direction of departure.
