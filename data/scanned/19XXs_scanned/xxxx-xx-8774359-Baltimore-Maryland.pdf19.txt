  STATEMENT OF MAJOR ALBERT B. AUSTIN, USAF, INFORMATION OFFICER, DETACHMENT 1, TWENTY-FIRST WEATHER SQUADRON, SELFRIDGE AIR FORCE BASE, MICHIGAN. 
ON JULY 7, 1952, MAJOR AUSTIN WAS INTERVIEWED AT SELFRIDGE AFB BY CAPT JOHN J. WEAVER, PROJECT BLUE BOOK, AIR TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE CENTER, WRIGHT-PATTERSON AFB, OHIO, AND CAPT GORDON E. THAYER, INFORMATION OFFICER, DETROIT FILTER CENTER, SELF-RIDGE AFB, MICHIGAN.

HE STATED THAT HE RECEIVED THESE CALLS FROM APPROXIMATELY 8:00 PM UNTIL ABOUT MIDNIGHT AND THAT THE OBJECTS WERE REPORTED AS BEING STATIONARY AND MOVING AT HIGH SPEEDS.
