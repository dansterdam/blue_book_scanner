AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

1. IN COMPLIANCE WITH TELEPHONE REQUEST, THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION IS SUBMITTED:

ON 3 SEPTEMBER 1952 AT TUCSON, ARIZONA, IT WAS REPORTED THAT AT APPROXIMATELY 1500 HOURS AN UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT WAS SIGHTED. THIS OBJECT WAS ELLIPTICAL IN SHAPE AND OF A BRIGHT GLOW WHICH SEEMED TO PULSATE. IT WAS VISIBLE FOR APPROXIMATELY 3 TO 5 MINUTES. REPORTEDLY THE OBJECT WAS VERY HIGH IN THE SKY AND MOVED VERY RAPIDLY.

PERSONS REPORTING THE OBJECT WERE GEORGE FAWCETT, 6835 E LINDEN STREET, TUCSON, ARIZONA, AND HIS DAUGHTER. THEY STATED THE OBJECT WAS OBSERVED BY THREE PERSONS.

IT WAS REPORTED TO BE APPROXIMATELY ONE-HALF THE SIZE OF THE MOON AND WAS LOCATED NORTH OF THE CENTER OF TUCSON, ARIZONA AT APPROXIMATELY 45 DEGREES ELEVATION.

AIRCRAFT IN THE AREA WERE CHECKED AND NONE COULD BE LOCATED WHICH COINCIDED WITH THE DESCRIPTION OF THE OBJECT SIGHTED.

THE SIGHTING WAS INVESTIGATED AND IT WAS DETERMINED THAT THE OBJECT COULD NOT BE IDENTIFIED. AIR MATERIAL COMMAND HAS BEEN INFORMED OF THIS INCIDENT.
