Incident: 2014

At approximately 0230 31 Oct 14, Staff Met was observing the eastern sky when an object came in sight at a northerly direction at an estimated speed of 800-1000 mph. Altitude was estimated an 20,000-25,000 feet. this would give an approximate altitude of from 6000 to 8000 feet. Observation was made for ward and then backward but always within sight. After a short time it disappeared behind a cloud. No sound was heard.
