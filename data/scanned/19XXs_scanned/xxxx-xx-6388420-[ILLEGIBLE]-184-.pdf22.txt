2. The incident summaries 173 through 233 were checked against related information received by the Air Force News Service and Weather Bureau. Comments based on this review are included.

3. It is recommended that the Guide TO INVESTIGATION OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL OBJECTS be changed as follows:
   Item 12. "Direction of flight toward which the object appears to move" should be deleted, as this information is not consistent and consistent with past investigations.
