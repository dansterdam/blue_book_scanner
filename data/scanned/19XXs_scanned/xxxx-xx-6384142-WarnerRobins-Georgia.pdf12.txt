SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS (UFOS)

SUMMARY:
1. Submitted herewith, in accordance with the provisions of Air Force Letter 200-5, dated 29 April 1952, report of unidentified flying objects observed by this observer on 28 September 1952.
