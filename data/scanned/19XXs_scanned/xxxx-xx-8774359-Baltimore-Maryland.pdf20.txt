"30. Please add here any further comments which you believe are important. Use additional sheets of the same size paper, if necessary.

The following is a detailed report of a strange light seen in sky on night of Friday, August 22nd, 1952 at approximately 11 p.m.

Were brilliant white light, appearance and disappearance of automobile headlight, about 2 miles south of Observation Post 23a kilkenny-Md. High speed started by four object, disappearance: Vanishing and reappearance. Light suddenly appeared brilliant, remained motionless and no sound was heard. Light remained at same position for about 30 minutes or more and then suddenly disappeared as it had appeared.

Before reporting this incident to filter center, we watched light thoroughly to determine whether for a plane. The light could have been that of a searchlight that planes use in night landing. But other characteristics showed it to be different from a plane's behavior. It did not flash or blink in the usual manner of a plane's instrument lights. It remained steady and motionless the entire time it was observed. In addition, there was no sound heard. We have no definite explanation as to what it was."
