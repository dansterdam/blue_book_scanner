SAUCER-LIKE CRAFT SEEN IN SPAIN--TANGIER, ESPANA, 12 AUG 52

According to the Cifra news agency, on the evening of 10 August 1952, a resident of Cuenca, Spain, while walking along the Cuenca-Madrid road, saw an object similar to the so-called flying saucers. It crossed the sky at high speed and gave off two luminous trails.
