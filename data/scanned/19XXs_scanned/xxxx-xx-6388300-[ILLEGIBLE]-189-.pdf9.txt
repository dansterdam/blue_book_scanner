SUBJECT: Unidentified Flying Objects

TO : Commanding General
Air Material Command
Wright-Patterson Air Force Base 
Dayton, Ohio
ATTN: MCREXX-3

1. Reference is made to letter from your headquarters, CIAIS, dated 5 March 1949, subject "Unidentified Flying Objects" and first indorsement thereto by this headquarters, dated 31 March 1949.

2. The incident summaries 173 through 295 have been checked against records maintained by this headquarters and it has been determined that no reports have been forwarded to this headquarters in connection with any of these incidents.

3. It is recommended that the "Guide to Investigation of Unidentified Flying Objects" as described and forwarded as item 12, "Disposition of flight or objects," should be clarified to the extent that pertinent and pertable information is obtained as to the description towards which the object appears to move or maneuver, and that action is taken to obtain a maximum of details of this nature.

FOR THE CHIEF, AIR WEATHER SERVICE

/s/ W. A. WEST
