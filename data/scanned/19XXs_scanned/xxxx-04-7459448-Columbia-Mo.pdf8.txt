DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
HEADQUARTERS FOREIGN TECHNOLOGY DIVISION (AFSC)

SUBJECT: Request for Information

TO:  FAA Control Tower

     Columbia Municipal Airport

Request information on any known aircraft or similar object which could have accounted for the sightings. Possibly some type of air activity could have accounted for the sightings. The following is a list of dates and times of each sighting:

18 April 1957 - 2100 CST
25 April 1957 - 2130 CST 
29 April 1957 - 2115 CST

2. Any information you could provide would be greatly appreciated.

FOR THE COMMANDER

G.C. MANATT, Colonel, USAF
