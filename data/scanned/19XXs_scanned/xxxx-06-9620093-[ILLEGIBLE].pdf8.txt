SUMMARY: An unidentified object was sighted at 1930 CST, 9 June 1952, by 2nd Lt. KENNETH P. KELLY, 2372nd Beach Launch, Forbes AFB, Topeka, Kansas. 

The object was a brilliant red pulsating light. The shape resembled from circular to elliptical as it moved. It was observed for approximately 10 minutes.

The object did not move, make any sound and left no trail. It was observed for position and described as being below the horizon, approximately 10 miles distant.

Neither was CUVY, late afternoon daylight.
