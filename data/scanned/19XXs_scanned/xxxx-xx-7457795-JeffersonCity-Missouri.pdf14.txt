UFO Observation, 17 April 1957

Missouri State Highway Patrol 
Jefferson City, Missouri 63101

1. The Aerial Phenomena Office is investigating an unidentified flying object report which occurred on 17 April 1957 at Cedar Falls, Missouri.

2. The witness stated that he saw a disc shaped object from approximately 9:02 pm until 10:05 pm CDT. The object slowly moved southwest and finally disappeared. It seems likely that the object passed over Jefferson City.

3. Request information on whether you received any incident reports on the night of 17 April 1957 and if so, know what may have been the cause for this observation. Thank you for your assistance in this matter.

VICTOR QUINTANILLA, JR., Major, USAF
Chief, Aerial Phenomena Office
