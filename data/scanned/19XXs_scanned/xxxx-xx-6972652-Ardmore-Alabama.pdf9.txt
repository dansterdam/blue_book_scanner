1. The Aerial Phenomena Office is in receipt of an unidentified flying object (UFO) report from the Decatur, Alabama Airport. The observer, which occurred on 4 November 1967, is approximately 7:15 PM CST. The observer was unable to give a positive identification on the object. He said the object was about the size of a basketball and was hovering at about 2,000 feet. The object then accelerated and disappeared to the west. The observer said the object moved in an irregular manner and was red in color.

2. Request information on whether anyone may have reported a similar object in the area at that time. Do you have any comments as to the plausibility of the sighting as described to you at that time? Do you have any other observations?

3. Thank you for your assistance in this matter.
