EUCLID, Ohio

USAF/SPFI (MAJ) Quintanilla/70315/aps/23May)
UFO Observation, 4 May 1967

1. A control Tower RAPW: Watch Supervisor
Cleveland International Airport
Cleveland, Ohio 44135

2. Our office is in receipt of an observation of an unidentified flying object on 4 May 1967 at 12:13 GMT from the shield, Ohio area. The object was the shape of half a surfboard or the wing of a aircraft. It was approximately 3/4 the size of a full moon and was observed over the vicinity of the airport at the depth of the moon. It was white in color and had red stripes.

3. Request information on any type of aircraft under your jurisdiction which could have accounted for this sighting. Possibly some type of conventional craft with red markings could have been responsible for the sighting.

RKES C. MANATT, Colonel, USAF
Director of Technology and Systems
