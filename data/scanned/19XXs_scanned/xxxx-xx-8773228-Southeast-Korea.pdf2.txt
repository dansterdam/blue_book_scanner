AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT

SUBJECT: FLYOREPT
AREA REPORTED: Southeast Korea 
DATE OF REPORT: 25 August 1952
REPORTING OFFICER: Charles J. Malven, Captain, USAF (ALLO)
SYNOPSIS: This report concerns visual and radar sightings of an unidentified flying object by a pilot and a ground radar observer of the 1st Marine Aircraft Wing, Korea.

1. This report concerns visual and radar sightings of an unidentified flying object by a pilot and a ground radar observer of the 1st Marine Aircraft Wing.

2. The object, flying at an altitude of approximately 10,500 feet in the vicinity of K-3, was reported as a moving ball of fire with a streamer of flame.

3. The "object" has been tentatively identified as a meteor by the Commanding Officer, Marine Ground Control Intercept Squadron 3, Marine Air Control Group 2, 1st Marine Aircraft Wing; however, if such is the case, the object's speed was probably underestimated.

APPROVED:
CHARLES J. RANFTLE
Colonel, USAF
