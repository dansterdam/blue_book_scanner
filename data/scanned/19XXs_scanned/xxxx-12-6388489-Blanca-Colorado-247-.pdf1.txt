Incident 3327

Blanca, Colorado
SUBJECT: Report of Unidentified Flying Object
1. On 12 Sept 48, this Base received a report of an Unidentified Flying Object (UFO) sighted in the vicinity of Blanca, Colorado.
2. Details of this incident are as follows:
WHO: Mr. and Mrs. Cyril F. Williams
LOCATION: Blanca, Colorado
DATE: 12 Sept 48
TIME: 7:55 AM
DESCRIPTION OF OBJECT: Bright silvery object, round or disk-shaped, estimated diameter 30-40 feet. Object moved very rapidly at very high altitude, estimated speed in excess of 500 mph. Object visible for approximately 25 seconds.
ACTIONS TAKEN: Contacted the Air Force. No aircraft reported in area.
FURTHER COMMENTS: Case under investigation. Report filed 12 Sept 48 - Roswell, Colo.
