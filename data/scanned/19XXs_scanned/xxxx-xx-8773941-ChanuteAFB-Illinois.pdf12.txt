AIR INTELLIGENCE INFORMATION REPORT
LOCATION: CHANUTE AFB, ILLINOIS
DATE: 15 AUGUST 1952
SOURCE: - 
SUMMARY: On 15 August 1952, Major - a senior pilot, with approximately 3,000 hours of flying time, was interviewed in his office and stated in substance:
On 15 August 1952, at approximately 1800 hours, while flying a T-33 jet trainer aircraft at an altitude of approximately 1,000 feet, he sighted an unidentified object approximately 2 to 3 miles north of CHANUTE AFB, Illinois. The object was described as 3 feet in diameter, circular in shape, and appeared to be metallic in color. The object was observed to be hovering motionless over the landscape and then accelerated and disappeared behind a group of trees on the south edge of CHANUTE AFB, Illinois. The object was in view for approximately 10-15 seconds. Major - stated that he could not estimate the altitude of the object, but that it appeared to be at approximately the same altitude as his aircraft. There was no apparent oscillation, rotation, or propulsion system visible. The air was clear and visibility was excellent at the time of the sighting. All four persons saw substantially the same thing and were definitely in a state of shock.
