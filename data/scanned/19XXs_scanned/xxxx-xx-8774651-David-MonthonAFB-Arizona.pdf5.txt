TR-136-D2
31 August 1952 - 27 August 1952
Capt. R. H. Pennoyer
25 Aug 52 C-2 4. On 27 August 1952, 2 jet aircrafts seen at approximately 35,000 ft. by a radar operator. The targets were tracked for approximately 25 minutes and disappeared from the radar scope. Two operators simultaneously observed these targets on their own radar scopes. The targets appeared as two bright returns which moved rapidly in a weaving fashion at approximately 600 knots. No identification or designation of these targets has been established.
